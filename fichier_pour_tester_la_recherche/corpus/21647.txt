 Hypochlorous acid is a chemical compound Its chemical formula is HClO or HOCl It is not stable It contains chlorine in its oxidation state It is a powerful oxidizing agent It is made by reacting sodium hypochlorite with an acid that cannot be oxidation oxidized such as phosphoric acid If the acid can be oxidized the hypochlorous acid will be destroyed It is used to make some chemicals It breaks down into hydrochloric acid and oxygen in sunlight It reacts with hydrochloric acid to make chlorine gas It is a powerful disinfectant

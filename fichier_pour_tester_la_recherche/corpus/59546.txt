 Romantic Poetry was one branch of the romanticism Romantic movement The first generation of Romantic poets were primarily Samuel Taylor Coleridge Coleridge William Blake and William Wordsworth Wordsworth The second generation was at its culmination in the s with poets such as Percy Bysshe Shelley Shelley Lord Byron Byron and John Keats Keats The movement showed an interest in the Gothic Medieval art and nature The ballad form was re introduced and the poems often took their form from fairy tale s and folk song s Poets were enjoying writing for themselves rather than senior nobility and later they were the source of inspiration for the Pre Raphaelite painters

 Badrinatha is an important pilgrimage place for the Hinduism Hindus The place is in Uttarakhand state of India and is located at a high wikt altitude altitude in the Himalayas The Hindu scripture s say that the place is sacred to Vishnu one of the Deity gods of Hindus Badrinath gets its name from a tree s name The name of that tree is badri tree or Indian Jujube tree The Botany botanical name of a badri tree is Zizyphus jujba Lam The modern Badrinath temple was established by Adi Shankaracharya in the ninth century after the old temple fell to ruin The Badrinath area is referred to as Badarikaashram in Hindu scriptures The temple was renovated many times because of age or damage by avalanche In the th century the temple was renovated by the kings of Garhwal After the great Himalayan earthquake it was rebuilt by the King of Jaipur

A sport coat is an article of clothing worn by men as part of business or formal attire It is similar to a jacket or suit coat has long sleeves and is often made of cotton or wool Sport coats come in many colors and patterns but the most common are black navy blue brown grey and tweed a special type of grey pattern If a sport coat is cut a slightly different way it is called a blazer The term sport coat came from the fact that they were originally worn when hunting

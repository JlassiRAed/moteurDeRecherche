 Good Morning Revival is the fourth studio album by United States American pop punk band Good Charlotte Good Morning Revival was released on March It was the first Good Charlotte album to feature Dean Butterworth on the drums after Chris Wilson left the band in Benji Madden came up with the name of the album It was the last album to be released on the Daylight Records due to Good Charlotte signing with Capitol Records The Japanese edition featured two bonus tracks Keep Your Hands Off My Girl and Face the Strange

 Mace Windu is a fictional character in the Star Wars series He was portrayed by Samuel L Jackson as a Jedi Master in the prequel movies He was Master of the High Council of the Jedi in episodes I to III He appears in Star Wars Episode I The Phantom Menace Star Wars Episode II Attack of the Clones and Star Wars Episode III Revenge of the Sith He also appears in the computer and television versions of the movies In Star Wars Episode III Revenge of the Sith Windu is betrayed by Anakin Skywalker and killed by Supreme Chancellor Palpatine stub

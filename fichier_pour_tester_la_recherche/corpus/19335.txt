 Comiskey Park was a baseball stadium park in Chicago Illinois For more than years it was the home field of the Major League Baseball team called the Chicago White Sox It opened in the middle of the baseball season and closed after the end of the season It was replaced by the park now known as Guaranteed Rate Field which was built across the street to the south of Comiskey Park The old ballpark was made of steel concrete and bricks Tearing it down was a slow process It took all summer of to finish the job

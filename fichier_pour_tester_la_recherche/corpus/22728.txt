A minesweeper is a small naval warship designed to sweep the water clear of naval mine mines Minesweepers keep oceans and rivers clear for shipping In Great Britain naval leaders knew before World War I that sea mines were a threat to the nation s shipping The real threat was not invasion but blockade aided by mines Fishing net Fishing nets pulled behind boats gave an idea on how to clear mines The Royal Navy used fishermen and their f fishing trawler ishing trawlers to keep the English Channel clear of mines The dedicated purpose built minesweeper first appeared during World War I By the end of the war naval mine technology had grown beyond the ability of minesweepers to detect and remove Minesweeping made big advances during World War II World War Fighting nations quickly adapted ships to the task of minesweeping After World War allied countries worked on new types of minesweepers

A stairlift or stair lift is a special type of chair The user sits in the chair and it moves him or her up the stairs Stairlifts are often used by people who are handicap ped or cannot walking walk up the stairs Stairlifts travel on rails that are bolted to the staircase not the wall Most stairlifts have a battery powered motor The battery allows the user to ride the lift even if the power goes out

 Kataklysm are a Canada Canadian death metal band from Montreal Quebec Kataklysm was formed in The band released their debut studio album Sorcery but it was less chaotic and with a little bit of thrash metal Iacono had vocal lessons for the death metal genre after he was complaining that he was losing his voice during or after the band performed concerts Their fifth studio album Epic The Poetry of War was released on September The album featured a little bit of a melodic approach to death metal however they did not step into the boundaries of melodic death metal The band released their sixth studio album Shadows Dust on October The album is considered to be one of the most successful albums by the members of Kataklysm because of the positive attention that the album received and the sales it gathered Their seventh studio album Serenity in Fire was released on March The album featured Martin Maurais temporarily replacing Max Duhamel on drums Duhamel said that he forced out of the band because of injuries but recovered from his injuries and played with the band on their eighth album In the Arms of Devastation which was released on February The band is known for using a frequently used drum pattern which they call Northern Hyperblast The band was ranked on AOL Radio s list of the best Death Metal bands

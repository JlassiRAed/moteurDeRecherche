 Bad Reichenhall is a spa town and capital capital of the Berchtesgadener Land district in Upper Bavaria Germany It is located near Salzburg and encircled by the Chiemgauer Alps Mount Staufen are both near Bad Reichenhall Bad Reichenhall is a traditional center of salt production obtained by evaporating water saturated with salt from brine ponds The town was the site of a Bad Reichenhall ice rink roof collapse disaster on January when the roof of the town s ice rink collapsed under snow killing and injuring others

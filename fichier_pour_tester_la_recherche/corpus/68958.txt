The Milgram experiment is the name for a number of controversial experiment s in psychology They were done by Stanley Milgram in the s Milgram wanted to find out if people would follow order s even if the orders went against their conscience He proved they would There have been many repetitions and variations with similar results The experiments do not answer the question as to why apparently normal people commit atrocities in wartime However obedience to authority is built into the experiment since the subjects were paid and acting under the instructions of a senior university professor Many were students

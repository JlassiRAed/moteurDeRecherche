 Houston Texans TJ Yates b May is an United States American American football football quarterback He plays for the Houston Texans of the National Football League He played college football for the North Carolinia Tarheels Yates was picked by Houston in the th Round of the NFL Draft Yates became a starter after Matt Schaub suffered a season ending injury He threw three touchdowns next to three interceptions in the regular season but guided the Texans to their first ever playoff appearance Yates played well in a Wild Card win over the Cincinnati Bengals but threw three interceptions in a divisional loss to the Baltimore Ravens

 Saint Genevieve is a saint in Roman Catholic and Eastern Orthodox tradition She is the patron saint of Paris Her feast day is kept on January the rd In she led a prayer marathon in Paris that was said to divert Attila s Huns away from the city In Childeric I besieged the city and conquered it She negotiated to collect food and convinced Childeric to release his prisoners Clovis I founded an abbey where Genevieve could minister She was later buried there The church witnessed numerous miracles at her tomb Genevieve was Canonization canonised

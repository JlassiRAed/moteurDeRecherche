 Fasciola hepatica is the common liver fluke It is a parasitism parasitic trematode in the phylum Platyhelminthes It infects the liver s of various mammal s including humans The disease caused by the fluke is called fascioliasis It has been classified as a neglected tropical disease F hepatica is distributed worldwide and has been known as an important parasite of sheep and cattle for hundreds of years It causes great economic losses in sheep and cattle Because of its size and economic importance it has been the subject of many scientific investigations and may be the best known of any trematode species

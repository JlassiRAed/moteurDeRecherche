 Metroid Prime Trilogy is a compilation video game for the Wii Nintendo Wii console It was released on August in North America on September in Europe and on October in Australia It includes the games Metroid Prime Metroid Prime Echoes and Metroid Prime Corruption In this game Metroid Prime and Metroid Prime Echoes have the same control system used in Metroid Prime Corruption They also have shorter load times and better graphics

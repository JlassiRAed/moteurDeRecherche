 Freiberg is a town in the Free State of Saxony Germany capital of the Freiberg Rural District The town was founded in and has been a centre of the mining industry in the Ore Mountains for centuries In the Flossenburg concentration camp built a subcamp outside the town of Freiberg It housed over female survivors of other camps including Auschwitz Birkenau Altogether or so SS women worked in this camp until its evacuation in April The female survivors later reached the Mauthausen concentration camp in Austria

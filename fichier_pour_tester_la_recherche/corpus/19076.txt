 Lepus is a constellation south of the celestial equator Its name is Latin for hare The hare is not from any figure in Greek mythology Lepus was one of the constellations listed by the nd century astronomer Ptolemy It is one of the modern constellations It is below the constellation Orion It is known as a hare being chased by Orion This constellation should not be mixed up with Lupus Lupus the wolf

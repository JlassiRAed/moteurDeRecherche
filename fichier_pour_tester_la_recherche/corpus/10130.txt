 Jennifer Alice Clack Fellow of the Royal Society FRS is an England English paleontology paleontologist She is curator at the Cambridge University Museum of Zoology and Professor of Vertebrate Palaeontology at Cambridge University Her career is devoted to studying the early evolution of tetrapods Lobe finned fish in the Devonian evolved into tetrapods in a series of transitional fossils Clack adopted the term fishapod to describe these animals They then colonized the freshwater swamp s of the Carboniferous period

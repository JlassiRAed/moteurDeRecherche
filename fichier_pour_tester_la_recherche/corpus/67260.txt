The American Girl series is a series of books and accompanying doll s about fictional girl s who live in different time periods in United States of America America s history The American Girl series was first created by The Pleasant Company in founded by Pleasant T Rowland Today the dolls and books are made by Mattel a large toy company Each doll has her own line of accessories that includes a series of book s about her life furniture clothing pet s and things that she would use or play with For some dolls a doll of her best friend is included in the collection Four of the dolls have had movies made about their stories

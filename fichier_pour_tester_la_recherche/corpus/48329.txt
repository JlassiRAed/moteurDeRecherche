The Charioteer of Delphi is a Greek bronze sculpture from the early Classical period about BC It stands m tall It was discovered in the sanctuary of Apollo at Delphi during excavations by French archaeologists in With the statue were found fragments of a sculpted chariot sculpted horses and a small sculpted person presumably the charioteer s groom All would have been part of a large monument The charioteer monument was commissioned by Polyzalos a Sicilian tyrant to commemorate his victory in the chariot races about BC At some point according to Greek practice the statue was buried probably because of irreparable damage

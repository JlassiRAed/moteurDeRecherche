 Asbarez Newspaper began its publication in August in Fresno as a weekly After several successful decades the offices and publishing headquarters moved to Los Angeles in the early s Today it is still active Asbarez is the official publisher of the Armenian Revolutionary Federation Western United States Central Committee and mainly serves the more than strong Armenian American community in the Western states On May when Asbarez began publishing its English language English section Today that section which became a daily publication in January is the voice of the community and a source of information within the halls of Congress in libraries around the country and newsrooms of major media outlets

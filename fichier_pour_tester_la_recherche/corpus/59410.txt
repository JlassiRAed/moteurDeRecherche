 Coelurosauria is the clade containing all theropod dinosaur s more closely related to bird s than to Carnosauria carnosaur s This is a varied group that includes tyrannosaur s Ornithomimus ornithomimosaurs Compsognathus compsognathids and Maniraptora maniraptors Maniraptora includes bird s the only descendents of coelurosaurs alive today All Paraves discovered so far have been coelurosaurs It is probable that all coelurosaurs were feathered Some diagnostic characteristics of coelurosaurs include elongated arm s and well developed hinge like ankle s An increase in the proportion of the brain occupied by the cerebrum seems to have occurred in the Coelurosauria and continued throughout the evolution of maniraptorans and early birds

 The Giant s Causeway is an area of about interlocking basalt columns the result of an ancient volcanic eruption It is in County Antrim on the northeast coast of Northern Ireland It is a World Heritage Site by UNESCO It was named and a National Nature Reserve in by the Department of the Environment for Northern Ireland In a opinion poll poll of Radio Times readers the Giant s Causeway was named as the fourth greatest Wonders of the World natural wonder in the United Kingdom The tops of the columns form stepping stones that lead from the cliff foot and disappear under the sea Most of the columns are hexagon al although there are also some with four five seven and eight sides The tallest are about metres high and the solidified lava in the cliffs is metres thick in places The Giant s Causeway is today owned and managed by the National Trust It is the most popular tourist attraction in Northern Ireland

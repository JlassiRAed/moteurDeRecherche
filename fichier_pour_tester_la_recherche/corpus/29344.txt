 Prose is the ordinary form of the written plays The word prose comes from the Latin prosa meaning straightforward hence the term prosaic Prose writing is usually adopted for the description of facts or the discussion of whatever one s thoughts are incorporated in free flowing speech It may be used for newspaper s novel s magazine s encyclopedia s broadcast media letter s story stories history philosophy biography and many other forms of media Prose generally has no formal structure like meter meter or rhyme that is often found in poetry Therefore it is used to describe literature which is non poetic and non theatrical There is however a blend of the two forms of literature known as prose poetry

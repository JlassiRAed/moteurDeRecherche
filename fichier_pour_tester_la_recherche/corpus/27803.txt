 The Pacific hurricane season began on May in the east Pacific Ocean Pacific and on June in the central Pacific It ended on November These dates conventionally delimit the period of time when tropical cyclone s form in the east Pacific Ocean This season had a below average number of storms There were twelve tropical cyclones Of those four were tropical storms eight were hurricanes and four were major hurricanes that reached Category or higher on the Saffir Simpson Hurricane Scale In the central Pacific two tropical storms and two tropical depressions formed One of the depressions and one of the storms crossed the dateline to become typhoons

The Ruwer is a river in Germany with a length of a tributary of the Mosel River The valley of the Ruwer is a part of the wine growing region Mosel Saar Ruwer near Trier in Rhineland Palatinate It is famous for its Riesling wines At the lower Ruwer valley are the villages Waldrach Kasel Mertesdorf Trier Eitelsbach and Ruwer Ruwer

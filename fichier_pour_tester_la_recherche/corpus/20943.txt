 Mama is the first mini album of South Korean band Exo EXO produced and released by S M Entertainment on April The album was produced by Lee Soo man who is also the main producer of the group The title of the album is a term that is commonly used in Korea in one person in the family similar to your Highness or your Majesty The main single was composed by Yoo Young jin who is also the co author of other singles of the mini album What Is Love and History

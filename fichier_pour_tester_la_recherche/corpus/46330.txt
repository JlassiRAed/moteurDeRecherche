 Gouache also spelled guache is a type of paint It is also commonly called poster paint It consists of pigment a binding agent and some additives to make it opaque An inert white pigment such as chalk is usually added The essential idea is that gouache covers up whatever is underneath Water colours in contrast are somewhat transparent The fast drying quality of gouache makes it a favourite for en plein air paintings It was also a favourite of J M W Turner Gouache also refers to paintings that use this type of paint

A hallucination is seeing hearing tasting smelling or feeling things that do not really exist Usually people have to be awake and conscious to have hallucinations That way a person who sees or hears something that is not really there has a hallucination Hallucinations are different from dream s When people dream they are not awake Hallucinations are also different from illusion s Illusions are based on real perceptions which are distorted or interpreted in a wrong way Certain drugs can cause hallucinations Some illegal drug s are taken because they cause hallucinations Certain mental illness es can also cause hallucinations In some cases withdrawal of a drug can also cause hallucinations This has especially been reported by people trying to stop taking sleeping pill s or by alcoholic s People who have hallucinations are not necessarily ill lack of sleep can also cause hallucinations Hallucinations just before going to sleep or just after waking up are considered normal

 Tyche is the nickname given to a possible gas giant planet in the Solar System s Oort cloud It is one of several proposed planets beyond Neptune Tyche was first proposed in by astronomer John Matese of the University of Louisiana at Lafayette Matese and Daniel Whitmire say that the points of origin for long period comet s gives a pattern that suggests Tyche s existence They noted that Tyche if it exists should be seen in the archive of data that was collected by NASA s Wide field Infrared Survey Explorer Infrared telescope

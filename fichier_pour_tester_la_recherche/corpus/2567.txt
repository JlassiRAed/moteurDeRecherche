 Isan is the name for a number of closely related dialect s which are spoken in the Northeast of Thailand and neighbouring countries Isan is mostly used for informal communication Isan consists of a number of dialects there is no official language People who speak one dialect usually understand the others When Isan is written down the Thai alphabet is used most of the time Very rarely the Lao alphabet which is also used to write the Laotian language is used About milllion people speak the Isan language as their first language About a third of the population of Thailand and of the speakers of Laotian understand it

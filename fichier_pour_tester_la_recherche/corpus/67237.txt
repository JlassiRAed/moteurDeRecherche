 Tropical Storm Olga was the fifteenth named storm of the Atlantic hurricane season In the second week of December after the official end of the hurricane season a low formed east of the northernmost Lesser Antilles It slowly gained tropical characteristics and late on December the NHC declared it Subtropical Storm Olga while just north of Puerto Rico It is the first post season storm since Tropical Storm Zeta Tropical Storm Zeta in the Atlantic hurricane seasonThe storm impacted many areas affected by Hurricane Noel Santiago Province Two deaths were also reported in Haiti

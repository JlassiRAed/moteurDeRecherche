The Sonian Forest is a large forest in the south eastern part of Brussels Belgium The forest is in the Flemish parts of Sint Genesius Rode Hoeilaart Overijse and Tervuren Uccle Watermael Boitsfort Auderghem and Woluwe Saint Pierre in the Brussels Capitale Region and in the Walloon towns of La Hulpe and Waterloo Thus it stretches out over the three Belgian Regions It used to be connected with Bois de la Cambre and Hallerbos but they split up in It is maintained by Flanders There are some parts of the forest privately held forest and the Kapucijnenbos the Capuchin Wood which belongs to the Royal Trust

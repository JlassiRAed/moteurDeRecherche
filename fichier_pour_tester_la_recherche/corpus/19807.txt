 Romeo is the son of Lord and Lady Montague in William Shakespeare s play Romeo and Juliet He is one of the two protagonists of the story Romeo falls in love with Juliet Capulet and secretly marries her Juliet fakes her death to rid herself of a parental marriage arrangement with Count Paris Romeo is not aware of the fake death He believes Juliet is truly dead and kills himself Leonardo DiCaprio Leonard Whiting Leslie Howard and Douglas Booth have played Romeo in movie adaptations of the play Romeo is also a character in opera and ballet adaptions of the play

 Maya Mikhaylovna Plisetskaya was a Russians Russian ballerina choreographer director and actress She was born in Moscow Soviet Union Plisetskaya gained an interest in ballet at a young age and was active during the Soviet period She has been called by some such as Nikita Khrushchev one of the best ballerinas of all time She was married to composer and pianist Rodion Shchedrin from until her death

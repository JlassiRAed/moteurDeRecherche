 Clara Josephine Zetkin was a German Marxism Marxist theorist and advocate for women s rights Clara Zetkin was born in Wiederau She trained as a teacher at Leipzig Teachers College for Women During her studies she became involved with the women s and labour movements In she joined the Socialist Workers Party of Germany In she proposed the celebration of International Women s Day which is celebrated today every March Clara was a member of the Spartacus League with Karl Liebknecht and Rosa Luxemburg Zetkin died near Moscow

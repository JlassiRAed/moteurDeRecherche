 Temple of the Dog was an United States American Rock music rock band that was formed in by Chris Cornell of Soundgarden He started the band as a tribute to his friend Andrew Wood Before he died Andrew had been the lead singer of Malfunkshun and Mother Love Bone Members of the band included Stone Gossard on rhythm guitar Jeff Ament on bass guitar Mike McCready on lead guitar Matt Cameron on drums and Eddie Vedder providing backing vocals The band only released one album which was also named Temple of the Dog The album was released in and had two hit songs Say Hello Heaven and Hunger Strike Cornell sang the lead vocals on Say Hello Heaven and Vedder sang the lead vocals on Hunger Strike

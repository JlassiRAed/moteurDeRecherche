A chakra is an energy centre in the human body as taught in India n yoga and some related cultures Chakras are also believed in by some New Age adherents Chakra means wheel in Sanskrit A chakra is an area in the body connected with life energy There are seven chakras in the body each is an interface for the flow of life energy A chakra vitalizes a physical body and is associated with interactions of a physical or mental nature Current science is not able to detect or measure chakras

 Constantine Mitsotakis or Konstantinos Mitsotakis is a Greece Greek politician He is the former Prime Minister of Greece from April through October He was born in Chania Crete to a political family his father and grandfathers were members of parliament and the great liberal leader Eleftherios Venizelos was his uncle He graduated in law and economics from the University of Athens He was married to Marika Mitsotakis from until her death on May In January Mitsotakis announced that he would retire from Parliament at the March election years after his first election

 The Federation of Saint Kitts and Nevis is a federal nation made up of two small island s in the Caribbean Sea It is the smallest independent country in the Western Hemisphere The capital city is Basseterre It is on the main island of St Kitts The island of Nevis has some rights of self government and two seats in the federal parliament St Kitts and Nevis is about one third of the way from Puerto Rico to Trinidad and Tobago The headquarters of the Eastern Caribbean Central Bank is in St Kitts That bank controls the East Caribbean dollar

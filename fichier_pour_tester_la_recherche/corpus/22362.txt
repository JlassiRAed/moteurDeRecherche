A parody mass is a musical setting of the mass The parody mass was very popular during the Renaissance Giovanni Pierluigi da Palestrina Palestrina wrote about parody masses Some examples of early parody masses include the Missa Malheur me bat Missa Mater Patris and Missa Fortuna desperata by Josquin Desprez By the middle of the th century a large number of masses composed used the parody technique

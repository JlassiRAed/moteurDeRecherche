 Jean Baptiste Le Moyne de Bienville He helped colonise North America for France and was governor of French Louisiana separate times between and He is also known as Sieur de Bienville The name Jean Baptiste Le Moyne de Bienville is pronounced in the French manner as Zhan Bap teest Lay Moin day Bee ahn Veel or Vill depending on regional accents in Canada America or France In the U S the Anglicized pronunciation is typically Bee ENN vill The title Sieur is spoken as either sure or sir His older brother was the explorer Pierre Le Moyne d Iberville

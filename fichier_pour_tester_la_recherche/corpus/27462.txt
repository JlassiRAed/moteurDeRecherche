 Bacolet is a town on the island of Tobago Trinidad and Tobago Republic of Trinidad and Tobago The town itself lies beneath Fort King George at the Bacelot Bay It is near Scarborough Tobago to the south east of the island capital It is one of the most developed parts of Tobago where the high society of the island lives There are also many villas and hotels for tourists The Dwight Yorke Stadium is located in Bacolet

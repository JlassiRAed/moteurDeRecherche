 Commodore VIC was a home computer released in It was made by Commodore International Commodore Business Machines It was the first computer that sold over million sets The Commodore VIC was powered by a CPU running at MHz Though the system reportedly had KB of RAM users only had access to about KB for BASIC programs It had no internal permanent data storage but could save programs on standard audio magnetic tape tapes and later on inch floppy disks In early this computer was discontinued

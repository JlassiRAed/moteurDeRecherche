 Sodium bisulfate also known as sodium hydrogen sulfate is a chemical compound Its chemical formula is NaHSO It is made of sodium and bisulfate ion s It is a white crystalline solid It is an acid though not as strong as sulfuric acid It can be made by reacting smaller amounts of sodium hydroxide and sulfuric acid If too much sodium hydroxide is used sodium sulfate is made instead It is also made by reacting sodium chloride and sulfuric acid when heated This reaction also makes hydrogen chloride It is used to lower pH

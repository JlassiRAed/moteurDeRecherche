 Mikhail Botvinnik grandmaster and three time World Chess Championship World Chess Champion He was an Electrical engineering electrical engineer one of the few chess masters who achieved distinction in another career while playing top class competitive chess Botvinnik was the first world class player to develop within the Soviet Union This put him under some political pressure but also gave him considerable influence within Soviet chess He played a major role in the design of the World Chess Championship system after World War II After his retirement as a player he coached a few select pupils They included three future World Champions Anatoly Karpov Garry Kasparov and Vladimir Kramnik

 Orix Buffaloes is a Japanese baseball team This team was formed by the Orix BlueWave and the Osaka Kintetsu Buffaloes after the NPB season The team is based in Osaka and Kobe and play home games at Kyocera dome and Kobe Hotto Motto stadium The team has not had good results in recent seasons A former player Ichiro Suzuki now plays for the New York Yankees

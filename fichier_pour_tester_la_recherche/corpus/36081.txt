The Three Peaks Challenge is a challenge in less than hours The three mountains are the highest of each of the three countries in the island of Great Britain Each year many people attempt Many people do not do the challenge for charity They do it simply because they enjoy walking in the mountains and they want to try something hard These people usually do it in small groups Some of them will do the walking and others will do the driving and the cooking for them Some people have completed the challenge very quickly The record is fifteen hours

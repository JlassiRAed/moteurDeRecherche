 Balochistan or Baluchistan is an arid region on the Iranian plateau The plateau is in Southwest Asia and South central Asia between Pakistan Iran and Afghanistan The area is named after the numerous Baloch tribes The Baloch are of Aryan and other races who moved into the area from the west around A D All natives are considered Baloch even if they do not speak the Balochi language Pashto language Pashto Persian language Persian Hazaragi Hazaragi language and Brahui language Brahui languages are also spoken in the region The southern part of Balochistan is known as Makran Now it is recognised by UNPO as an Unrepresented Nation

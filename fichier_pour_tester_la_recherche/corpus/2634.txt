 His Holiness Tenzin Gyatso is the fourteenth Dalai Lama His full name is Jetsun Jamphel Ngawang Lobsang Yeshe Tenzin Gyatso means the superior one Western usage has taken it to mean the priest of the Buddhism of Tibet The term Dalai Lama therefore means the Lama whose wisdom is as deep as vast and as embracing as the ocean In he was awarded the Noble Peace Prize for his work encouraging understanding between different religions and encouraging people and countries to care for one another

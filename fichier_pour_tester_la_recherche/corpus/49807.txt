 Max Payne is a third person shooter video game made by Finnish Software developer developers Remedy Entertainment and published by Gathering of Developers in July for Microsoft Windows Later in ports for PlayStation Xbox and GameBoy Advance were published by Rockstar Games A Apple Macintosh Macintosh port was published in July by MacSoft in North America The game features a neo noir type style and uses graphic novel comic strips in place of animated cutscene s to narrate the game This is because it is inspired by hard boiled detective novels by authors like Mickey Spillane and it was the first game to feature the bullet time effect made famous in The Matrix Max Payne got very good reviews from critics and was praised for its exciting gun play and use of noir storytelling devices As of the Max Payne Max Payne franchise has sold over million copies

The Lithuanian National Prize The prize is formally bestowed on February This is when the decorations and diplomas are presented to the winners at Presidential Palace Vilnius Presidential Palace This is also the commemorating of the anniversary of the Act of Independence of Lithuania The award honors significant recent achievements in cultural fields and works by artists or art collectives The honorees may be citizens of Lithuania or members of the Lithuanian World Community When the award was first established works accomplished during the previous five years were considered this period was extended to seven years in The candidacies are open each year until September and the winners are announced on December Each Lithuanian National Prize consists of a memorial sign a diploma and a monetary grant A single person can only receive the National Prize once Current candidates for the Prize are nominated by professional associations and societies Before the nominations could be made by members of the public The winners are selected by a special committee As of May there were people who had been given the Prize

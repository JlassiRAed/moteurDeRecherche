The Monarchy of Australia is a form of government in which a hereditary monarch is the Sovereignty sovereign of Australia according to the rules of the Australian Constitution This means that the job of King or Queen of Australia passes down through the House of Windsor Royal Family When the King or Queen dies the job passes to their son daughter or next in line to the throne As monarch of Australia they then have ceremonial and constitutional duties as part of the government of Australia The Governor General of Australia does the work of the monarch for the national Government State Governors do the work of the monarch for State Governments of Australia

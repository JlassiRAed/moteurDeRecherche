 Downing Street is a street in London close to Whitehall and Buckingham Palace The official residences of the two most senior Government ministers are here These are the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom Prime Minister and the Chancellor of the Exchequer Downing Street is a few minutes walk from the Houses of Parliament The street was built in the s by Sir George Downing st Baronet on the site of a mansion called Hampden House The Prime Minister the Chancellor of the Exchequer and the Chief Whip all have official residences in buildings along one side of the street The houses on the other side were all replaced by the Foreign and Commonwealth Office Foreign Office in the nineteenth century

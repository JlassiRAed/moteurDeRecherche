 Alan Irwin Menken is an American musical theater movie composer and pianist He is best known for his scores for films produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios His scores for The Little Mermaid Beauty and the Beast Aladdin and Pocahontas have each won him two Academy Awards He also composed the scores for Little Shop of Horrors The Hunchback of Notre Dame Hercules Mulan Tarzan Newsies Home on the Range Enchanted Tangled The Shaggy Dog Swan Lake and Mirror Mirror

A native speaker is someone who speaks a language as his or her first language or mother tongue Native speakers can often speak the language well since it was part of their childhood development A native speaker s language is usually the language their parents speak and or the language of their country of origin It is the only language of a monolingual person and likely the first language of a multilingual person which is acquired naturally in their native environment and which serves as the basis for their sociolinguistic identity It is also a term used to describe native English speaking language instructors who teach English in non English speaking countries stub

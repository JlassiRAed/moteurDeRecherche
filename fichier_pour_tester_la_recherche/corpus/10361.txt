 Dorothea Sophie of Neuburg was a Countess Palatine of Neuburg by birth and later the wife of the two sons of Ranuccio II Farnese Duke of Parma Married to the Hereditary Prince of Parma in she was the mother of two children including the future Elisabeth Farnese Queen Elisabeth of Spain Her first husband died after just three years of marriage Remaining in Parma she was engaged to her brother in law the new Duke of Parma Retiring from the court at the death of her last husband her grandson the future Charles III of Spain conquered Parma after the extinction of the Farnese and Dorothea Sophie acted as regent of the state till his official majority in January She died aged seventy eight just weeks before her second grandson Philip Duke of Parma Infante Felipe of Spain became the next duke of Parma

 Anthropomorphism is making something which is not a human more human like in a story or art This can mean making the thing shaped more like a human It can mean giving it human emotion emotions goals or the ability to talk It is often used in stories The thing or object may be an animal plant deity sound storm idea or anything else The story of the Three Little Pigs has a wolf and pig s who talk and act like humans Mickey Mouse also talks and acts like a human These are examples of a type of anthropomorphism called furry The novel The Call of the Wild also uses anthropomorphism The main character is a dog named Buck Many other characters are dogs and wolves In the story the animals think and act more like humans than real dogs do

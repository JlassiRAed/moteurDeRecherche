A bookmaker bookie or Turf Accountant is person or company that collect gambling bets for sporting events Bets are placed and winnings are paid when the chosen team wins or event happens The most popular bet will be for winning team If chosen team wins person betting will win their money back with some earnings Bookmakers are very popular in United Kingdom UK and Republic of Ireland Ireland The person betting can chose from many variations Most bets are made in sports like soccer football horse racing and Rugby football rugby Some bets can be placed for events too like Oscar or presidential winners The first bookmaker was Harry Ogden opened in s

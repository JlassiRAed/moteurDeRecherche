In geology a rift is caused by tectonic plate s in the Earth s lithosphere moving apart Rifts are usually accompanied by orogeny volcano es and earthquake s The Great Rift Valley in East Africa is an example A rift is the result of the pulling apart or extension of the continental crust crust and lithosphere Mantle upwelling brings hotter asthenosphere up into the colder lithosphere This rise occurs with the thinning and stretching of the lithosphere

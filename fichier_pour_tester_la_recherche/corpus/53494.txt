 Karaikal district is one of the district s of Puducherry union territory It is the nd biggest district of the union territory after the Puducherry district It has the population of more than lakhs The capital is Karaikal port city The district is made of communes Kottucherry Nedungadu Thirunallar Neravy and Thirumalairajanpattinam The Karaikal district formed an integral part of the Pallava kingdom in th century eighth century Poet Sekkizhar describes the town as vanga malik kadar karaikal The famous Thirunallar Saniswaran Temple Karaikal Ammaiyar Temple are in this district The rivers Arasalar River Arasalar Veerasolan River Kodamurutti River Kaveri River s other tributaries reach the sea here The Karaikal Port is one of the Big biggest in South India The Karaikal Airport is being constructed It will be the first airport builted entirely with Private company private Capital capital

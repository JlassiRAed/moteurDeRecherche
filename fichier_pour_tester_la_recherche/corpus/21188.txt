 Pinckney s Treaty is a treaty between Spain and the United States It is also known as Treaty of San Lorenzo or Treaty of Madrid It defined the boundaries of the United States with the Spanish colonization of the Americas Spanish colonies and guaranteed the United States navigation rights on the Mississippi River The treaty s full title is Treaty of Friendship Limits and Navigation Between Spain and the United States Thomas Pinckney negotiated the treaty for the United States and Don Manuel de Godoy represented Spain Among other things it ended the first phase of the West Florida Controversy a dispute between the two nations over the boundaries of the Spanish colony of West Florida The treaty was presented to the United States Senate on February and after several weeks of debate was ratified on March It was ratified by Spain on April and ratifications were exchanged on that date The treaty was proclaimed on August

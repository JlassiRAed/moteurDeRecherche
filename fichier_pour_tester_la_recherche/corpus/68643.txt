 Sun angle is the angle of incidence at which sunlight strikes the Earth at a particular location time of a day and season It controls the amount of heat energy received at this location on the globe and affects the climate due to the Earth s orbit around the sun and the Earth s rotation Seasonal change in the angle of sunlight caused by the tilt of the Earth s axis of rotation axis is the basic mechanism that results both in warmth of the weather and in length of the day

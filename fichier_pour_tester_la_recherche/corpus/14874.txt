 Banlamese more commonly referred to as Hokkien Taiwanese Holo or Hoklo is a language spoken in southern China in the southern part of Fujian Hokkien It is also spoken throughout Taiwan Singapore and two southern counties of Zhejiang province in China Other areas that it is spoken in can be the Zhoushan archipelago off Ningbo in Zhejiang and the part of Guangdong province around Shanwei Soaboe outside China by minorities in many cities in Myanmar Malaysia Indonesia and the Philippines

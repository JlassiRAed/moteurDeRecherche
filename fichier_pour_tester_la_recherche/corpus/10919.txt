 Flint or flintstone is a kind of sedimentary rock made of silica Bands of flint are found in chalk which is an Upper Cretaceous rock laid down in the continental shelf continental shelves of a tropical sea When the chalk is erosion eroded the hard flint nodules survive as pebble s on a shingle beach It may happen that the pebbles later get cement ed into another rock such as a puddingstone In this way they make up a sedimentary rock for the second time

 Guangxi is a provincial level autonomous region of the People s Republic of China where mostly Zhuang people live Guangxi s capital in Nanning Other major cities are Baise Beihai Chongzuo Fangchenggang Guigang Guilin Hechi Hezhou Laibin Liuzhou Qinzhou Wuzhou and Yulin It is near Vietnam and has many mountains It is one of the border frontiers of Chinese civilization Even in the th century it was considered an open wild territory The current name Guang itself means expanse and has been associated with the region since the creation of Guang Prefecture in AD It was given provincial level status during the Yuan Dynasty and in was reformed as one of China s five autonomous regions

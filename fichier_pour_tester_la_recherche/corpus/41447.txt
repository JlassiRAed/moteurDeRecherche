A license allows someone to do something that they otherwise are not allowed to do A person usually has to pay some money and maybe pass a test to get a license A license is usually written but it does not have to be Most kinds of licenses can only be used by the person they were given to Licenses may be temporary or permanent Some licenses can not be taken away once they are given A person with a license is called a licensee For example John has a license to sell vegetables in the market for years from to John grants an exclusive license to Jane to sell vegetables for years from to Here John will be referred as licensor and Jane will be referred as licensee In many countries if a person tries to do something without the correct license to do it he might have to pay a fine or go to prison

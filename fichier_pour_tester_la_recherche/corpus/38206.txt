The Transcontinental Railroad was a railroad built across the United States of America The building of it started when Abraham Lincoln signed the Pacific Railroad Act of into law Much of it was built by the Central Pacific Railroad building east from Sacramento California and the Union Pacific Railroad building West from Omaha Nebraska Much of it was built by people who came to the U S from China and Ireland It cut the time to go across the United States from months to weeks and later days At the time of its completion it was one of the longest railroads in the world

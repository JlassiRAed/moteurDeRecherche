 Wind power in Texas consists of many wind farm s with a total installed generating capacity of MW Several forces are working to the advantage of wind power in Texas the wind resource in many areas of the state is very large large projects are relatively easy to site and the market price for electricity is relatively high because it is set by natural gas prices The Roscoe Wind Farm is the world s largest wind farm Other large wind farms in Texas include Horse Hollow Wind Energy Center Sherbino Wind Farm Capricorn Ridge Wind Farm Sweetwater Wind Farm Buffalo Gap Wind Farm King Mountain Wind Farm Desert Sky Wind Farm Wildorado Wind Ranch and the Brazos Wind Farm

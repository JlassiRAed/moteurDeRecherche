 Xtranormal is a website that hosts text to speech based computer animation animated video clips featuring animated three dimensional characters speaking in monotone computer voices Xtranormal launched in October after four years of earlier software development In the short movie Sleeping with Charlie Kaufman by director J Roland Kelly animated entirely with Xtranormal premiered at the Little Rock Film Festival and was shown at The Rome International Film Festival in Rome Georgia On July Xtranormal was shut down officially

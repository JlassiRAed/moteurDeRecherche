 There are four sorts of links in MediaWiki internal links to other pages in the wiki external links to websites Interwiki links interwiki links inter language links to other language versions of the same wikiTo add an internal link enclose the name of the page you want to link to in double square brackets When you save the page you ll see the new link pointing to your page If the page exists already it is displayed in blue empty pages are displayed in red Selflinks to the current page are not transformed in URLs but displayed in bold The first letter of the target page is automatically capitalized and spaces are represented as underscores

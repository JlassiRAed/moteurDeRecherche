The ouroboros or oroboros is an ancient symbol of a snake serpent or dragon self cannibalism eating its own tail The ouroboros symbol symbolizes eternity or cyclicality It is the sense of something constantly re creating itself Like the Phoenix phoenix it symbolises cycles that begin again as soon as they end The first known appearance of the ouroboros motif is in KV the tomb of Tutankhamun in the th century BC The text is about the god Ra and his union with Osiris in the Duat underworld In an illustration two serpents with their tails in their mouths coil around the head and feet of an enormous god The whole figure represents the beginning and the end of time

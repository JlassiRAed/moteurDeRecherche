 Vercingetorix was a chieftain of the Gallic tribe of the Arverni He led the great Gaul Gallic war against Roman Republic Roman imperialism in BC His name in Gaulish means over king of the marching men the marching men would now be called infantry Vercingetorix was probably one of the first to unite some tribes of Gaul against a common enemy This was the last major uprising of the peoples of Gaul against the Roman invaders

A carboxylic acid is any molecule that has a COOH group This group contains a carbonyl base these compounds are called acid s An example of a carboxylic acid is Acetic acid which is also known as vinegar Carboxylic acids are found a lot in food Many types of fat molecules are actually carboxylic acids For example chocolate and coconut s have these acids They are also used a lot in soap s and detergent s The smaller carboxylic acids dissolve in water The bigger ones dissolve only in organic solvent s

A base load power plant is a power station that usually provides a continuous supply of electricity throughout the year with some minimum Electricity generation power generation requirement Base load power plants will only be turned off during periodic maintenance Upgrade upgrading overhaul or service Base load power plant has the character of slow demand respond a mechanism to match generation with the Electrical load load it supplies Examples of base load power plants are coal fired power plant geothermal power plant tidal power plant nuclear power plant etc

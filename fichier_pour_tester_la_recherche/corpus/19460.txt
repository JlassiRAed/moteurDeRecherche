An unmanned aerial vehicle is an aircraft that is not operated by a pilot on board Drones may have an onboard computer to take care of adjustments to wind and changes in air pressure Sometimes they are programmed to a particular target Important decisions are usually supervised by people on the ground Large UAVs are mostly used by military forces for example for reconnaissance or unmanned combat Some of them are programmed to be a target or to be shot at In addition drones also have civilian uses such as Firefighter firefighting or taking photograph s Drones come in different sizes Wingspans range from a few centimetres to about the size of regular manned aircraft

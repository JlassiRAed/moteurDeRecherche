 The Snowy Mountains known informally as The Snowies is a region of southeastern Australia and the highest mountain range on the island It contains the Australian mainland s highest mountain Mount Kosciuszko height The Snowy Mountains are in southern New South Wales and are part of the larger Australian Alps and Great Dividing Range Unusually for Australia the mountain range have large natural snowfalls every winter Snow normally falls the most in June July and early August Therefore most of the snow usually melts by late spring The Tasmanian highlands are the other alpine region in Australia The range is host to the mountain plum pine a low lying type of conifer The Alpine Way and Snowy Mountains Highway are the major roads that go through the Snowy Mountains region

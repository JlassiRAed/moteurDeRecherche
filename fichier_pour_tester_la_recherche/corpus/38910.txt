 Capitol Hill is the largest historic residential neighborhood in Washington D C The neighborhood covers an area east of the United States Capitol along wide avenues It is one of the oldest residential communities in Washington Its two square miles house people so it isone of the most densely populated neighborhood The phrase Capitol Hill can sometime mean the United States Congress instead of the neighborhood Capitol Hill is also a hill as well as a neighborhood The hill rises in the center of the District of Columbia and extends eastward Pierre L Enfant as he began to develop his plan for the new Federal City in chose to locate the Congress House on the crest of the hill facing the city a site that L Enfant characterized as a pedestal waiting for a monument A large portion of the neighborhood is now designated as the Capitol Hill historic district The name Capitol Hill is often used to refer to both the historic district and to the larger neighborhood around it To the east of Capitol Hill lies the Anacostia River to the north is the H Street Southeast Southwest Freeway and the Washington Navy Yard and to the west are the National Mall and the city s central business district

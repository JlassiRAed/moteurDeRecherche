The Wii Zapper is a light gun add on for the Wii Remote made for the Wii game console It was first released in Japan on October with Ghost Squad It was later released in a package with Link s Crossbow Training a first person shooter shooter video game based on The Legend of Zelda Twilight Princess It was released in North America on November in Europe on December and in Australia on December It allows players to aim at the screen and hit targets To hit targets players have to point the device at the television and pull the trigger It is mainly used for shooter games including first person shooter s and third person shooter s The name is a reference to the NES Zapper a light gun accessory released for the Nintendo Entertainment System

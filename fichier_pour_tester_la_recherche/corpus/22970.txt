 Bahrain was referred to by the Greeks as Tylos It was the centre of pearl trading when Nearchus came to discover it serving under Alexander the Great It is not known whether Bahrain was part of the Seleucid Empire With the lessening of Greek power Tylos was added into Characene or Mesenian From the third century BC to the arrival of Islam in the seventh AD Bahrain was controlled by two other Iranian dynasties of Parthians and Sassanids

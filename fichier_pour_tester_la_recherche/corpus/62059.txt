 Ngupulya Pumani is an Australian Aboriginal art ist from Mimili in the north west of South Australia Ngupulya began painting in for Mimili s community art co operative Mimili Maku Ngupulya s paintings depict scenes and concepts from her family s Dreamtime Dreaming Her mother s ngura and Ngupulya most often paints stories from this Dreaming She uses pale earthy colours in her backgrounds to depict the desert landscape and this is contrasted with patterns of intense bright dots and lines to represent symbols figures and their journeys

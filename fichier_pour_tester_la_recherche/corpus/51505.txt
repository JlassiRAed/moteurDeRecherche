 Planetary system is the general term for a star with planets and other objects in orbit around it It is now clear that many other stars have planetary systems The st century has become a golden era of planetary system discovery such planets in planetary systems including multiplanet systems Hundreds more systems are unconfirmed The closest confirmed system is Gliese at light year s with one confirmed planet whereas the closest unconfirmed system is Alpha Centauri at ly with a planet of Earth mass The closest multi planet system is Gliese at ly with four confirmed planets Of particular interest to astrobiology is the habitable zone of planetary systems This is thought to be the region with the most potential to develop extraterrestrial life

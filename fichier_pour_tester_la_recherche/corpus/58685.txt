 LAM Mozambique Airlines Flight was a scheduled international passenger flight by carrier LAM Mozambique Airlines from Maputo International Airport of Maputo Mozambique It crashed on November into the Bwabwata National Park in northern Namibia en route to Quatro de Fevereiro Airport of Luanda Angola All crew members and ten passengers were from Mozambique nine from Angola five from Portugal one from France one from Brazil and one from China

 The Battle of Hatfield Chase was fought on October at Hatfield Chase near Doncaster in present day Yorkshire The pagan King Penda of Mercia and the Christian king of Gynedd Cadwallon ap Cadfan joined forces to attack Northumbria King Edwin of Northumbria Edwin met them in the southern part of his kingdom In the battle Edwin was killed and his army was defeated Cadwallon destroyed as much of Northumbria as he could This led to the end of Northumbrian dominance over southern England for a short time

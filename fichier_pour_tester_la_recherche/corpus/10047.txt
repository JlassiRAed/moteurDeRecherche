 Bombylius major the large bee fly is a fly which is a bee mimic It is a parasitoid on bees and wasp s The eggs are flicked by the adult female toward the entrance of the underground nests of solitary bee s and wasp wasps After hatching the larvae find their way into the nests to feed on the Larva grubs They can be found in April to June in Europe and North America and some parts of Asia

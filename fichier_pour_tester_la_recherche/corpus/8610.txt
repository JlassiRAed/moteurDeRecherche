 A Hazy Shade of Winter is a hit song performed by Simon and Garfunkel in the s It was covered by The Bangles two decades later The song was released by itself at first but was later released as part of the album Bookends released in This single single is about sadness during winter The Bangles version was released in November Both versions became major hit singles In the late s the Bangles version became more popular than the first version

 Marnie is a American drama movie directed by Alfred Hitchcock and based on Marnie the novel of the same name It starred Tippi Hedren and Sean Connery It tells the story of Marnie a compulsive thief and liar played by Hedren She is befriended by and later marries a rich businessman called Mark Rutland played by Connery He tries to find out more about the tragic events which took place in her past and finally forces her to face up to these events and the secret which her mother kept from her since she was a child

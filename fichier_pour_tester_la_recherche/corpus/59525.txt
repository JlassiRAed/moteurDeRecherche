 Coke is a fuel used mainly in blast furnace s to reduce iron oxide to produce iron It contains carbon and is similar to coal It is made from coal by cooking out things that are not carbon volatiles Volatile parts of the coal such as water gas and tar are driven off by baking This is done in an airless furnace or oven at temperatures as high as Coke is used as a fuel and as a reducing agent in smelting iron ore in a blast furnace The carbon monoxide produced by its combustion reduces iron oxide to the iron product Although coke is more expensive than coal it can be used in households as a clean fuel relatively free of smoke and impurities At the present day its use in domestic heating has been less than oil or gas but in the th century it was often used in kitchen stoves

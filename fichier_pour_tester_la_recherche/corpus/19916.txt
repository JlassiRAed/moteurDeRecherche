 Flying squirrels are a tribe of species in the squirrel family Sciuridae They are sociable noisy rodent s that glide from tree to tree using a flap of loose skin that connects their front and hind leg s They can glide up to feet steering with their tail and landing on tree trunks gripping it with all four feet These squirrels are nocturnal They have a life span of about five years in the wild and about years in captivity Flying squirrels live in forests in North America Europe and Asia

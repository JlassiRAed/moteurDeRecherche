This is a list of political party political parties in Azerbaijan Azerbaijan is a one party dominant state meaning that is a system of majority rule where one political party in this case the ruling New Azerbaijan Party has successively won election victories by a very large majority and is therefore the dominant ruling party which does not have to form coalition s with other smaller political parties as a result The one party dominant state differs from a one party state because opposition parties against the New Azerbaijan Party are allowed but are widely considered to have no real chance of gaining power

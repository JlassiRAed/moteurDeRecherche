The caste system is a system in India that was once mainly used by Hindu s It started as a job system but later became a social class system There are four castes in this system Brahmin priests Kshatriya warrior ruler politician administrative worker Vaishya trader merchant farmer Shudra peasant servant Untouchables or dalit s came much later after the other four groups and consisted of people who swept ashes after cremations or who tanned animals skins There are also castes in some countries other than India Now the caste system is illegal because it is seen to be racist discrimination Last year eighteen people were killed due to violent protests against the system In the past Dalits have been punished for letting their shadows lay on the people that are higher in society than themselves

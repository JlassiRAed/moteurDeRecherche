A kitchen stove cooking stove cookstove or cooker is a Home appliance kitchen appliance designed for cooking food Kitchen stoves rely on the application of heat transfer Conduction direct heat for the cooking process and may also contain an oven used for baking Modern gas stoves were developed already in the s but these remained isolated experiments At the Great Exhibition in London in a gas stove was shown but only in the s did this technology start to become a commercial success The main factor in this delay was the slow growth of the gas pipe network There are still many traditional stoves made which burn wood or solid fuel The look and feel may be very similar to many traditional stoves but the way fuel is burnt has evolved Many also have back boilers for domestic heating

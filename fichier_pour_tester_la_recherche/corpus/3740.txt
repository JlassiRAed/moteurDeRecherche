 Bani Jamra is a village in the north west of Bahrain It is east of Budaiya and south of Diraz It is in the Northern Governorate Before the discovery of oil in Bahrain most of the people were involved in farming especially date palm s Bani Jamra is also famous as a center of traditional fabric weaving a rapidly dying art Almost all of the people of Bani Jamra are Baharna Shia s Leading cleric and political activist Abdul Amir al Jamri and his son Mansoor Al Jamri are from the village

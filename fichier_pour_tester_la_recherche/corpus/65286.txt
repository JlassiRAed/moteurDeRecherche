 Benign paroxysmal vertigo of childhood is a medical problem that happens in children Benign means harmless paroxysmal means something comes and goes suddenly and vertigo means dizziness So BPVC is harmless dizziness in children that comes and goes suddenly The dizziness usually makes children feel like they are moving or spinning or that the inside of their head is moving or spinning Usually BPVC starts in children between two and five years old It often goes away by the age of eight But in some children BPVC may start when they are much younger BPVC is the most common cause of dizziness in children who do not have Deafness hearing loss or a Medical diagnosis diagnosed ear disease It is a common problem that happens to about of children

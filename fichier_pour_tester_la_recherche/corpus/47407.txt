 Finsbury Park Station is a busy transport interchange in North London The interchange consists of an interconnected National Rail station London Underground station and two bus stations The main entrances are by the eastern bus station on Station Place The National Rail ticket office here lies in between one entrance marked by the Underground roundel symbol while the other is marked by the National Rail symbol and provides direct access to the main line platforms There is another exit by the western bus station along Wells Terrace incorporating the Underground ticket office plus a narrow side entrance to the south on the A road A Seven Sisters Road The complex is located in Travelcard Zone

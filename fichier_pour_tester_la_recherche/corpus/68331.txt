 Santa Maria is a municipality in the province of Bulacan Philippines It lies kilometers northeast of Manila and is located at the eastern portion of the province It is surrounded by the towns of Angat and Pandi in the north by San Jose del Monte City in the south by Norzagaray in the east and by Marilao and Bocaue in the west According to the Philippine national census it has people It is divided into barangays These barangays are Bagbaguin Balasing Buenavista Bulac Camangyanan Catmon Caypombo Caysio Guyong Lalakhan Mag asawang Sapa Mahabang Parang Manggahan Parada Poblacion Pulong Buhangin San Gabriel San Jose Patag San Vicente Santa Clara Santa Cruz Silangan Tabing Bakod Tumana

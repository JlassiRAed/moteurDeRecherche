 Nadezhda Filaretovna von Meck was a rich Russia n businesswoman She is remembered today for her strange relationship with the famous composer Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky For many years she paid him a lot of money each month so that he could compose They wrote passionate love letters to one another yet they never actually met one another She also gave financial support to other people in the arts and music especially to the composers Nikolai Rubinstein and Claude Debussy

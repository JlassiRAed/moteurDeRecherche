 The Misadventures of Merlin Jones is a Disney movie starring Tommy Kirk Leon Ames and Annette Funicello It is a family oriented comedy about a high school boy who experiments with hypnosis and creates a mind reading machine It was directed by Robert Stevenson This movie was the next to last Disney movie for both Kirk and Funicello They would make The Monkey s Uncle for Disney in then sign with American International Pictures for their teen beach party movies

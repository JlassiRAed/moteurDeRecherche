 Gurjar or Gojri are an ethnic group of Jammu and Kashmir India and northern and central Pakistan Alternative spellings are Gojari and Gujjari They are also related to the Rajput tribe Gurjari language is close to Rajasthani and Marwari As per historical accounts parts of Rajasthan and Gujarat were known as Gurjar bhumi or Gurjaratra prior to Mughal period in India However in later times circa the th century AD onwards their hold reduced to small principalities During th th century there were Gurjar kings in areas of Meerut and Dadri After this time they gradually fell into a decline They became poor nomads and started living in jungles and forests

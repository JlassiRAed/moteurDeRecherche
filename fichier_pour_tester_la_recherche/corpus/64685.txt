 Falafel is a kind of vegetarian food The word is IPA chart for English pronounced Falafel Falafel is made from chickpea s or fava bean s Spice s are added to these Falafel is either made into a ball or a disc which is then frying fried The dish originally came from Egypt Today it is a popular form of fast food in the Middle East where it is also served as a meze mezze

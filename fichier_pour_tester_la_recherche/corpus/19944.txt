The Piccadilly line is a line of the London Underground It is coloured dark blue on the Tube map It is the fifth busiest line on the Underground network judged by the number of passengers transported per year It is mainly a deep level line running from the north to the west of London via List of stations in London fare zone Zone However there are a number of surface sections mostly in its westernmost parts Out of the stations served are underground It is the second longest line on the system after the Central Line

 Mansehra Tehsil is a subdivision of the Mansehra District Hazara Division Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Pakistan The main or central city of Mansehra is also located in this area It is a major stop for tourists on the Karakoram Highway which leads to China It is also a major transit point to the Northern Areas and locations such as the Kaghan Valley Naran Shogran Lake Saiful Mulook and Babusar Top

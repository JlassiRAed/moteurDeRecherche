 Ludovico Ariosto The poem is a continuation of Matteo Maria Boiardo s Orlando Innamorato It tells about the adventures of Charlemagne Orlando and the Franks as they fight the Saracens with diversions into many side plots Ariosto composed the poem in the ottava rima rhyme scheme and introduced narration narrative commentary throughout the work Ariosto spent the greater part of his life at Ferrara including many years in the service of Cardinal Ippolito d Este and then of Duke Alfonso I His poem Orlando Furioso is designed to exalt the Este family in its final form it was first published in It is the greatest of the Italian romantic epics Ariosto also wrote lyric poetry in Italian and Latin some satires and four comedies Minor works

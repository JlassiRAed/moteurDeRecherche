 George Hilton sometimes credited as Jorge Hilton is a Uruguay an actor He is known for his many spaghetti western performances He was born Jorge Hill Acosta y Lara on July in Montevideo Uruguay but was raised in England Hilton began his career as an extra with bit parts in the mid fifties In he appeared to great effect as Stranger in Any Gun Can Play He went on to become one of the major stars of spaghetti westerns eventually playing Sartana in the last movie of the series I Am Sartana Trade Your Guns for a Coffin

A cassette single is a recording of a single single song sold on a compact audio cassette Record companies introduced this sales format in the s At that time vinyl record album sales were declining in favor of cassette recordings the cassette single was introduced to replace the vinyl record in a similar way The sales of cassette singles did not exceed vinyl single sales to the same extent as cassette albums had As compact disc player s became more common cassette singles were for the most part replaced by CD single s

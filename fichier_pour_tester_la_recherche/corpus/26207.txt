 The swift is a bird in the family Apodidae It is in the order Apodiformes with treeswifts and hummingbirds The swift is very good at flying It spends all of its time in the air The scientific name for the swift Swifts do not want to land on the ground If they land on ground they cannot fly up again Some people say if you see a swift on the ground you should pick it up and throw it up in the air so it can fly again

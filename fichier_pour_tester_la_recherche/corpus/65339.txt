 Attabad Lake is a lake in Hunza Valley in northern Pakistan The lake was created by a landslide which happened in the village of Attabad on January The landslide killed twenty people It blocked the flow of the Hunza River for five months The flooding caused by the blockage covered the Karakoram Highway Six thousand people had to move from upstream villages Another people had to stay where they were because roads were blocked By the first week of June the new lake was deep At that time it flowed over the landslide dam completely submerging lower Shishkat and partly flooding Gulmit By June water outflow from the lake had increased to cubic foot cu ft sec

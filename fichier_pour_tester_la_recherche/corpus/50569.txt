 Grunge music or Seattle sound is a style of rock music that has elements of punk rock and Heavy metal music heavy metal It started in the mid s in the Pacific Northwest of the United States especially in the states of Washington and Oregon It became more popular in the early s Grunge songs often had lyrics with nihilism depression unhappiness or apathy as themes The widespread use of heroin as a recreational drug found its way into the lyrics and careers of numerous grunge rockers notably Kurt Cobain and Layne Staley of Alice in Chains The songs often had light guitars in the choruses The line up for these bands was the typical rock band of guitars bass drums and vocal Although grunge as a movement became less popular by the last s its influence is still heard in many bands today

 Gordon Freeman is the main hero of the Half Life Half Life video game series He is a theoretical physicist from MIT During the series Gordon must win in hard situations often without backup To try to make the game more belivable the character never speaks and there are no cut scenes all action is seen as if the player was Gordon with the player being in control of Gordon s actions at nearly all times The character is seen first as a theoretical physicist working at Black Mesa Research Facility and working in an experiment which accidentally opens an portal releasing confused harmful aliens into Black Mesa In the first Half Life Half Life Gordon Freeman fights through the facility with other workers fighting the aliens as well as a military unit sent in to stop the incident and silence any surviving witnesses In the second game Half Life Gordon is introduced to a military world years after the Black Mesa incident along with a force known as the Combine that took over the world

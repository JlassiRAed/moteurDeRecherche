 Canine distemper is a Virus viral disease that affects animals in the families Canidae Mustelidae Mephitidae Procyonidae and some Felidae It is most commonly associated with domestic animals such as dogs and ferret s although it can infect wild animals as well It is a single stranded RNA virus of the family paramyxovirus and thus a close relative of measles and rinderpest Despite extensive vaccination in many regions it remains a major disease of dogs category Animal diseases category Diseases caused by viruses

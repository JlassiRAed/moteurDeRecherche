 In Wales was re divided for local government in the United Kingdom local government purposes into thirty seven districts Districts were the second tier of local government introduced by the Local Government Act being subdivisions of the eight Administrative divisions of Wales Counties counties introduced at the same time This system of two tier local government was abolished in and replaced with the current system of unitary Subdivisions of Wales principal areas Each district was administered by an elected district council The council was entitled to petition for a charter granting borough status whereupon the district became a borough and the district council a borough council headed by a mayor In addition a district could be granted Letters Patent granting city status

 Homo ergaster also called African Homo erectus is an extinct chronospecies There is still disagreement on the subject of the classification and ancestry of H ergaster However it is now widely accepted as the direct ancestor of Asian Homo erectus Homo neanderthalensis Homo heidelbergensis and hence Homo sapiens It is one of the earliest members of the genus Homo possibly ancestral to or sharing a common ancestor with Homo erectus The name ergaster means workman in reference to the Acheulean hand axe industry developed by the species The species period in eastern and southern Africa coincided with the first part of the Pleistocene the cooling of the global climate and the start of the ice age s These events would have shrunk the rainforest s in Africa and expanded the savannah s and open forests to which ergaster was well suited Turkana boy

 Chondroitin sulfate is a sulfated glycosaminoglycan It is usually found attached to protein s as part of a proteoglycan A chondroitin chain can have over individual sugars each of which can be sulfated in variable positions and quantities Chondroitin sulfate is an important structural component of cartilage and provides much of its resistance to compressive stress compression Along with glucosamine chondroitin sulfate has become a widely used dietary supplement for treatment of osteoarthritis It is commonly extracted from shark cartilage

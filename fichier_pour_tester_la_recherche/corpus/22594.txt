 Surabaya the second largest city in Indonesia is the capital of East Java The meaning of Surabaya is combination of sura meaning shark and baya meaning crocodile There are about million people living in Surabaya and they speak a dialect of Malay language Malay called Suroboyoan Surabaya is the second largest city in Indonesia after Jakarta Surabaya is located on the edge of the northern coast of Java Island and dealing with the Madura Strait and Java Sea

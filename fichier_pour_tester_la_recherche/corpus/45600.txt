 Vexillology is the study of flag s The word comes from both the Latin word vexilla little sail for flag plus the Ancient Greek language Greek suffix ology that means study of The vexillum was a particular kind of flag used by the Roman legion s Vexillology is a kind of study of symbol s related to heraldry A person who studies flags is a vexillologist and a person who designs flags is a vexillographer Because they are flown outside where they move with the wind flags are usually designed with bold images and bright basic colors Flag design also follows a historical process where later flag designs are based on earlier flag designs This means that many flags in an area may look similar because they are all based on one historical flag Examples include the Pan African colors Pan Arab colors and the Nordic Cross

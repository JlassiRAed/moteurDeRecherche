The Roerich Pact is a treaty on Protection of Artistic and Scientific Institutions and Historic Monuments signed by the representatives of states in the Oval Office of the White House on April As of January the Roerich Pact had been ratified by ten nations Brazil Chile Colombia Cuba the Dominican Republic El Salvador Guatemala Mexico the United States and Venezuela It went into effect on August The Government of India approved the Treaty in but did not take any further formal action The Roerich Pact is also known as Pax Cultura The most important part of the Roerich Pact is the legal recognition that the protection of culture is always more important than any military necessity

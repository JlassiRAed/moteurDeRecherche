A wall is a vertical dividing surface It divides space in buildings into rooms or protects buildings It is usually made of stone or brick Walls have two main purposes to support the top part of buildings and to divide space giving protection from invasion and weather Before artillery was invented many cities had protective walls Since they are not suitable for defense any more most of these walls have been removed The term the Wall usually referred to the Berlin Wall built during the Cold War which fell in but may also refer to the Pink Floyd album of the same name

 Sepals are one of the four basic parts of a flower They are found below the petal s The sepals are often small and green Some flowers do not have petals Then the sepals may be big and colorful In some flowers the petals and sepals look the same They are both called tepal s All the sepals together are called calyx The number of sepals is different for different flowers Both petals and sepals surround the reproductive parts inside the flower The term for sepals plus petals is the perianth The main job of the sepals is to keep the parts of the flower safe before it opens They are usually green and leaf like

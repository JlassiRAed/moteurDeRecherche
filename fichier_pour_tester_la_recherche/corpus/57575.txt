 Jacobus Arminius Arminius was a professor of theology at the University of Leiden from until he died in He wrote many books and papers on theology which is the study of God and his views became the basis for the Dutch Remonstrants After he died his views on theology were most widely known for being the opposite of the five points of Calvinism though Arminius only objected to three unconditional election limited atonement and irresistible grace and he doubted the perseverance of the saints His view on total depravity agreed with John Calvin only changing the view held by Calvinists a little bit

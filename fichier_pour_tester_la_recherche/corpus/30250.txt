 On the Buses is a United Kingdom British sitcom set in fiction al Luxton in Essex It ran from February until May The show stars Reg Varney as bus driver Stan Butler Bob Grant Stephen Lewis as their inspector Blakey Anna Karen plays Stan s sister Olive Rudge and Michael Robbins plays her husband Arthur Rudge The role of Stan s widow ed mother Mabel Butler was played by Cicely Courtneidge in series and by Doris Hare in the other series

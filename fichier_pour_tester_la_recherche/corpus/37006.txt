 Lode Van Den Bergh pseudonym Aster Berkhof and Piet Visser is a Belgium Belgian writer He is married to Nora Steyaert He was born in Rijkevorsel He started his career as a monitor at the Catholic University of Leuven He also worked as a teacher at the Royal Athenaea in Antwerp Brussels and Koekelberg Finally he became a professor at the University of Antwerp Saint Ignatius Faculty in Antwerp

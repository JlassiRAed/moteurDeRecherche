A sticky note is a small piece of paper with a strip of glue along one edge that make it sticky so you can stick it to things The most common sticky note is the Post It which is made by a company called M In a man named Arthur Fry came up with the idea for the sticky note He used a glue that was originally too weak to be used for anything else so the notes would come off easy M started to sell Post Its in In the patent for the glue that M uses on Post It notes expired so many other companies now make similar sticky notes

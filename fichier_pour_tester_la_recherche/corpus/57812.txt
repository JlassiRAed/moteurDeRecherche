 Lories and lorikeets are small to medium sized arboreal parrot s They have special brush tipped tongues to feed on nectar of blossoms and soft fruits preferably berries They are a monophyletic group in the parrot family Psittacidae Sequence analysis and morphology show that the group is in the middle of other groups They are widely distributed throughout the Australasia n region including south eastern Asia Polynesia Papua New Guinea Timor Leste and Australia and the majority have very brightly coloured plumage Those studies show that the lories and lorikeets form a single group closely related to the budgerigar and the fig parrot s

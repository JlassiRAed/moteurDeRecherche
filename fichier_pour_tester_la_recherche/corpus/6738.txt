 Peter Paul and Mary were an United States American folk music group most famous during the s The members are Peter Yarrow Paul Stookey and Mary Travers Their songs include If I Had a Hammer Puff the Magic Dragon Leavin On a Jet Plane written by John Denver and Blowin in the Wind written by Bob Dylan They recorded other Dylan songs helping his early career They made records toured to perform and appeared on television shows including Smothers Brothers The Smothers Brothers Comedy Hour Peter Paul and Mary disbanded in but reunited later made tours and recorded new music Mary Travers died on September after chemotherapy treatments for leukemia

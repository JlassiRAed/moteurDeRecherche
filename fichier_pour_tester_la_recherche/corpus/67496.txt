 Undergraduate education is a level of education wherein a student completes their first Academic degree degree The first degree of an undergraduate degree is normally named a bachelor s degree Therefore for many subjects in many systems of education undergraduate education is between secondary education and a bachelor s degree This education usually takes place in a university In the United States a person starting at a university is known as undergraduate In some other educational systems and subjects undergraduate education is between secondary education and a master s degree for example in some science and engineering courses in Britain and some medicine courses in Europe

A candela is the SI base unit of luminosity luminous intensity It measures how bright something is It is one of the basic Measurement units of science A light bulb is bright but the Sun is much brighter so the Sun has more candelas than the light bulb The frequency chosen for measuring candelas is in the visible spectrum near green This is a wavelength of about nanometres The human eye is most sensitive to this frequency when adapted for bright conditions A candela sometimes is called candle

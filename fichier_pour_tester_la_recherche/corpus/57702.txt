 The swallows and martins are a group of passerine bird s in the family Hirundinidae which are characterised by their adaptation to aerial feeding Swallow is used colloquially in Europe as a synonym for the Barn Swallow Within the swallow the name martin tends to be used for the squarer tailed species and the name swallow for the more fork tailed species however there is no scientific distinction between these two groups The family contains around species in genera category Passeri

 Eryops is a genus of extinct semi aquatic temnospondyl amphibia They are found mostly in the Lower Permian aged Eryops averaged a little over meters It probably had few predators although the apex predator of the time was the synapsid Dimetrodon which was even larger Several complete skeleton s of Eryops have been found in the Lower Permian but skull bones and teeth are the most common fossil s Eryops was better adapted to a terrestrial environment than its ancestors Sturdy limbs and a strong spine supported the body while out of water

 Mary Leakey was a British archaeologist and anthropologist who spent much of her life working in East Africa in Tanzania and Kenya She discovered the first fossilized Proconsul Proconsul skull an extinct primate They had a mixture of Old World monkey and ape characteristics and their position in the evolution of the apes is still not clear Leakey also discovered the robust Zinjanthropus skull at Olduvai Gorge For much of her career she worked together with her husband Louis Leakey in Olduvai Gorge uncovering the tool s and fossils of ancient hominin s She developed a system for Taxonomy classifying the stone tools found at Olduvai She also discovered the Laetoli footprints In she became director of excavation at Olduvai and subsequently took it over building her own staff

 The Secret of NIMH Timmy to the Rescue is a American direct to video animation animated movie It is the sequel to The Secret of NIMH produced by Metro Goldwyn Mayer Animation It tells the story of a mouse named Timothy Brisby the youngest son of Jonathan and Mrs Brisby who goes to Thorn Valley wanting to become a hero like his father The movie was made without the support of Don Bluth the animator who created the original movie

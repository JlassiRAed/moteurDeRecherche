 Metonymy is a term for a figure of speech One of the most common metonyms is the use of the capital city of a country to mean the government of that country which is usually in the capital city For example mass media uses the word Washington D C Washington to talk about the Federal government of the United States federal government and national politics of the United States Other examples of metonyms often use place names to mean major industry industries which are based there For example the stock market s of the US are located on Wall Street in New York City so Wall Street is used as a general word for the economy of the United States US economy Here is a list of some well known metonyms

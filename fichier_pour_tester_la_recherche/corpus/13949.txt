 Chill out music began in Europe during the early s It is an umbrella term for different types of electronic music that are meant to have a mellow style as well as mid tempo beats Ambient music Ambient ambient house trip hop nu jazz New Age and some other Genre sub genres of downtempo are mainly associated with chill out music The music genre is played in chill rooms at dance clubs to allow dancers a chance to chill out from the more vigorous and fast tempo type of music that is played on the main dance floor In a UK regulation required dance clubs to have chill out rooms and free water because of a number of Ecstasy related injuries and deaths

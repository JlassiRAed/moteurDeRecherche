 Parallel ATA is a way to connect storage devices such as Hard disk s solid state drive s and CD ROM drives inside personal computer s The standard is maintained by X INCITS committee T It used to be called Advanced Technology Attachment with Packet Interface Parallel ATA standards allow cable lengths up to only inches Because of this length limit the technology normally appears as an internal computer storage interface For many years ATA provided the most common and the least expensive interface for this application As of it has largely been replaced by Serial ATA in new systems

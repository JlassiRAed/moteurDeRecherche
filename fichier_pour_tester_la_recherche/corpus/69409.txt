 Cocoa butter also called theobroma oil is the pale yellow pure vegetable fat of the cacao bean It is used to make solid chocolate bars and mixed with cocoa powder to make solid pieces of chocolate Cocoa butter is removed from the cacao beans and can be used to make chocolate pharmaceuticals ointment s and toiletries Cocoa butter has a mild chocolate flavor and Odor smell It is one of the most stable fats known which means that it takes a long time to go rancid

The yellow eyed penguin Megadyptes antipodes is an endangered species of penguin and the third largest penguin in the world The penguin get s it s name from the yellow coloring on it s head They live and breed on the sea shore of New Zealand and some also in the Antarctic islands in the south They have a high pitched call which can be heard very clearly They eat small to medium sized fish red cod and squid They make a nest out of twigs grass and leaves carefully formed into a bowl shape The chicks take to days to hatch and both parents share keeping the eggs warm

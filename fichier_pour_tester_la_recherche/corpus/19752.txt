 Ashford Black Marble is a kind of dark limestone which was mined from Ashford in the Water in Derbyshire England Once cut turned and polished its shiny black surface becomes very beautiful Strictly speaking Ashford Black Marble is not a marble formed by heat but a smooth sedimentary rock a hard mass of dusty minerals To make art objects Ashford Black Marble can be cut as materials of them and put together with other fine stones Derby Museum and Art Gallery Derby Museum has a diagram of Ecton Staffordshire Ecton Hill made from Ashford Black Marble and other minerals

The North American Free Trade Agreement is a trade agreement between Mexico the United States and Canada The agreement was signed by U S President George H W Bush Canadian Prime Minister Brian Mulroney and Mexican President Carlos Salinas on December in San Antonio Texas and took effect on January It removed taxes on products traded between the United States Canada and Mexico It also protects copyright Patent patents and Trademark trademarks between those three countries It was updated with the North American Agreement on Environmental Cooperation which helped set more environment regulations and helped reduce pollution It was also updated with the North American Agreement for Labor Cooperation which helped people fight for better work conditions

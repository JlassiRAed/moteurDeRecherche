 An icon is an image picture or representation which has a religious meaning It is a sign or a likeness that stands for a real object or person Although the word icon can be used for images of other religions it is most often used to describe a painting on a wooden panel that has been done in the Eastern Christianity Orthodox Christian tradition and may also be used for carved ivory panels and panels of silver or gold These icons show a holy being such as Jesus Virgin Mary Mary a saint or an angel They also show scenes from the Bible such as the Crucifixion and scenes from lives of the saints In Orthodox Christianity an icon is thought of as a window through which a person can get a view of God s truth

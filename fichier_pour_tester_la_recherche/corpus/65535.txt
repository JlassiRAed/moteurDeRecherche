The Anti Counterfeiting Trade Agreement is an international treaty that wants to make it easier to fight product piracy to protect intellectual property This includes fighting counterfeit s and copyright infringement and imposing stricter regulations on generic drug s ACTA is based on an earlier agreement called Agreement on Trade Related Aspects of Intellectual Property Rights TRIPS Japan is the only country that has ratified this European Union also has signed it but many people in Europe is against the Agreement because it can violate human rights especially the freedom of expression and privacy

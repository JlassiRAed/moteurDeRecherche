 Lassie Come Home is a MGM movie directed by Fred Wilcox It stars Roddy McDowall Elizabeth Taylor and dog actor Pal It tells the story of a dog who travels hundreds of miles to be reunited with the boy she loves It is based on the book Lassie Come Home by Eric Knight The movie was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Cinematography Color The Lassie character became the main character in six other MGM Lassie movies In Lassie Come Home was selected for preservation in the United States National Film Registry by the Library of Congress as being culturally historically or aesthetically significant movie stub

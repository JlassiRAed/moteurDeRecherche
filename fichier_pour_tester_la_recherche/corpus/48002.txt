 Aberdeen Airport is an international airport in Aberdeen Scotland It is about nautical mile s northwest of Aberdeen city centre The airport is owned and operated by BAA Limited BAA which is a Spain Spanish company It operates six British airports including Heathrow Airport Aberdeen Airport is a base for BMI Regional and Eastern Airways The Airport is also the main heliport for the Scottish offshore oil industry The airport has one main airport terminal passenger terminal serving scheduled and charter holiday flights There are three terminals for North Sea helicopter operations used by Bristow Helicopters CHC Scotia and Bond Offshore Helicopters There is also a small terminal next to the main passenger terminal Broomfield House used for oil company charter flights to Scatsta in Shetland operated by Eastern Airways

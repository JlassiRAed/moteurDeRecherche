 Valya Mladenova Balkanska is a Bulgaria n folk music singer She is most famous for performing the song Izlel je Delyo hajdutin which was sent to space on board the Voyager and Voyager space probe probes in Valya Balkanska was born in a hamlet consisting of seven houses near the village of Arda in the Rhodope Mountains In she became the main soloist of the Rhodopa State Ensemble for Folk Songs and Dances She was only years old at that time Up to this day Balkanska has performed more than folk songs having been accompanied by the bagpipes bagpipe player Petar Yanev for the last years The most important moment in Balkanska s career came with the folk song Izlel je Delyo hajdutin which she recorded together with the bagpiper Dimitar Petkovski In it became part of the Voyager Golden Record a collection of songs sounds and images from our planet In Balkanska released an album called Glas ot vechnostta comprising some of her most popular songs such as A bre yunache ludo i mlado Goro le goro zelena and Maychinko stara maychinko

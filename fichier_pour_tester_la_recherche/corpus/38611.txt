 Green Arrow is a fictional character publishing published by DC Comics Green Arrow was created by Mort Weisinger and George Papp and first appeared in More Fun Comics in His secret identity is Oliver Queen and his friends call him Ollie He is a billionaire and once was a mayor of the fictional city Star City Green Arrow is a superhero but lacks any superpowers He is similar in appearance and abilities to Robin Hood Green Arrow is an Archery archer who uses his expert skill in archery to fight crime He wanted to help citizens when he fell off his boat and got stranded on an island He also is a highly trained martial artist Green Arrow is blond dresses all in green and as a goatee beard Green Arrow uses many trick arrows to fight crime plus supervillains For example trick arrows include glue arrows net arrows explosive arrows grappling hook arrows and boxing glove arrows Since Green Arrow has usually been written as being explicitly left wing politically and fighting for social justice Green Arrow has been a member of the Justice League and the Outsiders

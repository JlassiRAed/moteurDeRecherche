 The Burke and Hare murders were Serial killer serial murders in Edinburgh Scotland from November to October The murders were done by Irish people Irish immigrants William Burke and William Hare They sold the corpse s of their victims to Physician Dr Robert Knox Knox was a Fellow of the Royal Society and the Conservator of the Museum of Comparative Anatomy in Edinburgh He was a teacher of human anatomy to Edinburgh medical students so needed bodies for his students to dissect The murderer s accomplices included Burke s mistress Helen McDougal and Hare s wife Margaret Laird

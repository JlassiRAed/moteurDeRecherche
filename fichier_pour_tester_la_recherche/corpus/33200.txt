 Pasargadae was a city in ancient Persia and is today a historical site in Fars Iran Pasargadae was the first capital of the Persian Empire The building of the capital city by Cyrus the Great begun in BCE but was left unfinished for Cyrus died in BCE Pasargade remained the Persian capital unti Darius I built the Persepolis The tomb of Cyrus the Great is in this city Pasargadae is a UNESCO World Heritage Site

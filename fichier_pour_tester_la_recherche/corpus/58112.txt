 Conducting in the musical sense means beating time to help a group of musician s to play well together If a large orchestra is playing music it is important that they all play exactly together They need to know exactly when to start what speed to go how loud or quietly to play and what the mood of the music should be If two three or four people play music together they can talk about this amongst themselves and one person can nod with his her head or with a violin bow or flute to help the group to start and finish together With an orchestra there are so many people that they need a conductor

 Aestivation also spelled estivation in the USA is a state of animal dormancy It is somewhat similar to hibernation The animals are inactive and have a lowered metabolism metabolic rate The state is entered in response to high temperatures and arid dry conditions Lungfish have been doing this since the Devonian period Invertebrate and vertebrate animals are known to enter this state to avoid damage from high temperatures and the risk of desiccation drying out Both Land land and aquatic water living animals can undergo aestivation

 Seasonal affective disorder is a when you feel sad at a certain time of year most of the time in winter People who suffer from this usually feel better in spring Doctors often cure SAD with light therapy Sometimes a light comes on when you are asleep in the morning and gets brighter like a sunrise Sometimes a person sits in front of a light box for half an hour or longer in the morning Some symptom s are Feeling sad grumpy anxious or moody Losing interest in your usual activities Eating more carbohydrates Gaining weight Sleeping more and feeling sleepy during the day

 The Buick Enclave is a full sized luxury Sport utility vehicle SUV that is built by General Motors s Buick brand The Buick was first shown at the North American International Auto Show The Buick won the Best New SUV CUV award from the AJAC in The Enclave replaced the Terraza minivan the Rainier SUV and the mid sized Rendezvous CUV The Enclave s styling is based off the Buick Centieme concept crossover

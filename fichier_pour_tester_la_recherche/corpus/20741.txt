The Football Writers Association of America is an organization of about sports writer s in the United States They attempt to achieve better working conditions for writers Each year the FWAA picks an All America team of college football players The organization also selects the Eddie Robinson Coach of the Year the Bronko Nagurski Trophy winner the Outland Trophy winner the Grantland Rice Award winner a freshman All America team and weekly defensive player of the week as well as developing scholarship programs and surveys for better working conditions Since the association has awarded the Grantland Rice Award Grantland Rice Trophy to the college football team they choose to be the National Champion

 ECW Anarchy Rulz is a professional wrestling video game Video game developer developed by Sculptured Software Acclaim Studios Salt Lake City and Video game publisher published by Acclaim Entertainment The game is based on Extreme Championship Wrestling It was the second and final video game based on their promotion before its bankruptcy It was released for the PlayStation on August in North America and in in Europe It was the sequel to ECW Hardcore Revolution which was released six months earlier Acclaim tried to make up for their last game by adding many new match types Some of the matches that they added in were a Street Fight Tables Match Inferno Match Dumpster Match Lion s Den Hate Match and the Team Rumble There was supposed to be a Nintendo version but it was cancelled because console was approaching the end of production

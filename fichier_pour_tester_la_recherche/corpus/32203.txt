 Misery Guts is a book by the award winning Australian author Morris Gleitzman This is a story about a kid named Keith who lives in London and his parents who own a fish and chips fish and chip shop This parents for a long time have not had many customers Keith is dedicated to trying to make the store better and cheer up his parents so they won t be Misery Guts for the rest of their lives He tries everything including some crazy stunts The story continues in the hilarious sequel Worry Warts The book did not sell very well As of November the Misery Guts Worry Warts combination was ranked in books sold by Amazon A television series was made based on this book

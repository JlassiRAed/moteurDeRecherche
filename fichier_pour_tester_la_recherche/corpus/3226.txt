In economics barriers to entry refers to the things that makes it difficult for a wikt firm firm or a person to enter a market These are called wikt obstacle obstacles and makes it harder for a firm or a person to enter a market Examples of barriers to entry include government regulation for firms and the need for a certain level of education for a person Barriers to entry helps to protect existing firms in the market from new firms entering a market and thus reduces competition in the market which causes prices to be higher than what is accepted by society Monopoly Monopolies and market power is thus formed due to the existence of barriers to entry

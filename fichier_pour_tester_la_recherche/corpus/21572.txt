A Scherzo is a piece of music which is quite fast In the th century Romantic music Romantic composers started to write scherzos in their symphony symphonies and sonata s instead of the traditional minuet s They are similar to a minuet always with three beats in a bar but they are faster and less polite Some composers like Frederic Chopin Chopin and Johannes Brahms Brahms wrote scherzos as separate pieces for the piano

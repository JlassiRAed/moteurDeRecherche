 George Brettingham Sowerby I He was the second son of James Sowerby With his brother James De Carle Sowerby he continued his father s work on fossil shells They published the last parts of the Mineral Conchology of Great Britain Sowerby published about papers on mollusc s and started several comprehensive illustrated books on the subject the most important the Thesaurus Conchyliorum a work that was continued by his son George Brettingham Sowerby II and his grandson George Brettingham Sowerby III

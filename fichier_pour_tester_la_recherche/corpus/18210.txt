The Year of the Four Emperors was a year in the history of the Roman Empire AD in which four emperors ruled in a remarkable turnover The four emperors were Galba Otho Vitellius and Vespasian The suicide of emperor Nero in was followed by a brief period of civil war the first Roman civil wars Roman civil war since Mark Antony s death in BC Between June of and December of Ancient Rome Rome witnessed the successive rise and fall of Galba Otho and Vitellius until the final accession of Vespasian the first ruler of the Flavian Dynasty This was an example of the political disturbances which occurred in the history of the Roman Empire The military and political anarchy created by this civil war had serious repercussions such as the outbreak of the Batavian rebellion The Jewish Revolt was already ongoing

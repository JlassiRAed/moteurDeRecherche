 Massa s in de Cold Ground is a minstrel song by Stephen Foster It was one of only four songs Foster published in The song is written in D major to time An initial phrase is repeated five times beginning every line except one The phrase ends with a octave leap upwards in the melody Massa is a slow sad song in dialect written for the minstrel stage The song tells of slaves weeping at the grave of their deceased white master After the American Civil War another Cold was entered in the title the title then becoming Massa s in de Cold Cold Ground When the novel Uncle Tom s Cabin was first performed as a dramatic adaptation in November in Pittsburgh Pennsylvania Massa s in de Cold Ground and two other Forster melodies were inserted into the production

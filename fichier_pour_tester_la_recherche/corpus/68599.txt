The La Brea tar pits are a famous cluster of tar pit s in central Los Angeles Complete skeleton s of many thousands of large animals have been found here They date mostly from to years ago Hancock Park was formed around the tar pits in the heart of Los Angeles Asphalt or tar has seeped up from the ground here for tens of thousands of years The tar is often covered with water Over many centuries animals that came to drink the water fell in sank in the tar and died In the tar their bone s turned into fossil s The George C Page Museum is dedicated to research ing the tar pits and displaying specimens from the animals that died there The La Brea Tar Pits are now a registered National Natural Landmark

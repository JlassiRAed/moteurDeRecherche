A public private partnership is a contract between the public sector or government and a private company The purpose for such public private wikt partnership partnerships is to provide public service s like education and transportation This is without changing the private business into a government operated service One way of doing this is to allow the government to pay the business for providing free services to the public The business provides the services for free and then charges the government for wikt compensation compensation in which the government agrees Supporters of public private partnerships argue that it reduces inefficiency and provides the free market with greater flexibility

In music sharp or Diese means higher in pitch In musical notation sharp means higher in pitch Under twelve tone equal temperament B sharp for instance is enharmonic ally equivalent to flat Double sharps also exist these have the symbol and raise a note by two semitones or a whole step Less often there are half or three quarter sharps Although very rare and used only in modern classical music a triple sharp can sometimes be found This raises a note by three semitones The Circle of fifths tells you how many sharps are in a key signature In Musical tuning Tuning practice tuning sharp can also mean slightly higher in pitch If the same note is played on two instruments and one is slightly higher it is too sharp compared to the other In Unicode the sharp symbol is at U DA Some fonts do not show these symbols

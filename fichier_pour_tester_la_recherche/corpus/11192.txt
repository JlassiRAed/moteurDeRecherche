 Shirley Temple s Storybook is a television series hosted and narrated by actress Shirley Temple Its first season of episodes aired on NBC as The Shirley Temple Show between September and July Fairy tales children s stories and some maturer material like The House of Seven Gables were adapted for the series Well known stars like Charlton Heston and Elsa Lanchester appeared in the series Several books such as Shirley Temple s Stories That Never Grow Old were published around the time the series aired In the series was nominated for a prime time Emmy Award for Outstanding Achievement in the Field of Children s Programming Selected episodes have been released to VHS and DVD

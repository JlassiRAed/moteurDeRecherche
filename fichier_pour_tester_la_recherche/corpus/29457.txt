 Tropical Storm Lee was a weak Tropical cyclone tropical storm that hardly reached tropical storm status for a short time while over the central Atlantic Ocean during late August and early September Atlantic hurricane season Lee was the twelfth named storm of the Atlantic hurricane season Tropical Storm Lee formed east of the Lesser Antilles as a tropical depression on August On the next day the depression weakened into a tropical cyclone tropical low The low then moved north and became stronger It became a tropical storm for a short time on August Lee quickly weakened again into a remnant low and was absorbed by a cold front on September

 Chaohusaurus is an extinct genus of basal ichthyopterygia n or ichthyosaur It was closer in time and form to Cymbospondylus and Mixosaurus than to more advanced genera like Ichthyosaurus It did not have the dolphin like form of later ichthyosaurs but it was fully aquatic The tail fin is wide based and short The neck is rather long whereas later ichthyosaurs have no neck There is no dorsal fin It was one of the smallest ichthyosaurs about to cm long Its weight was about kg

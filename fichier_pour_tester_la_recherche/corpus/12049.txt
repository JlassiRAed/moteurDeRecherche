 Mega Man Anniversary Collection is a compilation video game for the Nintendo GameCube Playstation and Xbox It was released on June for the GameCube and Playstation and on March for the Xbox It contains the first eight Mega Man games as well as two spin off fighting game s in the series It also contains extras such as original artwork and remixed music For the first six games a Navi Mode is available This allows players to get hints from helpers if they are in need of help It also changes the look of the health and weapon meters as well as the weapon screen for the first three games

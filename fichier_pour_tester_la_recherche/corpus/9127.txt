The Hebrew calendar or Jewish calendar is the calendar used in Judaism It is used to set the dates of the Holiday Jewish holidays Jewish holidays and the weekly public reading of the Torah It is used to set the date for a Bar Mitzvah or Bat Mitzvah the day a young person is considered an adult in Judaism It sets the Yahrzeit the anniversary of the death of a kinship relative The daily Jewish Service of worship Judaism prayer service changes depending on the day of the Hebrew calendar

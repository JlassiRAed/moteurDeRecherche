 I Have a Dream is the name of a speech Martin Luther King Jr gave on August while standing on the steps of the Lincoln Memorial in Washington D C In his speech he spoke of his wish for the future His wish was that people of different race races could live together peacefully in the United States The speech was given to over supporters He spoke of the discrimination that the black men have faced even though they were to be treated as equals after the great Abraham Lincoln signed the momentous decree the Emancipation Proclamation The speech is very famous It has been called by many the best speech given in the th century

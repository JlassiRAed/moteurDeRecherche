 The Fame Monster is an extra long EP recording created by United States American artist Lady Gaga in The Fame Monster reached the top of the charts in Australia Germany Ireland New Zealand Poland and the United Kingdom while reaching the top ten in other major markets Lady Gaga did a tour called the Monster Ball that lasted from late until mid The singles on The Fame Monster are Bad Romance Telephone and Alejandro The album and its singles received three Grammy Awards The main music genres which make up the album are pop dance pop and electropop

 Acanthomintha is a genus of the Lamiaceae or mint family The Acanthomintha genus is commonly referred to as Thornmint There are a number of species within this genus including the endangered species Acanthomintha duttonii All four thornmints are native to the California Floristic Province The origin of the genus name is from the identical Greek word meaning thornmint There are four species of Acanthomintha Acanthomintha duttonii Jokerst San Mateo thorn mint Acanthomintha ilicifolia San Diego thorn mint San Diego thornmint Acanthomintha lanceolata Santa Clara thorn mint Acanthomintha obovata San Benito thorn mint

 Metaphysics is a major branch of philosophy It concerns existence and the nature of things that exist Altogether it is a theory of reality Ontology is the part of metaphysics which discusses what exists the categories of being Apart from ontology metaphysics concerns the nature of and relations among the things that exist The metaphysical idea that reality exists independently of one s mind and yet can be known is called realism The metaphysical idea that no mind independent reality exists or can be known is idealism These are two main battlegrounds of metaphysics

 George Mamua Telek commonly known simply as Telek is a musician from Papua New Guinea He is one of the country s most successful musicians Telek was born in Raluana a village near Rabaul in New Britain He sings in his native language Kuanua and also in Tok Pisin Telek sang with various bands in Papua New Guinea early in his career most notably with the group Painim Wok famous in the s Telek met Australian musician David Bridie in Rabaul in the mid s Together they recorded the very successful album Tabaran Telek recorded his first solo album Telek outside Papua New Guinea in This album won an Australian Recording Industry Association ARIA award for Best World Music Album He recorded his second international album Serious Tam in at Peter Gabriel s studios in London The next album he released was Amette in He recorded it in Bridie s studio in Melbourne His most recent album Akave was released through the Wantok Music label

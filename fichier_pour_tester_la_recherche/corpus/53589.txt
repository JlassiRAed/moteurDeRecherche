 TransPennine Express is a British train operating company It was formerly jointly owned by First Group After April it is fully owned by First Group which kept the franchise It runs regular passenger trains in northern England including trains linking the west and east coasts across the Pennines TransPennine Express runs three routes which provide rail links for large cities like Edinburgh Glasgow Liverpool Sheffield Leeds York and Newcastle upon Tyne Newcastle All three routes stop at Manchester Piccadilly station Manchester Piccadilly and Manchester Airport

 Legionnaires disease is a form of pneumonia caused by any type of Legionella bacteria Over of cases are caused by Legionella pneumophila The incubation period length of time between exposure to the bacteria and the appearance of symptoms is generally two to ten days It can be as much as days People with Legionnaires usually have fever chills and a cough Some also have muscle aches headache tiredness loss of appetite diarrhea or vomit ing People of any age may have Legionnaires disease It is most common in middle aged and older people

The Grand Canyon is a famous canyon in Arizona formed by the Colorado River It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a National Park of the United States It is also one of the Wonders of the World Seven Natural Wonders of the World Seven Natural Wonders of the World The Grand Canyon is miles deep in places Evidence suggests the Colorado River established its course through the canyon at least million years ago Since that time the Colorado River continued to erode and form the canyon to the point we see it at today Local erosion in the western Grand Canyon began about million years ago but a through flowing Colorado River to the Pacific Ocean did not exist until about million years ago most of the downward cutting has been concentrated in the last million years

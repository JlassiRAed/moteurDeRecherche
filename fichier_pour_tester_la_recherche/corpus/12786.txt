 Golant is a small village in Cornwall United Kingdom It is in the parish of St Sampson on the shore banks of the Fowey estuary and between St Austell and Polperro The church in the villiage is dedicated to Saint Sampson St Sampson Golant railway station was at one time by the river to the south of the village It was served by Railway trains on the Great Western Railway s Fowey branch line from July until January The station has since been removed The space is now used for a car park Annie Vernon the Olympic games Olympic Rowing rower first learned her sport at Golant s Castle Dore Rowing Club

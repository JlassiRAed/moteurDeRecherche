 Camillo Paolo Filippo Giulio Benso Count of Cavour of Isolabella and of Leri Cavour was born in Turin Until he worked as a military officer Later he decided to travel in Europe to learn more about the effects of the industrial revolution These trips helped him to know and understand the principles of the British Liberal system After four years he returned to Piedmont He took charge of agriculture and the economy in general He worked for the spread of schools During that time his business and banking activities made him one of the richest men in the Piedmont From to Cavour was the mayor of Grinzane In Cavour became famous because he advocating the Siccardi Law that diminished the privileges of the Catholic clergy In the same year the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Sardinia Massimo D Azeglio chose him as Minister of Agriculture Trade and Navy Later he also became Minister of Finance After D Azeglio resigned on November Cavour became Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Sardinia Cavour s political program wanted to become the Kingdom of Sardinia a constitutional State based on moderate and progressive liberalism so he dedicated himself to a radical renewal of the economy He modernized and valorized agriculture strengthened the industrial system and promoted trade with the major European powers In he signed a treaty of alliance between the Kingdom of Sardinia and the Second French Empire French Empire against the Austrian Empire In the following year there was the Second Italian War of Independence In that war the Piedmontese and the French defeated the Austrians who at the time held power in Italy After the armistice of Villafranca and Giuseppe Garibaldi s expedition in the South the unification of Italy was completed Cavour became the first President of united Italy He was also the first Minister of Foreign Affairs

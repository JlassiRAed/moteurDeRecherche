The Forbidden City now known as the Palace Museum is a large historical palace and an art museum in the historical center of Beijing China The site is a UNESCO World Heritage Site It is very important to the history and architecture of China The Forbidden City was the palace of the Emperor of China  from the  Ming Dynasty  to the end of the  Qing Dynasty For almost years it was as the home of emperors and their houses as well as the ceremonial and political center of Chinese government

 I Saw Mommy Kissing Santa Claus is a Christmas song with words and music by British songwriter Tommie Connor The original recording by year old Jimmy Boyd rose to on the Billboard Billboard charts in December The words tell of a child who sees Mommy kissing Santa Claus underneath the mistletoe The Roman Catholic Church in Boston hated the song because it is a Christmas song that also mentions sex Radio stations in several markets banned it Boyd went to church leaders to talk about the words The ban on the song was lifted The song has been performed by various musician s including but not limited to the Ronettes Jackson Jessica Simpson John Mellencamp Amy Winehouse and other singers throughout the years

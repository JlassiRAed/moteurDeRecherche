 Fat Lip is a punk rock song by the Canada Canadian pop punk band Sum and is the first single from their debut studio album All Killer No Filler which was released in The song was written by Raine Maida and was produced by both Arnold Lanni and Raine Maida Fat Lip has been featured in several movies and video games such as EA Sports NHL American Pie Guitar Hero and Smallville The band has also performed Fat Lip on Saturday Night Live The music video was ranked as on MuchMusic s Best Videos

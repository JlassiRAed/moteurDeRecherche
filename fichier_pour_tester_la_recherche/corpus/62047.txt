 Interstate is an Interstate Highway in the United States It goes from San Francisco California east to Teaneck New Jersey The route is long and passes through states I is the Interstate Highway that most closely follows the route of the historic Lincoln Highway the first road across United States America The highway roughly traces other historically significant travel routes in the Western United States the Oregon Trail across Wyoming and Nebraska the California Trail across most of Nevada and California and except in the Great Salt Lake area the entire route of the First Transcontinental Railroad From near Chicago Illinois east to near Youngstown Ohio Interstate is a toll road containing the majority of both the Indiana Toll Road and the Ohio Turnpike It also has a small part of the Illinois Tollway I and Interstate share the same road from near Portage Indiana to Elyria Ohio I becomes the Keystone Shortway The eastern end of I is its junction with the New Jersey Turnpike and Interstate just west of the George Washington Bridge entering New York City

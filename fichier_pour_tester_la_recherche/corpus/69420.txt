 Sir Godfrey Kneller st Baronet was the leading portrait painter in England during the late th and early th centuries He was court painter to Kingdom of England English and British monarch s from Charles II of England Charles II to George I of the United Kingdom George I His major works include The Chinese Convert a series of four portraits of Isaac Newton a series of ten reigning European monarchs including King Louis XIV of France over Kit cat portrait s of members of the Kit Cat Club and ten Hampton Court Beauties beauties of the court of William III of England William III The beauties series was to match a similar series of ten Windsor Beauties beauties of the court of Charles II The earlier portraits were painted by court painter before him Sir Peter Lely

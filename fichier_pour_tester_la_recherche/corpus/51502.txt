A Wikipedia Disambiguation disambiguation page should not have many pages linking to it These pages are Wikipedia What is an article not articles They are there to clarify and help readers in cases where there are two or more topics with a similar name For example if two or more famous people have the same name a disambiguation page helps the reader find the article they want Ideally Wikipedia Namespaces article pages should not link to disambiguation pages except when the ambiguity redirect to make it clear that the link is on purpose Links from namespaces other than articles are allowed but still may need to be disambiguated Wikipedia Redirect Redirects to disambiguation pages are not considered to be within the article namespace for purposes of this guideline

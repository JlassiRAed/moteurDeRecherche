 Ronald Selmour is a Canadians Canadian actor He is well known for his role as Firecracker Man in American Broadcasting Company ABC s Kingdom Hospital Sundown in the Discovery Channel s TV miniseries Klondike and The Butcher in the CW s Arrow Selmour has been involved in the performing Arts from the early years of his life Though Profoundly religious his father was very involved with local theatres taking him to see presentations throughout Port au Prince Haiti In the early s the family made the difficult decision to let him go to Canada stayed with his aunt while pursuing his studies In Montreal Quebec there he made the acquaintance of several instrumental people in his life teachers professors and a mentor name Solly Levy who introduced him to the Comedia dell arte Ever since then the love for the craft became the guiding light in his life

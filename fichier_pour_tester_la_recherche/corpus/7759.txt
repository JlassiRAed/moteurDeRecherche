 unbulleted list The United States Minor Outlying Islands are eight island territories of the United States They are Baker Island Howland Island Jarvis Island Johnston Atoll Kingman Reef Midway Atoll Palmyra Atoll and Wake Island in the Pacific Ocean and Navassa Island in the Caribbean Sea The islands are grouped together for Statistics statistical reasons They are not administered together As of none of the islands have any people living there permanently The only human population consists of temporarily stationed scientific and military personnel The United States CensusThe islands are all together represented by the ISO alpha code UM The ISO introduced the term United States Minor Outlying Islands in From until five of the islands were grouped under the term United States Miscellaneous Pacific Islands with ISO code PU The code of Midway Atoll was MI the code of Johnston Atoll was JT and the code of Wake Island was WK The Pacific islands are surrounded by large Exclusive Economic Zone s

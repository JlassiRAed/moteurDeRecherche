 Teenage Dream is the third studio album by United States American pop singer Katy Perry The album was released in the United States on August and went straight to number one on the Billboard charts Billboard selling copies in its first week There have been six singles released from Teenage Dream and two from its re release all of which have achieved massive chart success The album was nominated for Album of the Year and Best Pop Vocal Album at the Grammy Awards Teenage Dream was nominated for Best Female Pop Vocal Performance California Gurls was nominated for Best Pop Collaboration with Vocals In Firework was nominated for Record of the Year and Best Solo Pop Performance

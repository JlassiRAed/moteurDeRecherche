A scholarship is financial help given to someone who wants to study Scholarships can be given by school s or by university universities or college s or any other institution where people can study or where research needs to be done It is similar to a bursary There are very many kinds of scholarships Some scholarships will cover all the tuition fees others may just help towards the tuition fees Some scholarships may include money for other things such as food and accommodation Sometimes the students are expected to do something in return for having a scholarship They may for example be expected to do some particular work after they finish their studies or they may need to help the institution in some way Quite often the amount of money a student gets will depend on how much money the family has Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Discrimination against Women says that scholarship must be equally given for girls without discrimination

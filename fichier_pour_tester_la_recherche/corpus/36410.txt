 Baruch is a text that was written in the late first century or the early second century CE It was probably written after the destruction of the temple in Jerusalem in CE and before the Bar Kokhba revolt second Roman Jewish War CE It is attributed to Baruch ben Neriah and is included in some editions of the Old Testament and the Peshitta but neither Jew s nor Christian s regard it as scripture The Syrian Orthodox Church includes it in the Bible The text has sections and is also known as Syriac Apocalypse of Baruch and Letter of Baruch to the Nine and a Half Tribes

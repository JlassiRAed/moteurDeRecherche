A haplotype is a term in genetics It is short for haploid genotype A haplotype is a collection of specific allele s SciTable haplotype haplotypes A second meaning of the term haplotype is a set of single nucleotide polymorphism s So finding a few alleles of a haplotype sequence identifies all other sites in its region Such information is very valuable for investigating the genetics of common diseases Comparing haplotypes of two or more people shows the degree of genetic relationship between their respective lines It is done in genetic genealogy projects

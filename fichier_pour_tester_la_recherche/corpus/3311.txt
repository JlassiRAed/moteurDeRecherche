 Akkad was an ancient city in Mesopotamia It was the centre of the Akkadian Empire The ruins have not been found but it is believed to be on the River Euphrates The empire comes after a long relationship with Sumer and before the ethnic Akkadian empires of Babylonia and Assyria According to the Sumerian king list Akkad it was one of the cities started by Nimrod Also in the earliest records when Mesopotamian kings started calling themselves lord of the four quarters around Sumer these were listed as Martu Shubar Elam and Uri ki As the capital city of the Akkadian Empire beginning with Sargon the city ruled from around to BC when it was destroyed by the Gutians who invaded from the mountains

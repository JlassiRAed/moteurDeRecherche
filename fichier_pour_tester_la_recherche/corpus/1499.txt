 Bigpoint GmbH is a game developer based in Hamburg Germany which produces browser based MMORPG games that are unique in that do not require any downloads to play Founded by Heiko Hubertz in December of Bigpoint has experienced record growth since its creation and won numerous business awards including the Red Herring Award in placing it among the top most innovative businesses and the Ruban d Honneur at the European Business Awards in In June of a controlling share of Bigpoint GmbH was purchased by NBCU s and GE s joint venture Peacock Equity Fund and UK PE Firm GMT Communications Partners in a m deal that gave each a share of the company This allowed the three initial venture capital investors Aurelia Private Equity European Founders Fund and United Internet Beteiligungen to sell out while CEO Heiko Hubertz re invested his share of the sale to retain a stake in the company Heiko Hubertz currently acts as the company s Managing Director Bigpoint has a portfolio of more than games translated into languages with the two largest being SeaFight with approximately million registered users and Dark Orbit with approximately million registered users The company claims to receive more than new user registrations each day A complete list of Bigpoint s games can be found Here

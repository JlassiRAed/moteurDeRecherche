 South Pacific is a musical It is based on two short stories from James A Michener s book Tales of the South Pacific The musical s book was written by Oscar Hammerstein II and Joshua Logan The lyrics were written by Hammerstein The music was written by Richard Rodgers The musical won the Pulitzer Prize for Drama and the Tony Award for Best Musical South Pacific opened on Broadway on April at the Majestic Theatre and closed on January after performances It starred Mary Martin and Ezio Pinza South Pacific opened on the West End theatre West End at the Theatre Royal Drury Lane on November The musical was adapted to a Twentieth Century Fox movie in starring Mitzi Gaynor and Rosanno Brazzi

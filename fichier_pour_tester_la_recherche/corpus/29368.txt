The Book of the Dead is a name for an Ancient Egypt Egyptian text It is also known as The Book of Coming or Going Forth By Day or as the papyrus of Ani It contains a number of writing text s and magic spells These allow the dead person to safely get to the place of the afterlife The book of the dead was most commonly written on a papyrus scroll It was placed in the coffin of the dead person or their burial chamber The book of the dead in its most familiar form was first used in the New Kingdom but many of the spells had their origins in the Funeral funerary texts of the Old Kingdom Old and Middle Kingdom s The name Book of the Dead was the invention of the Germany German Egyptology Egyptologist Karl Richard Lepsius who published a selection of the texts in Missing sections of the book were found in the Queensland Museum in Historians are hoping that the missing pieces will give them a copy of the complete book

 Mallinson Girls School is a school for girls in Kashmir It is located in Srinagar The school was founded by Ms Fitze in at Fateh Kadal and later moved to Lal Chowk in Srinagar The school was started by Christian Mission missionaries and named after Mallinson It still has affiliations with the Church Mission Society Its motto is Faithful in the Least The school s principal is currently Parvez Sameul Kaul

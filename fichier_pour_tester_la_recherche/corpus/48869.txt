 Army Burn Hall College formerly Burn Hall School is a school and college in Abbottabad Pakistan In a year after Pakistan gained independence from the British Empire a few missionary fathers moved to Abbottabad with a handful of students from Srinagar Kashmir where the original school was and founded a branch here Between and the school was run by these missionary fathers and supervised by the Board of Education of the Rawalpindi Diocese of the Church of Pakistan However in the fathers were forcibly evicted by the Pakistan Army which took over the institution They made several changes to the school and its spirit and mode of education gradually bringing it into line with their own goals and objectives Since it is no longer a proper public school along the England English model but a military style graduate college with separate branches for boys and girls

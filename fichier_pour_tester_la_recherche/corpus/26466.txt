 The Jeep Renegade is a subcompact crossover SUV It is built in Melfi Italy on the same assembly line as the Fiat X which it shares its platform with The Renegade started production in as a model It is the first Jeep model built only outside of North America It is sold in South Africa Europe North America and Brazil In the US the Renegade is available in the following trims Sport Latitude Limited and TrailHawk All trims except TrailHawk are available with front and four wheel drive The TrailHawk features four wheel drive as standard The Renegade has two four wheel drive systems Active Drive I and Active Drive Low Both are mated to Jeep s Selec Terrain System

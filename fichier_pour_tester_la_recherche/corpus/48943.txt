 IIHM or International Institute of Hotel Management is a chain of hospitality and hotel management schools It was founded in at Kolkata India IIHM has campus sites at Kolkata Bangalore Delhi Pune Hyderabad Jaipur Ahmedabad and in Bangkok Thailand The school offers a Bachelor s degree in International Hospitality Management A large number of student study at these campuses Students also may complete their degree at Edinburgh Napier University Edinburgh or the University of West London in London

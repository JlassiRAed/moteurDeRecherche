 Toast is bread which has been grilled until hot and crispy A toaster grill or open fire is used Toasted bread is both darker in color and crunchier than normal bread Toast can be eaten with a range of condiments spread on the surface such as butter jam honey though these are optional as some people prefer their toast without condiments Toast can also be used to make toasties where a filling is compressed and heated between two slices of buttered bread in a toastie maker Popular fillings include cheese jam peanut butter and chocolate spread

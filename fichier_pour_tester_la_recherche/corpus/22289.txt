 Kingdom Hearts is the first game of the Kingdom Hearts series It was made for the PlayStation and was released in Japan on March and in North America on September In it the player controls Sora a year old boy who lives on a small chain of islands called Destiny Islands but is sent to different worlds along with his friends after their world is destroyed Along the way he meets lots of Walt Disney Pictures Disney characters like Donald Duck and The Little Mermaid Ariel and Final Fantasy characters like Cloud and Yuffie Sora must save the worlds while searching for his friends

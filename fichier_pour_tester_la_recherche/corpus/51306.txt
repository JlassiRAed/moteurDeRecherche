A frigate is a warship The term has been used for warships of many sizes and roles for a few century centuries During the age of sail they were small agile ships that were extensively used in the American War of Independence and later against the British Their main use was shore defense In the modern day the term Frigate covers a broad range of ships This is primarily for political reasons The generally accepted term is of a warship less that dwt and greater that around dwt Its primary purpose is usually anti submarine warfare It can also be a long range patrol vessel or in smaller navies an anti air vessel

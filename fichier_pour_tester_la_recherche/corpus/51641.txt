 Serosa organ s of the abdomen abdominal cavity known as the serous cavity It secretes serous fluid and a thin connective tissue layer They secrete a lubricating fluid which reduces friction from muscle movement Serosa is not to be confused with en adventitia adventitia a connective tissue layer which binds together structures rather than reducing friction between them The serous membrane covering the heart and lining the en mediastinum mediastinum is called the pericardium the serous membrane lining the en thoracic cavity thoracic cavity and surrounding the lungs is called the pleura and that lining the en abdominopelvic cavity abdominopelvic cavity and the viscera is called the en peritoneum peritoneum

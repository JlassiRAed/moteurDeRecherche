 General VG character Sonic the Hedgehog is a video game character He is a year old blue hedgehog who can run at the speed of sound By curling into a ball and spinning quickly he can do a helpful Spin Attack which he uses to defeat the bad guys and save the day Because he is very fast he hates waiting and he can be a bit rude but he does care about his friends and really hates evil He has been in many games made by the company Sega but also in television series book s and comics With them he fights against Doctor Eggman an evil scientist who tries to take over the world Doctor Eggman was also called Doctor Robotnik in America for some time until it was changed to Eggman to match what he was called in Japan

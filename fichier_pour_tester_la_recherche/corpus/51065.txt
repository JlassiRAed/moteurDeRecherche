A Multiplayer Online Game is a multiplayer video game which can be played over the internet with other players around the world Some examples of this are Battlefield Halo Counter Strike Quake StarCraft Call of Duty Black Ops NBA K and the Sega Meganet These games are different from Massively multiplayer online role playing game MMORPGs because they do not create a persistent world but create a playing arena for the purpose of a single game or round

The tobacco mosaic virus was the first virus to be discovered That is to say it was the first to be known as a virus It is a single stranded RNA virus which infects many plants especially tobacco and other members of the family Solanaceae The infection causes mosaic like discolouration on the Leaf leaves Although it was known from the late th century that an infectious disease was damaging tobacco crops it was not until that the infectious agent was known to be a virus

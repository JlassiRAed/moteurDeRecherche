 Yass is a town in the Southern Tablelands of New South Wales Australia It is in the Yass Valley Council Yass Valley Shire The name probably comes from an Indigenous Australian Aboriginal word Yarrh which means running water Yass is about kilometre km southwest of Sydney and is km north of Canberra It is on the Hume Highway which is the main road between Melbourne and Sydney The new Hume Freeway goes around the town The Yass River flows past the town It later flows into the Murrumbidgee River Yass has an impressive and historic main street It includes many th century hotels with large verandah s It is popular with tourist s some from Canberra and others taking a break from the Hume Highway The Pride of Erin Festival and Yass Show are held in March The Yass Arts and Crafts Festival is held in November

The Thames Barrier is the world s second largest movable flood wikt barrier barrier after the Oosterscheldekering in the Netherlands It is downstream of central London Its purpose is to prevent London from being flooded by exceptionally high tide s and storm surge s moving up from the North Sea It is raised only during high tide at ebb tide it is lowered to release the water which backs up behind it The barrier s northern bank is in the London Borough of Newham and its southern bank is in the London Borough of Greenwich The report of Hermann Bondi into the North Sea flood of affecting parts of the Thames Estuary and parts of London was instrumental in the building of the barrier

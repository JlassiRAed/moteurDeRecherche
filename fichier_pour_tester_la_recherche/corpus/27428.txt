A horse harness is a piece of equipment that allows a horse to pull a vehicle such as a carriage wagon or sleigh Harnesses may also be used to hitch animals to other loads such as a plow plough Harnesses can be in leather or in biothane with stainless or chrome Draft horses can be hitched on a cart two wheel buggies or wagons Light horses can be hitched on sulky four wheel buggies or antic wagons stub

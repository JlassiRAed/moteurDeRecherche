 Pikes Peak is a foot mountain near Colorado Springs Colorado United States It is one of the world s most famous mountains and can be seen from over miles on clear days It was named after Zebulon Pike who explored the area in The summit of the peak can be reached either by car or railway The spectacular view from the top is what inspired Katharine Lee Bates to write America The Beautiful in and there is a plaque with the words to the song at the summit as well It is also the home of the second oldest car race in the United States

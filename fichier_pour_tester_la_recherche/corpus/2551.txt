 Francis Lee Bailey Jr commonly referred to as F Lee Bailey is an Americans American former attorney Bailey was born in Waltham Massachusetts He worked in Massachusetts and Florida He was a criminal defense attorney He served as the lawyer in the re trial of physician Sam Sheppard He was also the supervisory attorney over attorney Mark J Kadish in the court martial of Captain Ernest Medina for the My Lai Massacre He was one of the lawyers for the defense in the O J Simpson murder case

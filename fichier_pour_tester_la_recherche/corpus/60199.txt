 Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Racial Discrimination is a treaty against racism and racial segregation as one of the series of international human rights law The United Nations adopted this convention on December in response to the apartheid policy of South Africa at that time The Article of the Convention prohibits all inciting of racism including hate speech and discrimination s Article gives a person or group of a country who has suffered any discrimination prohibited by this Convention the right to submit a claim to a committee of the United Nations But not all countries that ratified the treaty include this Article

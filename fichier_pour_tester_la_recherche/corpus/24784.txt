 Structured settlement is an insurance or financial arrangement It is a legal agreement in which an insurance company agrees to make regular payments to a specific individual over a period of time This is done because company want to avoid trial costs and bad publicity Structured settlements created as a way for insurance companies to payout settlements in installments as opposed to large lump sums as a result of a case that involved children to be born with mutation s and defect s Structured Settlements require the paper holder to wait before they will get money However there are companies offering to buy such a structured settlement for upfront money Basically such companies allow person to switch yearly payments over years to instant payment of lower value Such financing can be used to pay for a house send a child to college or pay debts Such financing need to be approved by judge and the insurance company Structured Settlement is being regulated by law in the section of Internal Revenue Code

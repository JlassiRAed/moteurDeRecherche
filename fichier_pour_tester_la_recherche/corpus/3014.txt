 Cardcaptor Sakura is a manga series from Japan It is also an anime series Cardcaptor Sakura is sometimes abbreviated as CCS Cardcaptor Sakura was created in by a team of artists named CLAMP CLAMP has created many other series but Cardcaptor Sakura is one of the most popular The manga was first published in Nakayoshi from to The story is about Sakura Kinomoto a middle school student who accidentally frees a deck of magical cards from a book hidden in her father s study The book s guard Keroberos gives Sakura the task to find and capture the troublemaking cards The series focuses on themes of love and family Sakura and her friends and their different relationships The anime aired on Japanese television from to changing parts of the manga s story and introducing new characters and has two films as well The English version of the anime was licensed by Nelvana renamed Cardcaptors and the story was changed a lot to suit an American audience

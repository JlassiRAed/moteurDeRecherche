The Ain Diab Circuit was a Formula One road circuit built in southwest of Ain Diab in Morocco using the existing coast road and the main road from Casablanca to Azemmour The km course was designed by the Royal Automobile Club of Morocco It was given a full blessing from Mohammed V of Morocco Sultan Mohammed V It was built in only six weeks The site hosted an non championship F race in On October the course was the venue for the Moroccan Grand Prix the final round in the Formula One season It was won by Stirling Moss driving a Vanwall completing the laps in h m s There was a terrible accident during the race The engine on the Vanwall of Stuart Lewis Evans seized up The car spun and crashed He was severely burned and died in a hospital in England six days later

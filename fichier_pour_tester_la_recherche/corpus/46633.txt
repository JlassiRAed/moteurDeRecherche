 Genies or jinns or djinns are supposed to be magical beings or creatures with free will They are originally found in ancient myths and legends of the Middle East especially Arabia Nowadays people in many Muslim countries like Saudi Arabia Kuwait Qatar Lebanon Palestine Egypt Iraq Syria Libya Algeria Tunisia Turkey Iran parts of Central Asia Afghanistan and Pakistan believe in these Jinns or Djinns Jinns are also common in legends and stories from the Middle East for example in the One Thousand and One Nights The holy book of Muslims the Quran also mentions Jinns and says that they are made from fire or smoke Together the jinn humans and angels make up the three wise creations of God Like human beings the jinn can be good or bad

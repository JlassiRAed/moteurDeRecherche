The Thar Desert also known as the Great Indian Desert is a desert About per cent is in western Republic of India and the rest in southeastern Pakistan It is mostly in the Indian state of Rajasthan It extends into the southern portion of Haryana and Punjab India Punjab states and into northern Gujarat state In Pakistan the desert covers eastern Sindh province and the southeastern portion of Pakistan s Punjab and on the west by the Indus River Its boundary to the large thorny steppe to the north is not well defined Depending on what areas are included or excluded the nominal size of the Thar can vary

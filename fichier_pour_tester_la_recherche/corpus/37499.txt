 Capricornus is one of the constellation s of the zodiac It is often called Capricorn especially when referring to the corresponding Capricorn Capricornus is one of the modern constellations and was also one of the constellations listed by the nd century astronomer Ptolemy Under its modern boundaries it is bordered by Aquila Sea or Water consisting of many water related constellations such as Aquarius Pisces and Eridanus

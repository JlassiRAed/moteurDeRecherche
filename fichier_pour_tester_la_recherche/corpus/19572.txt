 It Won t Be Soon Before Long is the second studio album by United States American music pop rock group Maroon It was released under A M Octone Records on May in the United States It debuted atop the Billboard albums chart and on the Billboard Digital Albums chart The lead single off the album Makes Me Wonder was released on March and peaked atop the Billboard Hot songs chart The album includes other singles such as Wake Up Call Wake Up Call Won t Go Home Without You If I Never See Your Face Again and Goodnight Goodnight

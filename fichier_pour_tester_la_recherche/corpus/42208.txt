 Liberal conservatism is a political ideology that combines elements of conservatism and economic liberalism Liberal conservatives believe in the free market and laissez faire economic policies combined with traditional conservative values such as the belief in natural inequality the importance of religion and the value of traditional morality In a way liberal conservatism is the opposite of classical liberalism a belief that supports both economic and individual freedom This term should not be confused for conservative liberalism which is an offshoot of liberalism not conservatism

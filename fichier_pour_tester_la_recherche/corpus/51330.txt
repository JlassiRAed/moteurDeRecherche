 Paolo Veronese was an Italian painter of the Renaissance He was a Venice Venetian He is remembered for paintings such as The Wedding at Cana and The Feast in the House of Levi He became known as Veronese from his birthplace in Verona His best works are elaborate narratives These are executed in a dramatic and colorful Mannerism Mannerist style They are full of majestic architectural settings and pageantry His large paintings of biblical feasts are especially notable

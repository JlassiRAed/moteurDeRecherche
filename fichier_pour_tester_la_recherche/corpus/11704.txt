The International Bluegrass Music Association or IBMA is a trade association to promote bluegrass music Formed in IBMA established its first headquarters in Owensboro Kentucky In they announced plans to create the International Bluegrass Music Museum as a joint venture with RiverPark Center in Owensboro In IBMA established the World of Bluegrass a combination trade show concert and awards presentation This was originally set in Owensboro before moving to Louisville Kentucky in Nashville Tennessee hosted this event in and and will continue as the host location in the future In IBMA established the International Bluegrass Music Hall of Honor at the International Bluegrass Music Museum to recognize lifetime contributions to bluegrass both by performers and non performers In IBMA relocated its offices to Nashville Tennessee

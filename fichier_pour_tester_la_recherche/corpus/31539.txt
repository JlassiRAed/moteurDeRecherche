Hip Hop is a type of pop music popular music which started in the s in African American communities in big cities of the United States Hip hop uses a style of singing called rapping which has rhythm and rhymes Hip Hop is also a culture which involves Hip hop music as well as a style of dressing called urban wear an African American dancing style called breakdancing or popping and a type of street art in which people paint pictures or words on walls which is called graffiti

 Aruviyodu Village is a hilly Hamlet Neyyar in India It is down south in Neyyattinkara in the district of Thiruvananthapuram from Perumkadavila Aruviyodu is surrounded by Neyyattinkara Taluk towards the west Parassala towards the south Melpuram Taluk towards South Munchira Taluk towards the south This Place is in the border of the Thiruvananthapuram District and Kanniyakumari District

 Mansehra is a city in Pakistan It is in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and is the capital of Mansehra District The district has sub divisions namely Mansehra Tehsil Oghi Tehsil and Balakot It also has a tribal territory called Kala Dhaka which has however now been given a separate district status also The district currently has million people The important towns in the district are Baffa Shinkiari Kaghan Naran Garhi Habibullah Balakot Phulra Oghi Darband and Khaki The Karakoram Highway Road also commences in the district and has a km stretch up to Babusar Pass passing through the district The MNJC is being developed as an alternate route to the Northern Areas of Pakistan

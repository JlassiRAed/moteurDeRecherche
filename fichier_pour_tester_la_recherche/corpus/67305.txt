 Edmund Spenser was an important poet from England He is most famous for his epic poem The Faerie Queene which talks about different knight s who fight against evil The poem also praises Queen Elizabeth I of England He wrote also many sonnets and poems The sonnets were collected under the name of Amoretti Edmund Spenser invented a new rhyme scheme for sonnet These forms are today called Spenserian sonnet and Spenserian stanza Spenserian stanza was later used by many poems among others by Lord Byron George Gordon Byron Percy Bysshe Shelley John Keats and Alfred Tennyson

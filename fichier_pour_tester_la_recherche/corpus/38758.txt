The proletariat is a term used by communist s for a segment of society It describes the class of workers in urban areas who work in industry or manufacturing These urban workers are different from the peasant s who are workers who do farming jobs in rural areas The political philosophy way of thinking of communism states that the proletariat should have a revolution and take over the means of production They are often sarcastically referred to as wage slaves The proletariat is the laboring bulk of society that does most of the useful necessary work and pays most of the taxes It is also perceived by capitalist s as being necessary for those reasons labor and taxes plus being available to exploitation exploit to produce profits

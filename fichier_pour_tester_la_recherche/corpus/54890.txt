A lychee is a tropical fruit in the genus Litchi It comes from southern China Since it is tropical it will only grow in a warm wet climate The inside of a lychee is a clear pinkish white colour The outside of the fruit is a hard spiky shell Its seed is brown in colour and very hard The seeds are poison ous and should not be eaten The lychee has a sweet taste and may be frozen made into sauces jam puree or preserves When frozen the lychee tastes similar to sorbet

 Scary Monsters and Nice Sprites is the second Extended play EP by Americans American electronic music producer Skrillex Scary Monsters and Nice Sprites was released on October Skrillex said that he recorded the EP in at his apartment using a laptop The EP won two Grammy Award Grammys at the th Annual Grammy Awards for Best Dance Recording and for Best Dance Electronica Album The EP also received generally positive reviews from music critics and reached on the Billboard as well as on the Billboard Heatseekers Albums chart

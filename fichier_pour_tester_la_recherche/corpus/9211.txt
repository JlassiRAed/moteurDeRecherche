 White Trash Two Heebs and a Bean is the fourth studio album by United States American punk rock band NOFX White Trash Two Heebs and a Bean was released on November The producer of the album was Donnell Cameron NOFX The original album title was going to be White Trash Two Kikes and a Spic but guitar player Eric Melvin changed it after feeling guilty It is also the first album with guitar player El Hefe

 Teak is the common name for the tropics tropical hardwood tree species Tectona grandis and its wood products The word teak comes from the Malayalam word theka or tekka This tree is mentioned in the seventh century literature of Tamil popularly known as the Tevaram Tectona grandis is a large deciduous tree that is dominant in mixed hardwood forests It has small fragrant white flowers and papery leaves that are often hairy on the lower surface

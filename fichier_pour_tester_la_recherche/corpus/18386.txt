 B major or B flat major is a major scale starting on B flat Its key signature has two flats Its relative key relative minor is G minor and its parallel key parallel minor is B flat minor B minor B flat major is a good key for most wind instrument s especially those for which it is their home key such as clarinet s trumpet s saxophone s and the flute s in B flat Because of this many works for concert band s are written in this or a closely related key such as F major or E flat major Joseph Haydn Haydn s Symphony No which had both trumpet and timpani is known as the first symphony that anyone had written in that key Actually his brother Michael Haydn had written one earlier However Joseph Haydn still gets credit for writing the timpani part at actual pitch with an F major key signature Five of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart Mozart s piano concerto concerti are in B flat major

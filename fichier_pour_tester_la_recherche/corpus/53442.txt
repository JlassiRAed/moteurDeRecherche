A sparrow is a member of the genus Passer They are small passerine birds which belong to the family family Passeridae They are also known as old world sparrows Sparrows often make their nest s near house s or building s This means they are one of the easiest birds to see in the wild The genus has about species round the world The best known of these is the house sparrow Passer domesticus Some authorities place other genera in the sparrow group Petronia the rock sparrows Carpospiza the pale rockfinch and Montifringilla the snowfinches

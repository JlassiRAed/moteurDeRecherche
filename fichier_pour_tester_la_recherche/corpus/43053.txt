A megalith is a large Rock mortar or cement The word megalith comes from the Greek language Ancient Greek lithos meaning stone It has been used to describe stone structure s built by people from many parts of the world living in many different times Many megaliths were used to figure out the dates of the solstice s and equinox es These structures were built mainly in the Neolithic period They continued to be built into the Bronze Age Some were built even earlier in the Mesolithic period A famous example of a megalith is Stonehenge

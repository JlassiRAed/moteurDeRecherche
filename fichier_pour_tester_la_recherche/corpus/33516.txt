 Ballet d action is an th century ballet movement It was started by French choreographer Jean Georges Noverre in The movement focused on character and emotional expression through dancers bodies and faces instead of elaborate costumes and props Noverre started the movement because he was unhappy with the attention focused on technical expertise He expressed his views in his book Lettres sur la danse et les ballets He stated there that the purpose of ballet is to to represent characters and express their feelings through movement rather than costume

 Michael James Grier is a retired ice hockey right winger and left winger that played a combined seasons in the National Hockey League NHL with the Edmonton Oilers Washington Capitals San Jose Sharks and the Buffalo Sabres He was picked by the St Louis Blues with the th overall pick in the NHL Entry Draft He is African American and is one of a small number of black hockey players in the league He is also the first African American NHL player that was born and trained in the United States

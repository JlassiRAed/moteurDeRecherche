 Autophagy is one of the basic cell mechanisms It allows the controlled breaking down of cell parts which do not work or are not needed There are several ways this happens Targeted cell parts may be isolated from the rest of the cell in an autophagosome The autophagosome fuses with lysosome s and the contents are broken down and recycled In the extreme case of starvation the breakdown of cellular components promotes cellular survival by maintaining cellular energy levels The name autophagy was coined by Belgian biochemist Christian de Duve in

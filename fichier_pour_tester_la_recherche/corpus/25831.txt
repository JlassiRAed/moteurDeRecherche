 Cob or clom in Wales is a natural building material Cob is made from subsoil lime The contents of subsoil varies and if it does not contain the right mixture is can be modified with sand or clay Cob is fireproof resists seismic activity and inexpensive It can be used to create artistic sculptural forms and has been revived in recent years by the natural building and sustainability movements Cob material is known by many names including adobe The material has a long life span even in rainy climates provided a tall foundation and large roof overhang are present

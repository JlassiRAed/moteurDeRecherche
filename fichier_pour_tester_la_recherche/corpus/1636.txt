 Andouille is a type of pork sausage It is spicy but the name indicates that it may have come out from France and the most famous sort in the United States USA is the Cajun style Other sorts are French andouille and German andouille they are less spicy than Cajun Cajun has extra sodium chloride salt black pepper and garlic Andouille makers smoke the sausages over pecan wood and sugar cane for a maximum of seven or eight hours at about degrees Fahrenheit

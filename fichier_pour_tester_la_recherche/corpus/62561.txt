 The Ecclesbourne Valley Railway is a heritage railway in Derbyshire Its operations are based at Wirksworth station It is currently planned to extend the railway along the former Midland Railway branch line which joined the main line at Duffield railway station Duffield When complete it will be one of the few Heritage Railways that operates a whole branch line in its original form and entire length The line is operated by diesel locomotives which haul former multiple unit trailer cars and also uses heritage diesel multiple unit s

 The Ngaanyatjarra Pitjantjatjara Yankunytjatjara Women s Council is a community based organisation that serves women of the Ngaanyatjarra Pitjantjatjara people Pitjantjatjara and Yankunytjatjara communities in remote central Australia It was formed in with its headquarters in Alice Springs It provides a range of community family research and advocacy services and represents the interests of women and their communities

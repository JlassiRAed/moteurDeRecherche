 I II and III Meqabyan are three books in the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church Ethiopian Orthodox Old Testament Biblical canon These books are very different in content from the books of Maccabees Maccabees in Catholic and Eastern Orthodox Bibles but they are sometimes called Ethiopic Maccabees or Ethiopian Maccabees The Maccabees described in these books are not those of the Hasmonean dynasty and the Five Holy Maccabean Martyrs here do not match Woman with seven sons who were also called Maccabees and are revered throughout Orthodoxy as the Holy Maccabean Martyrs These books were only written in Ethiopic for a long time but have recently been translated into English

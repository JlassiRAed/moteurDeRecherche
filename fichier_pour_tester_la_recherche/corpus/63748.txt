 Survivor Series was a professional wrestling pay per view show made by WWE It was held on November at Scottrade Center in St Louis Missouri It was the twenty eighth Survivor Series event held by the WWE The PPV was offered for free to new subscribers of the WWE Network The main event saw Team Cena in a traditional on Survivor Series elimination tag team match A stipulation was added to the match that if Team Authority lost they would be removed from power If Team Cena lost every member of Team Cena except for Cena would have been fired The PPV was also notable for the first appearance of former World Championship Wrestling WCW and Total Nonstop Action Wrestling TNA wrestler Sting Sting on WWE who was a long hold out on signing with the company

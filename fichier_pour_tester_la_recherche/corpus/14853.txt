The Gulf of Guinea is the northeasternmost part of the tropical Atlantic Ocean It is between Cape Lopez in Gabon north and west to Cape Palmas in Liberia The intersection of the Equator and Prime Meridian is in the gulf Among the many rivers that drain into the Gulf of Guinea are the Niger River Niger and the Volta River Volta The coastline on the gulf includes the Bight of Benin and the Bight of Bonny

 Grover Underwood is a fictional character in Rick Riordan s Percy Jackson and the Olympians series He is a satyr a mythological Greek being that is half goat and half human He has the upper body of a human and the legs and horns of a goat He uses fake feet when he wants to look like a human His horns are usually hidden by his curly brown hair and sometimes an orange rasta cap Although he is years old he is like a sixteen year old human because satyrs age half as fast as humans do Grover is played by Brandon T Jackson in the movies Percy Jackson the Olympians The Lightning Thief and Percy Jackson Sea of Monsters the film adaptations of the first and second books in the series The Lightning Thief and The Sea of Monsters

A shore or shoreline is the land at the edge of a large body of water such as an ocean or river Where the water is a sea the shore is also called a coast Shores are influenced by the topography of the surrounding landscape as well as by erosion caused by water such as wave waves The geology geological makeup of rock and soil controls the type of shore which is created Erosion of sedimentary soils can make a sand y or pebble pebbly shore usually called a beach A shore includes the intertidal zone that is alternately exposed and covered by waves and tides A shore will often have a lighthouse on it to help ships in the sea

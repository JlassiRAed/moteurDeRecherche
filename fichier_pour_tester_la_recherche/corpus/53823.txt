 Tottenham Hale is a National Rail and London Underground Victoria Line station in Tottenham North London north London It is on Hale Road near the Tottenham Hale one way system The station is in Travelcard Zone The station opened on September as Tottenham It was on the Northern and Eastern Railway Northern Eastern Railway line from Stratford station Stratford in east London to Broxbourne railway station Broxbourne in Hertfordshire It was renamed Tottenham Hale on September when it became an interchange station with London Underground on the opening of the first stage of the Victoria line

 The Slim Shady LP is the second studio album by United States American rap music rapper Eminem The Slim Shady LP was released on February and was produced by Dr Dre Mark Jeff Bass Eminem and Mel Man The album got critical and commercial success It debuted at number two on the Billboard Billboard charts Two singles from the album were released Just Don t Give a Fuck and My Name Is It earned Eminem a Grammy Award for Best Rap Album and was included in Rolling Stone s list of the greatest albums of all time However it started a lot of controversy over its lyrics and impact on the American youth

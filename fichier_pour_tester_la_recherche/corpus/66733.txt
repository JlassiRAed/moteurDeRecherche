 Shamarpa or more formally Kunzig Shamar Rinpoche is Tibetan people Tibetan Buddhism Buddhis teacher Together with Karmapa he is the second lineage holder of the Karma Kagyu school of Tibetan Buddhism Shamarpa is regarded as manifestation of Amitabha Buddha He also is the second lama who consciously incarnates in subsequent order of Buddhist teachers The first Shamar Rinpoche Khedrup Drakpa Senge was the principal disciple of the rd Karmapa Rangjung Dorje

 Albert Dean Rosellini for two terms Rosellini died of pneumonia At the time of his death he was the oldest living person to have been a governor of a U S state having lived longer than any other State Governor in US history and was the first Italian American Roman Catholic Catholic to become a governor of a state west of the Mississippi River He was called Taxellini by people who did not think he was doing a good job as a governor while he was called The Gov by people who did think he was doing a good job

An art critic is a person who writes about art He writes reviews which are published in newspapers magazines books and on web sites He often goes to museum s and art exhibition s and then writes about the painting s and sculpture s on display Professional art critics should know a lot about art history People often discuss what the art critic has said or written It may lead to a lot of discussion among art lovers When art critics write good things about young artists it may help them to become famous If they write bad things then it makes it difficult for young artists to have a good career So art critics have a lot of influence on art culture Critics such as Roger Fry have helped the public to understand the Post Impressionist movement and Lawrence Alloway helped to explain Pop Art

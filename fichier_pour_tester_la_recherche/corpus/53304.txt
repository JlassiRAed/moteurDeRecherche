 The Nasrid dynasty or Banuu Nasri was the last Arab and Muslim dynasty in Spain The Nasrid dynasty came to power after the defeat of the Almohad dynasty in at the Battle of Las Navas de Tolosa Twenty three different emir s ruled Granada from the founding of the dynasty in by Muhammed I ibn Nasr until January when Muhammad XII of Granada surrendered to the Christian Spanish kingdoms of Aragon and Kingdom of Castile Castile Today the most visible evidence of the Nasrids is the Alhambra palace built under their rule

 Sir David s Long beaked Echidna also known as the Cyclops Long beaked Echidna is one of three species from the genus Zaglossus All of them are found in New Guinea It is named after Sir David Attenborough It lives in the Cyclops mountains in Western New Guinea Papua province of Indonesia near the cities of Sentani Jayapura The species was described from a single damaged animal which was collected in the Dutch colonial era It has not been collected since that time It is the smallest member of the genus It is closer in size to the Short beaked Echidna than other members of the genus It has five claws on its fore and hind feet It has dense short fur

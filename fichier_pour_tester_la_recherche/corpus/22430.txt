An antenna or aerial is a metal device made to send or receive radio wave s Many electronic devices like radio television radar wireless LAN cell phone and GPS need antennas to do their job Antennas work both in air and outer space The word antenna is from Guglielmo Marconi s test with wireless equipment in For the test He used a meter long pole antenna with a tent pole called l antenna centrale in Italian So his antenna was simply called l antenna After that the word antenna became popular among people and had the meaning it has today The plural of antenna is either antennas or antennae

 Nasi goreng is a traditional dish from Indonesia It is a style of fried rice The name is Malay language Malay nasi means rice and goreng means to Frying fry Nasi goreng is often served for breakfast It is also popular for late night supper sold from street vendor s It can be found almost anywhere in Indonesia It is also popular in Malaysia and Singapore In CNN International announced that nasi goreng is the number two of their World s Most Delicious Foods list

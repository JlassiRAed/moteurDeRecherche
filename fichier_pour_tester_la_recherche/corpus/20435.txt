 Plug and play is the name for a number of technology technologies used in computing Technologies that support plug and play allow to connect new device s to a computer After a short while these devices can simply be used When they are no longer needed they can be disconnected again Plug and play does not require the user to load a specific driver to access the device Plug and play is used to refer to both the configuration of devices at boot time as well as to system that support adding a device later on Examples for systems that support plug and play are Universal Serial Bus USB and Firewire

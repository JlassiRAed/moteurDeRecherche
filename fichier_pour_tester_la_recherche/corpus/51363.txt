A chairperson is a person who presides over meetings such as a board of directors for a corporation or any other meeting The chairman of a corporation is not the chief executive officer and only conducts the meetings in most companies Although chairman has been used for both men and women some prefer to use the terms chairperson madame chairman or chairwoman The conduct of meetings is governed by the organization s by laws or charter or more formally Parliamentary procedure for government organizations or large meetings generally Rarely the chairperson is called the chair though in educational institutions a chair indicates a specific endowed position on a faculty

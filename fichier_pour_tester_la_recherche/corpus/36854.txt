A mango is a type of fruit The mango tree is native to South Asia from where it has been taken to become one of the most widely cultivated fruits in the tropics Pakistan and India share the major export market of mangoes It is also their national fruit Ripe mangoes are very sweet in taste but they are bitterly sour before they ripen There are varieties of mangoes found in India Langra Dussheri Chausa Tota Safadi Alphonso are types of mangoes which are generally found in various parts of India at different time

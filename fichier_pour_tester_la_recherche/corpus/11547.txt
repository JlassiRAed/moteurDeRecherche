 Ayya Vaikundar of the Lord Narayana according to Akilattirattu Ammanai the holy book of the Ayyavazhi religion officially a Tamil Hindu branch The physical human body of Ayya Vaikundar was born with the name Muthukutty However Muthukutty is referred to as Sampooranathevan a deva The Ekam as soul incarnated with the spirit of Narayana in the body of Muthukutty

 Tor Johnson was a Sweden Swedish professional wrestler and movie and television actor During his wrestling career he was billed as the Super Swedish Angel Johnson was born in Sweden the son of Karl J Johansson and Lovisa Petersson He began appearing in movies in usually as boxing boxer s or wrestlers In the s he played lumbering thugs and scowling plug uglies He appeared with Bob Hope and Bing Crosby in Road to Rio He is best known though for his appearances in the low budget movies directed by Ed Wood He played Lobo in Bride of the Monster and Night of the Ghouls and the zombie zombified Inspector Clay in Plan from Outer Space In the early s he had guest appearances as wrestlers thugs and circus strongmen in various television series including Peter Gunn Bonanza and The Shirley Temple Show Johnson died in San Fernando California from heart failure in He was buried at the Eternal Valley Memorial Park in Newhall California

The Leo Burnett Building is a skyscraper in Chicago Illinois near the Chicago River It is located on West Wacker Drive at North Dearborn Street It is a story foot tall skyscraper When built in it was the th tallest structure in Chicago It was designed by Kevin Roche John Dinkeloo and Associates and Shaw Associates It is a postmodern design made with granite masonry glass steel and concrete The windows are divided by stainless steel bars which is typical of Chicago school Chicago windows

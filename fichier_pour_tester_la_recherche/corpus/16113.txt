 Leishmania is a protist organism which causes Leishmaniasis It is a Trypanosomatid parasite in the Euglenozoa phylum It is spread by sandflies of the genus Phlebotomus in the Old World and of the genus Lutzomyia in the New World Their primary hosts are vertebrates Leishmania commonly infects hyrax es canids rodents and humans Leishmania currently affects million people in countries The parasite was named in after the Scottish pathologist William Boog Leishman

The Intolerable Acts are laws passed by the Parliament of the United Kingdom British Parliament in The British Parliament passed these law s to punish the Massachusetts colony for the Boston Tea Party One of the laws closed Boston Harbor until the colonists paid for the destroyed tea Another law banned the Committees of Correspondence Still another law allowed Britain to house troops where ever it wanted to this was called the Quartering Act The Administration of Justice Act allowed the trials of accused royal officials to take place in Great Britain if the Governor decided that the defendant could not get a fair trial in Massachusetts The Quebec Act gave the Ohio country to Canada These Laws were so harsh that the colonists called them the Intolerable Acts Other colonies offered Massachusetts their support They sent supplies to Boston The Committees of Correspondence also called for a meeting of all colonies This meeting would decide what to do about their problems with Britain which led to the Continental Congress

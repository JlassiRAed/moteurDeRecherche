The Presbyterian Church in Canada is a Protestantism Protestant Christian denomination in Canada Presbyterian churches in Canada were started in the late th century by Scottish and French Huguenot s immigrants These churches became orgainized into four denominations in the early s The four denominations merged in as the Presbyterian Church in Canada It became one of the largets Protestant denominations in English speaking Canada by the early th century In of the Presbyterian congregations merged into the United Church of Canada The Presbyterians who did not join in the union continued using the name Presbyterian Church in Canada

 Sarcosuchus is an extinct genus of giant crocodile It lived in the Lower Cretaceous of what is now Africa to million years ago Strictly speaking it was not a modern type of crocodile but a kind of pre crocodile These earlier types are called Crocodylomorphs True crocodiles arrived in the Upper Cretaceous Sarcosuchus was one of the largest giant crocodile like reptiles that ever lived It was almost twice as long as the modern saltwater crocodile and weighed about tonnes Until recently all that was known of it was a few fossil ised teeth and armour scutes which were discovered in the Sahara Desert in the s or s In and Paul Sereno discovered half a dozen new specimens including one with about half the skeleton intact and most of the vertebral column spine All of the other giant crocodiles are known only from a few partial skulls so which is actually the biggest is an open question

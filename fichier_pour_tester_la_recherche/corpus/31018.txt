A dress is one of the basic styles of female clothing It is a garment of a skirt The colouring and style of the dress gives the effect of a one piece garment A dress is also known as a frock or a gown In Europe and the Americas dresses are worn by females of all ages as an alternative to a separate skirt and blouse or trousers There are many types of dresses A dress is usually worn at formal occasions such as a wedding or a dance It is also worn in the summer to keep cool Younger females tend to prefer shorter skirts than older females

 The British Rail Class is a type of electric locomotive built from by BREL British Rail Engineering Limited They were the flagships of British Rail s electric locomotive fleet until the late s when the British Rail Class Class s started to come onstream The privatisation of British Rail saw all but one of the fleet transferred to Virgin Trains they continued their duties until the advent of the new British Rail Class Pendolino trains when they were transferred to other operators or withdrawn The last Class in UK mainline use was withdrawn from service on December Most have now been sold for reuse in Bulgaria

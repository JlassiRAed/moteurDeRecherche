 Sailing is the craft of controlling a boat that uses the force of the wind as a source of movement Sailing boats were very important to move cargo and people Starting at the end of the th century they were gradually replaced by the boats that used steam to generate motion Today sailing is a hobby some people have In some regions of the world sailing vessels still keep their importance Many military forces also have sailing vessels for the purpose of training Sailing is also a competitive sport It is one of the sports in the Olympic Games

In computing multi touch is the name for a piece of technology that lets you use a trackpad or touchscreen by pressing on more than one Somatosensory system place on the screen at one time This often lets you do things that you could not do by other ways such as pinch to zoom One of the first products to have multi touch was the iPhone Its iOS operating system was meant to be used using gestures such as tapping pinching spreading rotating and more At the time Apple Inc Apple the company who made the iPhone tried to register the word multi touch as a trademark but they could not do so as the term became used by everyone else Today multi touch can be found on many consumer electronics electronic devices with touchscreens such as smartphone s tablet computer s and even some personal computer PCs

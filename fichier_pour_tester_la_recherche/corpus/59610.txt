 Achtung Baby is the seventh studio album by Republic of Ireland Irish rock music rock band U U It was put in stores on November The album came almost two years after the lead singer Bono said that the band would have to go away and dream it all up again after their album Rattle and Hum was criticized Achtung Baby shows a very big change in U s sound because they began to use alternative rock and electronic music electronic sounds in the rhythm more production and more guitar effects also including darker and more personal song words The results were much more adventurous and less traditionally rock based than their previous albums and Achtung Baby is one of U s best selling and most liked albums

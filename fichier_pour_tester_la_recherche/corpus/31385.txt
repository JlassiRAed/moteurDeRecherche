 The Firestone Tire and Rubber Company is a tire company founded by Harvey Firestone in They supplied air filled tire s for wagons buggies and other forms of wheeled vehicle s common at the time Firestone soon saw the possibility for increasing sales by making tires for Car automobiles The company was a early leader in the mass production of tires Firestone become the original equipment supplier of Ford Motor Company automobiles They also sold a lot of replacement tires In the company was sold to the Japanese Bridgestone Corporation It is now known as Bridgestone Firestone

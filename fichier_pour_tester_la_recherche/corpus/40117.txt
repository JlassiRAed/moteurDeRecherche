 Surfing with the Alien is the second album by instrumental rock solo artist Joe Satriani released in Surfing with the Alien contributed greatly to establishing Satriani s reputation as a respected rock guitarist The album contains fast and complex songs such as Surfing with the Alien to vary the album The album cover shows the Marvel Comics character the Silver Surfer on the front and the hand of Galactus on the back The image is from Silver Surfer The artist John Byrne has said that the image was used without his permission and that he never got paid for its use The art is from panel of page of SILVER SURFER published in However the album liner notes mention a Marvel Characters Inc copyright and say the Silver Surfer likeness is used with permission

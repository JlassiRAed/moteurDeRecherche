A Canon in a church is an official He may or may not be a priest The Chapter of a cathedral is an official group made up of the Dean and Chapter of canons who meet in a chapterhouse They run the cathedral especially the business affairs of the cathedral They are the body which has legal responsibility for the cathedral and for electing the bishop Originally a canon was a cleric who lived in the precinct of the cathedral The word meant a priest who was under a bishop In the Anglican church some canons are laypersons The title is also given to some senior or retired priests as an honorary title

 Fumigation is the making of toxic fumes It may be done for several reasons The most common use is to kill insect s that hide in various places in a house or other building There are a few steps to fumigate First the house or room is sealed Then the fumigant is placed in the area Then the fumigant is left to flow through the area Finally the fumigant is released before the people move back in The gases are very toxic and the person fumigating has to make sure no one goes into the fumigation area or they might get poisoned

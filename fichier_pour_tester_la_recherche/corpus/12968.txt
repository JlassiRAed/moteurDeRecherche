Since October Northern Ireland is made of district s Each district has a council which looks after local services The districts are made of wards Each ward elects councillor There councillors in Northern Ireland and there is an election every years The voting system used to elect each councillor is Single Transferable Vote Some of the councils are known as Borough or City councils Every year each council elects a chairman In Borough and City councils the chairman is called the Mayor In Belfast City Council the chairman is called the Lord Mayor In Borough and City councils up to of councillors are given the title Alderman

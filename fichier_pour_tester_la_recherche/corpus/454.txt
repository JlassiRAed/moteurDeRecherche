 David Usher Moist Usher was born in Oxford England He lived in many different places such as Malaysia New York City California and Thailand before his family decided to finally settle in Kingston Ontario Kingston Ontario Usher attended high school at Kingston Collegiate and Vocational Institute and he attended Simon Fraser University in Burnaby British Columbia He majored in political science While attending Simon Fraser Usher formed Moist with guitarist Mark Makowy and keyboardist Kevin Young Usher is also a Humanism humanist and he has been involved in causes like War Child War Child Canada White Ribbon Campaign and Amnesty International He was featured in the MuchMusic special Musicians in the WarZone a humanitarian documentary which was produced by War Child Canada and was directed by filmmaker Liz Marshall

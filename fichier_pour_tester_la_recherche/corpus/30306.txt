 Agaya Gangai is a waterfall in Tamil Nadu It is in the Kolli Hills of the Eastern Ghats The name means that the waterfall is The Ganges of the Sky Agaya Gangai Waterfall starts near the Arapalleswarar Temple in Namakkal district of Tamil Nadu The ft waterfall joins Aiyaru river It is in a valley that is surrounded by mountains on all sides The caves of Korakka Siddhar and Kalaanginatha Siddhar are in the nearby forest

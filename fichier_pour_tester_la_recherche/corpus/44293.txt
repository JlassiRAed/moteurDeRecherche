 Alan Richard Al Michaels is an Americans American sportscaster He was best known working for ABC Sports for nearly three decades He is the lead voice of NBC Sunday Night Football Michaels has won many awards during his career including the Sports Emmy Awards Emmy Award for Outstanding Sports Personality five times the NSSA Award from the National Sportscasters and Sportswriters Association three times and Sportscaster of the Year once each from the American Sportscasters Association and the Washington Journalism Review In October Michaels was awarded a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame Michaels had an acting role in a episode of Hawaii Five O He has appeared as himself in the movies Jerry Maguire and BASEketball as well as on several TV shows including Coach Miracle

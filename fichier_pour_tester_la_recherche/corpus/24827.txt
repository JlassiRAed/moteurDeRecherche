 Barbelo is a goddess in Gnosticsm She is also called Thought Providence Forethought or Foreknowledge She is believed to be the first emanation of Bythos In the Apocryphon of John a writing in the Nag Hammadi Nag Hammadi Library Barbelo is described as The first power the glory Barbelo the perfect glory in the aions the glory of the revelation The Apocryphon of John continues This is the first thought his image she became the womb of everything for it is she who is prior to them all the Mother Father the first man the holy Spirit the thrice male the thrice powerful the thrice named androgynous one and the eternal aeon among the invisible ones and the first to come forth

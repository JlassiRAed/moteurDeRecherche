 You Know You re Right is the first song off American grunge band Nirvana self titled compilation album The song was released on October It was the last song that was recorded by Nirvana before Kurt Cobain s death On the Billboard Billboard charts You Know You re Right was able to peak at on the Hot Mainstream Rock Tracks Mainstream Rock There was a legal battle between Cobain s widow Courtney Love and Nirvana bandmates Dave Grohl and Krist Novoselic Both Grohl and Novoselic wanted to use the song for a Nirvana box set that was planned but Love did not allow this and blocked it from being released Love said that the song would have been wasted by using for a box set and said that it would be better suited for a single disc collection

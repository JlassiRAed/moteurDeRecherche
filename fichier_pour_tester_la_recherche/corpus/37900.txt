The Asian emerald cuckoo is a species of cuckoo in the Cuculidae family family It lives in Southeast Asia in Bangladesh Bhutan Cambodia China India Indonesia Laos Malaysia Myanmar Nepal Sri Lanka Thailand and Vietnam The Asian emerald cuckoo is a protected animal in Malaysia Its scientific name is Chrysococcyx maculatus This bird is not in the Cuculinae subfamily and so it is not a nest parasite They raise their own young like most birds Emerald cuckoos lay egg egg s in a nest built by the parents

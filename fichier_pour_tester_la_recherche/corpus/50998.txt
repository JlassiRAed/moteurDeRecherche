The anthropic principle says that the universe is how it is because it must allow for the eventual creation of us as observers The anthropic principle was thought of in by the astronomer Brandon Carter Douglas Adams explains this concept quite well using a puddle as an analogy There are two different kinds of anthropic principles the weak anthropic principle and the strong anthropic principle The original thoughts that Brandon Carter had were Weak anthropic principle We must be prepared to take into account the fact that our location in the universe is necessarily privileged to the extent of being compatible with our existence as observers Strong anthropic principle The Universe must be such as to admit the creations of observers within it at some stage

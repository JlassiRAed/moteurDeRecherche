 Invader Zim branded as Invader ZIM is an American cartoon series that was created by comic artist Jhonen Vasquez for Nickelodeon The series started on March and was cancelled on December The show centers around Zim an extraterrestrial being from the planet Irk who inhabitants participate in an event called Impending Doom After failing it miserably Zim is determined to prove himself to his leaders the almighty tallest and conquer earth However the tallest only sent him to his planet as a secret mission only to get rid of him The series had cost too much to air and the age demographic was aimed toward preteens but not many were watching the show Nickelodeon then cancelled the show on January allowing the remainder of Season to air and The Most Horrible X mas Ever at the series finale

 The grey wolf or just wolf is a mammal of the order Carnivora and the ancestor of the domestic dog It is sometimes called timber wolf A recent study found that the domestic dog is descended from wolves tamed less than years ago south of the Yangtze River in China There are many different wolf subspecies such as the Arctic wolf Some subspecies are listed on the endangered species list but overall Canis lupus is IUCN graded as least concern

The Garden of Eden is the place where the first man Adam and the first woman Adam and Eve Eve lived after they were created by God In the Christian Bible the story of the creation of the world in the book of Genesis describes how Adam and Eve lived in Eden in peace with all of the animals They tended the garden and could eat from any tree except the Tree of Knowledge After they ate from the Tree of Knowledge God punished Adam and Eve by making them leave the Garden of Eden forever The story is told in Book of Genesis in the old Testament of the Bible and the Tanakh The Tanakh translates the Garden of Eden as Paradise

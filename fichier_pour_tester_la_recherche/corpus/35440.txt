 Victoria Island is an island in the Canadian Arctic Archipelago and the border between Nunavut and the Northwest Territories runs through the island It is the th biggest island of the world and at is Canada s second largest island and nearly double the size of Newfoundland The western third of the island belongs to the Inuvik Region in the Northwest Territories and the rest is part of Nunavut s Kitikmeot Region As of live on Victoria Island Most of the people live in its biggest city Cambridge Bay

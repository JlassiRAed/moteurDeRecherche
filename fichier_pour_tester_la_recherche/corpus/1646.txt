 No Control is the fourth album by American punk rock band Bad Religion released on the California n independent record label Epitaph Records on November It was the follow up to the band s highly acclaimed reunion album Suffer Suffer Like the previous Bad Religion album many people consider No Control to be one of the best punk albums of the s while some consider it to be the band s finest effort featuring Change of Ideas No Control I Want to Conquer the World Big Bang and You all of which are staples of their live show to this day Stylistically No Control is a faster album than its predecessor owing more to hardcore punk

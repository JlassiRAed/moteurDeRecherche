 Never S A Y Never is the second album by United States American R B singer Brandy Norwood Brandy released by Atlantic Records on June in Europe and on June in North America Chiefly produced by Rodney Jerkins helped to make Jerkins Timbaland inspired production sound popular which he later used for recordings by Whitney Houston Destiny s Child and others Never Say Never earned Brandy five Grammy Award nominations and became her biggest selling album to date

An ingredient is a part that forms part of a mixture For example in cooking recipes specify which ingredients are used to prepare a specific dish Many commercial products contain a secret ingredient that is purported to make them better than competing products In the health industry an active ingredient is that part of a recipe that yields the effect required by the buyer National laws usually require prepared food products to show a list of ingredients and require that certain bad things be listed In most developed countries the law requires that ingredients be listed according to their weight in the product If an ingredient itself consists of more than one ingredient then that ingredient is listed by what percentage of the total product it occupies with its own ingredients showed next to it in brackets

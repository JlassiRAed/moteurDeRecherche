A recipe is a list of instructions that shows how to prepare or make something such as a food dish Modern day recipes are usually made up of several parts The name of the food being made How much time it will take to make the food The ingredients needed along with how much of it will be needed The equipment needed A list of steps that need to be followed in order to make the food The number of people that the recipe will be able to feedRecipes from earlier times often included much less information Older recipes usually just reminded a person of the ingredients needed

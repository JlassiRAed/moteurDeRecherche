 Twin towns are town s or city cities in different countries which are paired to encourage human contact and cultural links In Europe the pairs of towns are called twin towns but other languages say friendship towns or partner towns in North America and Australasia the towns are called sister cities Brother cities was the name of twinned cities in the old Soviet bloc Twin towns often have similar populations industries and other characteristics Sometimes even larger areas enter into twinning agreements such as that between the province s of Hainan in People s Republic of China China and Jeju in South Korea

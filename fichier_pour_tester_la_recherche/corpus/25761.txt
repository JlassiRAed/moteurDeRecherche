 Mussaurus was an early plant eating prosauropod dinosaur from the Upper Triassic about million years ago It was found in southern Argentina It gets its name from the small size of the skeletons first discovered They were large eyed hatchlings these are the smallest dinosaur skeletons yet found However Mussaurus is now known from adult specimens Mussaurus possesses anatomical features suggesting a close possibly transitional fossil transitional evolutionary relationship with true sauropods

A yogi is a term for a male who practices various forms of the path of Yoga maintaining a steadfast mind the process of transcending the lower self These designations are mostly reserved for advanced or daily practitioners In contemporary english language English yogin is an alternative rendering for the word yogi This word is often used to describe buddhism Buddhist monks or any lay person or householder who is devoted to meditation The Shiva Samhita text defines the yogi as someone who knows that the entire cosmos is situated within his own body and the Yoga Shikha Upanishad distinguishes two kinds of yogins those who pierce through the sun and drink the nectar

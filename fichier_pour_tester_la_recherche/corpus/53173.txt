 American Pharoah American Triple Crown and the Breeders Cup Classic in In winning all four races he became the first horse to win the Grand Slam of American horse racing He won the Eclipse Award for Horse of the Year and Champion three year old He was bred and owned throughout his racing career by Ahmed Zayat of Zayat Stables trained by Bob Baffert and ridden in most of his races by Victor Espinoza He now stands stud farm at stud at Ashford Stud in Kentucky

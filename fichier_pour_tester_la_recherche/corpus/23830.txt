 A toad is a type of amphibian Toads look like frog s and they belong to the same animal group All toads are frogs but not all frogs are toads Toads mainly evolution evolved in dry climate s so many toads have rougher browner skin Many toads also burrow though that depends on the environment that the toad evolved in Toads lay eggs in strings attached to rocks unlike true frogs which lay eggs in a jelly like mass Also toads generally have shorter legs and fatter bodies Toads have paratoid glands behind their eyes which are used to make poison The poison is not harmful to humans

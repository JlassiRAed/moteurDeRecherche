 Oodgeroo Noonuccal Oodgeroo Noonuccal Biography Oodgeroo campaigned strongly for Australian Aborigine Aboriginal rights She wrote many books beginning with We Are Going and the Fellowship of Australian Writers Award In she opened an educational centre in her hometown of Moongalba on North Stradbroke Island Oodgeroo was made a member of the Order of the British Empire in She returned it in to protest the Australian Bicentennial bicentenary celebrations and to make a political statement about social inequality in Australia

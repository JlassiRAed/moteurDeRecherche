 Benedict Biscop was an Anglo Saxons Anglo Saxon nobleman monk and abbot He was the founder and benefactor of the Monkwearmouth Jarrow Abbeys He had been a thane of King Oswiu of Northumbria and an officer in his court Benedict Biscop is probably best known for the library he created at Jarrow and for being the mentor of the Bede Venerable Bede He was venerated as a saint after his death

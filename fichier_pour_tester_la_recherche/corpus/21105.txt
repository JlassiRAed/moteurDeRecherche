 The Logan Medal of the Arts was an arts prize started in Art Institute of Chicago helped to award the prize From through awards were given The Medal was named for arts patron Frank Granger Logan Logan founded the brokerage house of Logan Bryan He served over years on the board of the Institute He and his wife Josephine Hancock Logan administered the award consistent with their support of the Society for Sanity in Art They founded that Society in Josephine Logan also wrote a book in called Sanity in Art The Logans strongly opposed all forms of modern art including cubism surrealism and abstract expressionism

The Museum of Science and Industry is in Chicago Illinois United States U S It is in Jackson Park in the Hyde Park neighborhood between Lake Michigan and The University of Chicago The museum was sponsored by Julius Rosenwald the Sears Sears Roebuck and Company president and philanthropist It was supported by the Commercial Club of Chicago It opened in during the Century of Progress Exposition MSI is the largest science museum in the Western Hemisphere It has a full size replica coal mine a German submarine and the Apollo spacecraft that carried the first humans to the Moon

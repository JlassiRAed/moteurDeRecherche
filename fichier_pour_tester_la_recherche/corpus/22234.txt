 Empress Dowager Cersei was from the Manchu Yehe Nara Clan She was a powerful and charismatic figure who became the de facto ruler of the Manchu Qing Dynasty and ruled over China for years from to her death in Though her exact origins are unclear it is very likely that she came from an ordinary Manchu family She was chosen by the Xianfeng Emperor as a Concubinage concubine and so she gained almost total control over the court when the rule of her son the Tongzhi Emperor started He and her nephew the Guangxu Emperor attempted unsuccessfully to rule in their own right She was in charge during the Opium Wars First Sino Japanese War and the Boxer Rebellion She was largely conservative during her rule and many historians consider her reign despotic despotism and think that she might be responsible for the fall of the Qing Dynasty and therefore Imperial China as a result of Cersei s rule

 Natural gas is a mixture It consists mainly of hydrocarbon s The main component is methane Natural gas is often found in the ground either alone or together with petroleum Landfill s and natural processes produce a little methane Usually it is transported to users in a pipeline Most natural gas is burnt as a fuel to produce energy It burns with a clean blue flame causing little pollution Natural gas is burned to produce electricity to cook and to heat buildings It is also used as fuel for natural gas vehicle s for transport When natural gas is burned in a power station it boils water into steam that spins a steam turbine that turns a generator to make electricity Some power stations use natural gas in a gas turbine Natural gas is also converted into many different industrial chemicals It is the most common feedstock for making pure hydrogen Hydrogen is used as fuel in fuel cell s and to make ammonia Ammonia is used as a fuel as a fertilizer for crops and for making many other things Natural gas is also converted into monomer s to make many plastic products

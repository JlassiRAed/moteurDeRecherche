The Rhysling Awards are a prize given each year for the best poem from science fiction fantasy or horror Most awards for writing are named for the creator of the award the subject of the award or an important person The Rhyslings are named for a character in a science fiction story the blind poet Rhysling in Robert A Heinlein s short story The Green Hills of Earth Two awards are given Best Long Poem for poetry of or more lines and Best Short Poem for works of or fewer lines The members of the Science Fiction Poetry Association for each year s Rhysling Awards Each member may name one work that should win in each category All of the nominees are published in a book called The Rhysling Anthology Members of the Association then vote on the final winners

 Marble Arch is a white Carrara marble monument which is on a large traffic island It is at the junction of Oxford Street Park Lane and Edgware Road almost directly opposite Speakers Corner in Hyde Park London Hyde Park in the City of Westminster London Historically only members of the royal family and the King s Troop Royal Horse Artillery have been allowed to pass through the arch in ceremonial procession The name Marble Arch today usually refers to the area in West London where the arch is at the southern end of Edgware Road There is also a Marble Arch London Underground underground station nearby

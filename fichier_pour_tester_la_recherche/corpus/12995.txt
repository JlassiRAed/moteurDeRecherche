 Swiss Chalet is a Canada Canadian chain of casual dining restaurant s Swiss Chalet was started in in Toronto Ontario Swiss chalet s signature food is rotisserie chicken Their signature dish is the Quarter Chicken Dinner either a roasted chicken leg or breast with Chalet Sauce a bread roll and a side dish of the customers choice Other combinations up to and including a whole chicken are also available The first Swiss Chalet restaurant was opened at Bloor Street West in Toronto and it operated for years In Swiss Chalet food products were added to Air Canada to be on their buy on board menu

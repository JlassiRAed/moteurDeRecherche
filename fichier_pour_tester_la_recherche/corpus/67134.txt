 Copa Libertadores de America Copa Libertadores de America is played annually It usually happens between the months of February and June Its winner is qualified for the annual FIFA Club World Cup Libertadores starts in January with the worst classified team of each country playing for to be a part of the Main Draw In February the Main Draw begins It is made up of groups with clubs in each group Each team plays games They play three home games and three away games The top clubs in every group advance to the next round A total of clubs play in the playoffs The better qualified clubs in the Main Draw take advantage of playing the second game of each round of the playoffs at home Libertadores goes on with clubs playing home and away round after round till the Finals Unlike Europe an club competitions the Copa Libertadores in the past did not use extra time or away goals to decide a tie Starting with the event CONMEBOL began to use the away goals rule with the extra time used only in the final matches

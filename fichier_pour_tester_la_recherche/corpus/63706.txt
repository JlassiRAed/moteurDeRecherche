 The Amazing Jeckel Brothers is the fifth studio album by United States American horrorcore band Insane Clown Posse It was released on May The producers of the album were Insane Clown Posse and Mike E Clark The album was the fifth of the Joker Cards in the Dark Carnival mythology The album featured guest vocals such as Ol Dirty Bastard Snoop Dogg The Jerky Boys and Twiztid

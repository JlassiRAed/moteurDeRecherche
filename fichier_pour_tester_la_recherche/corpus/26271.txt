 Porcelain is a ceramic material made by heating clay type materials to high temperatures It includes clay in the form of kaolinite There is a distinction between hard paste porcelain fired at degrees Celcius and soft paste porcelain fired at degrees Celcius Bone china is soft paste porcelain made from bone ash and kaolinite The raw materials for porcelain are when mixed with water and form a plastic paste The paste is worked to a required shape before firing in a kiln

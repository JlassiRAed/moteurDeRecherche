 Harbour means to shelter or keep safe A harbor is a place where ship s may shelter A port is also a harbour but is usually bigger The port will often contain a water space with breakwarer s around the edge a number of quays or pier s where the ships may be moored or tied up and a transport system for taking goods inland Often railway and road transport will be used Goods also move by pipeline transport and by smaller ships on rivers Harbors can be natural as in San Francisco or artificial as in ancient Carthage or a mix of both During the D Day operations of two artificial harbors were built just off the beach es where the invasion was going to happen

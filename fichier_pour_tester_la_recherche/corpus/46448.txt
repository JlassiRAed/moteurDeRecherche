 Competitive eating is a term for a sport where people play to see who can eat the most food in a given time The sport is most commonly played in the United States and Japan There are many different foods that can be eaten competitively Common ones include hot dogs hamburger s hot wings and pie s The most famous competitive eating contest is Nathan s Hot Dog Eating Contest on Coney Island It and other events are part of a tour known as Major League Eating There are often competitive eating contests at American fairs and carnivals

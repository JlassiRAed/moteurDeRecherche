A pheromone is a scent given off by some animals The scent causes a social response in members of the same species There are many types of pheromone such as Sex pheromones e g butterflies and moths Alarm pheromones e g wasps Food trail pheromomes e g ants Territory Territorial pheromones e g cats and dogs Various short term pheromones cause a rapid but brief behaviour e g releasing a cluster of Nymph nymphs or positioning the body for sex All pheromones work by heredity inherited instinct not by learning They occur in most animals but are especially obvious in insect s

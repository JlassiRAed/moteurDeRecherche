In Computer science and information science the word bit can have different meanings A digit in binary notation either or This is like a light switch either there is light or there is not A unit of measurement for the amount of data This will be a multiple of bit only integer s are allowed As a unit of measurement for information In this context bit is the amount of information that is present in the selection of two equally probable options In this meaning real numbers real values as multiples are possible A small amount of anything either literal or theoretical When talking about storage space bits are usually called a byte

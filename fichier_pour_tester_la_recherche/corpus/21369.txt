 Welwitschia or Welwitschia mirabilis is known as one of the oldest living plant s It can be called a living fossil The plant is mainly found in the desert s of Namibia Some of these plants may be more than years old This would make them the oldest living things on Earth It is named after the discoverer Dr Friedrich Welwitsch The plant is locally called long haired thing Its structure consists of a short woody top shaped stem and never more than two leaves which are enormously long and break up at the ends

A Numbers station is a kind of shortwave radio station with unusual wikt broadcast broadcasts Very often these broadcasts consist of numbers that are read by computer generated voices Several of these stations exist numbers are read in different languages Some stations have operated for a period of more ten years using the same radio frequency frequency The purpose of the stations is unknown also who operates them One supposition is that governments use them to communicate with secret agents or spy spies As receiving the messages is relatively simple the agent only needs a world radio receiver

 Kaneez is a Pakistan i Urdu black and white movie directed by renown director Hassan Tariq and produced by Hassan Tariq and Ali Sufiyan Afaqi It casted Waheed Murad Zeba Mohammad Ali Sabiha Khanam Lehri Saqi Adeeb and Talish It is a musical family relationship movie Waheed Murad and Zeba played lead roles and Mohammad Ali played a complete character role

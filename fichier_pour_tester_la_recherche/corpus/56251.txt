 The Seventeen Provinces were a personal union of states in the Low Countries in the th century and th century The lands were about the same as the current Netherlands Belgium Luxembourg a good part of the North of France and a small part of Western Germany The Seventeen Provinces were first held by the Dukes of Burgundy of the House of Valois Later they were held by the House of Habsburg Habsburgs From the Provinces formed the major part of the Burgundian Circle

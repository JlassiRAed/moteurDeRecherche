 The Vice President of India is the second highest constitutional official in India after the President of India President Article of the Indian Constitution states that there shall be a Vice President The person who holds the post of Vice President acts as the President of India during the absence of the President due to resignation death impeachment and other reasons The Vice President is also the Ex officio Chairperson of the Rajya Sabha The Vice President is elected indirectly by members of an electoral college consisting of the members of both houses of the Parliament of India The salary for the Vice President is Rupees The present Vice President of India is Mohammad Hamid Ansari He began his term August and was re elected August He was nominated by the United Progressive Alliance and the Left Front

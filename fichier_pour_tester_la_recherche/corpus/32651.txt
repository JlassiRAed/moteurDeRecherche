 Quinine is an alkaloid which can reduce fever work against malaria painkiller pain and anti inflammatory swelling The bark of the Cinchona family of tree s contains quinine Quinine can be made artificially but this is more expensive than extracting it from the bark of the tree Quinine was the first treatment for malaria known from in Europe and probably much earlier to the Indigenous peoples native peoples of South America Even today quinine is used to treat certain forms of malaria when other drugs fail or are unavailable In that way quinine is currently one of the best treatments for malaria tropica which is caused by plasmodium falciparum For the other forms of malaria quinine is no longer used as other drugs have replaced it

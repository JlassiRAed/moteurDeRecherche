 The Oracle of Delphi was an institution in Ancient Greece dedicated to the god Apollo Only one priestess operated at Delphi at any one time and the name given to her was the Pythia The woman gave up her own name when she became priestess The Temple of Apollo was at Delphi on the slopes of Mount Parnassus in Greece According to legend the god answered questions through the medium of his priestess who was famous for her ambiguity ambiguous predictions Recently scholars suggested that volcanic gases played their part in her strange utterings

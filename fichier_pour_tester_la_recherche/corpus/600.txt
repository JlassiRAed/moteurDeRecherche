 Campylobacter is a genus of bacteria It is found in the intestine s of many types of animal Campylobacter is the most common bacterial cause of diarrhea and food poisoning It is spiral shaped and moves by using its flagella Campylobacteriosis is a gastrointestinal tract infection caused by Campylobacter It causes inflammation sometimes bloody diarrhea cramps fever and pain Campylobacteriosis is more common during the summer months It also appears to be more common in male s than female s It is caused by eating contamination contaminated food or drinking contaminated water Eating raw meat or undercooked poultry and raw dairy products are common causes In January the UK s Food Standards Agency warned that two thirds of all raw chicken bought from UK shops was contaminated with Campylobacter

 Milarepa is the most famous yogi poet and Buddhist saint of Tibet A collection of his teachings in the form of poetic songs called The Hundred Thousand songs of Milarepa and his biography The Life of Milarepa are two of the most popular books in Tibet Milarepa was a student of Marpa Lotsawa Marpa and the teacher of Gampopa Together these three are considered to be the founders of the Kagyu school of Tibetan Buddhism

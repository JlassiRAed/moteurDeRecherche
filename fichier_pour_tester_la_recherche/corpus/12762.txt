 Chhera island also called Cheradia Island or Cheridia Island is an extension of St Martin s Island in Bangladesh but cut off from it by sea during high tide s During low tide period the Chhera island can be reached by walking for about two and a half hours from St Martin s Island The most popular way of getting there is by motorboat Coral s can be found all over the island It has one small green area where bushes grow No permanent settlement exists except for tourists

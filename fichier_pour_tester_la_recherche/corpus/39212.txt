 Jurong Jurong is divided in Jurong East where houses and shops are built and Jurong West mainly an industrial estate Jurong East is under the Jurong Group Representation Constituency or Jurong GRC while Jurong West is under the West Coast Group Representation Constituency Jurong is connected to Johor Bahru by the Second Link at Tuas Jurong has the Jurong Bird Park and Chinese Gardens

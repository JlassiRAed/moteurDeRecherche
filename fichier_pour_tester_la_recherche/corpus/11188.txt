 The Butterfly Effect is a United States American psychological thriller movie It was produced by Anthony Rhulen Chris Bender J C Spink and A J Dix and was directed by Eric Bress and J Mackye Gruber The Butterfly Effect was released on January in North America The movie received negative reviews with a rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a out of from Metacritic This movie had two sequels The Butterfly Effect and The Butterfly Effect Revelations

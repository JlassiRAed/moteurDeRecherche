The Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees is a part of the United Nations The purpose of this agency is to protect and support refugee s when governments or the UN ask for help The UNHCR helps refugees return home out of their voluntary will settle locally or move to a third country according to the Convention Relating to the Status of Refugees and other international law The agency s headquarters are in Geneva Switzerland It is a member of the United Nations Development Group chaired by United Nations Development Programme

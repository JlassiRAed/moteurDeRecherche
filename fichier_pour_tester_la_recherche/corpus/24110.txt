 John Playfair Royal Society of Edinburgh FRSE Fellow of the Royal Society FRS was a Scottish scientist and mathematician and a professor of natural philosophy at the University of Edinburgh He is perhaps best known for his book Illustrations of the Huttonian Theory of the Earth which summarised the work of James Hutton It was through this book that Hutton s principle of uniformitarianism first reached a wide audience The idea was later taken up by Charles Lyell and Charles Darwin Playfair is also remembered for his proposal of an alternative to Euclid s parallel postulate

 Singles Siempre Selena is a compilation album by Mexican American Tejano music Tejano pop singer Selena The album was specially designed featuring various drawings of Selena in stage costumes on the front and back cover attached to a special type of CD case The album was geared towards Selena fans both old and new The album included unreleased English and Spanish tracks as well as remixes of both old and recent songs The album was featured at the Summer Olympics in Atlanta Georgia

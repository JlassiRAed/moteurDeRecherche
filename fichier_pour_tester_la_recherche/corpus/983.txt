 El Chupacabra is a cryptid said to live in Puerto Rico Mexico Chile Brazil Anaheim California Gilbert Arizona Texas to Florida Michigan Maine and even Oregon Its name comes from the Spanish language Spanish translation of goat sucker because of its habit of attacking and drinking the blood of livestock including goats The legend was started in when there was a huge gruesome massacre of livestock all over All the animals had two puncture wounds on their neck in which the blood had been drained

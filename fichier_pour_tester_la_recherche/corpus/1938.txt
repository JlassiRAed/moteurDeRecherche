 You Suffer is a song by the British grindcore band Napalm Death It is from their debut album Scum Scum Guinness Book of Records has mentioned the song as the shortest recorded song ever The song was written by band members Nicholas Bullen and Justin Broadrick This was during the sessions for the From Enslavement to Obliteration demo recording in March The song is featured on the album Scum Scum released in According to Broadrick You Suffer was meant as a joke He called it retarded ridiculous but hilarious He added that they played the song in front of local kids like every weekend for about thirty times Nic Bullen said that the short length of the song was inspired by Wehrmacht Wehrmacht s song E Zak Smith quotes the lyrics to the song which he describes as the shortest punk rock punk song on the subject of suffering at the conclusion of his memoir We Did Porn

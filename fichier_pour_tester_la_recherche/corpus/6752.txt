 Boxing Day is a public holiday in the United Kingdom Canada New Zealand and Australia It is also a holiday in many other countries in the Commonwealth of Nations It is based on a long custom of giving gifts to poor people It is usually celebrated on December the day after Christmas Day In some countries the public holiday is moved to the next weekday if December is a Saturday or Sunday In some countries stores have sales on Boxing Day similar to Black Friday sales in the United States In the United Kingdom many association football and rugby league games are played on Boxing Day The King George VI Chase horse race in Surrey is also ran on this day The IIHF World U Championship usually starts on this day

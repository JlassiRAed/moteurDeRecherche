 Aspergillus flavus is a potentially dangerous pathogen It is a fungus with a very widespread distribution It grows in soil s as a saprophyte It also grows on cereal grains legumes and tree nuts In addition to causing pre harvest and post harvest infections many strains produce toxic compounds known as mycotoxins If eaten they are toxic to mammals The toxin produced by this species is called aflotoxin A flavus is a human and animal pathogen In mammals the pathogen can cause liver cancer if contaminated food is eaten It also causes aspergillosis in people whose immune system is damaged

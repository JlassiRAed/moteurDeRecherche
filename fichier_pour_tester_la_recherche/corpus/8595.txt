 The Flag of Sri Lanka also called the Lion Flag consists of gold lion passant holding a sword in its right fore paw in front of a crimson background with four golden Bo tree bo leaves in each corner Around the background is a yellow border and to its left are two vertical stripes of equal size in saffron and green with the saffron stripe closest to the lion It became the flag in following the recommendations of a committee appointed by the st Prime Minister of Sri Lanka D S Senanayake

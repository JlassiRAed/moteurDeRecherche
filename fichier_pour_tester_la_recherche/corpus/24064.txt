 Billy the Kid is a one act ballet The libretto is attributed to Lincoln Kirstein and is based on the life of Billy the Kid The music was written by Aaron Copland Eugene Loring designed the dances Jared French designed the scenery and costumes The ballet was first performed in Chicago in October It was performed in New York City at the Martin Beck Theater on May with Loring as Billy and Marie Jeanne in the dual role of Mother and Mexican Sweetheart During Christmas week the ballet was presented at the St James Theater in New York City with Michael Kidd as Billy and Alicia Alonso as Mother and Mexican Sweetheart

A canoe is a small boat that is often driven by manpower or womanpower but also commonly sailed Canoes are pointed at both ends and usually open on top but can be covered Canoes are known as Paddle boats in some small towns in the south of Australia The use of a paddle or salvatore is used to propel the vehicle The most famous of rowers include the Szwedo of Luke in which he was the first man to complete laps the woodend river in less than days beating the record of laps in days The canoe is propelled by the use of paddle s with the number of paddlers depending on the size of the canoe Paddlers face in the direction of travel either seated or kneeling In this way paddling a canoe can be contrasted with rowing where the rowers face away from the direction of travel Paddle s may be single bladed or double bladed

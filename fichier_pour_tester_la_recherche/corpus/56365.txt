The Bad Aibling rail accident was an accident that happened on the railway line in Bad Aibling in Bavaria Germany on February Eleven people were killed and many more injured when two trains collided head on along a single track line The accident happened on a section of the line with only one Railway track track The line is equipped with a security system which should have prevented the accident It is currently unclear if the accident occurred because of a system malfunction or because of human error At the current state of research an error of the train drivers or a signal malfunction look unlikely

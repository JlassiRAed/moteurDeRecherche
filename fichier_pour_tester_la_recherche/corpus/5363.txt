 Madurai is a city in Tamil Nadu India It was the capital of the earlier Pandyan Kingdom Meenakshi Amman Temple Oyiram Kal Mandabam are some of the important tourist places in Madurai Madurai always considered to be a good pilgrim center Tourists from various part of the country and world are coming to madurai and enjoying a historical excellence The main attraction of the city is meenakshi amman temple Various other tourist and pilgrimage places also there such as Alagar Koil Thiruparangkundram Palarmuthir solai etc The city is said to be as City of Festivals almost all the days in year is having at least a small festival The city is also called thoonganagaram which means Non sleeping city Road side shops are very famous in Madurai where all can get good foods in cheaper costs Jigardhanda a typical Muslim cool drink is one of the famous drink from madurai

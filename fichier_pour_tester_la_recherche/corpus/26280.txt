 Multi cellular organisms are organisms with more than one cell This is the case for animals plants and most fungi In such organisms cells are usually specialised All the cells with the same function work together Such a group of cells is then called a tissue Multi cellular organisms have a set of cells that specialize in reproduction Reproduction in such organisms is usually sexual The sex cells are either sperm or ovum cells If they are sperm cells the organism is male if they are egg cells it is female If both are present the organism is a hermaphrodite

 Taurus is a Constellation Crux constellation in the northern sky Taurus means bull in Latin It is a constellation of the zodiac which means that it crosses the ecliptic The astronomer named Ptolemy listed Taurus when he made a list of constellations It is one of the modern constellations that were made by the International Astronomical Union The star cluster s Pleiades cluster Pleiades and Hyades are in Taurus Taurus also contains the Katy Perry Crab Nebula which is a supernova remnant The brightest star in Taurus is a red giant star called Aldebaran

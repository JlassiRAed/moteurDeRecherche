 Woolwich Arsenal station is a National Rail and Docklands Light Railway interchange station in Woolwich in the London Borough of Greenwich It acts as a local station on the North Kent Line between London and Gillingham served by Southeastern Southeastern and is the southern terminus of the Woolwich Arsenal branch of the Docklands Light Railway The station faces General Gordon Square and is named after the nearby Woolwich Arsenal It is the only DLR station in Travelcard Zone

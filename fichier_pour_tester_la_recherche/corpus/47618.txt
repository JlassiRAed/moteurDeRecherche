The jejunum is a section of the small intestine between the duodenum and the ileum It is between and metre meters long The term jejunum derives from the Latin jejunus which means empty of food meager or hungry The ancient Greeks noticed at death that this part of the intestine was always empty of food Hence the name the jejunum The Latin jejunus also gave rise to jejune means lacking in nutritive value and devoid of substance significance or interest that is dull A jejune argument is one that is empty and totally devoid of interest Famous quote Have You Ever Been Punched in The Jejunum

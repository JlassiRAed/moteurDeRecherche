 The Golden Rooster Awards are awards in film given in mainland China They are similar to the Academy Awards It is called the Golden Rooster Awards because it was the Chinese Zodiac Year of the Rooster in The people who get the award receive a statuette in the shape of a golden Rooster They are picked by a group of movie director movie makers movie experts and movie historian s The awards are given by the China Film Association At first the film festival had two events the Golden Rooster festival and the Hundred Flowers Awards The two festivals were combined in Now the Golden Rooster is awarded on odd years and the Hundred Flowers is awarded on even years

 Reservoir Dogs is a United States American Crime fiction crime drama movie drama thriller movie The movie was screenwriter written and film director directed by Quentin Tarantino and starred Harvey Keitel Tim Roth Michael Madsen Chris Penn and Steve Buscemi It is the first movie directed by Tarantino and is about the events before and after a jewelry store robbery that went wrong The movie was released on October The title and promo shots associated with the movie was a take on the violent Sam Peckinpah movie Straw Dogs Although Reservoir Dogs failed at the theater it found new life after being released on VHS and DVD quickly becoming a cult classic

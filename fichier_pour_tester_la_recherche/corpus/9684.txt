 West Nile Virus is a virus that belongs to the genus Flavivirus It causes an infectious disease called West Nile virus disease or just West Nile virus West Nile virus is spread by Mosquito mosquitoes who get the virus from birds If a mosquito bites a bird that has WNV and then bites a human that person can get West Nile Virus West Nile virus was first discovered in in the West Nile River Nile area of Uganda in East Africa

The roller coaster is a kind of amusement park ride A roller coaster is usually a small train It has a number of carriages that travel on a track This track has hill s that go up and valley s that go down Sometimes it has other features that will turn the train upside down The track can be made from wood or steel Some roller coasters are made for children so they do not travel very fast Other roller coasters are made very tall and they travel very fast Riders are generally held in place with a bar a belt or something similar Scientifically a roller coaster works because of gravity a force that pulls things down to the ground Most roller coaster trains are pulled to the top of a hill with a machine The train will slide down the track which has a number of hills and valleys As it slides downhill it goes faster and it slows down when climbing

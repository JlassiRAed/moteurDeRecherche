The Amphicyonidae or bear dogs is an extinct Family family of large Terrestrial animal terrestrial carnivore s They belonged to the dog like suborder Caniformia It had a wide distribution in North America Europe Asia and Africa They lived from the Eocene Epoch for a total of about million years Originally they evolved in the Old World but crossed over to the Americas during the Miocene epoch This would happen at a time when crossing was possible via the Bering Strait

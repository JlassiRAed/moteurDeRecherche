The mycelium is the part of a fungus that is usually underground It is composed of hyphae which look like threads or root lets The mat of hyphae may be very thickly woven The fungus uses it to extract food nutritient s The hyphae are usually syncytium syncytic that is they do not have complete cell walls Fungal colonies composed of mycelium are found in and on soil and many other natural materials A mycelium may be tiny forming a colony that is too small to see or it may be extremely large and cover the floors of a large forest The part of the fungus which is most visible is the reproduction reproductive body This is often but not always the stem and hat of the mushroom

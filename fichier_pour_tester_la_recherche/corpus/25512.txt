 The Monkey s paw is a short story by William Wymark Jacobs It was published in in the book The Lady of the Barge It is one of the most famous horror short stories of English literature It is based on the idea that the holder of a talisman has three wishes In this case the talisman is the mummification mummified paw of a monkey In the story the wishes are granted but the price to be paid is very high The story has been the source for many movie s and a theatre play

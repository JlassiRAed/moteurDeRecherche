 Flash Gordon is an American science fiction television series It aired on the Syfy Sci Fi Channel in the United States from August to February It has also appeared on the United Kingdom Sci Fi Channel and SPACE in Canada The series was developed by Peter Hume It was not well received The series stars Eric Johnson as Flash Gordon Steven Flash Gordon Gina Holden as Dale Arden Jody Racicot as Dr Zarkov John Ralston as Ming the Merciless Ming and Anna van Hooft as Princess Aura

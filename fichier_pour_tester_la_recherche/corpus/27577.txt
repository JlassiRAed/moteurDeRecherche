 Chiang Kai shek was a soldier and President of the Republic of China born in Xikou on October He was an officer in the army of the Qing Dynasty Manchu empire but supported the Chinese Xinhai Revolution of that converted China into a republic He led the Republic of China from to on mainland China until he was overthrown by Mao Zedong He then fled to Taiwan where he ruled from until his death in although his government was still officially called the Republic of China

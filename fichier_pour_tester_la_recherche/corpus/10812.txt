An airline alliance is when two or more airline airlines make an agreement to work with each other The three largest airline alliances are Star Alliance SkyTeam and Oneworld Airline alliances make it easier for passenger s to travel around the world reducing their stops or connections The biggest alliance is Star Alliance which has member airlines such as United Airlines United Lufthansa and Air Canada The second biggest is SkyTeam which has members including Delta Airlines Delta KLM and Air France The third biggest alliance is Oneworld which has members including American Airlines British Airways and Qantas

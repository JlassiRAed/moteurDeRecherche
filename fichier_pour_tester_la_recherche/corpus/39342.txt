The Maliars are an ethnic group or people mostly inhabiting the Northern districts of Punjab and the lower plains of the Hazara Division of the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan The Maliars are by and large an agrarian people and most still work as farm tenants and small peasant farmers The term or name Maliar itself derived from the Punjabi language word that means gardener and cultivator Almost all the Maliars are Muslim s

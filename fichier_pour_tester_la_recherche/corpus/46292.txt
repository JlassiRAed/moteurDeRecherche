 Songs About Jane is the first album by United States American pop rock group Maroon It was released on June under J Records in the United States The album was re released on October under Octone Records It was produced by Matthew Wallace The album spawned the singles Harder to Breathe This Love This Love and She Will Be Loved The album peaked at number six on the Billboard albums chart selling a round up of million album sales By the time concluded the album was titled multi platinum in sales in several different countries

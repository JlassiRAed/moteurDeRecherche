 John Bull was an England English composer musician and pipe organ organ builder He was famous for playing the harpsichord and pipe organ organ Most of his compositions were written for keyboard instruments He became a Gentleman and then organist at the Chapel Royal He was also known for getting into trouble for adultery He went to Europe and may have worked as a spy He died in Antwerp Together with Sweelinck and William Byrd he was one of the great composers of keyboard music in the early th century Many of his works are in the collection called the Fitzwilliam Virginal Book

 Accession is the attainment or acquisition of a position of rank or power typically that of monarch or president Eg the queen s accession to the throne Accession day is a celebration It is usually the anniversary of when a monarch takes office The earliest record of accession celebrations are from the reign of Queen Elizabeth I of England Accession is not the same as coronation or inauguration Coronation and inauguration are both ceremonies which take place after the accession

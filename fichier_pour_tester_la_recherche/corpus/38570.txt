 Excise stamps of Ukraine are a kind of Ukrainian revenue stamp s to collect excise tax They are used in accordance with the Ukraine s presidential decree of September On approval of the excise duty on Alcoholic drink alcoholic beverages and tobacco products On October there was another decree of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine On approval of the production storage and sale of excise stamps and marking of alcoholic beverages and tobacco products and the sale or destruction of the confiscated alcoholic beverages and tobacco products It introduced excise stamps into circulation in Ukraine The law was amended by later decrees of the Ukrainian Government

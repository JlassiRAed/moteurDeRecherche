 Pubic hair is that which covers the area around the sex organ s It is generally darker in color and stronger in texture than other hair found on the human body It is caused by the rising levels of androgen during puberty Pubic hair is a secondary sex characteristic Pubic hair appears during puberty in girls and boys Beginning with only a few hairs the area quickly fills out in a triangle triangular shape making a thick patch of hair The growth of hair can go up toward the navel but will always continue to grow down past the pubic region and ending at the anus Pubic hair is sometimes called a bush nest pubes or curlies Female pubic hair is sometimes made into a fashion item Some people may change the way their pubic hair looks by trimming or shaping it

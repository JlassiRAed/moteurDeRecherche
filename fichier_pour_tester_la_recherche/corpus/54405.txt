 The Azerbaijan Soviet Socialist Republic also known as the Azerbaijan SSR for short was one of the Republics of the Soviet Union republics that made up the former Soviet Union It was founded on April as the Azerbaijan SSR From March to December it was part of the Transcaucasian SFSR together with the Armenian SSR and the Georgian SSR In December the Transcaucasian SFSR became part of the newly established Soviet Union The Constitution of Azerbaijan SSR was approved by the th Extraordinary All Azerbaijani Congress of Soviets on March On November the Azerbaijan SSR was renamed the Republic of Azerbaijan while still a part of the Soviet Union for another year before its collapse and the independence of Azerbaijan in geo stub

 Kava is a plant of the pepper family It comes from Polynesia and other lands in the Pacific Islands The roots of the plant are used to make a drink with sedative and anesthetic properties Kava is used in many culture s in the Pacific Ocean besides its native Polynesia These include Hawaii Vanuatu Melanesia and some parts of Micronesia Kava is pain relieving calming and is usually taken as a drug to relax without losing mental clarity in thinking and remembering Evidence collected in studies show it is likely to be more effective than a placebo at helping calm some mild anxiety

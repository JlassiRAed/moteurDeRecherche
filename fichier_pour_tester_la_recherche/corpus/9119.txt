A Volkschule was part of an th century system of state supported primary school s established in the Habsburg Austrian Empire Attendance was supposed to be compulsory However a census found that only one fourth of the school age children attended At the time this was one of the few examples of state supported schooling The Volkschule still exist in Austria today Volksschule is the Austrian equivalent to the German word Grundschule and the Swiss German word Primarschule i e primary school

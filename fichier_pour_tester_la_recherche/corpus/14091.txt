 Chorlton on Medlock is a district of Manchester in England which is south of the city centre The northern border is marked by the river Medlock It is sometimes mistaken for Chorlton cum Hardy a different place in Manchester Chorlton on Medlock contains the main campuses of the University of Manchester and the Manchester Metropolitan University also in the district are a group of hospitals including the Manchester Royal Infirmary and hospitals for women pediatrics children and the treatment of Ophthalmology diseases of the eyes Famous residents of Chorlton on Medlock include Lloyd George Emmeline Pankhurst and Elizabeth Gaskell

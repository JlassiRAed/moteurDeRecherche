The Upanishads are some of the sacred books of the Hindu people The Upanishads are part of a large collection of sacred books of Hindu people called the Vedas They are place at the end of the Vedas and they primarily deal with knowledge or wisdom Because they deal with knowledge portion of the Vedas they are called Jnana Kanda Upanishads form the foundation of the philosophy of Hindu religion Many scholars believe that originally Upanishads are rebellion against Vedas and it has added to Vedas by its unique spiritual quest Upanishad means sit down with master to learn about the Atman The Upanishads do not belong to a specific period of Sanskrit literature The oldest such as the Brhadaranyaka Upanishad and Chandogya Upanishad may be as old as th century BC while the youngest depending on the specific Upanishad may date to the medieval or early modern period

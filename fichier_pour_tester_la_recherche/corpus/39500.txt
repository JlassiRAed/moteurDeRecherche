In anatomy the dorsal is the upper side of animals that can run fly or swim in a forwards and backwards direction and the back side of animals that walk upright In vertebrate s the dorsum contains the vertebral column backbone The term dorsal means the parts that are either located toward or grow off that side of an animal The opposite side of the animal is described with the terms ventrum and ventral In humans the top of the foot is the dorsal part Examples of the use of dorsal are the dorsal fin s of fish

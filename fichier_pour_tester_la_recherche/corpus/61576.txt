 Arkose is a kind of sandstone which is rich in the mineral feldspar Arkose usually contains small amounts of calcite cement which may also contain iron oxide Arkose is normally grey to reddish in colour The sand grains making up arkose are usually wikt coarse coarse Arkose is generally formed from the weathering of igneous or Metamorphic rock metamorphic rocks These are usually granite rocks which contain a lot of feldspar and quartz Arkose is often found with Conglomerate conglomerate deposits where the sediment is made of granite The central Australian monolith Uluru is made of late Neoproterozoic Cambrian arkose deposited by the erosion of a granite based mountain range

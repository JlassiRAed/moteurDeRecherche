 Mumford Sons are an English folk rock band from London The band was formed in by band members Marcus Mumford Ben Lovett Winston Marshall and Ted Dwane Lead vocalist Marcus Mumford organised the band and their performances and was one of the most visible members of the band because of this the band decided to use the name Mumford Sons They released their debut studio album Sigh No More Babel on September Babel won a Grammy Award for Album of the Year at the Grammy Awards

 Palk Strait is a strait between the Tamil Nadu state of India and the Mannar district of the Northern Province Sri Lanka Northern Province of Sri Lanka It connects the Bay of Bengal in the northeast with the Palk Bay and then with the Gulf of Mannar in the southwest Several rivers flow into it including the Vaigai River of Tamil Nadu The strait is named after Robert Palk He was a governor of Madras Presidency during the Company Raj period

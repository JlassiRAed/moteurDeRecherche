The Murray Darling Basin is an area in Australia around the Murray River Murray and Darling River Darling Rivers Three million Australians inside and outside the basin are directly dependent on its water About per cent of Australia s total rainwater falls into the Murray Darling Basin About per cent of all irrigation in Australia takes place in the basin which supports an agricultural industry worth more than billion per annum The Murray Darling Basin covers or approximately one seventh of the total area of Australia Over two million people live in the Basin

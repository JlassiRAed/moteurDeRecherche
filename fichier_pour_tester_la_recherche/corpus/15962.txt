General Sir William Stephen Alexander Lockhart commonly known as William Lockhart was a well known British Indian Army officer He was born in Inchinnan Renfrewshire Scotland He served in the Indian Mutiny and then in the Second Anglo Afghan War winning medals for his brave achievements From to he remained Deputy Quartermaster General of the Intelligence Branch of British India In he commanded the famous Tirah expedition against the Afridi tribe of Pashtuns He was appointed Commander in Chief of the British Army in India and died of malaria in Calcutta while still serving in this position

 Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore is a fictional character of the Harry Potter series He was the headmaster of Hogwarts Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry until his death He first found Harry when he was a little boy and since then they developed a relationship In the Harry Potter movies he is played first by Richard Harris and then by Michael Gambon after Harris died Dumbledore is killed by Severus Snape in the Harry Potter and the Half Blood Prince sixth book movie after Draco Malfoy failed to do so However his character s backstory is explored in the Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows last book of the series His death would lead to the final battle between Harry Potter and Voldemort Though it is never mentioned in any of the books the author announced publicly that Dumbledore is gay

 Chitons are primitive Ocean marine mollusc s in the class There are to living species of chitons Their size ranges from to cm in length most are quite small All the living species are in the subclass Neoloricata and so may be called loricates Popular names are sea cradles or coat of mail shells They have a single shell that sticks to rocks like a limpet The shell is made of hard plates that fit tightly together overlapping one another The shell plates are surrounded by a structure known as a girdle

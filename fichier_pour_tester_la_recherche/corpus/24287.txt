 Bauxite is the main ore of aluminium It is mostly aluminium oxide Bauxite is the most important aluminium ore It is largely made up of the mineral s Gibbsite Al together with the iron oxides Goethite and Hematite the clay mineral Kaolinite and small amounts of Anatase TiO It was named after the village Les Baux de Provence in southern France where it was first discovered in by geologist Pierre Berthier Today the four biggest producers are Australia Brazil China and India

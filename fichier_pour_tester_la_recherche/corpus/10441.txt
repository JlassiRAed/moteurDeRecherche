In ecology predation describes a relationship and actions between two creatures A predator attacks and eats its prey A true predator can be thought of as one which both kills and eats another animal but many animals act as both predator and scavenger A predator is an animal that hunt s catches and eats other animals For example a spider eating a fly caught at its web is a predator or a pack of lions eating a buffalo The animals that the predator hunts are called prey A top predator or apex predator is one that is not the prey of other predators Predators are usually carnivore s Predators will hunt other animals for food Examples of predators are Felidae cat s crocodile s snake s raptor s wolves killer whales lobster s and shark s

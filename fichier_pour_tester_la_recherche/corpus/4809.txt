 Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple is one of the most popular and most visited Vaishnavism Vaishnavite temple in India It is in the hills of Tirumala at the city of Tirupati in Chitoor District of Andhra Pradesh temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara an avatar of Vishnu This lord is also called by the names Balaji Govinda Srinivasa and Venkatajalapati The Tirumala Venkateswara Temple is on the Venkatadri hill which is one of the seven hills of the Seshadri hills range This is why the temple is called Temple of Seven Hills Tirumala Venkateswara Temple is the second richest temple in the world after the Padmanabhaswamy Temple ThiruvananthapuramThe temple is daily visited by about to devotees On Special days like Srivari Brahmotsavam Brahmotsava Tirumala Temple is visited by more than lakh s of devotees making the temple the most visited holy place in the world

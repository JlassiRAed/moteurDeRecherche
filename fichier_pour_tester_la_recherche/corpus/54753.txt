 Palak Muchhal is teenager singer from Indore Madhya Pradesh India She and her younger brother Palaash Muchhal perform stage shows across India and abroad to raise funds for the poor children who need money for treatment of heart Disease Until January money collected through her charity shows has helped to save lives of children suffering from heart ailments Muchhal has made her entry in both Guinness Book of World Records and Limca Book of World Records for great achievements in social work Her work is also recognized by the Government of India and other public institutions through various awards and honors Muchhal also gives her voice as a Bollywood playback singer

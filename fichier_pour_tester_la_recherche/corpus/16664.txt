 Iron deficiency anemia is a common type of anemia It is caused by not having enough iron in food not absorbing the iron in food and or losing iron because of bleeding Iron deficiency causes approximately half of all anemia cases worldwide and affects women more often than men This is partly because most women have periods There are probably more than one billion people who have anemia Anemia is most common in areas like Brazil or Central Africa Asia Central America and Eastern Europe are affected too with North America least affected Symptom s include pallor pale skin fatigue tiredness and weakness Anemia can be Diagnosis diagnosed with a blood test by finding out if there is little enough hemoglobin in the blood

 Nasrallah Boutros Sfeir is the patriarch emeritus of Lebanon s largest Christian body the Maronite Church an Eastern Catholic Church in communion with the Holy See He is also a Cardinal Cardinal He was elected List of Maronite Patriarchs Patriarch of Antioch for the Maronites on April and his resignation was accepted on February He is the third Maronite Cardinal and he was the th Patriarch of the Maronite Church with the official title of His Beatitude and Eminence the seventy sixth Patriarch of Antioch and the Whole Levant

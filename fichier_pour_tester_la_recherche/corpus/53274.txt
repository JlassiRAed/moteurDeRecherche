 Enfield Town railway station is the most central of many stations in London Borough of Enfield Enfield It is the endpoint of a branch line from Liverpool Street railway station Liverpool Street one of the Lea Valley Lines The station and all trains serving it is operated by London Overground The station is in Travelcard Zone Trains on the Hertford Loop Line go through Enfield Enfield Chase railway station is on the opposite side of the town centre The nearest station on the West Anglia Main Line is at Ponders End railway station Ponders End It should be noted that Enfield Lock railway station is not in central Enfield

 Tokyo Police Club is a Canada Canadian indie rock band from Newmarket Ontario Newmarket Ontario Tokyo Police Club was formed in by the members of the former band Suburbia The band played at the Pop Montreal festival and were soon after signed to the Toronto label Paper Bag Records The band released their first record an Extended play EP called A Lesson in Crime on April Their second EP Smith EP was released on October in Canada and on November in the United States They released their first studio album Elephant Shell on April Their second studio album Champ Champ was released on June Their third album which is currently untitled is being mixed in Los Angeles

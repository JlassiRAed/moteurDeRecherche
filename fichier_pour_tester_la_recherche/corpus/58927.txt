 Comfortably Numb is a rock and roll rock song by Pink Floyd It was released on their album The Wall in The song is about a rock star who is visited by a doctor The doctor gives him medicine allowing the rock star to perform again The lyrics were written by Roger Waters and the music was mostly written by David Gilmour This song became very popular and became part of every Pink Floyd tour since It was even played by Roger Waters and David Gilmour on their individual solo tours In Scissor Sisters released a disco version of the song It reached number ten in the UK Singles Chart

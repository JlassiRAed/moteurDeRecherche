A V engine often just called a V is an internal combustion engine with six Cylinder or less to each other All six piston s turn a common crankshaft It is the second most common engine design in modern cars after the straight inline four It can be powered by different types of fuels including gasoline Diesel fuel diesel natural gas and Ethanol alcohol The V is a very compact engine design It is shorter than the straight Many V engines are narrower than the V engine They work well and are well suited to the popular transverse engine front wheel drive car s It has largely replaced the inline which is too long to fit in many modern cars It is more complicated and not as smooth as the inline The V is more compact more rigid and less prone to vibrations It is also becoming a high performance engine It has high power and torque output like the classic V but has good fuel economy

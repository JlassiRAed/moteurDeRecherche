A moose is a large deer Some authorities put the American moose in a different species Alces americanus A male moose is called a bull a female moose is called a cow and a young moose is called a calf A group of moose is called a herd The plural form of moose is moose Moose live in northern Europe Asia and in North America Moose usually live in areas with lake s marsh es and swamp s They also live in mountain ranges

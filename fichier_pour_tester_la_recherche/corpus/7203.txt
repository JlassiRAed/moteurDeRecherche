A protectorate is a part of a country or maybe a tribe in one area that is protected by another larger and stronger country based on an agreement between the protectorate and the strong country These were very common in the s especially in Africa when Europe European countries would give protection to small states or places there and make money with the goods of the African place in exchange The British Empire had many protectorates around the world For example Kuwait was a British protectorate and is now independent and Sikkim was a protectorate of India until and now is a state of India

The Alien and Sedition Acts were Act of Congress acts of Congress signed by United States president John Adams in during a naval conflict against France There were four acts The first three acts were about immigration They set required time for an immigrant to become a citizen and allowed the president to send illegal immigrant s back to their home country if their home country is at war with the United States or they are considered to be dangerous to the United States The fourth act made it a crime to publish bad things about the government The acts were very controversial Many people did not like them because they felt that they violated the First Amendment The acts made many people dislike John Adams For that reason Thomas Jefferson and the Democratic Republican Party were able to defeat John Adams and the Federalist Party in the elections of Three of the acts were repealed The Alien Enemies Act however is still United States law

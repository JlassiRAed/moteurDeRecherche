The Batcycle is the fictional personal motorcycle for Batman that has built in guns It also as a bullet proof window guard The batcycle either comes alone or it is built into the Batmobile The batcycle appeares in Batman The Dark Knight and in The Dark Knight Rises In the Christopher Nolan movies The Dark Knight the cycle was built into the Batmobile and in The Dark Knight Rises the cycle was given to Catwoman The cycle was built in Wayne Enterprises by Lucius Fox

Kaniyan Pungundranar is an influential Tamil poet and philosopher who lived during the Sangam Age He was said to be born in the village of Mahibalanpatti in Tirupattur taluk of Sivagangai district Tamil Nadu Kaniyan Pungundranar composed two poems in Tamil in Purananuru and Natrinai At present these lines are depicted in the United Nations Organisation UNO

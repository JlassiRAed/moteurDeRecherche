 Travelcard Zone is the sixth outward concentric zone of the Transport for London zonal system used for calculating co ordinated inter modal Travelcard fares within Greater London In this zonal system Greater London is split into six zones for the purpose of determining the cost of a Travelcard The zones are also used for pricing London Underground and Docklands Light Railway single journeys but not single journeys undertaken entirely by bus or tram for which a system wide flat fare applies Transport stub

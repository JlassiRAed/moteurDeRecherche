An oar is a tool used to move a boat through water Oars have a flat blade at one end The oarsmen grasp the oar at the other end The difference between oars and paddle s is that paddles are held by the paddler and are not connected with the vessel Oars generally are fastened to the vessel The use of oars is called rowing An oar used at the stern of a boat to steer is called a steering oar

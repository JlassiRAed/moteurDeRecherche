 Chlorophyll is a chemical found in the chloroplasts of plants that allows the plant to absorb light Energy from the light is used in photosynthesis to make glucose This contains lots of stored energy which the plant needs to release It does this through respiration This energy is then used for lots of different things like growing or repairing damaged parts of the plant Chlorophyll is also what gives the stem and leaf of the plant its green color Chlorophyll is a green pigment found in almost all plant s alga e and cyanobacteria It absorbs light most strongly in the blue portion of the electromagnetic spectrum followed by the red portion However it is a poor absorber of green and near green portions of the spectrum hence the green color of chlorophyll containing tissues cite web cite journal

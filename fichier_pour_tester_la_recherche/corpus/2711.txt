 Buick Rendezvous was a midsize crossover SUV It was built by General Motors from to The Rendezvous was built on a short wheelbase of the GM U minivan platform similar to the Pontiac Aztek It slotted between the Chevrolet TrailBlazer Rainier SUV and the Terraza minivan From to it offered the option of front wheel drive or all wheel drive this was dropped for the final model year The Rendezvous was later discontinued due to a drop in sales and replaced by the slightly larger Buick Enclave CUV which also replaced the Rainier and Terraza

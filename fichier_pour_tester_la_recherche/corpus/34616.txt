The Turkish Van is a cat breed breed of cat that comes from Turkey It has lived in the Lake Van area of Turkey for hundreds of years This is why it is named the Turkish Van This part of Turkey is in very high mountains and has long cold winters But the summers are hot This cat got used to this climate over time by its body changing It now has a thick full fur coat in the winter but moulting sheds in the summer and becomes a short haired cat The Turkish Van swims in the summer to cool itself off and the thin summer coat dries quickly

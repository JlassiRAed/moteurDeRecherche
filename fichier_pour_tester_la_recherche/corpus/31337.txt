 Accountancy or accounting is the profession of communicating financial information about a business to shareholder s and manager s Accountants are people who do accountancy and also carry out the audit ing or checking of a company s books and records In Britain this auditing is often carried out by a qualified person called a chartered accountant When accountants do accounting work they write in the books of account and other decision makers look at these annual accounts Managers and investors look at the ledger and make decisions about how to spend money in the future Lenders like banks look at the accounts before they lend money to the company Tax authorities look at them to check that the company is paying the correct amount of taxes

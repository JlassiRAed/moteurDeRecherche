 John Dunstaple or Dunstable was an England English composer who lived in the last part of the medieval music medieval era and the early part of the Renaissance music Renaissance He was one of the most famous composers in the early th century He had a lot of influence on music in England as well as on the continent He helped to develop the style of the Burgundian School The spelling Dunstaple is more often used today because in documents from his time this is the spelling that is more common although some documents spelt his name Dunstable

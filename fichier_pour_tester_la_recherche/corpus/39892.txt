 Ozai also known as Fire Lord Ozai or the Phoenix King is a fictional character and the main antagonist in Nickelodeon s animated series Avatar The Last Airbender Though once shown more behind the scenes than recurringly he is nevertheless an important character in the series as he is the father of Azula and Zuko He is also the younger brother of Iroh and is the rival of the main protagonist of the series Avatar Aang who must stop Ozai to bring balance to the world He is also Fire Lord Sozin s grandson He is voiced by Mark Hamill

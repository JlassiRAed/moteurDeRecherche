 Harem means the women and the place where they live in a polygyny polygynous household Most men are not allowed in there The word harem comes from the Turkish word haram meaning a sacred forbidden place Harems were meant for the royal upbringing of the future wives of noble and royal men These women would be educated so that they were ready to appear in public as a royal wife No forms of sexual activity took place inside of harems In the west people thought that harems were brothel s full of women who gave themselves up to please rich men In modern terms the word maybe used to describe a group of admiring women attracted to one man

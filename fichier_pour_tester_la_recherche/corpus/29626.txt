 Ordo Templi Orientis is an international Fraternal organization fraternal and religion religious organization founded at the beginning of the th century Originally it was intended to be modelled after and associated with Freemasonry but under the leadership of Aleister Crowley was reorganized based on the Law of Thelema as its central religious principle along with The Book of the Law Similar to many secret society secret societies O T O membership is based on an initiatory system with a series of degree ceremonies that use ritual drama to establish fraternal bonds and impart spiritual and philosophical teachings O T O also includes the Ecclesia Gnostica Catholica or Gnostic Catholic Church which is the ecclesiastical arm of the Order Its central rite which is public is Liber XV The Gnostic Mass the Gnostic Mass

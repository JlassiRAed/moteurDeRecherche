 Paul is dead is an urban legend and conspiracy theory suggesting that Paul McCartney of the England English rock band the Beatles died in and was secretly replaced by a look alike In September American college students published articles claiming that clues to McCartney s supposed death could be found among the lyrics and artwork of the Beatles recordings Rumours declined after a contemporary interview with McCartney was published in Life Life magazine in November Popular culture continues to make occasional reference to the legend and McCartney poked fun at it with a live album titled Paul Is Live whose cover parodied clues allegedly on the cover of the Beatles Abbey Road album

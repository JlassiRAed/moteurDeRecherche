A sickle is a curved tool used in agriculture for harvesting grain crop s It was the tool used before machine s were used for harvesting It has a curved blade with an attached handle The inside part of the blade is sharp so that the user can swing the blade against the crop s base catching it in the curve and slicing it In Greek mythology the Greek deity deities Cronus and Perseus used the sickle as a weapon A sickle and a hammer are the symbols of the Soviet Union

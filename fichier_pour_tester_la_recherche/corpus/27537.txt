 Jumping is a form of movement in which an organism propels itself through the air along a ballistic trajectory Jumping can be distinguished from running galloping and other gaits in which the entire body is temporarily airborne by the relatively long duration Some animal s such as the kangaroo employ jumping as their primary form of locomotion while others such as frog s use it only as a means to escape predator s Jumping is also a key feature of various activities and sports including the long jump high jump and show jumping

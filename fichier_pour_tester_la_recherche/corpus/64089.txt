 Agaves are a type of succulent plant from Mexico and the southwestern parts of the United States They have thick sharp leaves Agave plants flower only once produce seeds and then die Before flowering they usually live for to years Because they live for a long time they are sometimes called century plants Agave is used to make things like tequila agave nectar agave syrup and pulque They were an important food for many Indigenous peoples indigenous people in the southwestern United States such as the Navajo people Navajo and the Hokoham

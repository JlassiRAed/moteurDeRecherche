The polar vortex is a persistent large scale cyclone located near geographical pole s of a planet On Earth a polar vortex is usually in the middle and upper troposphere and stratosphere The cyclone surrounds the polar highs Polar storms lie in the wake of the polar front They strengthen in the winter and weaken near summer These vortexes span usually between to miles They circulate counter clockwise in the Northern Hemisphere Similar to tropical cyclone hurricanes a Coriolis effect causes the rotation of a polar vortex The Arctic vortex has two main centers one is over the Baffin Island the other is over northeastern Siberia

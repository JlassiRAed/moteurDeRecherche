The Mausoleum at Halicarnassus or Tomb of Mausolus was a tomb built between and BC at Halicarnassus It was bult for Mausolus a satrap in the Achaemenid Empire Persian Empire and Artemisia II of Caria who was both his wife and his sister The building was designed by the Ancient Greece Greek architect s Satyros and Pythius of Priene It was such an accomplishment that Antipater of Sidon Antipater of Sidon said it was one of the Seven Wonders of the World The word mausoleum has come to be used generically for any grand tomb

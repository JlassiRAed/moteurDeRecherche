 Archway tube station is a London Underground station in north London underneath the Archway Tower at the intersection of Holloway Road Highgate Hill and Junction Road in the area known as Archway London Archway It is on the High Barnet tube station High Barnet branch of the Northern line between Highgate tube station Highgate and Tufnell Park tube station Tufnell Park It is in both Travelcard Zone and Travelcard Zone Zone

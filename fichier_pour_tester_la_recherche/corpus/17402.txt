 Interwikis are links between wiki s These normally show links to articles in other languages but can also be used in the article text to link to other projects for example Wiktionary and Wikimedia Commons It is important to have interwikis to other languages They help people to find more information and other Wikipedias Most articles on the Simple English Wikipedia have articles in the English Wikipedia The process for adding interwikis is now organized through Wikidata This allows you easily link an article to the same article in all the other languages You can help add interwikis to other languages if they do not already exist

 Regulate G Funk Era is the debut studio album by United States American rapper Warren G It was released in June by Violator Records and distributed by the Def Jam Recordings Def Jam record label The album s biggest hit was the eponymous single Regulate Bad Boys The album received some positive reviews from critics with Robert Christgau commenting positively regarding the coolly menacing nature of the music Warren G also received two Grammy nominations This D J was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Rap Solo Performance while Regulate was nominated for a Grammy for Best Rap Performance By A Duo Or Group

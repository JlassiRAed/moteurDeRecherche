 Dr Thomas Yayi Boni is a Beninese politician and banker He served as the President of Benin from April to April He was re elected to a second term in March From January to January he was the Chairperson of the African Union On March Yayi Boni survived an assassination attempt Several of the people travelling with the president were wounded in gunfire between the presidential guard and the would be assassins On October the BBC said that his doctor niece and former commerce minister had been arrested in a plot to poison the president The niece changed his medicine to a toxic substance while he was on a state visit to Brussels

A boyar was a member of the ruling nobility in medieval Russia and some other Slavic countries such as Bulgaria The boyars held the most important jobs in the army They met in a group called the duma and gave advice to the ruling prince or in later times the tsar In the th and th centuries they were rich landowners who could advise the prince but they were free to go and work for another prince if they wanted to In Moscow from the th to the th centuries they were a closely bonded aristocracy There were about boyar families Their ancestor s were former princes or Moscow boyar families or foreign aristocrats In the th century they started to lose power In the early th century Tsar Peter the Great abolished the title of boyar

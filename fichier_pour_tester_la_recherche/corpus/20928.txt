 Elf is an United States American Christmas comedy movie directed by Jon Favreau It stars Will Ferrell James Caan Bob Newhart Zooey Deschanel Peter Dinklage Mary Steenburgen Jon Favreau Andy Richter Ray Harryhausen Leon Redbone and Ed Asner Will Ferrell plays a human named Buddy who is raised by elves in the North Pole He lives the way the elves do and helps in Santa s workshop He travels to New York City to find his father He is used to how elves live which is very different from how humans live with New York being a perfectly opposite way of living compared to the North Pole The movie was released to very positive reviews on November by Warner Bros The film inspired the Broadway musical Elf The Musical and NBC s stop motion animated television special Elf Buddy s Musical Christmas

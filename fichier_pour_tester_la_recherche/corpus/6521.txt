A waiter is a person who waits on tables often at a restaurant or cafe A female waiter is called a waitress She takes orders and delivers food to customers A good waiter can also help the customers by recommending the best food Many waiters and waitresses are required by their employers to wear a uniform Most uniforms used are black and white or all black Historically the term waiter was used to describe customs officers who waited on the high tide for vessels to come in carrying goods to tax

 Dinosaur Comics is a comic by Ryan North It shows dinosaurs who have adventures and talk about philosophy and some other topics The art is the same every day but the words are different This comic is intended to be funny Things in the comic that are not true Dinosaurs did not talk There were no cabins cars or women when dinosaurs lived The dinosaurs in this comic did not live at the same time or even in the same places Tyrannosaurus Rex was not friendly

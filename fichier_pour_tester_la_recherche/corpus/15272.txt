 Suicide Squad is an United States American superhero movie based on the DC Comics team Suicide Squad of the same name The movie is written and directed by David Ayer It stars a cast featuring Will Smith Jared Leto Margot Robbie Joel Kinnaman Jai Courtney Cara Delevingne Adewale Akinnuoye Agbaje Karen Fukuhara Jay Hernandez Adam Beach and Viola Davis with Ben Affleck as Batman and Ezra Miller as The Flash By February a Suicide Squad movie was being made at Warner Bros Warner Bros Pictures David Ayer signed on to write and direct in September and by October the casting process had begun Principal photography began on April in Toronto Ontario Canada and in Chicago Illinois and ended in August that year The film is scheduled to be released on August in D D and IMAX D

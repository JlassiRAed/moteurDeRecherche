 The Norwegian Refugee Council is an independent non governmental organisation in Norway which helps refugee s and internally displaced persons around the world The NRC protects the rights of people who have been forced to leave their countries or their home s within their countries It is the only Norwegian group that specialises in international efforts for these types of people The NRC was established in with the name Aid to Europe to help refugees in Europe after World War II Today the NRC is an independent private organisation It works closely with the United Nations and other organisations around the world as well as in Norway

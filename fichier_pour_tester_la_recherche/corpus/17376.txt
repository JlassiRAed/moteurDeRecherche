 Mount Shasta is a dormant volcano in northern California in the United States The volcano is part of the Cascade Range It has a smaller peak abutting it Shastina and seven glacier s The mountain stands alone in a large expanse of flatter land rising feet above the surrounding terrain The Sacramento River flows south near the west edge of the mountain The town of Mount Shasta and Shasta Lake Reservoir formed by Shasta Dam are all named for Mount Shasta This stratovolcano is expected to erupt again someday

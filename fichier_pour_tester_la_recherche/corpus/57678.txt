 Doric order was one of the three orders of architecture in Ancient Greece the other two orders were Corinthian order and Ionic order Orders of architecture and the part supported at the top the architrave the frieze and the cornice Each order has its own style Doric was the earliest style and used by the Greeks for most of their buildings and temples including the Parthenon The style features heavy columns a saucer shaped capital on the top and no base

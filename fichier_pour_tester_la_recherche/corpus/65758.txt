 Reaganomics is a portmanteau word of Reagan and economics created by Paul Harvey It refers to the economics economic policies created by United States President U S President Ronald Reagan during the s and still widely practiced These policies are commonly used with supply side economics referred to as trickle down economics by political enemies and free market economy free market economics by political advocates The four main ideas of Reaganomics were to reduce the growth of United States federal budget government spending reduce the federal income tax and capital gains tax reduce government regulation and tighten the money supply in order to reduce inflation

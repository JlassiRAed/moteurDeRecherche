 Richard Lovelace was an English people English writer poet soldier and Royalist who is considered by many to be the perfect Cavalier Educated at Charterhouse and Oxford University he took part in expeditions by the troops of King Charles I against various rebels from to In returning to his estates he was nominated to present a Royalist petition to a hostile Parliament for which he was ordered to prison by this elected body During his short term in prison he wrote one of his most famous poems To Althea from Prison He later spent many years fighting in France and Spain

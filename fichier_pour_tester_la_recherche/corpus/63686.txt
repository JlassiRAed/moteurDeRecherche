The Berry Islands are a chain of islands and a District of the Bahamas The Berry Islands has about thirty island s and over one hundred small islands or cay s It is often called The Fish Bowl of the Bahamas There are about people Most of the people are on Great Harbour Cay The islands were settled in by Governor Colebrook and a group of freed slavery slaves The main attraction is big game fishing Some of the fish that can be found there are billfish tuna grouper tiger fish Lutjanidae yellowtail snapper wahoo king mackerel and many more There are also great spots for snorkeling and scuba diving

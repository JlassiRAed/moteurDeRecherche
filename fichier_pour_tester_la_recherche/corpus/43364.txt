 Koyashskoye or Koyashs ke is a salt lake on the coast of the Kerch Peninsula in Crimea It is separated from the Black Sea by a strip of land It is deep The water color varies from pink to the scarlet depending on the light because there are microscopic alga e living in the water When the water evaporates it leaves a deposit of salt on the stones and the shores The salt becomes crystal producing crystal stones and the scent of Viola viola This lake is considered healing and is appreciated by some local people It is not widely known to tourists

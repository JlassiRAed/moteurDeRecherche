 Bornean Orangutan Pongo pygmaeus Sumatran Orangutan Pongo abelii The orangutan is a great ape that has fur with a color between red and brown There are two species of orangutan They are from Southeast Asia There are very few of them left because loss of the jungle has reduced their habitat There are orangutans on view at the Singapore Zoo The name orangutan comes from two Malay language Malay words orang which means person and hutan which means forest so orangutan means person of the forest

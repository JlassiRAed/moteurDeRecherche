The Viverravidae are an extinct family family of early mammalian carnivores They are in the superfamily Miacoidea and lived from the early Palaeocene to the Eocene They first appeared in the Paleocene of North America about million years ago Viverravidae is a monophyletic family In viverravids the number of molar molars is reduced to two and the skull is long They are probably not related to any living carnivorans

 Hobo is a word for a homelessness homeless person who travels to different cities and towns to look for work Hobos usually travel by train hopping Hobos stay in very cheap hotel s in cities or camping camp in simple shelter s in shanty town s near the rail yards There were many hobos during the Great Depression in the United States because many people lost their jobs and homes Many people blamed President of the United States President Herbert Hoover for the Depression so hobo camps were sometimes called Hooverville Hoovervilles

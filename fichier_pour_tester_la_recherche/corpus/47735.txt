 Tolkaapiyam is a book on grammar of the Tamil language Tol means a literary work The name of the author could not be identified and the work apparently relates to Grammar in Tamil It is learnt that the author was one of the disciples of Sage Agastya the Father of Tamil Grammar This sage was living in the south near Madurai during the visit of Lord Ram to the south in search of his wife abducted by the Lankan King Ravan The age of Lord Ram precedes that of Lord Krishna and hence the work can safely be said to precede BC Lord Shiva and his Son Murugan is said to have laid the first tenets for the Tamil Language probably long long time before sage Agastya could compile the same

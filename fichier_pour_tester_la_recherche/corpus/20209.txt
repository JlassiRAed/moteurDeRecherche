 Cotonou is the economy capital capital of Benin It is the largest city in that country The city officially has people but it is thought that the actual number of people living in the city may be as high as million Many of the government and diplomacy diplomatic service s of Benin are in Cotonou Because of this the city is Benin s de facto capital even though the official capital is Porto Novo The city is best known as a major harbor port because of the Autonomous Port of Cotonou It is also home to an airport and a railway between it and Parakou Other features of Cotonou include Cotonou Friendship Stadium Cotonou Cathedral Cotonou Central Mosque and the hectare Dantokpa Market The National University of Benin is in Cotonou

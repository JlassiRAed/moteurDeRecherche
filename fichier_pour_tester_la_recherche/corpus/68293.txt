 Bedworth is a small market town in Warwickshire England It is north of the county town of Warwick The town is home to many tourism sights including The Bedworth Water Tower and The Nicholas Chamberlaine Almshouses It was also known for mining which is not done anymore They also are known for the vast amount of Charity Shops which are located in most parts of the town The town is Pronunciation pronounced Beduf by the people that live there

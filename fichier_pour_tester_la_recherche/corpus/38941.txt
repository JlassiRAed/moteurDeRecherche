In Political party political parties the Central Committee is a group of party members that make all decisions for the party The Committee is usually led by a Chairman The Committee usually elects it s Chairman to represent it and also elects other important members of the party In one party dictatorship s the Central Committee is all powerful and leads the country In the Soviet Union for example Joseph Stalin used his power as General Secretary to have complete control over the Communist Party of the Soviet Union s Central Committee and was able to become more powerful than the Committee itself

 Nicomachus He was one of the most important mathematicians of the ancient world He was strongly influenced by Aristotle and is best known for his works Introduction to Arithmetic and The Manual of Harmonics in Greek language Greek He was a Pythagorean In Introduction to Arithmetic Nicomachus writes extensively on number especially on the significance of prime numbers and perfect numbers and argues that arithmetic is the starting point that makes it possible to do the other mathematical sciences geometry music and astronomy

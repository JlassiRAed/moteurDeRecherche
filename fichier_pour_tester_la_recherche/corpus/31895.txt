 Heliantheae is the most well known Tribe The name comes from the Ancient Greek Greek word for sun flower The Heliantheae is the third largest tribe in the sunflower family which has genera and about species Only the tribes Senecioneae and Astereae are larger Most Heliantheae come from North America and South America and Mexico A few genera come from tropical areas Most Heliantheae are herb s or shrub s but there are some that grow to the size of small trees Leaves are usually trichome hairy and arranged in opposite pairs The anther s are usually black

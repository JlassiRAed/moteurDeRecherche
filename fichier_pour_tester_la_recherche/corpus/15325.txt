 Khushal Khan Khattak was a famous Pashtun poet warrior and tribal chieftain of the Khattak tribe of Pashtuns He lived at Akora Khattak a village on the Indus River in India under the Mughal Empire He wrote many Pashto language poems during the th century Some of his poetry is of a Romantic type but most is nationalistic advising the various Pashtun tribes to unite as one nation against the Mughals

The Turkish new lira Cents or British Penny Pennies The symbol for the currency is YTL In the last few years the Turkish lira stabilised and even rose against the U S Dollars and the Euro In late December the Grand National Assembly of Turkey passed a law that allowed for the removal of six zeroes from the currency and the creation of the new lira It was introduced on January replacing the previous lira at a rate of new lira old lira The official name of the currency is New Turkish Lira According to the Central Bank the word new is only a temporary measure The new was be removed on January The banknotes have different shapes and sizes to prevent forgery

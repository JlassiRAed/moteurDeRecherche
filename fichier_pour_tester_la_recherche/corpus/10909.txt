The term whatever is United States American slang It means whatever you say or I do not care what you say It is used to dismiss something someone else has said In the late th and early st century the word became a sentence in its own right The character Archie Bunker from the show All in the Family used the phrase often to dismiss his wife Edith in the s It was strongly prevalent among teenage girls in the s and s Whatever was made popular by the film Clueless

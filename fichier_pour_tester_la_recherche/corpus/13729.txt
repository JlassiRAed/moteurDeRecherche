 C longipes Compsognathus was a small carnivorous theropod dinosaur from the end of the Jurassic period million years ago It was about a metre long and ran on two legs using its long tail to keep its balance Compsognathus is known from two nearly complete specimens one from Germany The German specimen was found in the Solnhofen limestone in Bavaria over years ago The larger French specimen was discovered in in the Portland limestone near Nice in southeastern France Although originally described as a separate species called Compsognathus corallestris It is one of the few dinosaurs whose diet is known with certainty the remains of small agile lizard s are preserved in the gut bellies of both specimens

The Tiwana are a Punjabi people Punjabi Rajput clan from the Punjab region of India and Pakistan The Tiwanas in Indian Punjab are mostly Sikh s while the Tiwanas of Pakistani Punjab are almost all Muslims The best known member of this clan was late Malik Sir Khizar Hayat Tiwana who for some time remained Premier of the Punjab in British India following Sir Sikandar Hayat Khan s demise

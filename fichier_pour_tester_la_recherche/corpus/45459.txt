 The Silky shark The Silky shark gets it s name from the fact that it has a smooth silky texture The Silky shark feeds on bony fish such as tuna mackerel sardine s and mullet s and mollusc s such as squid and paper nautilus They have also been known to feed on the bodies of dead whale s The Silky shark is classified as Near Threatened by the International Union for the Conservation of Nature IUCN

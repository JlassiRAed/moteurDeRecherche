 Actinobacteria Actinobacteria The Actinobacteria are a group of gram positive Bacterium bacteria Most are found in the soil They include some of the most common soil life playing important roles in decomposition and humus formation Some like Streptomyces were originally classified as fungi because they form branching filaments like hyphae All Actinobacteria are aerobic A few forms are pathogen s such as Mycobacterium Some are thermophilic

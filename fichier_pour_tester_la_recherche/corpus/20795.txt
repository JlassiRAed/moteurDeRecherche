The Wilhelm scream is a movie and television sound effect first used in for the movie Distant Drums The scream is often used when someone is shot falls from a great height or is thrown from an explosion The sound is named for Private Wilhelm a character in The Charge at Feather River a western western in which the character is shot with an arrow This was believed to be the third movie to use the sound effect It was the first use of the sound from the Warner Brothers sound library

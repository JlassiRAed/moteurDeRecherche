 Encore is the fifth studio album by United States American rap music rapper Eminem Encore was released on November and was produced by Dr Dre Eminem Luis Resto Mike Elizondo and Mark Batson The album was given out of stars by Allmusic It was nominated for Best Rap Album Best Rap Performance by a Duo or Group for the song Encore and Best Rap Solo Performance for Mockingbird at the th Grammy Awards

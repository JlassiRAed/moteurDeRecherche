 Superstar is a comedy movie and Saturday Night Live spin off about a quirky socially incompetent girl called Mary Katherine Gallagher The character was created by the SNL star Molly Shannon and appeared as a recurring character on SNL in many skits The story tells about Mary Katherine trying to find her place in her Catholic high school The movie was directed by a former Kids in the Hall member Bruce McCulloch The movie stars the following people Molly Shannon Will Ferrell Harland Williams Elaine Hendrix SNL and Kids in the Hall alum Mark McKinney who appeared in many of the Mary Katherine Gallagher SNL skits on television also has a minor role as a priest Molly Shannon got a nomination for Blockbuster Entertainment Award Favorite Actress Comedy but lost to Heather Graham in Austin Powers The Spy Who Shagged Me

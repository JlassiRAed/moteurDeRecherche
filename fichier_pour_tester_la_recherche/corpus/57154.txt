 Looe is a small coastal town and fishing port in southeast Cornwall United Kingdom As of the census people lived there Looe is divided into two parts by the River Looe East Looe and West Looe being connected by a bridge The town is about miles south of Liskeard The town is around a small harbour and along the steep sided valley of the River Looe which flows south to the English Channel beside a sandy beach Off shore to the west is the picturesque Looe Island Looe has a railway station at the end of the branch railway line from Liskeard

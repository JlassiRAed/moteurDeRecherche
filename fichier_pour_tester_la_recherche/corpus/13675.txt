 Kensington Palace is a royal residence set in Kensington Gardens in the Royal Borough of Kensington and Chelsea in London England It has been a residence of the British Royal Family since the th century Today it is the main residence of The Prince Richard Duke of Gloucester Duke and Birgitte Duchess of Gloucester Duchess of Gloucester the Prince Edward Duke of Kent Duke and Katharine Duchess of Kent Duchess of Kent and Prince Michael of Kent Prince and Princess Michael of Kent Kensington Palace is also used on an unofficial basis by Prince Henry of Wales Prince Henry as well as his cousin Zara Phillips

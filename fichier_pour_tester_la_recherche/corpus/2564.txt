 Barnard s Star also known as Barnard s Runaway Star is a very low mass red dwarf star approximately six light year s away from Earth in the constellation of Ophiuchus It is very old and moving relatively fast In the American astronomer Edward Emerson Barnard E E Barnard measured its proper motion as minute of arc arcseconds per year This is the largest known proper motion of any star relative to the Sun At a distance of about parsecs from the Solar System or just under six light years Barnard s Star is the nearest known star in the constellation Ophiuchus and the List of nearest stars fourth closest known individual star to the Sun after the three components of the Alpha Centauri system Despite its proximity Barnard s Star at a dim apparent magnitude of about nine is not visible with the unaided eye however it is much brighter in infrared light than it is in visible light

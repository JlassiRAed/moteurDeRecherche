The Southern Railway gave the designations SR Multiple Unit Numbering and Classification Cor Res Buf and Gri to the different types of electric multiple unit built to work the route between London Waterloo and Portsmouth Harbour The Cor type units survived long enough in British Rail ownership to be allocated TOPS Class The Cor designation had previously been used for the SR Class Pul Pul units and was reused by them during World War II when the Pullman car was stored but this stock was different from the Cor units

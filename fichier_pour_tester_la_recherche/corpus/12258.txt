 Enna is a province in the autonomy autonomous region of Sicily in Italy It is the only Sicilian province which is not next to the ocean It became a province in thanks to Benito Mussolini who was fascinated by the history of the province and in particular by the legend of Proserpina In the province there are lakes the most important is Pergusa s lake which is the only natural lake of the Sicily the other lakes are used for the agriculture

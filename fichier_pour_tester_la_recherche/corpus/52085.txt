 Album ratings We Invented the Remix is a remix album by Sean Combs P Diddy with Sean Combs the Bad Boy Family released on May It features remixes of hit singles by artists from Diddy s Bad Boy Records record label The album reached the top of the United States U S Billboard Billboard albums chart for a week and was later certified RIAA certification Platinum for shipments of over copies The album also reached in the United Kingdom UK on the UK Albums Chart The album featured the hit singles I Need a Girl which reached a rare occurrence of both parts of the same song both becoming big hits This was the last album Bad Boy would release under the Arista Records label

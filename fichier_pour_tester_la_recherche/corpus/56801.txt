 Ioannina is a Greek town in the northwest of Greece in Ipiros district Ioannina is the capital of Ioannina prefecture and Ipiros periphery people live there Ioannina is built on the bank of Pamvotida lake Around Ioannina there are many tall mountains Beside the town is Mitsikeli mountain In the east of Ioannina is the Pindos range

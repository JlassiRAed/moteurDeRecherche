 Giulio Cesare in Egitto is an opera in three acts with music by George Frideric Handel The Italian libretto was written by Nicola Francesco Haym It was based on an earlier libretto written by Giacomo Francesco Bussani The opera was written for the Royal Academy of Music The opera was first performance performed in London on February It was a success It was performed again in and It was also performed in France and Germany Giulio Cesare was not performed in the th century Many people had forgotten all about it It was not until that the opera was performed again Since then it has been performed around the world It is Handel s best opera It is known simply as Giulio Cesare

 Wii Sports Club is a sports video game for the Wii U It has high definition remakes of the sports seen in the Wii video game Wii Sports which can each be downloaded separately from the Wii U s eShop service Like in Wii Sports players use the Wii Remote to make movements like the ones seen in the real life sports including tennis bowling baseball golf and boxing Players can talk to other players with messages and drawings from the Wii U GamePad

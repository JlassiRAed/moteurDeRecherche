 Hemoglobin is a protein in red blood cells which contains iron It is used to transport oxygen around the human body It also occurs in some invertebrates Hemoglobin is involved in the transport of other gases It carries some of the body s respiratory carbon dioxide Red blood cells get their color from hemoglobin which is red There are millions of hemoglobin molecules in each red blood cell and millions of red blood cells in the human body When hemoglobin has oxygen attached it is called oxyhemoglobin

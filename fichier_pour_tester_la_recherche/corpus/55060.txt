 Lorraine Gary Car Wash Gary was born as Lorraine Gottfried in Forest Hills Queens New York on August to Betty and George Gottfried an entertainment business manager She began her acting career in the late s doing guest appearances on several popular TV shows These include Night Gallery The Virginian Dragnet McCloud Kojak and The F B I She began her first major acting role when she was a guest star on seven episodes of the TV series Ironside among them Tom Dayton Is Loose Among Us in which she played the substitute librarian Miss Kirk who pushes the unstable Tom Dayton too hard and In Search of an Artist as a woman with a drinking problem who may have been involved in a murder She has been married to entertainment industry executive Sid Sheinberg with whom she had two children since August She retired for eight years after but then came out of her voluntary retirement to reprise Ellen Brody in Jaws The Revenge

 Las Tunas is one of the provinces of Cuba Major towns include Puerto Padre and Amancio as well as the capital Victoria de Las Tunas The Las Tunas province was established in when it was split from the Oriente region The southern coast which opens onto the Golfo de Guacanayabo is marsh y and characterised by mangrove s This wet area is used to grow sugarcane in as the wet climate is well suited for the crop Cattle are also grazed in the province which was until the part of the Oriente province that was divided into five smaller provinces by the Cuban government Victoria de Las Tunas was just a small market town until that year and a large development program was started to modernise the town and connect it by road to Havana

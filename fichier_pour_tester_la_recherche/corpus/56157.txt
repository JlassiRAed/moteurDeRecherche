 The Windy Hill is a long book for children It was written by Cornelia Meigs It tells a story about a boy and girl who live in New England They meet a man who tells them stories The stories are about other people who used to live in New England The people in these stories are part of the boy s and girl s family Between these stories the main story of the book happens The main story is about a bad man who wants to take away the house where the boy and girl live The bad man is part of the boy s and girl s family too This book was first printed in Berta and Elmer Hader drew the pictures In this book was made a Newbery Medal Honor Book

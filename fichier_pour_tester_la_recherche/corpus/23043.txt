 Louise Hand is an Australia n diplomat She is a senior career officer with the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade On December Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Martin Ferguson made Hand the new Australian High Commissioner to Canada Before this appointment Hand was worked for the Department of Climate Change as the Ambassador for Climate Change In Canberra Hand has held the jobs of Assistant Secretary in the Arms Control Branch Hand holds a Bachelor of Arts and Masters Qualifying degree from the University of Queensland and an MBA from Deakin University She is married and has two daughters In January she was awarded a Public Service Medal for her work in Indonesia

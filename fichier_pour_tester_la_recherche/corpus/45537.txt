 Gaius Plinius Secundus better known as Pliny the Elder was a Rome Roman author naturalist and natural philosopher as well as naval and army commander of the early Roman Empire and personal friend of the emperor Vespasian Spending most of his spare time studying writing or investigating natural and geographic phenomena in the field he wrote an encyclopedic work Naturalis Historia which became a model for all such works written subsequently Pliny the Elder died on August AD He was attempting the rescue by ship of a friend and his family from the eruption of Mount Vesuvius The eruption had just destroyed the cities of Pompeii and Herculaneum

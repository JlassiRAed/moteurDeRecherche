The Ptolemaic dynasty also known as the Lagids or Lagidae was a Ancient Macedonians Macedonian Greek royal family who ruled over Egypt Their rule lasted for years from BC to BC They were the last dynasty of ancient Egypt Ptolemy I Soter Ptolemy was one of Alexander the Great s generals and deputies He was appointed satrap of Egypt after Alexander s death in BC In BC he declared himself King Ptolemy I later known as Soter The Egyptians soon accepted the Ptolemies as the successors to the pharaoh s of independent Egypt Unlike their previous rulers under the Achaemenid Empire they usually lived in Egypt Ptolemy s family ruled Egypt until the Rome Roman conquest of BC All the male rulers of the dynasty took the name Ptolemy Ptolemaic queens some of whom were the sisters of their husbands were usually called Cleopatra Arsinoe or Berenice The most famous was the last queen Cleopatra Cleopatra VII She was involved in the Roman political battles between Julius Caesar and Pompey and later between Augustus Octavian and Mark Antony Her death marked the end of Ptolemaic rule in Egypt

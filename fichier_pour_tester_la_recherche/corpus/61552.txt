A Ship of the line was a kind of warship Most ships of the line were built between the th and th century At that time battles were fought by many such ships which sailed in a line At first such ships were sailing boats but from about the s steam driven ships of the line were built The steam powered ships of the line were still made of wood From about the Ironclad warship replaced them At that time ships had cannon s Larger ships had room for more cannons and were therefore more likely to hit the enemy Today ship designs are noticeably different as cannons are no longer used

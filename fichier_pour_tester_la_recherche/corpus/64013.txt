 Pretty Vacant is a song by the English punk rock band The Sex Pistols It was released in as the third single from their album Never Mind the Bollocks Here s the Sex Pistols The song is infamous for vocalist John Lydon s phrasing of the word vacant emphasizing the last syllable making it sound like the vulgar word cunt The B side is a cover of The Stooges No Fun which they played on the spot without a proper rehearsal

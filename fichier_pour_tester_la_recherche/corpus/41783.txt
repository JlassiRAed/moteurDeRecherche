The Mandarin orange also known as the mandarin or mandarine oranges Mandarin oranges are usually eaten plain or in fruit salads The mandarin is tender and is damaged easily by cold It can be grown in tropical and subtropical areas The mandarin is easily peeled with the fingers and can be easily split into even segment s without squirting juice This makes it more convenient to eat as utensil s are not required to peel or cut the fruit Mandarin segments are also sold in cans During Chinese New Year Mandarin oranges and tangerine s are considered traditional symbols of good fortune During the two week celebration they are frequently displayed as decoration and presented as gifts to friends relatives and business partners

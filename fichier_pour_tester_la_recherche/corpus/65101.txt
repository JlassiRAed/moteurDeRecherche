 Wikivoyage is a free travel and tourism guide on the World Wide Web web It is written by volunteer authors using a wiki Anyone can edit articles The name Wikivoyage is a combination of the words wiki and voyage the French word for travel journey or trip Wikivoyage started publicly in December as a German language version that was supported by German and Swiss authors It was owned by a German non profit association named Wikivoyage e V The Italian version started in December In the content of Wikitravel in other languages was copied to Wikivoyage At the same time Wikivoyage became hosted by the Wikimedia Foundation a non profit organization that hosts other wikis such as Wikipedia

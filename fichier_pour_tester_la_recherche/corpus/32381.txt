The Mariinsky Theatre is a famous theatre of opera and ballet in Saint Petersburg Russia It was opened in and soon became the most important theatre for opera and ballet in Russia Many great operas and ballets of the th century were first performed there including the greatest stage works by Tchaikovsky Mussorgsky and Rimsky Korsakov The Mariinsky Theatre is home to the Mariinsky Ballet Mariinsky Opera and Mariinsky Orchestra The conductor Valery Gergiev has been the general director there since

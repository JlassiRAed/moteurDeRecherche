 Ikebana is the Japan Japanese art of making flower arrangement s The word Ikebana means to make alive flowers People who want to do Ikebana have to learn many things It is not just about putting flowers in a vase Japanese flower arrangements often have special meanings There are many ways in which Ikebana can be taught These schools include Ikenobo Sogetsu Ohara Ikenobo is very well known Until the Mid Edo Period it was only for upper class people Ikenobo was started by a Buddhist priest called Ikenobo Senkei in the th century It uses standing flowers Another form of Ikebana is called cha bana It is used in tea ceremony Today Ikebana is very popular among all classes of people in Japan million people in Japan practice Ikebana

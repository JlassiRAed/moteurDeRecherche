 	In Hindu mythology Adi Para Shakti is the Supreme Being She is the Goddess Devi and recognized as Para Brahman In many Shakta texts she is known as Parma meaning supreme and Satya meaning truth The Devi Bhagavata Purana Devi Bhagawata Mahapurana says that Adi Parashakti is the original creator observer and destroyer of the whole universe

 Gelderland is a province in the east of the Netherlands The capital city is Arnhem but Nijmegen and Apeldoorn are bigger Other important cities are Zutphen Doetinchem Harderwijk and Tiel It is surrounded by the provinces of Limburg Utrecht Flevoland and Overijssel and the Germany German federal state North Rhine Westphalia The most important rivers are the Rhine of the Flevopolder

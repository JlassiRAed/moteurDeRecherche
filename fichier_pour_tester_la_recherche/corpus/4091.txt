 Super Mario All Stars is a video game made by Nintendo for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System game console It was released in It puts together four old games into one package The four games are Super Mario Bros Super Mario Bros Super Mario Bros and Super Mario Bros The Lost Levels The Lost Levels was originally called Super Mario Bros in Japan and was the original sequel to Super Mario Bros The old games feature new graphics and improved sound It was later re released in with Super Mario World as Super Mario All Stars Super Mario World

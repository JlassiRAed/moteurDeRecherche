A tire is a ring of material that covers the rim of a wheel Most road vehicle s and many other vehicles use rubber tires Tires help vehicles to move smoothly Some rapid transit metro train s and tram s also use rubber tires on their steel wheels to provide better traction than the steel tires of other train s Tires need to be changed after their tread s wear away Driving with worn tires is very dangerous It can cause the tire to explode and the driver to lose control

 Tamara Platonovna Karsavina was a Russia n ballerina She began her career in the Imperial Russian Ballet and later danced with the Diaghilev s Ballets Russes Karsavina created the title role in The Firebird the Ballerina in Petrushka and The Young Girl in Le Spectre de la Rose After she settled in Hampstead London she began teaching ballet professionally She assisted in setting up The Royal Ballet and was a founding member of the Royal Academy of Dance Her pupils included Dame Alicia Markova and Dame Margot Fonteyn She wrote two books the autobiography Theatre Street

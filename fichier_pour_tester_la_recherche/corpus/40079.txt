In combat sport s like fencing boxing karate etc the cover is a defensive movement permitting to protect oneself against strikes by placing a weapon or a body part like the fist forearm elbow arm or shoulder before the aim aimed by this attack This form of defence so called passive is different from the block parry that are considered active The cover has nearly the same meaning as protection especially in boxing when a fighter raises his arm in order to protect his face or head against a possible attack One puts cover to use

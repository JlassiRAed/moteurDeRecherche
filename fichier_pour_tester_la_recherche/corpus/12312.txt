 ECW Hardcore Revolution is a professional wrestling video game Video game publisher published and released by Acclaim Entertainment The game is based on Extreme Championship Wrestling It was the first video game based on their promotion It was released for the Nintendo on January in North America and March in Europe A sequel for the game titled ECW Anarchy Rulz ECW Anarchy Rulz was released six months after The gameplay was identical to WWF Attitude Most of it was the same except that the WWE WWF owned materials were switched to Extreme Championship Wrestling material

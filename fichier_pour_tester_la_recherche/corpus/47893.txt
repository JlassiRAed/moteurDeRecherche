 Gluten is a composite of the protein s gliadin and glutenin These proteins are found in the endosperm s of some grass like cereal grains such as wheat rye and barley Gliadin and glutenin make up about of the protein contained in wheat seed As they are solution insoluble in water they can be got by washing away the starch in the grain Worldwide gluten is an important source of food protein both in foods prepared directly from grains with gluten in them and as an additive to foods that are low in protein

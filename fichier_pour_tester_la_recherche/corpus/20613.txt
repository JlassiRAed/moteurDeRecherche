 Snuff is the fifth single single from the album All Hope Is Gone by metal music metal band Slipknot It was released on September The song reached on Billboard Billboard s Hot Mainstream Rock Tracks It was the last single that was released from the band before the death of their bassist Paul Gray Vocalist Corey Taylor performs an acoustic version of the song as a tribute to Paul Gray during Stone Sour shows The song was nominated for Best Single at the Kerrang Awards but it lost to Liquid Confidence by You Me at Six

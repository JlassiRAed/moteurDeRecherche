The Auld Alliance The alliance dates from the treaty signed by John Balliol and Philip IV of France in against Edward I of England The terms of the treaty said that if either country was attacked by Kingdom of England England the other country would invade England This happened at the Battle of Flodden Field The alliance played an important role in conflicts between both countries and England such as the Wars of Scottish Independence the Hundred Years War the War of the League of Cambrai and the Rough Wooing

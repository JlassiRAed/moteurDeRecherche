 Reading railway station is a major rail junction and station in the England English town of Reading Berkshire Reading It is on the northern edge of the town centre close to the main retail and commercial areas and also the River Thames Next to the railway station is a bus interchange served by most of Reading s urban and rural bus services Reading was opened on March by the Great Western Railway It is a major junction point on the National Rail system and as a consequence the railway station is a major transfer point as well as serving heavy local traffic The station is sponsored by the bank ING Direct and the University of Reading There are many local buses regional buses and a coach to Heathrow Airport South West Trains First Great Western and CrossCountry serve the station

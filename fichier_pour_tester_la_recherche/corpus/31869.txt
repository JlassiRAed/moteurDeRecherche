There were two sets of Nuremberg Trials The first and most well known were the trials of the leaders of Nazi Germany This was organized by the International Military Tribunal The judges and prosecutors were from the four wartime Allies France the USSR the United Kingdom and the United States The second set of trials were the follow up trials The original plan was for the IMT to hold several trials and the Allies to hold their own trials of local officials as well Cooperation between the Soviet Union and the Western Allies quickly broke down The IMT only held the one big trial So the US held trials of the next most important Nazis themselves

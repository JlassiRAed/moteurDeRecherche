 Contract bridge or simply bridge is a trick taking card game which is played by two pairs of players one pair against the other pair Millions of people play bridge worldwide in clubs tournaments online and with friends at home It is one of the world s most popular card games The World Bridge Federation is the governing body for international competitive bridge The game consists of a number of deals page Most club and tournament play uses duplicate bridge where the cards are not re dealt during a session The same deals are played by most or all players to allow comparative scoring

 Surface mining is a method which operates on the surface of the land Machines remove the overburden to get at material such as coal below it Surface mining includes strip mining open pit mining and mountaintop removal mining It is mining in which soil and rock overlying the mineral deposit are removed It is the opposite of underground mining in which the overlying rock is left in place and the mineral removed through shafts or tunnels Surface mining gained popularity throughout the th century and became the main way of coal mining in places such as Appalachian Mountains Appalachia and America s Midwest In most forms of surface mining heavy equipment such as engineering vehicle earthmover s first remove the overburden Next huge machines such as dragline excavator s or Bucket wheel excavator s extract the mineral

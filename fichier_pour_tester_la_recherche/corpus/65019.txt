 The bivalves are a large class of mollusc s also known as pelecypods They have a hard calcareous Animal shell shell made of two parts or valves The soft parts are inside the shell The shell is usually bilateral symmetry bilaterally symmetrical There are over species of bivalves including the fossil species There are about living species in genera and Family families All of them live in the water most of them in the sea or in brackish water Some live in fresh water All are filter feeder s they lost their radula in the course of evolution A few are carnivorous eating much larger prey than the tiny micro algae eaten by other bivalves The best known examples of bivalves are clam s mussel s scallop s and oyster s

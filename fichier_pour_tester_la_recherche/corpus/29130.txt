 Polygar War or Palayakarar Wars refers to the wars fought between the Polygars of former Madurai Kingdom in Tamil Nadu India and the British India British colonial forces between March to May The British finally won after carrying out long and difficult protracted jungle campaigns against the Polygar armies and finally defeated them Many lives were lost on both sides and the victory over Polygars made large part of territories of Tamil Nadu coming under British control enabling them to get a strong hold in India

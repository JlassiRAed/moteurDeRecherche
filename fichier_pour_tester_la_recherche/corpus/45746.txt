The Lancaster Barnstormers are an Atlantic League of Professional Baseball team from Lancaster Pennsylvania They have won Atlantic League championship and are the first team in the league to win by a four game sweep Since the Barnstormers home stadium is Clipper Magazine Stadium The word barnstormer refers to Lancaster County Pennsylvania s agricultural tradition and its own baseball heritage Barnstorming baseball players visited Lancaster in the early s to play exhibition baseball games on the county s farmland The Lancaster Barnstormers rival is the York Revolution

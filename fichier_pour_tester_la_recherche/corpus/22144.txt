Sir Currimbhoy Ebrahim was a prominent businessman philanthropist and political leader from Bombay British India He succeeded his father Sir Mohamedbhoy Ebrahim as Baronet and as managing director of many family owned firms He also became director or chairman of many charitable trusts in the Bombay area and in also started the Muslim Peace and Relief Committee In the same year he became a member of the All India Muslim League and an active political leader of this party

 The Orpheum is a theatre and music venue It is located on Granville Street in Vancouver British Columbia The Orpheum is a part of the Vancouver Civic Theatres group of live performance venues along with the Queen Elizabeth Theatre and Vancouver Playhouse Vancouver Playhouse It was opened on November as a vaudeville house although they had their first show the day before When vaudeville was slowing down from its popularity the Orpheum found new owners in Cineplex Entertainment Famous Players and became mainly a movie house but hosted live events every now and then In Famous Players wanted to turn the place into a Multiplex multiplex because of economic reasons The Orpheum was then saved It is the home of the Vancouver Symphony Orchestra

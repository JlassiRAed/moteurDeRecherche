The fictional character s the X Men are a team of superhero es who were born with special Mutant mutant powers making them different from regular humans The X Men fictional character characters are owned by Marvel Comics which publishes comic book s featuring them as well as many other superheroes The comic book was one of the first to combine social issues and commentary into a superhero story set in a world where people with Mutation mutant abilities are hated by those born without them This is similar to acts of racism seen throughout history Unlike some mutants the X Men try to be good and show tolerance toward humans and other mutants The idea behind the superheroes were inspired by the African American equality supporters like Malcolm X and Martin Luther King

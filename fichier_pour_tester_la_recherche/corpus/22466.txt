 Sir Khawaja Nazimuddin KCIE of Dacca who had come from Kashmir and settled there He received his education from Dunstable Grammar School in England then Aligarh Muslim University and later Trinity Hall Cambridge University until the mid s After returning to British India he became involved in politics in his native Bengal Nazimuddin was initially the Education Minister but climbed the ranks to become the Chief Minister of the province prior to independence Nazimuddin also became the head of the Muslim League in Eastern India

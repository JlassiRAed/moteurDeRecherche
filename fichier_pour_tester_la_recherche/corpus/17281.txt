 Christina Lake is a lake in British Columbia in Canada It is near the Crowsnest Highway It is about north of the United States border The lake is named after a daughter of a Hudson s Bay Company officer The Trans Canada Trail Dewdney Trail and the Kettle Valley Railway Trail all merge near the lake The Gladstone Provincial Park covers the northern half of the lake The Christina Lake Provincial Park is at the southern part of the lake Both parks have public beach es along the lake multistub geo Canada

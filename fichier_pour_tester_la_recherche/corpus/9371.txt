 Ganesha or is the elephant headed Deity god in Hinduism He is the son of Shiva and Parvati Ganesha is a very popular god of Hindus Hindu tradition states that Ganesha is a god of wisdom success and good luck He is also giver of different types of favours The Hindu tradition calls Ganesha as the Vighneshvara Vighneshvara in Sanskrit language means one who is the lord of obstacles or difficulties Thus the Hindu tradition states that by worshiping Ganesha one can remove all obstacle and difficulties

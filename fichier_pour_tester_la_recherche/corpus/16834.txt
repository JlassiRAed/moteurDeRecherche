 Heated shot is the heating of round shot before firing from muzzle loading cannon s The purpose is for setting fire to enemy warship s buildings or equipment The use of hot shot dates back centuries It was stopped when vessels armored with iron replaced wooden warships in the world s navies It was a powerful weapon against wooden warships where fire was always a hazard It was mainly used on shore batteries and forts due to the need for a special furnace to heat the shot The use of heated shot from a ship was so dangerous that it was against Royal Navy regulations The American ship USS Constitution USS Constitution had a shot furnace installed for hot shot to be fired

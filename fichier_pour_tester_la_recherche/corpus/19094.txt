 Armin T Wegner was a soldier in World War I a writer and a co creator of Germany German Expressionism a political activist for Armenia n and Jewish human rights and a victim of Nazism Nazi persecution He was a war reporter for several Germany German newspapers at the same time While staying in Turkey he saw the Armenian Genocide genocide of Armenians with his own eyes After Hitler came to power Wegner was against the fascists and spoke against the treatment of Jews After writing a letter to Hitler he was arrested and tortured Wegner survived and escaped and was based in England and in Italy after the end of World War II

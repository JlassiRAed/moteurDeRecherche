 Aaron Rome He has also played in the Western Hockey League WHL for a combined seasons with the Saskatoon Blades Kootenay Ice Swift Current Broncos and the Moose Jaw Warriors He formerly played in the NHL for the Columbus Blue Jackets Anaheim Ducks and the Vancouver Canucks He was on the Anaheim Ducks when they won the Stanley Cup in but he did not qualify to have his name engraved on the cup He did however get a Stanley Cup ring and a day to spend with the cup in the off season On June Rome was given a four game suspension because of a late hit that he did on Boston s Nathan Horton during game three of the Stanley Cup finals The hit gave Horton a concussion and he was sent to the hospital

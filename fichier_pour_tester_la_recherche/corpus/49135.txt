 An avocado is a berry fruit It has medium dark green or dark green bumpy or smooth skin depending on the variety The flesh of an avocado is deep chartreuse green in color near the skin and pale chartreuse green near the core It has a creamy rich texture Avocado trees come from Central America and Mexico They can grow in many places as long as it is not too cold Avocados have much more fat than most other fruit but most is fat that is healthy to eat Avocados have lots of potassium B vitamin s and vitamin E and K The Mexican food called guacamole is made of avocados Many other foods are also made from avocado Avocado is poison poisonous to some animals Many animals will get very sick or die if they eat avocado

 Sai Baba of Shirdi was a Spiritualism spiritual master who lived in India His followers believed that he was a saint or satguru Some say he was an incarnation of Lord Krishna while others think of him as an incarnation of Lord Dattatreya He lived the longest part of his life in a village in India called Shirdi Although Sai Baba died almost years ago still today large numbers of people follow him or have great respect for him He is very well respected in India both by Hinduism Hindus and Islam Muslims There are many temple s built all over India to honor Sai Baba of Shirdi

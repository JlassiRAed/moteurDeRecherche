 Metropolitan Vickers Metrovick or Metrovicks was a United Kingdom British heavy electrical engineering company of the early to mid th century formerly known as British Westinghouse Highly diversified they were particularly well known for their industrial electrical equipment such as Electrical generator generators steam turbine s switchgear transformer s electronics and railway traction equipment Metrovick holds a place in history as the builders of the first commercial transistor computer the Metrovick and the first British axial flow jet engine the Metrovick F Their factory in Trafford Park Manchester was for most of the th century one of the biggest and most important heavy engineering facilities in Britain and the world

 Rajput s constitute one of the major Hindu Kshatriya groups from India They claim descent from ancient royal warrior Dynasty dynasties of Kshatriya s They are identified with the word Rajanya found in ancient Indian literature and trace their roots to Rajputana or Rajputstan In ancient times the son of a king was referred as to Rajput Raja putra The Rajputs consist of different clans These are divided into three different lines of descent They are Suryavanshi descendants of the sun god Surya Chandravanshi descendants of the moon god Chandra Agnivanshi descendants of the fire god Agni

 Hurdle race is a type of track and field athletics track and field race There are sprint hurdle races and long hurdle racing races The standard sprint hurdle race is meters for men and meters for women Men and women run meters in the standard long hurdle race Each of these races is run over ten hurdles and they are all Summer Olympic Games Olympic events Other distances are sometimes run particularly indoors The sprint hurdle race indoors is usually meters for both men and women although races meters or meters long are sometimes run

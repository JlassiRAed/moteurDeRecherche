 Schizanthus family Solanaceae The genus is made up of twelve species Schizanthus alpestris Schizanthus candidus Schizanthus grahamii Schizanthus hookerii Schizanthus integrifolius Schizanthus lacteus Schizanthus laetus Schizanthus litoralis Schizanthus parvulus Schizanthus pinnatus Schizanthus porrigens and Schizanthus tricolor

 Anarcestida Clymeniida Goniatitida Prolecanitida Ceratitida Phylloceratida Lytoceratida Ammonitida Ancyloceratina Ammonites were Ocean marine cephalopod mollusc s of the Class subclass Ammonoidea Their widely known fossil s show a ribbed spiral form shell in the end compartment of which lived the tentacled animal These creatures lived in the seas from at least to million years ago They became extinct at the K T extinction event Their nearest living relatives are the octopus squid cuttlefish and Nautilus Nine orders are recognised in the Ammonoidea five in the Palaeozoic and four in the Mesozoic

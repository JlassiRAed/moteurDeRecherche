 Bob Lamey is an Americans American sportscaster He is the radio voice of the Indianapolis Colts He has been with the franchise since they moved to Indianapolis from Baltimore except for the seasons During that time he was replaced by Joe McConnell He was also the voice of the Indiana Pacers of the National Basketball Association NBA He also called ice hockey games for the Indianapolis Checkers and Indianapolis Racers as well as the voice of the Carolina Cougars of the original American Basketball Association and served as a turn reporter for the Indianapolis Motor Speedway Radio Network

The Lieutenancies Act is an Act of Parliament in the United Kingdom A Lord Lieutenant is the Queen s representative in a county or other area The Lieutenancies Act sets out the areas in which Lords Lieutenant work in Great Britain It came into force on July These areas are usually called ceremonial counties or preserved counties in Wales In Scotland a Statutory Instrument defines the size of the lieutenancy They are usually one local council area but some council areas are too small or to large for one Lord Lieutenant For a list of the areas in England see Ceremonial counties of England Definition Ceremonial counties of England

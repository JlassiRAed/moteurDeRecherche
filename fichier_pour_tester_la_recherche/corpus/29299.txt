 Oriole Park at Camden Yards is a baseball stadium park in Baltimore Maryland It is the home field of the Major League Baseball team called the Baltimore Orioles It opened in replacing the Orioles long time home Memorial Stadium Memorial Stadium The ballpark was built on the site of a railroad area called Camden Yards The long brick building outside the right field area was part of that rail yard The birthplace of Babe Ruth is also nearby It is a museum which is a couple of blocks west of the ballpark

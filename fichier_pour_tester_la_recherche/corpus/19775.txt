The Gutenberg Bible is a printed version of the Latin Vulgate translation of the Bible that was printed by Johannes Gutenberg in Mainz Germany in the th century Although it is not the first book to be printed by Gutenberg s new movable type system it is his major work and of central importance for the start of the Gutenberg Revolution and the Age of the Printed Book It is a very rare and expensive book as only a few copies of it are still around The earliest known book with movable type was published years before in Korea and is known as the Jikji

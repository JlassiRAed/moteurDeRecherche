 Geobox River Culgoa River is a river in the South West Queensland district of Queensland and the Orana New South Wales Orana district of New South Wales Australia The river is the western branch of the Balonne River in southern Queensland near Dirranbandi It flows southwest across parts of the Darling Riverine Plains The river is joined by ten tributary tributaries It flows into the Darling River near Bourke New South Wales Culgoa is an Indigenous Australians Aboriginal word meaning running through or returning

 Straight pool also called continuous or simply is a cue sports pocket billiards game It was the common sport of championship competition until faster playing games like nine ball and to a lesser extent eight ball became more popular This is the classic game from the history of pool The game was especially popular in the United States and was the subject of the classic film The Hustler In straight pool the person shooting may attempt to pocket any ball on the table The aim of the game is to reach a set number of points The amount of points needed to win is agreed to before the game One point is scored for each ball pocketed legally A typical game might require a player to score points This means that at least balls must be pocketed to win In professional competition straight pool is usually played to points Straight pool is a call pocket game This means that the player must call what pocket they mean to sink the ball into on every shot It does not matter how balls reach the pocket As long as no foul is involved and the balls goes into the pocket that is called a point is scored Straight pool is well known in the United States Europe Japan and the Philippines but the game has become more obscure elsewhere Probably the all time greatest player Willie Mosconi holds the game s record high run Here is what he said

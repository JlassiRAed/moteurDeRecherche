The Everglades is a wide and flat tropical river and mangrove swamp in the southern part of Florida near the city of Miami The geography of the area is typical of a tropical river delta running slowly through a low lying Basin basin near sea level The Everglades are covered by plant s and many animal s live there It is the only place in the world that crocodile s and alligator s live together The Everglades is a National Park It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site

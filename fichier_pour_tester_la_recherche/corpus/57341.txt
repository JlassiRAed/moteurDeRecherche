 The Shirelles were a popular American girl group They were very active from to Some of their songs were I Met Him on a Sunday and Tonight s the Night This group had four African American female singers They had many hit record s The group broke apart in the very early s The group was formed by Shirley Owens who was the lead singer At one time they had six songs in the Top Ten Two of their songs Will You Love Me Tomorrow and Tonight s the Night were selected by Rolling Stone on its list of the greatest songs of all time Their song soldier Boy sold over five million copies

 Khat is a flowering plant native to tropical East Africa and the Arabian Peninsula It is also known as qat qaat quat gat jaad chat chad chaad and miraa The plant is grown commercially in Kenya Oman Yemen and Ethiopia Khat is a shrub When grown commercially the shrubs grow to a size of about m in the wild they can reach about m Khat is used as a stimulant in Yemen Ethiopia and Somalia the effects are comparable to those of drinking tea or coffee

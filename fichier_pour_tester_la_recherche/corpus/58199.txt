 Pentathletes is a painting on an ancient Greek terracotta drinking cup from Athens It is said to be the work of the Carpenter Painter It is dated about BC The cup measures x x in In the circular painting at the bottom of the bowl of the cup a youth pulls an older man to him for a kiss The outside of the cup depicts bearded men and youths exercising with discus and javelin A youth in a long robe plays music The pictures reflect the interests of the aristocrat s who would have used such a cup at a drinking party

 Georg Holtzendorff was a th century Germany German landscape painting painter from Saxony He moved to England because of the Franco Prussian War Holtzendorff worked for the Royal Crown Derby Royal Crown Derby Porcelain Company He sketched the landscape of Derbyshire and these were used as designs for porcelain His main work was the decoration of the Gladstone Dessert Service presented by the Working Men s Club and Institute Union Liberal Working Men of Derby to Prime Minister William Ewart Gladstone in Watercolour by Holtzendorff with a view of Becket Street Derby with the Derby Museum and Art Gallery in the background is the only remaining study on paper linked to the Gladstone service

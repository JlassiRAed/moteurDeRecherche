 Seoul Metropolitan Subway often called Seoul Subway is a rail transit system of subways and commuter trains that connects Seoul and suburban areas in its vicinity The system is consisted of numeric named lines Gyeongui Line Bundang Line Shinbundang Line A REX Jungang Line Gyeongchun Line Suin Line Incheon Line Everline and U Line Since all stations have smartphone like inch touchscreen kiosk so that passengers can look at the map make free phone calls or get tourist information All lines use special RFID cards such as T money or NFC technology to impose traveling fee

The New Red Sandstone is a sandstone rock in Britain and elsewhere It was laid down in desert conditions from the Permian It underlies the Jurassic Lias which are Ocean marine rocks such as the Blue Lias The term distinguishes it from the Old Red Sandstone which is largely Devonian in age The New Red Sandstone is much brighter and redder in colour than the Old Red Sandstone as the examples show The colour is caused by iron oxide s in the rock

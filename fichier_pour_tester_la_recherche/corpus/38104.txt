 Non penetrative sex is a form of sexual activity which does not involve penetration of the vagina anus or mouth unlike the more common penetrative sex Medical professionals accept the term of non penetrative sex It is a way to practice safer sex and of birth control as bodily fluids are not exchanged between partners The form of sex is practised between partners who do not feel ready are not physically able or cannot have socially appropriate penetrative sex but still wish to have a sexual act

 Vyankatesh Madgulkar was one of the most popular and well known Marathi writers of his time He became well known mainly for his realistic writings about village life in a part of southern Maharashtra called Maandesh These writings about Maandesh are set in a period of to years before and after India s Independence Vyankatesh Madgulkar was a man of many interests He also read a lot of books on different subjects He was lovingly called Tatya by his admirers friends and family members

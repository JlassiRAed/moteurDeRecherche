 Law Order Trial by Jury was an United States American television show It was a spin off of the police drama Law Order It is the third spin off of Law Order after Law Order Special Victims Unit and Law Order Criminal Intent It aired on NBC from March to January The show focused mostly on the criminal trial of the accused It showed the prosecution s and defense defense s preparation for the trial as well as the trial itself Things such as jury selection and deliberations in the jury room that were rarely shown on Law Order were the focus of this show

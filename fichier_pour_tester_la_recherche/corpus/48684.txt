 Kratos the Ghost of Sparta is a fictional video game character from Sony Santa Monica s God of War series which is loosely based on Greek mythology Greek mythology Kratos first appeared in the video game God of War which led to the development of six additional games featuring the character as the protagonist Kratos also appears as the protagonist of the God of War comic series and novels He has been consistently voiced by Terrence C Carson and Antony Del Rio voiced the character as a child in God of War Ghost of Sparta

 Sir Lamorak is a fictional character in the King Arthur stories He is one of the Knights of the Round Table His father is King Pellinore His brothers are Percival Sir Tor Tor and Aglovale His cousin is Sir Pinel le Savage Lamorak was known for his strength and fiery temper and fought off thirty knights on at least two occasions Thomas Malory refers to him as Arthur s third best knight only lower than Lancelot and Tristan

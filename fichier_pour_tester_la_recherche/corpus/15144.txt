 Makassar Strait is a strait between the islands of Borneo and Sulawesi in Indonesia To the north it joins the Celebes Sea To the south it meets the Java Sea The Mahakam River of Borneo empties into the strait Ports along the strait include Balikpapan in Borneo and Makassar and Palu in Sulawesi The city of Samarinda is km from the strait along the Mahakam The strait is a common shipping route for ships too big to fit through the Straits of Malacca

 Antipsychotic drugs or Neuroleptics are drug s that act on the central nervous system and that usually have sedative sedating properties The name neuroleptic was introduced in from the s the term antipsychotic is used more frequently Neuroleptics make the patient calm Today they are mostly used for the treatment of psychosis as it occurs in schizophrenia and bipolar disorder They can also be used to treat Depression depression and mood swings Very strong antipsychotics can be combined with an opioid to produce a special analgesic the patient will be conscious but heavily sedative sedated and feel very little pain

The Airedale Terrier is a breed of the terrier type that first came from Airedale a geographic area in Yorkshire England It Tradition traditionally was called the King of Terriers because before the creation of the Black Russian Terrier the Airedale was largest of the terrier breeds The breed has also been called the Waterside Terrier because it was bred to hunt otters in and around the valleys of the River Aire in Airedale In England this breed has also been used as a police dog

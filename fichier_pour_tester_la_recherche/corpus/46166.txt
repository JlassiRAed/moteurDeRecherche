The D C United Academy is a program for young soccer players that is run by D C United a pro soccer team that plays in Major League Soccer The Academy trains these young soccer players to possibly one day play soccer either in a pro league or in college D C United s Academy has several different levels including a U team a U team a U team and then Pre Academy teams Pre Academy teams are for players who are not yet part of the Academy on a normal schedule The teams compete in the United States Soccer Federations Development Academy league

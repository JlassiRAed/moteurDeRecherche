A Dame school was a private elementary school in English speaking countries The word Dame means a woman who has the title of a Knight The children were usually taught by women and did not learn very much The schools were most common from the th century to the th century Most of these schools were located in Britain the United States and Australia The Dame schools were also a common place for girls to be educated because they could not go to other schools which were only for boys The girls at Dame schools often learned how to sew or do other crafts

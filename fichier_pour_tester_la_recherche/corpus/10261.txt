The Tribute in Light is a memorial with two beams of light shooting straight up into the sky done to remember the September attacks It is lit up at dusk every September lasting until dawn the next day It was done again on the second anniversary of the attacks on September Since then it has been done on every September On clear nights the lights can be seen from over miles away It can be seen in all of New York City as well as most of Northern New Jersey and Long Island Fairfield County Connecticut Fairfield County Connecticut Westchester County New York Orange County New York and Rockland County New York

 Marc Potvin He played with the Detroit Red Wings Los Angeles Kings Hartford Whalers and Boston Bruins He was the second cousin of Hockey Hall of Fame Hall of Famer Denis Potvin and cousin of Jean Potvin Before he played in the NHL he played years of college hockey at Bowling Green State University He was drafted with the th overall pick by the Detroit Red Wings in the NHL Entry Draft Potvin also played in the American Hockey League AHL with the Adirondack Red Wings Providence Bruins and Portland Pirates as well as one season in the International Hockey League IHL with the Chicago Wolves When he retired he became the assistant coach of the Adirondack Red Wings After that became the head coach of the Mississippi Sea Wolves in the ECHL for a year He went on to coach the Springfield Falcons of the AHL for seasons in the United Hockey League UHL with the Adirondack Frostbite Adirondack IceHawks for seasons and season when it was renamed the Adirondack Frostbite On January Potvin was found dead in his Kalamazoo Michigan hotel room He was found hours before the Frostbite were set to play the Kalamazoo Wings On February police ruled that his death was a suicide Police said that he hung himself with a belt from the shower rod in his hotel bathroom

 The Australian Defence Force defence of Australia It is made up of the Royal Australian Navy the Australian Army the Royal Australian Air Force and a number of joint groups At first the Government of Australia Australian Government set up three separate armed services In the government decided to have a single military headquarters Over time the three services have combined headquarters logistics and training camps The ADF is small but uses a lot of technology With full time active duty service people and part time reserve forces it is the largest military force in Oceania It is much smaller than most Asian military forces The ADF does not have enough people in some highly skilled areas but it is still able to send forces in many regions outside Australia

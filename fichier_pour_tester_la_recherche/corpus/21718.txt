 Captain Planet and the Planeteers is an United States American animated environment alist television program based on an idea by Ted Turner and produced by Andy Heyward Robby London Barbara Pyle and Nicholas Boxer The series was developed and co produced by Turner Program Services along with the partnership of DiC Entertainment and ran new episode s from September until December A second series The New Adventures of Captain Planet ran for three seasons and was produced by Turner Broadcasting and then corporate sibling Hanna Barbera Productions Both programs continue today in syndication

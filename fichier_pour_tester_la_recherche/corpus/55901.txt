The Kosovo declaration of independence was an act of the Provisional Institutions of Self Government Assembly of Kosovo It was adopted on February by all of the members that were present which was the minimum number needed for it to pass It declared Kosovo to be independent from Serbia It was the second declaration of independence by Kosovo s Albanian people Albanian majority political institutions The first was proclaimed on September The government of Serbia said that the declaration was illegal It took the case to the International Court of Justice The ICJ said that the declaration did not violate international law

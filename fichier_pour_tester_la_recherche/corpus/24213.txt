 Canada a country and sovereign state in the northern part of North America is made up of thirteen administrative division s ten province s and three territory territories The different levels of government in Canada are based on the principles of a federation those of each of the provinces and territories share power with the Government of Canada federal government The territories governments have a more limited set of powers versus the federal government than that of the provinces The provinces are in the south of Canada near the border with the United States They go from the Atlantic Ocean in the east to the Pacific Ocean in the west The territories are to the north where fewer people live close to the Arctic Circle and Arctic Ocean Here is a list of the provinces and territories and their standard abbreviation s with their capital city capital s and largest city cities Canada s national capital where the federal government meets is Ottawa

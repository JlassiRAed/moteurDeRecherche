 Staple food are food items that can be stored easily and eaten throughout the year Different kinds of staple foods are used in different parts of the world The term means the common basis of the everyday diet in a place Potato es and rice are the most usual examples but bread is not a staple food because it is a manufactured food which cannot be stored Of course it can always be made since wheat is a staple food Fruit is not a staple because it usually grows at a certain time of year Meat is not a staple because it may not be available or is expensive to buy Green vegetables may be a staple in some countries but not others Foods which need special facilities such as refrigerator refrigeration are not staple foods Here are some examples of staple foods

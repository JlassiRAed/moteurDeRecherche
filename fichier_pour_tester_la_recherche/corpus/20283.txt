This Wikipedia Category category is for people about whom there is a mystery as to their identity immediate origins or life It should not include people just because few facts are known about their lives There are some people who live or lived very secret lives or about whom there is not much documentation but that is not a reason to include them in this category This category should include unidentified criminals only if a better category in which they fit in does not exist Finally the category should not include pseudonyms nonexistent people characters from folklore unidentified murder victims or people probably created through a work of fiction

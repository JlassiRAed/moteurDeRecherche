 Karaite Judaism is a kind of Judaism that accepts only the Hebrew Bible as authoritative and rejects any religious authority in Mishnah and Talmud which are explanations of the Bible by rabbi s in the first few centuries of the Common Era Karaite Jews believe that each person should be able to explain the Torah for themselves instead of following the explanations of the rabbis Today the largest communities of Karaites are in Israel and San Francisco

 Orange Is the New Black is an American comedy drama series on Netflix Jenji Kohan created it She is known for creating Weeds Weeds It is based on the memoir of the same name by Piper Kerman In April the series was renewed for a fourth season to be released on June In February the series was renewed through seven seasons Orange Is the New Black is the first series to get Emmy Award nominations in both comedy and drama categories The series has also received six Golden Globe Award nominations six Writers Guild of America Award nominations two Producers Guild of America Award s an American Film Institute award and a Peabody Award

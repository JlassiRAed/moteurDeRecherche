 SA is the name of a weapons family used by the British Army and Royal Marines All are of bullpup design this means the magazine is behind the trigger so the weapon is shorter yet still has a long barrel The series includes an assault rifle known as the L and a Light Support Weapon designated the L as well as a carbine variant for helicopter crews and a bolt action army cadet variant The LSW variant is far more accurate than its predecessor and is used in army FSGs Nevertheless the SA family has been plagued by poor reliability They have had an upgrade by the Germany German arms company H K when it was British owned This resulted in the A series but not all models have been upgraded The SA also has a cadet force variant that is the same size and is used throughout the ACF It has a success rate far higher than the old bolt action A version

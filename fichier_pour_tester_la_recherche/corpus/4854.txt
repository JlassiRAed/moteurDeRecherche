 Nintendo Direct is an online presentation produced by Nintendo It is used to give information about upcoming Nintendo content and products such as video game s and video game console consoles It began in Japan on October It started to air in other countries later on There are five different types of Nintendo Direct presentations Nintendo Direct A minute presentation about all different types of Nintendo products They take place every two months Wii U Direct A minute presentation about the Wii U Nintendo DS Direct A minute presentation about the Nintendo DS Nintendo Direct Mini A minute presentation about all different types of Nintendo products Special Nintendo Direct editions A minute presentation about a specific Nintendo or third party franchising franchise

 The Mighty Death Pop is the twelfth studio album by United States American Horrorcore band Insane Clown Posse It was released on August The Mike E Clark produced the album The album is the second in a set of six Joker s Cards since the conclusion of the original Joker s Cards The album features guest singers such as Hopsin and Tech Nne on the song Skreem They released their first single Chris Benoit from the album and it s about the professional wrestler Chris Benoit who died in a murder suicide after he killed his wife and son

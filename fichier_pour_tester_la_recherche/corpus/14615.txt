 Hula is a type of dance dancing from Hawaii Hula is a word in the Hawaiian language Hawaiian language Male dancer s and female dancers can both dance hula A long time ago hula dance was used only in special ceremonies Now hula dance is mainly for entertainment A hula dancer usually wears a grass skirt or a leaf skirt a flower necklace and sometimes a flower in the hair Traditionally the hula dancer dances to a chant to create the rhythm There are many kinds of hula dance For example the Hawaiian hula dance style is slow and the Tahitian hula dance style is fast

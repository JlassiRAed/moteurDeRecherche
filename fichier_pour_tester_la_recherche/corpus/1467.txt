 Nicholas Yonge Music publishing was quite a new thing in those days Musica transalpina was a collection of Italy Italian madrigal s The Italian words of the madrigals were also translated into English Musica transalpina had separate pieces by Italian composers including Alfonso Ferrabosco Alfonso Ferrabosco and Luca Marenzio Ferrabosco was living in England at the time The publication of this book had a huge influence on music in England It made madrigal s very popular in England and during the next twenty years composers such as Thomas Morley John Wilbye and Thomas Weelkes composed a large number of English madrigals In Yonge published a second book of Musica transalpina which was also very influential The title Musica transalpina means music from across the Alps because the music came from Italy which was the other side of the Alps

 New Territories West or NT West means the western part of New Territories in Hong Kong It includes Tsuen Wan District Kwai Tsing District Tuen Mun District Yuen Long District and Islands District It is one of the Geographical constituency geographical constituencies in the Legislative Council of Hong Kong the largest geographical constituencies in Hong Kong Although New Territories West is not the official administration name in Hong Kong many government departments use it as one of main regions for administration divisions

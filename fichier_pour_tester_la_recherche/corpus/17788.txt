A quorum is the wikt minimum minimum number of wikt member member s of an organization who must be present in order for their meeting to be legal or official In the case of legislature s and government bodies the requirements for a quorum are often set by statute or in their constitution Some bodies use a fixed number for a quorum while others use a percent age of the members The noun quorum is the plural of the Latin word qui meaning of whom A similar term quorum busting is a wikt tactic tactic used by members of a group when they know they will lose a vote

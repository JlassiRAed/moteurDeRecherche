 Reptile is the common name for one of the main groups of land vertebrate s It is not used so much by biologists who use more accurate terms The name reptile comes from Latin language Latin and means one who creeps All living reptile species are cold blooded have scaly skin and lay cleidoic egg s Many important groups of reptiles are now extinct The great marine reptiles of the Mesozoic era the ichthyosaur s plesiosaur s and mosasaur s are extinct We used to say the dinosaur s were extinct but they survive in the form of their feathered descendants Ancient reptiles that do survive include the turtle s the crocodile s and the Tuatara the lone survivor of its group The great majority of present day reptiles are snake s and lizard s The study of living reptiles is called herpetology

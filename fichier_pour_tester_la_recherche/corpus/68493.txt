 Pane sciocco is a variety of bread very common in Tuscany and in Umbria two regions in Italy Differently from the others kinds of Italian bread pane sciocco doesn t have salt Probably the reason why there is no salt in the pane sciocco comes from the s when salt was a rare product with a high cost Pane sciocco is often eaten with typically Tuscan ham or sausage s famous for being salty

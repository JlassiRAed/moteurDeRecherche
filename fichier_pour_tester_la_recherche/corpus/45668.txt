 Simpsons character Troy McClure is a character in The Simpsons voice acting voiced by Phil Hartman He first appeared in the episode Homer vs Lisa and the th Commandment McClure is a washed up actor usually shown doing low level work such as hosting infomercials and educational movies He appears as the central character in A Fish Called Selma in which he marries Selma Bouvier to protect his career from failing and quash rumors about his personal life McClure also hosts two episodes named The Simpsons th Episode Spectacular and The Simpsons Spin Off Showcase McClure was based on B movie actors Troy Donahue and Doug McClure as well as Hartman himself Following Hartman s murder on May the character no longer appeared on the show making his final appearance in the tenth season in Bart the Mother McClure is often cited as one of the series most popular characters in IGN ranked him first on their list of the Top Simpsons Peripheral Characters

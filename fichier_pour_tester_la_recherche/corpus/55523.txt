The Electric guitar is a type of musical instrument It was made in by George Beauchamp It is a string instrument usually played with a plectrum pick and sometimes with the finger s It uses things called electric pickups which are wire wound magnets that change the vibration of the strings into electric current This electric current is then sent to an amplifier that changes it to sound This electric current can also be changed to produce effects The main parts of an electric guitar are the neck the pickups the tuners the bridge the input the body the frets the pick guard the volume knobs and the tone knobs There may also be other parts that can change other sounds A normal electric guitar has one neck with strings on it although there are string guitars and guitars with more than one neck Guitars are made and sold in many parts of the earth world by many company companies They can be hand made or factory made Some companies that make electric guitars are Fender Gibson and Ibanez

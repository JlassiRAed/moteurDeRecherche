 Mycobacterium tuberculosis is bacterium that causes tuberculosis M tuberculosis was first described on March by Robert Koch The bacterium was first called the tubercle bacillus and is now also known as Robert Koch Koch s bacillus This disease is spread by the air and from getting coughed or sneezed on by an infected patient Tuberculosis used to kill over half the people who were infected The bacterium was almost wiped out by antibiotics but is now a threat again because strains have appeared which are antibiotic resistance resistant to all the usual antibiotics

The Melbourne Observatory in Melbourne Victoria Australia was built in It also kept the weather records and scientists were able to make weather predictions In the observatory installed the Great Melbourne Telescope which at that time was the largest steerable telescope in world It was destroyed in a bushfire in but there are plans to rebuild it and set it up as a display at the Melbourne Observatory The Melbourne Observatory is no longer used but the historic equipment is kept in working condition by the Astronomical Society of Victoria This is an amateur group of volunteers interested in astronomy They have regular tours talks and observation nights using the telescopes The historic buildings are now used as offices for the gardens a cafe and a shop

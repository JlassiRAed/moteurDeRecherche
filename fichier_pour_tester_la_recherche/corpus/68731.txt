A conflict is a struggle between people The struggle may be physical or between conflicting idea s The word comes from Latin language Latin conflingere Conflingere means to come together for a battle Conflicts can either be within one person or they can involve several people or groups Conflicts arise because there are need s value s or idea s that are seen to be different and there is no means to reconcile the dispute Very often conflicts lead to fight s or even war s Conflict between ideas is usually fought with propaganda

The goal of a legal guardian is to protect a person who cannot make legal decisions on their own The legal guardian makes decisions for the person who needs protection Usually a legal guardian has to be appointed A court may choose a legal guardian for a Minor in many different situations A court may also choose a legal guardian for an adult This happens when the court decides the adult is incompetent The court appoints a legal guardian to help protect the incompetent person The legal guardian then has the Rights right to make legal decisions for the ward

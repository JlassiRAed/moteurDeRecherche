 Antonio Pappano is a United Kingdom British conducting conductor and pianist of Italian origin He is the conductor of the orchestra of the Royal Opera House Royal Opera House Covent Garden in London and l Orchestra dell Accademia Nazionale di Santa Cecilia in Rome Pappano was born in England but his family had come from Italy When he was years old he moved with his family to Connecticut in the United States There he had his musical training learning to play the piano as well as Composition accompanist at the New York City Opera Pappano is the youngest conductor to lead the Orchestra of the Royal Opera House in London accompanying both the Royal Opera and Royal Ballet He is also an excellent piano accompaniment accompanist

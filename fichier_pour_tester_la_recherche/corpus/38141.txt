 A coonskin cap is a hat made from the fur of a raccoon Usually the tail of the racoon is attached to the back of the hat The coonskin cap is a symbol of the early United States frontier Coonskin caps were first worn by the Native American s who lived around Tennessee and Kentucky The United States American settler s who moved to this area liked the caps and wore them themselves Some famous American pioneer s who wore coonskin caps are Davy Crockett and Daniel Boone

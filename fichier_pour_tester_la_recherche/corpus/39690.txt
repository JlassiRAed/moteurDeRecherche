 A Storm of Swords is the third of seven planned books in A Song of Ice and Fire a fantasy series written by George R R Martin It was first released on August in the United Kingdom and in November in the United States A Storm of Swords won the Locus Award and Geffen Award for Best Novel and was nominated for the Nebula Award for Best Novel It was also put up for the Hugo Award but it lost to JK Rowling s novel Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

 Fennoscandia or Fenno Scandinavia is the region including the Scandinavian Peninsula Finland Karelia and the Kola Peninsula Fennoscandia is different to both Scandinavia and the Nordic countries Denmark is not in the Scandinavian Peninsula and Karelia and the Kola Peninsula are not a part of the Nordic countries They are a part of Russia The geography of Fennoscandia is very mountain mountainous and rough It is also very beautiful with sights such as fjords which take up a lot of space in Norway Socially Fennoscandia shows the historically close relationship between the Finns Finnish Norwegians Norwegian Russians Russian Sami people Sami and Swedes Swedish people

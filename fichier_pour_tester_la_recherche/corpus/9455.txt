 Xanthan gum is a polysaccharide It is added to certain foods such as salad dressing s to make them thicker It is also added to cosmetics to keep the ingredients from separating To make xanthan gum workers deliberately add a kind of bacterium Xanthomonas campestris to glucose or sucrose They let the mixture ferment for a while and then add isopropyl alcohol to separate the polysaccharide from the mixture They dry the polysaccharide grind it into a powder then add it to a liquid The xanthan gum is then ready to use

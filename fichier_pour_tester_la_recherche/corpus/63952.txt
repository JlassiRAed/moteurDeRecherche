 James Owen Sullivan known by his stage name The Reverend Tholomew Plague usually shortened to The Rev was an American musician best known as a co founder and drummer of the American hard rock band Avenged Sevenfold Sullivan was also the lead vocalist and pianist in the side project Pinkly Smooth along with Synyster Gates He was born in Huntington Beach California Huntington Beach California and started playing the drums when he was years old Before he joined Avenged Sevenfold he was a member of the Ska third wave ska band Suburban Legends The last album that Sullivan was featured on was Nightmare Nightmare which was released on July On the morning of December Sullivan was found dead in his Huntington Beach home Toxicology results later revealed that his death was from an overdose of Oxycodone oxymorphone diazepam nordiazepam and alcohol

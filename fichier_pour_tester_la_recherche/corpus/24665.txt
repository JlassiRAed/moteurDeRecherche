 Memphis was the ancient capital of the first Nome nome of Lower Egypt and of the Old Kingdom of Egypt from its foundation until around BC and later for shorter periods during the New Kingdom Its ancient Egyptian name was Ineb Hedj Memphis was also known in Ancient Egypt as Ankh Tawy because of the strategic position of the city between Upper Egypt Upper and Lower Egypt The ruins of Memphis are km south of Cairo on the west bank of the Nile In the Bible Memphis is called Moph or Noph

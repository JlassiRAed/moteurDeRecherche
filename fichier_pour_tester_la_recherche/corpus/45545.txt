 Lindy Hop is an United States American dance It started in Harlem New York City in the s and s It was originally associated with jazz music jazz and tap dance It was popular from the s to the s The Lindy Hop was a mixture of many dances before it like the Charleston tap and breakaway dances Frankie Manning helped develop the dance Lindy Hop today is danced as a social competitive or performance dance It is also sometimes called a street dance

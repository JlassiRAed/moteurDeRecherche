 Pterodactylus was a small pterosaur or flying reptile It lived in the later Jurassic period at the same time as many dinosaur s Pterodactylus is typical of the short tailed pterosaurs a sub order which continued to the end of the Cretaceous The first specimens were found at Solnhofen limestone Solnhofen in Bavaria Germany These carbonate strata formed in warm lagoon s also yielded the famous Archaeopteryx Pterodactylus may have lived on small islands in the lagoon or on the coast

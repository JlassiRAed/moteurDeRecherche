 Rincah is a small island near Komodo Komodo island East Nusa Tenggara Indonesia The island is famous for giant lizards called Komodo dragon Komodo dragons Rincah also has many other animals such as Boar wild pigs Bubalus buffalos and birds The island may also be called Rinca and Rindja Rincah is less popular than Komodo Island so it is an excellent place to see the Komodo Dragon in nature with fewer people around Day trips can be arranged from Labuanbajo on Flores by small boat at the park headquarters

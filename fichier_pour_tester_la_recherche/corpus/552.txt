 United Empire Loyalists were people living in the colonies in what was British North America in the s who did not agree with the American Revolution Many residents of the colonies Tories In many of the colonies those who openly expressed being against the Revolution and those who joined militia s loyal to King George were declared to be outlaw s and their property properties taken away by the people favouring the Revolution Some of these people wanted to remain in a place that was still part of the British Empire so they moved to what is now Canada In many places there they received a warm welcome and became a valued part of their new home area Many of them have maintained social groups which are limited to people whose ancestor s were some of these people and they are still called United Empire Loyalists

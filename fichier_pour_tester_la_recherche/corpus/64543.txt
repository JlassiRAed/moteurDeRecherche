 Would I Lie to You is a United Kingdom British comedy panel game shown on BBC One It began on June The first two series were presented by Angus Deayton Since then it has been presented by Rob Brydon There are two teams of three people a team captain and two celebrity guest s The captains are David Mitchell and Lee Mack During each episode the contestants take turns to make statements about themselves The other team try to work out whether the statement is true or false by questioning the person who has made the statement

 Agathis is a genus of evergreen conifer ous trees the kauri They grow mainly in the southern hemisphere including southeast Asia areas of the western Pacific Ocean Pacific and Australasia Agathis is a relatively small genus with only known species As a conifer it bears its seed s in conifer cones Mature Agathis can become quite large reaching feet in diameter The genus is part of the ancient Araucariaceae family of conifers a group once widespread during the Jurassic period but now largely restricted to the southern hemisphere

 Davis Strait is a northern arm of the Labrador Sea It is between mid western Greenland and Nunavut Canada s Baffin Island The strait was named for the English explorer John Davis who explored the area while seeking a Northwest Passage It is the world s broadest strait The water depth is between one and two thousand meters The strait is much shallower than the Labrador Sea to the south or Baffin Bay to the north It is on the coast line of Canada

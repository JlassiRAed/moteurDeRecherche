The NHL All Star Game SuperSkills Competition at first called the National Hockey League All Star Skills Competition is an event on the night before the NHL All Star Game First held at the st National Hockey League All Star Game in Pittsburgh in the NHL hosts the competition to show off the skills of the all star players Events include shot accuracy fastest skater puck control relay hardest shot breakaway challenge and an elimination shootout Players are put into teams in the same way the All Star Game is done which is by conferences After each event the winning conference is given points

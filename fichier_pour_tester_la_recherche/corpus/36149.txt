 The Metropolitan Cathedral Basilica of the Holy Saviour or Cathedral of San Salvador is a Roman Catholic cathedral and minor basilica in the centre of Oviedo in the Asturias region of northern Spain Many styles of architectural styles were used in building the cathedral They include Romanesque Gothic and Renaissance It began as a large Pre Romanesque basilica in the present location of the Gothic cathedral That first building was built by order of King Alfonso II of Asturias

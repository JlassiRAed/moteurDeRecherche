The Magellan spacecraft also called the Venus Radar Mapper was a NASA spacecraft designed to explore the planet Venus Venus It was launched on May and reached Venus on August The Magellan was the first spacecraft to be launched from a Space Shuttle the Space Shuttle Atlantis Atlantis It was also the first spacecraft to be deliberately crashed Magellan was made from parts left over from other space exploration programs including Voyager program Voyager The Magellan was named after the Portuguese people Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan

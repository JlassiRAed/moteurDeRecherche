 The Lone Gunmen was a short lived television show based on the show The X Files It focused on the three geeks that occasionally helped Agents Mulder and Scully Their names were John Byers played by Bruce Harwood Ringo Langly played by Dean Haglund and Melvin Frohike played by Tom Braidwood The series expanded the line up to include a character named Jimmy Bond played by Stephen Snedden and Yves Adele Harlow played by Zuleikha Robinson It is best known for predicting the events of in its first episode

The line of succession to the British throne is the order in which members of the royal family would come to the throne if the reigning king or queen died or abdicated At present the first in line is Charles Prince of Wales followed by his elder son Prince William Duke of Cambridge and then Prince William Duke of Cambridge s son George Traditionally males came before females in the line of succession However the law changed on March so at present for people born after October the succession is decided only by age older children come before younger children When someone who is in line to the throne has a child that child comes after them and their older children but before anyone else in the line of succession Excluded from the line of succession are Catholics and illegitimate children

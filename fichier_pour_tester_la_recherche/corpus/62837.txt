An ode is a type of Lyric poetry lyric poem The word comes from the Ancient Greek An ode is a poem which is written in praise of something or someone saying how wonderful they are Very often an ode will have the title Ode to followed by the name of the thing or person that it describes e g John Keats wrote a poem Ode to a Nightingale Odes were written by the poets of Ancient Greece as well as by the Ancient Rome Romans Horace was particularly famous for his odes and many English odes written centuries later were based on Horace s lyrical verses

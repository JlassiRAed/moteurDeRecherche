 Service awards are a simple way of thanking an editor for their work on the Simple English Wikipedia They are based on two things How long they have been an editor The number of edits they have made Unlike other awards which are only given by one editor to another these awards can be given to yourself though they can also be given by another editor It is done strictly by counting the time you have been an editor and number of edits There is no special process for getting these awards You decide which one you are wikt eligible eligible for and place it on your user page Please remember that time spent on the Simple English Wikipedia and number of edits made do not show the quality of an editor s work or how useful they are as a member of the community Service awards also do not show any level of wikt authority authority Senior editors have no more authority than novice editors If you would like to give someone an award based on the quality of their work please use the Wikipedia Barnstars

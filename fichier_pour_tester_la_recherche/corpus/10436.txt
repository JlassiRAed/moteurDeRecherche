 Does This Look Infected is the second studio album by Canada Canadian punk rock band Sum Does This Look Infected was released on November It s more heavy and less poppy than All Killer No Filler The album featured the singles Still Waiting It is the only Sum album that has a parental advisory sticker on it Drummer Steve Jocz is featured on the cover dressed as a zombie The band said on the DVD Sake Bombs And Happy Endings that the song title A N I C stood for Anna Nicole Smith Anna Nicole is a Cunt The band stopped including the song in their live performances after Smith died

 Nick on CBS was a Saturday morning children s programming block for CBS that began on September replacing CBS Kidshow This Saturday morning block presented programming from Nick Jr which at a time shared common ownership with CBS under Viacom When the block first properly aired it was hosted by Face from Nick Jr On September Nick Jr on CBS was re branded as Nick on CBS and airing both Nickelodeon and Nick Jr shows The Nickelodeon shows were removed in in favor for a return to an exclusively Nick Jr block The block s continuity was provided by segments featuring Piper O Possum At the beginning of New Year s Eve Viacom split into two different companies with CBS Corporation becoming its own standalone company and Nickelodeon going to new Viacom Nick Jr on CBS ended on September and was replaced by Cookie Jar TV KOL Secret Slumber Party on CBS on September as part of a multi year partnership between CBS and Cookie Jar Entertainment All programming during the block as is standard for Saturday morning network programming was labelled as meeting E I requirements as tennous as some of the claims of educational content may have been This is part of the reason why some of Nickelodeon s most popular programs did not appear on the block even during the more open formatted Nick on CBS era

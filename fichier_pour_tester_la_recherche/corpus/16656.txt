 Tecophilaeaceae is a Family order Asparagales It includes genera and about species These are mainly Southern Hemisphere genera although there is one genus with one species from North America The rootstock is a corm and the flowers are solitary or arranged on a simple or branched raceme Flowers are yellow white violet or blue and have six tepals and six stamens and a half inferior three chambered ovary The fruit is a capsule and the seeds are black ovoid and often wrinkled

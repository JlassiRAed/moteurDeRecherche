The topped lavender also known as French lavender is a perennial shrub that usually grows up to centimetres The leafs are centimetres long in a greyish tomentose tone The flowers are pinkish produced on spikes centimetres long each flower is subtended by a bract that is milimetres long At the top of the spike there are many other white or Lavender lavender bracts Many subspecies exist in Spain and other countries nearby

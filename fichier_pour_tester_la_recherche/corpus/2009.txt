 Frieza is a fictional character in Dragon Ball Z Frieza is one of the strongest aliens in Dragon Ball Z He enjoys invading other planets Frieza is a very mean person who thinks he is very strong He has destroyed planets without mercy He will even kill his own servants if he wishes to Frieza is always trying to get more power Because of this he ends up fighting the heroes of Dragon Ball Z so he can get the Dragon Balls The Dragon Balls are magical balls that can grant wishes to whoever collects all Frieza is what the creator Akira Toriyama thought monsters looked like in his childhood

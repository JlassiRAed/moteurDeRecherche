 Enfield Lock railway station is in the London Borough of Enfield in north east London and is in Travelcard Zone on the Tottenham Hale part of the Lea Valley Lines on the West Anglian Railway The station and all trains using it are run by Abellio Greater Anglia Enfield Lock was the main station for the Royal Small Arms Factory until it was closed in the late s and now it is used by the large housing development on the site known as Enfield Island Village and the Innova Science and Business park

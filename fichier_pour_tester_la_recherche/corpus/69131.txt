 Electroplating is the wikt coat coating of an object with a metal It is done by wikt immerse immersing the object and a bar of the metal in a Chemical solution solution containing the metal ion s Electric current is then applied the Positive charge positive goes to the metal and the Negative charge negative goes to the object The metal bar dissolves in the solution and plates out on the object forming a thin but durable coating of metal It is often used to gold plate objects for wikt decoration decoration or to stop corrosion Normally the metal becomes fragile and is only used for display tech stub

 Aulus Vitellius Germanicus September December AD was later known as Vitellius was an Roman Emperor and General officer general for eight months from April to December Vitellius was acclaimed emperor following quick succession of previous emperors Galba and Marcus Salvius Otho in the year of civil war known as Year of the Four Emperors Vitellius was the first add the honorific cognomen Germanicus to his name was instead of Caesar upon his accession the latter name had fallen into disrepute in many quarters because of the actions of Roman Emperor Nero

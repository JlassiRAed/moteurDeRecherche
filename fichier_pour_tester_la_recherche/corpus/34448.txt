 Abstraction is a process of removing or hiding details about something It concerns the ways by which higher less real concepts are derived from the usage and classification of literal data or other information Abstractions may be formed by reducing the information content of a concept or a phenomenon as viewed often to keep only the information which is needed for a particular purpose For example abstracting a leather soccer ball to the more general idea of a ball will keep only the information on general ball attributes and behavior omitting the traits of that particular ball Compare with word generalizing The opposite of the adjective abstract is commonly the word concrete

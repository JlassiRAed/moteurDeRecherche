 Me Against the World is the third studio album by United States American rap music rapper Tupac Shakur Me Against the World was released on March The album was released when Pac was imprisoned and it debuted at on the Billboard By having this happen it made him the first artist to have an album debut at number one on Billboard while serving time in prison It was nominated for Best Rap Album at the Grammy Awards The album s first single Dear Mama was nominated for Best Rap Solo Performance

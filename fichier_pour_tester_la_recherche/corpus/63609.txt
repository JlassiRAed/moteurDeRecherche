An elf is a mythical creature of various origins that is usually regarded as a good being that helps to make trees and nature good It is therefore commonly associated with paganism and witchcraft Elves are usually described as a taller human but with more firmer touch to them In the Lord of the Rings however elves were described as human sized hence changing the description of elves They are sometimes known to have long pointy ears In most modern fantasy scenarios elves are one of the main races although if these are described as the good races they then become one of races including orc s goblin s and troll s and or giant s

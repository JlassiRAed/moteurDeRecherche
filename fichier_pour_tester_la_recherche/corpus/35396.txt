 Album ratings Extremes is the third studio album released by United States American country music artist Collin Raye Extremes became platinum in the United States for sales of over one million copies The singles released from this album were That s My Story Man of My Word My Kind of Girl If I Were You and Little Rock My Kind of Girl was a Number One hit on the Hot Country Songs charts The other singles all reached Top Ten Dreaming My Dreams with You is a cover of the Waylon Jennings song A Bible and a Bus Ticket Home was later recorded by Confederate Railroad on their album Keep On Rockin

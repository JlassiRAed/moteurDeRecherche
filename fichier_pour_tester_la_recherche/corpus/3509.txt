 Scarp is a term in geology and geography with several similar meanings A scarp is a cliff or steep slope The surface of the steep slope is called a scarp face A fault scarp is a steep cliff made by movement along one side of a Fault fault An escarpment is a steep slope or long cliff that occurs from faulting and resulting erosion It separates two relatively level areas of differt height A cuesta is a ridge with an escarpment on one side and a gentle dip slope on the other side

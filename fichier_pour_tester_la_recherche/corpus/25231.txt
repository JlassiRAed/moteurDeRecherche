 Non sequitur means does not follow It is a type of logical fallacy a bad argument that makes no sense A non sequitur argument takes something that people accept is true and says that because this is true the conclusion is right The problem is that the conclusion has nothing to do with the premise Usually this argument seems convincing on the surface However consider the following example Knowledge is power so stupid people can never have power This is wrong because Stupidity stupid people can still know things just not have good ideas or use their knowledge well and knowledge is not the only source of power This argument tries use a statement that people agree with as bad support for a new idea that is not the logical conclusion of the statement

 Glass lizards or glass snakes are a genus of reptile s that look like snake s Although most species have no legs their head shapes movable eyelids and external ear openings show they are lizards A few species have very small stub like legs near their rear vent These are vestigial organ s Most described species are native to Asia a few occur in North America and at least one can be found in North Africa Like many lizards they have the ability to drop off part of the tail when attacked It can break into several pieces like glass The tail stays mobile distracting the predator while the lizard keeps still

 Miss Representation is a American documentary film It was written directed and produced by Jennifer Siebel Newsom It is about how the American Mass media media is sexist The documentary has interviews with Condoleezza Rice Margaret Cho Jane Fonda Lisa Ling Nancy Pelosi Katie Couric Rachel Maddow Rosario Dawson Cory Booker Geena Davis Dr Jackson Katz Dr Jean Kilbourne and Gloria Steinem On January the film was shown at the Sundance Film Festival Miss Representation won Outstanding Documentary at the Gracie Allen Awards

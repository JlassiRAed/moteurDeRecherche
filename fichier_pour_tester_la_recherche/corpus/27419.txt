 Lark Rise to Candleford is a United Kingdom British television costume drama series adapted by the BBC from Flora Thompson s Lark Rise to Candleford trilogy of semi autobiographical novels about the English countryside The first episode aired on January on BBC One and BBC HD Its cast includes actors from other late and early BBC costume dramas such as Sense and Sensibility and Cranford The story set in Oxfordshire It is about Laura Timmins a teenage girl from the village of Lark Rise who is sent by her parents to work as an assistant at the post office in the nearby town of Candleford The post office is run by Miss Dorcas Lane who is a cousin of Laura s mother Laura s father the stonemason Robert Timmins at first misses her but later becomes proud of her for doing well in her new job Laura s friend Alf Arless is in love with her but as Laura becomes used to life in Candleford he realises that she will never return his feelings The other post office workers are the postman Thomas Brown a very religious man who tries not to get involved in any kind of mischief or fun the maid Zillah who has been with Miss Lane for many years and considers herself in charge of the household and Mrs Macey the senior assistant who decides to leave when other people find out that her husband has been in prison A second series began in January introducing some new characters These include a new maid Minnie and a rich hotel owner James Dowland who falls in love with Dorcas Lane In the end the series had a four season run The BBC stopped a fifth season because the team felt that after four seasons the time was right to bring in new dramas It was said they hoped viewers would love the new programmes in the same way The last episode on BBC was in early

 The Port Authority of New York and New Jersey Police Department or Port Authority Police Department is a police department in New York and New Jersey It protects all the property owned by the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey and it enforces state and city laws The PAPD is one of the largest police departments in the United States with members The PAPD is owned by the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey a two state agency that runs the bridge s tunnel s airport s and seaport s along the New York New Jersey border

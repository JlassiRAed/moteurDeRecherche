 Igneous rock is one of the three main rock types the others being Sedimentary rock sedimentary and metamorphic rock Igneous rocks are rocks formed from molten magma The material is made liquid by the heat inside the Earth s Mantle mantle When magma comes out onto the surface of the Earth it is called lava Lava cools down to form rocks such as tuff and basalt Also the magma may slowly cool down to form rocks under the surface An example is granite

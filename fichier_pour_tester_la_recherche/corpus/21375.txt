 Sax Rohmer was Arthur Henry Ward After ghostwriter ghostwriting the autobiography of Little Tich a variety show music hall entertainer Ward issued the first Fu Manchu novel This was The Mystery of Dr Fu Manchu serial serialised from October to June It was an immediate success It had police official Denis Nayland Smith and Dr Petrie facing a worldwide conspiracy led by a mastermind It reflected the yellow peril scares of the times The phrase yellow peril was coined by Kaiser Wilhelm II of Germany in the s The Fu Manchu stories and other writing made Rohmer one of the most successful and well paid authors of the s and s

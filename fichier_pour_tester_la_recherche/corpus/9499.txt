A Sulfonamide is a kind of drug It is a man made antibiotic Their ability to kill infectious bacteria is their main function but some types have other medical uses For example sulfasalazine in addition to its use as an antibiotic is also used in the treatment of inflammatory bowel disease Sulfonamides contain the sulfonamide chemical group The best known of the antibiotic sulfonamide drugs is called Sulfanilimide

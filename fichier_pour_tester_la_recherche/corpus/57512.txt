 Rhabdodontids were herbivorous ornithopod dinosaur s from the Cretaceous They were similar to large robust hypsilophodont s with deep skulls and jaws The family family were defined as the most recent common ancestor of Zalmoxes robustus and Rhabdodon priscus and all the descendants of this common ancestor In Paul Sereno defined the family as the most inclusive clade containing Rhabdodon priscus but not Parasaurolophus walkeri Rhabdodontid fossils have been found in Europe and Australia in formations from to million years ago

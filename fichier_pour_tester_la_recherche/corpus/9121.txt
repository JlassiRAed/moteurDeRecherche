 Paul the Octopus was an octopus living in a tank at a Sea Life Centres Sea Life Centre in Oberhausen Germany Paul became famous around the world for correctly choosing the winner of Germany s seven matches at the FIFA World CupDuring the divination s Paul was shown two boxes with a mussel inside them for food Each box had on the outside a flag of a association football football team Paul would eat the mussel from the box with the flag of the winning team He did this correctly in four of Germany s six UEFA European Football Championship Euro matches and in all seven of their matches in the World Cup He correctly chose a win for Spain against the Netherlands in the World Cup final

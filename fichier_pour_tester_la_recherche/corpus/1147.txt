 Anantnag also called Islamabad is a city and a municipality located in the Islamabad District in the state of Jammu and Kashmir India It should not be confused with Islamabad the capital of Pakistan About people live in Anantnag Anantnag is the third largest city of the Indian state Jammu and Kashmir by population and largest according to area It is also regarded as the commercial and financial capital of the Kashmir Valley Valley of Kashmir It is the largest business and trading center of the valley

 Owls are bird s in the order order Strigiformes There are species and they are all birds of prey Most of them are solitary and nocturnal in fact they are the only large group of birds which hunt at night Owls are specialists at night time hunting They feed on small mammal s such as rodents insects and other birds and a few species like to eat fish as well As a group owls are very successful They are found in all parts of the world except Antarctica most of Greenland and some other small islands

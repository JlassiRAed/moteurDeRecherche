 Valery Ilyich Rozhdestvensky was a USSR cosmonaut Rozhdestvensky was born in Leningrad and studied engineering at the Higher Military Engineering School of the Soviet Navy in Pushkin From to he was commander of a deep sea diving unit in the Baltic Sea War Fleet He was chosen to be a cosmonaut on October and flew as Flight Engineer on Soyuz in After his space flight he continued to work with the space program at the Yuri Gagarin Cosmonaut Training Center He retired on June and worked with Metropolis Industries

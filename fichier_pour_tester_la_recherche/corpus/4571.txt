The word she is a grammatical person third person singular pronoun used to talk about a female When the name of a woman has been the subject then she takes the place of that name in the text While she is the subject form the word her is the object or possessive form where the word he would be used for a man or it for a neuter pronoun In the American Dialect Society chose she as the word of the past millennium The Society noted the word was not used before the year she was first seen in writings from the th century where the word heo had been used to mean either the feminine or mixed plural as with they

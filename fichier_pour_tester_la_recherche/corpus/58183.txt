 Cinderella or The Little Glass Slipper The story is about an ill treated girl who with the help of her fairy godmother travels to a Ball coach She flees the ball at midnight and loses her glass slipper A prince who has fallen in love with her finds her lost slipper and uses it later to find her Similar European tales are found in Bonaventure des Periers s New Recreations and Joyous Games Tales similar to Cinderella exist in Chinese Indian African Javanese Australian and Japanese literature and folklore The tale has been adapted to the Theatre stage movies television and other media

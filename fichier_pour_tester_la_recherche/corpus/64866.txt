 The Boeing sometimes called the Stratocruiser was an airliner made by Boeing after World War II It was made from the C Stratofreighter which was a type of Boeing B Superfortress used to move troops around The Stratocruiser first took off on July The Stratocruiser had four Reciprocating engine piston engine s It had a Cabin pressurization pressurized cabin and two decks Airlines could fly for much longer with the Stratocruiser to places like Hawaii However the Stratocruiser was a lot more expensive than the Douglas DC and Lockheed Constellation Only Stratocruisers were made for airlines

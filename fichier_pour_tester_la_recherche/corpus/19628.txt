A principality Some states claim to be principalities but are not recognised by other countries Sealand the Principality of Hutt River in Australia and the Principality of Minerva in the Pacific These states are examples of micronations Sometimes the word is also used to mean dependent territory territories in Africa Asia and India pre Columbian America and Oceania For these the word princely states is generally preferred especially if they came under the authority of a European colonial power

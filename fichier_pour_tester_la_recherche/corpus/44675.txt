 Thubten Zopa Rinpoche is a lama from Nepal He took his monks vows at Dungkar Monastery in Tibet and has received teachings from many high lamas Lama Zopa is the co founder with Lama Yeshe of Kopan Monastery and the FPMT Since the death of Lama Yeshe Lama Zopa has served as the FPMT s spiritual director Zopa s books are published by Wisdom Publications Free books and a vast online collection of his teachings are available from the Lama Yeshe Wisdom Archive There is an extensive biography of him in the book The Lawudo Lama by Jamyang Wangmo

 Lambing Flat was the name of a gold field in New South Wales Australia People were digging for gold at Lambing Flat in the s It was part of the Burrangong goldfields which also included Spring Creek Stoney Creek Back Creek Wombat Blackguard Gully and Tipperary Gully Burrangong is now the town of Young New South Wales Frank Gardiner a bushranger had a butcher shop at Lambing Flat Ben Hall who also became a bushranger sold cattle to Gardiner for his shop

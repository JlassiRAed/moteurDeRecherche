 Dalmatians II Patch s London Adventure is a direct to video sequel to the The Walt Disney Company Disney animation animated movie One Hundred and One Dalmatians which is voiced by Martin Short Jason Alexander and Barry Bostwick It was released on January and won awards for best animated feature best movie director director best editing and best Sheet music Score musical score Disney re released the movie on September The movie tells the story of Patch the loneliest dalmatian who felt lost in a sea of spots until he met his TV hero Thunderbolt Unlike many of the Disney direct to video sequels that just retell the original movie s story Dalmatians II Patch s London Adventure takes characters from the original movie in some new directions

 Initiation is a ceremony at the entrance or acceptance into a group or society It can also be a formal admission to adult hood in a community Examples of initiation ceremonies are Christian baptism or Confirmation confirmation Jewish Bar and Bat Mitzvah bar or bat mitzvah acceptance into a fraternal organization like the Freemasonry freemasons or religious order like the Benedictine s or graduation from school A person taking the initiation ceremony is called an initiate

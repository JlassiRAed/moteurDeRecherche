 William Whewell The son of a carpenter Whewell rose to the top For years he was a Professor and for years he was Master of Trinity College Cambridge He was one of the founding members and a president of the British Association for the Advancement of Science a Fellow of the Royal Society and President of the Geological Society It was the Prime Minister himself Robert Peel who recommended his appointment as Master of Trinity Whewell had wide interests He researched ocean tides He published work in mechanics physics geology astronomy and economics He composed poetry wrote books translated the works of Goethe and wrote sermon s and Theology theological tracts

 Behak Maken is a village in the Sargodha District of the Punjab Pakistan Punjab province of Pakistan It is km from Sargodha city and km away from Dhreema due to its geographical location the British Royal Air Force built an air base between Chak No NB and Chak No NB nearby It took on greater importance after the Independence of Pakistan for the Pakistan Air Force The population grew rapidly Approximately of the population of this village belongs to the Maken caste or clan of this region

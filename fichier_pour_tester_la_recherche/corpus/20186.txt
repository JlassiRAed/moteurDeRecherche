The Commonwealth of Independent States is the international organization or alliance of countries that used to form the Soviet Union On December leaders of Russia Belarus and Ukraine met at the nature reserve called Belovezhskaya Pushcha in Belarus They discussed the creation of the Commonwealth of Independent States They reached an agreement about many points and signed a document creating the CIS One of these points stated that the alliance would be open to all the republics of the Soviet Union Other nations with similar goals could also join the CIS The Soviet Union had republics Armenia Azerbaijan Belarus Kazakhstan Kyrgyzstan Moldova Russia Tajikistan Turkmenistan Ukraine Uzbekistan Georgia joined the CIS Before joining the CIS on st December leaders of these countries had met in Kazakhstan to accept the original CIS agreement of th December In December Georgia also joined the CIS On th August Turkmenistan left the permanent membership and became an associate member On th August Georgia left the CIS membership and Georgia s CIS membership officially ended on th August Many people believed that with the creation of the Commonwealth of Independent States the Soviet Union ceased to exist For them it was the dissolution of the Soviet Union Many others consider that with CIS Russia continues to have some control over the former republics of the Soviet Union The CIS has its headquarters at Minsk Belarus An Executive Director heads the CIS The CIS is not a successor country to the Soviet Union The CIS is an organization or alliance of independent countries It is more like the European Community The member countries of the CIS had also signed many agreements for economic cooperation and defense cooperation They have signed other agreements for cooperation in foreign policy and other matter

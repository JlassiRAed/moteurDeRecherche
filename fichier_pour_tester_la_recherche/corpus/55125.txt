 Santiago de Cuba Province is the second most populated province in the island of Cuba It has a population of about million people The largest city Santiago de Cuba The province has many material resources such as iron and nickel The economy however relies mostly on agriculture with large plantation s growing banana s cacao and coffee dotting the landscape Industry is growing around the capital as is tourism The natural beauty of the area is attracting many tourists from both the rest of Cuba and from overseas Santiago de Cuba province has been the site of many battle s both during the war for independence and the Cuban Revolution where much of the guerrilla warfare guerrilla fighting took place in the mountainous province

 Sumer Is Icumen In is a very old English song which can be sung as a round round It is the oldest example of a round that we know of The composer is unknown It was written down around the middle of the th century by a monk but we do not know whether that monk composed it or whether it had been composed earlier The title means Summer has come in The round can be sung in six parts There are also two parts which can be repeated again and again throughout the piece The language is Middle English and is based on the dialect spoken in Wessex England at the time

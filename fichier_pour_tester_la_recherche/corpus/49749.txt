 Vincent Thomas Lombardi was an American football coach that never had a losing season Lombardi was named NFL Man of the Decade in the s and won five National Football League NFL championships including winning the first two Super Bowl s The yearly trophy given to the Super Bowl champion was renamed the Vince Lombardi Trophy in the same year Lombardi was named to the Pro Football Hall of Fame He was head coach of the Green Bay Packers and the Washington Redskins

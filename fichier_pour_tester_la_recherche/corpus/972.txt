 Enter Shikari are a post hardcore band from St Albans Hertfordshire England The band is named after the Punjabi language Punjabi word Shikari meaning hunting hunter The band have played at festivals such as Reading and Leeds They are known for songs such as Sorry You re Not a Winner Arguing With Thermometers Juggernauts and Anything Can Happen in the Next Half Hour They have an album called Common Dreads that contains Common Dreads Solidarity Step Up Juggernauts Wall Zzzonked Havoc A No Sleep Tonight Gap in the Fence Havoc B Antwerpen The Jester Halcyon Hectic and Fanfare for the Conscious Man

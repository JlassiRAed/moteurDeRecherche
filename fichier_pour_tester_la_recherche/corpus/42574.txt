A summer park is a form of amusement park theme park with a summer time theme A summer park is only opened during the summertime season Playground s and swimming pool make up most attractions instead of carousel s Several summer parks have been built in Denmark The phenomenon was booming in Sweden by the late and mid s It expanded during the Roaring s economic boom before a decrease began in the early s during the Swedish banking rescue economc recession The phenomenon was also decreasing because of the introduction of a wider entertainment market

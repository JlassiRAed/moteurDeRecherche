 Fatima Jinnah was a Pakistan i dentist dental surgeon writer Politician stateswoman and one of the founders of Pakistan She was the younger sister of Muhammad Ali Jinnah who became the first Governor General of Pakistan She was a strong critic of the British Raj and a leading member of the All India Muslim League She advocated the separation of Pakistan as an independent state when the Raj ended After the independence of Pakistan Jinnah co founded the All Pakistan Women s Association which helped settle the migrants in the new country After the Muhammad Ali Jinnah Illness and death death of her brother she was a prominent philanthropist Her legacy is her support for civil rights her struggle in Pakistan Movement and her devotion to her brother Many institutions and public spaces have been named in her honour

 Mohenjo daro was one of the largest city settlements of the Indus Valley Civilization of south Asia It is in province of Sindh Pakistan The city was built around BC It was one of the early urban settlements in the world Mohenjo daro existed at the same time as the civilization civilisations of ancient Egypt Mesopotamia and Ancient Greece Greece The Archaeology archaeological ruins of the city are designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site In Pakistan it is one of the national icons of the distant past

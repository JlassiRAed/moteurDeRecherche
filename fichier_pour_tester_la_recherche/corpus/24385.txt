 Enthroned is a black metal band formed in Charleroi in by Cernunnos In along with Ancient Rites and Bewitched Bewitched Enthroned began a large tour of Europe In April as Enthroned was to begin recording Towards the Skullthrone of Satan Cernunnos took his life Enthroned recorded the album with a session drummer in honor of Cernunnos In April the band toured the European continent with Dark Funeral They also released the Mini CD MCD Regie Axese which was dedicated to their former drummer Cernunnos Later that year they toured again with Hecate Enthroned and Usurper Enthroned continued to play many live gigs in and around Europe as well as in the US In early Enthroned signed to Napalm Records In they played a South American tour Enthroned signed with Regain Records in January At this time some members of the band opened a recording studio Blackout Multimedia In September Enthroned said they would end working with Regain Records

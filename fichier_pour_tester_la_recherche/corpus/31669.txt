 Are You Smarter Than A Fifth Grader is a television game show based on asking grade school level questions to adults The format started with the United States version which first aired on FOX Broadcasting Company FOX in February The show was created by Mark Burnett the same person behind the hit show Survivor The show has since been made in many other countries some use the same title and some use different titles In Australia the show is on the Ten Network The top prize is Australian dollar AU A syndicated version of the show aired in the United States from September to March Contestants answer eight questions and can answer a th grade bonus question for ten times the amount they earned from those questions Unlike other versions of the show the game does not end after a wrong answer Instead the contestant loses their earnings up to that point

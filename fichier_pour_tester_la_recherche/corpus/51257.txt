 Satch Boogie is the fifth track from Joe Satriani s album Surfing With The Alien The piece is a hard driving rock boogie in shuffle time The solo is a mixture of A pentatonic blues and Dorian mode the chord sequence is A D F A The tapping solo uses Satriani s signature Pitch Axis Theory composition style the central pitch in this case is A and the broken chords revolve around this note

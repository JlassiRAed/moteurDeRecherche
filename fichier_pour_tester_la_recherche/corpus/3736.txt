 Avalon is a popular song It was written by Al Jolson Buddy DeSylva and Vincent Rose in Many jazz artists have recorded the song for example Cab Calloway The melody at the beginning of the song was taken from Giacomo Puccini s opera Tosca and Rose changed it only a little Because of this a court later decided that Jolson and Rose had to pay Puccini s publishers and all later profits from the song

 Dudiptsar lake or Dudipat Lake is a lake in Pakistan It is in the extreme north of Kaghan Valley It is about a four hour drive from the chief town Naran The word dudi means white and sar means lake The lake was named because it reflects the white color of snow on the mountain peaks around it The lake s water is greenish blue and very cold The lake used to have a lot of trout Now there aren t as many because of illegal fishing using dynamite and nets This lake is very little visited by people Getting to the lake takes four to six hours of tough hiking and trekking The earthquake on October in North Pakistan made it more difficult to get to the lake However the government of Pakistan decided on March to take all steps to restore tourism of the Kaghan valley That includes building new tourism spots and rebuilding previously destroyed ones

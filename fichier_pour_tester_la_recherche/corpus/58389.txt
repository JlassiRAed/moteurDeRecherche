 Mount Meager is a potentially active volcano in the Canadian province of British Columbia Canada kilometers north of Vancouver The top of the mountain is mostly covered by snow and glacier s It lies in the Garibaldi Volcanic Belt and is the northernmost volcano of the Cascade Volcanic Arc that extends down to northern California Its most common type of rock is lava that is almost a million years old The mountain is part of the Pacific Ranges of mountains and is meters high It was previously called Cathedral Mountain

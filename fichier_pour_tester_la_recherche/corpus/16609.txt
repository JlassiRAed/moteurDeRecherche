The Hijras of Southern Asia refers to individuals or humans in South Asia who are transsexual or transgender Most South Asians think hijras are only men who have feminine gender identity and wear women s clothing However some include female to male people also The word hijra is a Urdu Hindustani word It comes from the Semitic Arabic language Arabic root hjr It has been borrowed into Hindi The Indian usage has traditionally been translated into English as eunuch or hermaphrodite where the irregularity of the male genitalia is central to the definition However in general hijras are born with typically male physiology only a few having been born with male intersex variations

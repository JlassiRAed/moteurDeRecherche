 Brunhilde was a mighty female warrior one of the Valkyrie s She was also a heroine from the German epics especially in the Nibelungen saga in which she is an Icelandic princess She defied Odin and in punishment he imprisoned her within a ring of fire on earth She had to stay there until someone rescued her Siegfied braved the fire woke her and fell in love with her He gave her the ring Andvarinaut unaware of its curse Eventually she killed herself when she learned that Sigurd had betrayed her with another woman not knowing he had been bewitched into doing so by Grimhild

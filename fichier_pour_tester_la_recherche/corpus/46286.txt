An obelus division operation This symbol is also known as a division sign The word obelus comes from the Greek language Greek word for a sharpened stick spit or pointed pillar This is the same root as that of the word obelisk The obelus was first used as a symbol for division in in the algebra book Teutsche Algebra by Johann Rahn Today the obelus remains in occasional use primarily as a standalone symbol for the division operation itself

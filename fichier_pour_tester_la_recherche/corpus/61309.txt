 Kanjanur is a village in the Thiruvidaimarudur taluk of Thanjavur district Tamil Nadu India It is on the northern bank of the Kaveri River about kilometres north east of Kumbakonam It is one of the nine Navagrahasthalas of the Cauvery Delta It is famous for its Agniswarar Temple Kanjanur Agniswarar temple This temple is dedicated to Shukra The temple is one of the Navagraha Temples of the Cauvery Delta To the north of Kanjanur there are some low hillocks These are to feet high Magnesite witk deposit deposits have been discovered in these hills

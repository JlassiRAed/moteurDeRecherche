The Tomkinson Ranges is a mountain range in the northwest corner of South Australia The range consists of sandstone hills surrounded by Triodia spinifex grassland s Several wikt rockhole rock holes are located in and around the hills The mountains were named after politician Samuel Tomkinson The explorer William Gosse named them when he passed through the region in the early s Ernest Giles and his team reached the ranges shortly after and set up a base here from which to explore the Gibson Desert Gibson and Great Victoria Desert s Numerous prospecting expedition s were made through the region until the s Together with the Mann Ranges the Tomkinson Ranges are an important part of the Pitjantjatjara homeland The communities of Kalka South Australia Kalka and Pipalyatjara South Australia Pipalyatjara are located at the base of the mountains

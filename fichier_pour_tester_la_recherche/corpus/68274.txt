 South East Mutton Bird Islet is a steep islet in south eastern Australia It is part of the Mutton Bird Island Group at the southern end of the south western coast of Tasmania It is part of the Southwest National Park and part of the Tasmanian Wilderness World Heritage Site citation It is very small with an area of above sea level The island is part of the Port Davey Islands Important Bird Area so identified by BirdLife International because of its importance for breeding seabirds

The Statue of Herman is a monument in the Southern part of the Teutoburg Forest in the district of Lippe Rural District Lippe North Rhine Westphalia in Germany The monument commemorates the Cherusci war chief Hermann or Armin and the Battle of the Teutoburg Forest in AD when the Germanic tribes under Arminius fought three Roman legions and other troops under Publius Quinctilius Varus Varus The sword has the following inscription German unity Germany s might

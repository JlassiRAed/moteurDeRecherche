 Rodinia meaning to give birth is the name of a supercontinent It had most or all of Earth s landmass when the Neoproterozoic era began Rodinia existed between billion and million years ago It formed from parts of the older supercontinent Columbia Columbia Rodinia broke up in the first period of the Neoproterozoic the Tonian Later its continental fragments were re assembled to form Pangaea million years ago In contrast with Pangaea little is known yet about the exact position and history of Rodinia The extreme cooling of the global climate around million years ago and the rapid evolution of primitive life during the following Ediacaran and Cambrian periods may have been triggered by the breaking up of Rodinia

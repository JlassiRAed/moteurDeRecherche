 Southeast Asia or Southeastern Asia is the southeastern part of Asia There are ten countries in this region All the countries in Southeast Asia are a part of Association of Southeast Asian Nations ASEAN All of the countries in Southeast Asia are in the tropics except for the northernmost areas of Myanmar which is in the subtropics These are the countries in Southeast Asia The United Nations also includes the region of North east India and Bangladesh traditionally due to ethnicity similarities and culturally as part of Southeast Asia

 Gangsta rap is a subgenre of hip hop that reflects the violent lifestyles of some inner city youths Gangsta is a non rhotic pronunciation of the word gangster The genre was pioneered in the mid s by rappers such as Afroman and Ice Cube and was popularized in the later part of the s by groups like N W A After the national attention that Ice T and N W A created in the late s and early s gangsta rap became the most commercially lucrative subgenre of hip hop

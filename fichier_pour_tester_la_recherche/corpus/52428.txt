 Cuban music comes from the Caribbean island of Cuba Cuba has developed a wide range of musical styles which draw on its cultural origins in Europe and Africa Cuba s music has been hugely popular and influential throughout the world It has been perhaps the most popular form of world music since the introduction of recording technology The music of Cuba including the instruments and the dances is mostly of European and African origin Most forms of the present day are fusions and mixtures of these two great sources The original inhabitants of Cuba died out and little remains of their traditions

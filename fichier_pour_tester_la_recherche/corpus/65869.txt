The Ganges river dolphin is a subspecies of river dolphin The Ganges dolphin is found in the Ganges and Brahmaputra Rivers in Republic of India Nepal and Bangladesh The Ganges river dolphins are different to the Indus river dolphin s which are only found in Pakistan The Ganges river dolphin is primarily found in the Ganges River and Brahmaputra River s and their tributaries of the Gangetic Plains of India It is said to represent the purity of the holy Ganga River as it can only survive in pure and fresh water

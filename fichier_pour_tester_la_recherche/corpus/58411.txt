 Comilla Jagannath Temple is a Hindu temple in Comilla in southeastern Bangladesh Its terracotta brick construction is in the typical Bengal style of temple architecture The temple was originally built in the th century by Sree Sreejoktou Moharaja Rada Kishor Manikuo Bahadur who was the king of Tripura The statues of the deity deities of Jagannath Balabhadra and Subhadra were originally installed in a temple in Tripura from where they were subsequently shifted to this temple

In remixing a person takes a familiar song splits it into different parts called tracks and changes the song s music instruments layout and or vocals to create a new version of the same song It is called remixing due to mixing being the putting together of all the parts of a song and remixing being the putting together of the parts of the song differently than the original Remixers that is people who remix are musicians who use a variety of tools primarily electronic to create the new song versions Remixing can be simply moving song parts around it can also be creating new music for an old song lyric There are two common kinds of remixing production and mashups Production remixing uses new instruments with the old song vocal and mashups use two old songs mixed together to create a new song

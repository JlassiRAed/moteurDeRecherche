The cavatina Se vuol ballare is an aria from The Marriage of Figaro by Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart The lyrics are by Lorenzo Da Ponte It portrays Figaro s intent to foil Count Almaviva s womanizing but can also be read as a political attack on the power wielding nobility of the time The song is sung in act Figaro discovers Count Almaviva intends to exercise his feudalism feudal right to sleep with Figaro s wife Susanna before they consummate their marriage Figaro sings of how he will unravel the Count s schemes and thwart him

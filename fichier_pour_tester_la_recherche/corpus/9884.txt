 Guan Gong also known as Guan Yu or Guan Yun Chang was a General officer general who lived close to two thousand years ago in China He helped his sworn brother Liu Bei to fight against the dynasty of Wei Dynasty Wei that seized the throne from the last Han Dynasty Han Emperor He was also the sworn brother of Zhang Fei another warrior of Liu Bei s The Three Brothers conquered the Southeast of China and founded a Dynasty named Shu Han In later centuries Guan Yu became a Chinese deity and was given the name Guan Gong meaning Lord Guan

 Abd Manaf ibn Qusai is the person whom the clan of Banu Abd Manaf is named after His father was Qusai ibn Kilab When his father died he took charge of his work He too distinguished himself by his ability and he was famous for his generosity and good judgment He had five sons Nawfal ibn Abd Manaf Nawfal Abd Shams ibn Abd Manaf Abd Shams Hashim ibn Abd Manaf Hashim and Muttalib ibn Abd Manaf Muttalib

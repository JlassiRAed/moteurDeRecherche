 Baron is a wikt title title of honor in many Europe European peerage systems It is often a hereditary title that ranks as the one of the lower titles in a peerage In the UK peerage system the five peerage titles from highest to lowest are duke marquess earl viscount and baron The word baron comes from Old French baron itself from Franks Frankish baro meaning freeman warrior It later merged with Old English language Old English beorn meaning nobleman The lands of a baron are called a barony The female title for a baron is baroness The form of address for a baron is Lord and for a baroness Lady

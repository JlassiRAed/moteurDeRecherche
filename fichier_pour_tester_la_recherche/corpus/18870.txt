A clinometer is an instrument that is used for measuring the angle or elevation of slopes The name clinometer comes from Greek language Greek and English language English words Klinein means measurement A simple way to create a clinometer is to tape a straw to the bottom of a protractor then tape a piece of string to the degree line Finally you can add a small weight onto the string to allow it to stay straight It is recommended you use something with a hole in it like a washer or a potato

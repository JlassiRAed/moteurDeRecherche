The word felsic is a term used in geology to refer to silicate minerals magma and igneous rock s which are enriched in the lighter elements such as silicon oxygen aluminium sodium and potassium They are usually light in colour and have specific gravity specific gravities less than The most common felsic rock is granite but others include quartz muscovite orthoclase and the sodium rich plagioclase feldspar s In terms of chemistry felsic rocks are on the other side of the rock spectrum from the mafic rocks

 Fantasia is a American Animation animated movie It was produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios Walt Disney Feature Animation and released by Walt Disney Pictures It is a sequel to Fantasia The Sorcerer s Apprentice is the only segment that is in both movies The soundtrack was performed by the Chicago Symphony Orchestra with conductor James Levine A group of celebrities introduce each segment in live action scenes The celebrities include Steve Martin Bette Midler Penn Teller James Earl Jones Quincy Jones and Angela Lansbury Fantasia was first premiered at Carnegie Hall on December th It was part of a five city concert tour with performances in London Paris Tokyo and Pasadena California An exclusive release in IMAX theatres followed from January to April It became the first animated feature length movie in the IMAX format Fantasia was opened in the United States on June It has earned million in gross revenue worldwide

 	 James Hutton Doctor of Medicine MD was a Scottish geologist physician Natural history naturalist chemist and experiment al Agriculture farmer Hutton trained in medicine but never practised it Instead he did scientific research helped to start a chemical industry and farmed his estate He is one of the founders of modern geology He saw the Earth as the product of natural forces What could be seen happening today over long periods of time could produce what we see in the Rock rocks His book on the Theory of the Earth set out theories of geology and Age of the Earth geologic time These ideas supported the theories of plutonism and uniformitarianism

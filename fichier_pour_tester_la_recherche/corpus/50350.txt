 Paratyphoid fever also known as enteric fever is a waterborne disease caused by the bacterium Salmonella Paratyphi It is related to Typhoid fever but like Typhoid it is unrelated to Typhus Symptoms include Fever Abdominal pain Swelling of the liver or spleen Slow heart rate The disease is rarely fatal and people usually recover in a few days but some people can carry the bacteria for months

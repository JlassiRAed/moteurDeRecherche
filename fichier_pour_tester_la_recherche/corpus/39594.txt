 Carmine The Roman Falcone is a fictional Batman character who is the head of the Gotham Mafia He is sometimes called Don Falcone He has a son Alberto Falcone who is known as the Holiday Killer In some cases Two Face flipped his coin and killed Falcone His body was robbed many times before being frozen by Mr Freeze His business was taken over by Sal Maroni He was played by Tom Wilkinson in Batman Begins

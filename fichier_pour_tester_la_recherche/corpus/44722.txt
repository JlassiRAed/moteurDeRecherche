 Overexposed is the fourth album studio album by United States American pop rock band Maroon The album was released on June under A M Octone Records in the United States The album was announced on March on the group s newsletter The album s producers consist of One Republic frontman Ryan Tedder Benjamin Benny Blanco Levin Max Martin and Shellback The lead single off the album Payphone Payphone was released on April It features rapper Wiz Khalifa The song peaked at number two on the Billboard Hot singles chart and number one on the Digital Songs chart It sold digital downloads within its first week beating out the previous toppers Black Eyed Peas with their hit single I Gotta Feeling On May the band performed the song One More Night in Atlantic City publicly for the first time It was announced on May nd that it would serve as the album s second single before the time of the album s release on June And the third and fourth singles of the album were Daylight Daylight and Love Somebody

 Hartley is a village in Kent England It is miles south of Gravesend and Dartford The old church is called All Saints Hartley has many new buildings and some old houses it now joins Longfield the place in the north In there were persons living here There are also woods and a farm near the houses Most of the houses in Hartley are owned by the persons who live in them The name Hartley means place in the wood where the deer are It was a very small place but now is like a town No great events happened here and not many famous persons lived in Hartley

A microwave is a kind of high frequency radio wave Microwaves have many uses including communication radar radio astronomy and to heat food in a microwave oven When something moves it almost always has a wave pattern to its movement this can be seen in water most easily with ripples and ocean waves A very common type of wave in the universe is electromagnetic waves Light is an example of an electromagnetic wave and so is a microwave We can only see electromagnetic radiation in the visible light spectrum so microwaves are invisible

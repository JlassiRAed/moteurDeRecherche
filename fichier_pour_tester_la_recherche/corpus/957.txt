 Dhobi Ghat or Dhoby Ghaut is a word used in various parts of South Asia It means a place where the traditional dhobi caste of washermen and women go to wash clothes The word usually means a place near a river or stream in India Pakistan and Bangladesh but a Dhobi Ghat doesn t necessarily have to be near a source of natural water The world s largest Dhobi Ghat is said to be in Mumbai India It figured in the famous Dhobi Ghat

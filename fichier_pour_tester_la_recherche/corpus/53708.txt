 Jerusalem Botanical Gardens are the national botanical garden s of Israel The gardens are on the Hebrew University of Jerusalem campuses of Mount Scopus and Givat Ram The first garden was established in at Mount Scopus After when the university in Mount Scopus was occupied by Jordan it was opened in a new site in on the new campus university in Givat Ram After the war when Mount Scopus was reoccupied by Israel the garden in Mount Scopus was opened again The visitor center is in Givat Ram next to Nayot neighborhood

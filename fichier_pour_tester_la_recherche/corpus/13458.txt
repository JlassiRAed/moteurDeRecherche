 Animal Crossing New Leaf is a Life simulation game life simulation video game made by Nintendo for the Nintendo DS game console It was released on November in Japan on June in North America and on June in Europe It is the latest game in the Animal Crossing series It was first shown at E Like other Animal Crossing games in Animal Crossing New Leaf the player lives in a village where everyone is an animal The player can interact with villagers by talking and interacting with them Instead of a house the player starts the game living in a tent As the game goes on the player will be able to change and add new things to it The player is also able to swim in the ocean that surrounds the village Also new to this game is that you can become mayor of your town Becoming mayor allows you to change your town more than in past games

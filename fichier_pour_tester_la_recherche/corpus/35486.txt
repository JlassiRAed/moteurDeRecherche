 WrestleMania IX was a professional wrestling pay per view show made by WWE World Wrestling Federation It was held on April at Caesars Palace in Las Vegas Nevada It was the ninth WrestleMania event held by the WWF The main event saw Rodney Anoa i Yokozuna defeat Bret Hart to win the WWE World Heavyweight Championship WWF Championship Yokozuna won the match by pinfall after Yokozuna s manager Mr Fuji threw salt in Hart s eyes After the match Hulk Hogan came down to the ring to check on Hart Mr Fuji challenged Hogan to face Yokozuna in a impromptu match and Hogan accepted Hogan won the match and the championship in seconds after he hit Yokozuna with the leg drop and pinned him WrestleMania IX received negative criticism and was called a poorly booked event John Powell of Canoe ca SLAM Wrestling said that other than the Intercontinental and Tag Team Championship matches and the women that accompanied Lex Luger to the ring the rest of the pay per view was not good He also did not like some of the outfits that were worn at the event most notably Jim Ross s toga and Giant Gonzalez s spray painted suit

The spectacled bear also known as the Andean bear is the last of the lineage of short faced bear s Locally it is also called ukuku jukumari or ukumari The other short faced bears are now extinction extinct They lived during middle to late Pleistocene age The spectacled bear is a relatively small species of bear native to South America It has black fur with a distinctive beige coloured marking across its face and upper chest Males are larger than females Spectacled bears are the only surviving species of bear native to South America and the only surviving member of the subfamily Tremarctinae Their survival has depended mostly on their ability to climb even the highest trees of the Andes

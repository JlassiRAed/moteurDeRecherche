The Pakistan Declaration was a pamphlet published in January by Choudhary Rahmat Ali in which the word Pakistan was used for the first time The pamphlet was written when Pakistan was part of British India The pamphlet asked that the five Northern Province units of British India India P unjab Khyber Pakhtunkhwa become an independent state and not part of India After the pamphlet was published the name of Pakistan became very popular and led to the Pakistan Movement Fourteen years after the pamphlet was published in Pakistan became an independent state

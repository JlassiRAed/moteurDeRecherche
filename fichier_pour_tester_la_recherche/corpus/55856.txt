 Classical liberalism is a type of liberalism It is a belief in economics and a political ideology It emphasizes civil and economic freedom Classical liberals believe in a small government and believe that people will be ruled mostly by natural law They believe that people have inalienable rights They also believe in a free market and property rights Notable people who developed classical liberalism during the th and th century are Adam Smith Thomas Malthus and Thomas Jefferson More recently in the mid th century Ludwig von Mises Friedrich Hayek and Milton Friedman were in favor Many of the beliefs of classical liberalism are similar to those of libertarianism

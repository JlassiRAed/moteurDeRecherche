The DK Bongos are a special Bongo drum bongo like controller that can be used in some Donkey Kong games for Nintendo GameCube The bongos are shaped like the ones seen in the Donkey Kong games It has a built in microphone to detect clapping It was introduced in the music video game Donkey Konga The DK Bongos are seen in the Wii game Super Smash Bros Brawl They are a part of Donkey Kong s Final Smash attack

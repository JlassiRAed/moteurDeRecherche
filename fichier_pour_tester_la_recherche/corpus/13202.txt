 Pernicious anemia is a medical problem that causes a person to have too few red blood cells because their intestine s cannot absorb vitamin B properly If there is not enough intrinsic factor the small intestine is unable to get the vitamin B There are several causes of pernicious anemia These include a weakened stomach lining or a problem with the body s immune system which causes it to attack the intrinsic factor Intrinsic factor was discovered by Thomas Addison

 The Sten Mk II was the main submachine gun of the British military in World War II Early in the war the US lent many weapons to the Brits to fight off German forces including the Thompson submachine gun Thompson SMG However since the Thompson was so expensive to make in large numbers the British would made an SMG of their own one that would be cheap and quick to make The result was the Sten Mk II The weapon was only made of parts making it relatively fast to put together and take apart and therefore easy to ship and hide This feature made it popular among resistance fighters including the Free French It was also the first SMG to have a Suppressor silencer made for it making it useful for stealth and surprise However due to its low quality it was not the most reliable weapon as it had the tendency to Weapon jam jam a lot and to fire entire Magazine

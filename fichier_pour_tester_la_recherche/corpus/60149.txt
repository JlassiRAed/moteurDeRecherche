 Polymer clay is a type of sculpting modelling clay made from the polymer polyvinyl chloride It usually does not contain any clay mineral s but the way it is made has given it the colloquial name of clay A liquid is added to dry particles until it has gel like working properties and the part is put into an Kiln oven to harden Polymer clay is generally used for making arts and crafts arts and craft items There are many different brands of polymer clay

 Anarchist communism also known as Anarcho communism is the belief that no government private property or capitalism should exist and that the means of production should be held in common Many Anarcho communists support direct democracy and a network of voluntary association s workers council s and or a gift economy in which everyone will be free to satisfy their needs Some well known Anarcho communist writers are Peter Kropotkin Ricardo Flores Magnon and Nestor Makhno

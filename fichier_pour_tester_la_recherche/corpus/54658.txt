 Cassius Fairchild Fairchild was educated mostly in Ohio His father was Jairus C Fairchild who was the first Treasurer of Wisconsin and the first Mayor of Madison Wisconsin His brother was Lucius Fairchild Governor of Wisconsin He moved to Wisconsin and managed his family s cranberry and lumber businesses He also managed their rental properties Fairchild was elected to the Madison Wisconsin Common Council where he served as president He also served in the Wisconsin State Assembly At the time of the American Civil War Fairchild enlisted in the Union Army becoming a colonel in the th Wisconsin Volunteer Infantry Regiment Fairchild was badly wounded in the Battle of Shiloh and eventually died of his wounds

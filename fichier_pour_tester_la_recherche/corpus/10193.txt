The Phanerozoic eon is the current eon on the Historical geology Table of geologic time geologic timescale It is the one in which abundant animal life has existed It covers about million years and goes back to the time when hard shelled animals first appeared It was once thought that life began in the Cambrian the first Period period of this eon The time before the Phanerozoic formerly called the Precambrian is now divided into the Hadean Archaean and Proterozoic eons

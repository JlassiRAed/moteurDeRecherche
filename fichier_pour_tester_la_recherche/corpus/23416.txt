 Horatio Gates Spafford is the author of the hymn It Is Well with My Soul Horatio was a lawyer in Chicago He lost all of his possessions in the great Chicago fire After that he lost his only son to a disease at the age of four He then planned a family holiday and was held up for business reasons His family went ahead any way on the ship all four of his daughters died and his wife survived Whilst going to meet his grieving wife he got the inspiration for the song It Is Well with My Soul

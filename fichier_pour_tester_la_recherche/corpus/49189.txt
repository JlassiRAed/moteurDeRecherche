 Moves Like Jagger is a Pop music dance pop single released by United States American pop rock band Maroon on June internationally and digital digitally The single features Pop music pop singer songwriter Christina Aguilera and was produced by Shellback Benny Blanco and Ari Levine The song was later put on the Maroon album Hands All Over Overexposed which is scheduled for release on June The song s lyrics refer to a male protagonist s ability to impress a female with his dance moves which he compares to those of The Rolling Stones frontman Mick Jagger The song received a positive critical appreciation many of them complimented the vocal chemistry between Levine and Aguilera and labeled it an irresistible summer hit

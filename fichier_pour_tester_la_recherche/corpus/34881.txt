 Padangbai is a small town in eastern Bali Indonesia It serves as a ferry port for travel to Lombok Gili Islands The Gilis and other of the Lesser Sunda Islands It is also a lovely beach town that is popular with those who prefer a small town atmosphere over the large scale beach party that is Kuta Besides the beach near the ferry terminal there is a beach just to the north known locally as the Blue Lagoon and the especially nice Secret Beach just to the south of town These pleasant beaches have clusters of small family owned warung s that will be glad to prepare a meal and serve beer Image Padangbai White Sand Beach jpg The white sand beach near Padangbai from the northImage Padangbai White Sand Beach jpg The white sand beach from the southImage Padangbai White Sand Beach jpg The white sand beach from the Lombok StraitImage Padangbai Secret Beach jpg On the white sand beach

A curfew is an order which restricts people in their movements It is very often an order which forbids people to be out on the streets after dark Curfews may be made by governments in times of war or civil unrest This is supposed to stop violence Curfews may also be issued to young people They are told that they have to be indoors after a certain time The word curfew comes from the French language French phrase couvre feu which means cover the fire It was used to describe the time when all lamps and candles had to be blown out

New Forest National Park New Forest Designation list The New Forest is an area of southern England It includes one of the largest remaining tracts of open pasture land heathland and forest in the heavily populated South East England south east of England It covers south west Hampshire and extends into south east Wiltshire and towards east Dorset The name also refers to the New Forest National Park which has similar boundaries The New Forest local government district is a subdivision of Hampshire which covers most of the Forest There are many villages dotted around the area and several small towns in the Forest and around its edges

In Japan Fukurokuju is one of the Seven Lucky Gods He looks a lot like the Chinese star god Shou Some people think that before becoming a god he was a Chinese hermit and a reincarnation of a Taoist god Usually shown as being bald with long whiskers people say he is a living form of the Pole star Southern Polestar In many pictures Fukurokuju has a strangely high forehead He stands with a Crane crane and a turtle which are symbols of long life He is also sometimes with a black deer Ancient legend legends say a deer turns black if it is over years old He is the only member of the Seven Lucky Gods with the ability to bring the dead back to life

 Mate is a traditional drink in some countries in South America especially in Argentina Paraguay Uruguay and Brazil It is also common in parts of Chile Lebanon and Syria The drink which contains mateine Like other brewed herbs yerba mate leaves are dried chopped and ground into a powder called yerba The bombilla is both a straw and a sieve The end which is placed in the drink is wider with small holes or slots that let the brewed liquid in but block the chunky matter that makes up much of the mixture A modern bombilla uses a straight tube with holes or spring sleeve to act as a sieve Bombilla means light bulb in Spanish language Spanish but locally it is little pump or straw

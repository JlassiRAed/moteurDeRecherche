In Christianity different groups disagree about who can be a priest Protestantism Protestant groups say that anyone who was baptism baptized can be a priest Protestants say that anyone can directly talk to God and no ordination is necessary to make someone a priest In contrast Roman Catholics say that the priest has a special function He mediates between God and his people the function of the priest is to help people talk to God For this reason priests need special training Pope Paul VI issued a papal bull called Lumen Gentium In it he stated the position the Roman Catholic church held after the Second Vatican Council

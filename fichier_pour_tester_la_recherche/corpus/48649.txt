 Eugene Onegin is a novel in verse by Alexander Pushkin It is a classic of Russian literature It was published in parts between and The first complete edition was published in and the currently accepted version is based on the publication Almost the entire work is made up of stanza s of iambic tetrameter with the unusual rhyme scheme AbAbCCddEffEgg This form has come to be known as the Onegin stanza or the Pushkin sonnet The story is told by a narrator whose tone is educated worldly and intimate The narrator digresses at times commenting on aspects of the social and intellectual world This helps develop the characters and the drama of the plot

A toll road is a road for which vehicle s must pay a fee to use called a wikt toll toll Toll roads are there in part to control the number of people using it They may be built to allow some users to travel faster and avoid traffic congestion speeding up traffic In the past tolls were collected to give income to government s or landlord landowners Today tolls can be considered a form of tax which pays for the cost of construction road construction and wikt maintenance maintenance that are not charged on those who do not use that road

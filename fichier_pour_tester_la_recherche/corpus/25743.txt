 Lomonosov is a town in Russia near St Petersburg It used to be called Oranienbaum This was a German language German name which means Orange tree It used to have greenhouse s where plants from faraway countries were grown Oranienbaum was famous for making coloured glass In the town changed its name to the Russian name Lomonosov The old palace which dates from the early th century still stands Unlike the other famous palaces around St Petersburg this one was not captured by the Germans during the Second World War The famous composer Igor Stravinsky was born in Oranienbaum

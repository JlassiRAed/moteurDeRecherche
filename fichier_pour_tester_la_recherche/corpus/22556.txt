The Field Museum of Natural History is in Chicago Illinois United States U S It is on Lake Shore Drive next to Lake Michigan The museum has more than million exhibits The museum was founded in and had used old exhibitions that were shown from the World s Columbian Exposition of including exhibits about trains The Field Museum has a large collection of dinosaur skeletons in the Evolving Planet exhibit The human cultural anthropology exhibits have things from ancient Egypt the Pacific Northwest the Pacific Islands and Tibet The taxidermy collection has many large animals including two African elephants and the Lions of Tsavo featured in the movie The Ghost and the Darkness The Ancient Americas exhibit has Native American artifacts The museum also has Sue Sue the largest and most complete Tyrannosaurus skeleton currently known in the world

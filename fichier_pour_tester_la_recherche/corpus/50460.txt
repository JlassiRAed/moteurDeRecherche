The Missouri Valley Conference is a college sports conference in the United States that sponsors many sports but has not played American football football since the season The MVC has its roots in the Missouri Valley Intercollegiate Athletic Association a league formed in by several schools in the Midwestern United States The MVIAA split into two leagues in One league made up mostly of large schools kept the MVIAA name and years later became the Big Eight Conference The smaller schools formed the MVC Both groups claimed a founding date and the same history up to the split However the Big Eight merged in with several schools from the collapsing Southwest Conference to form today s Big Conference which does not claim any of the Big Eight s history Because of this only the MVC now has a claim to any of the MVIAA s history

 Pitfall is a platform game platforming video game made by Activision for Atari Atari Atari bit ColecoVision Commodore Intellivision and the Sega SG The game was a best seller for the Atari with over million copies sold In this game the player has to move a character who is known as Harry through a maze like jungle where he has to attempt to recover treasures in a minute time period The player has to avoid many hazards including pits quicksand rolling logs fire rattlesnake s scorpion s and crocodile s

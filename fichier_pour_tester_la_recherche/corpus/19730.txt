 Oakwell is a sports development in Barnsley South Yorkshire England It is used mostly by Barnsley F C Barnsley Football Club for playing their home games It is also used by their reserve team While the name Oakwell is usually used for the main stadium it also includes several neighbouring venues which form the facilities of the Barnsley F C academy This includes an indoor training pitch a smaller stadium with seating on the south and west sides for around spectators and several training pitches used by the different Barnsley FC squads Until the stadium and the large amount of land that is around it was owned by Barnsley Football Club However after falling into administration in the council purchased the main Oakwell Stadium This allowed the club to pay its creditors and remain participants in The Football League

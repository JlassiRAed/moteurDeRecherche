 Storms of My Grandchildren is the first book written by climate scientist James Hansen Hansen says that the burning of fossil fuel s is climate change changing our climate and that the earth is in danger Millions of species and humanity itself are threatened Hansen states that we immediately need to reduce atmospheric carbon dioxide to at most Parts per notation ppm in order to avoid disasters for coming generations He says that we need to reduce coal plant emissions and improve forestry and agricultural practices Storms of My Grandchildren has been reviewed in Nature Science James Hansen is director of the NASA Goddard Institute for Space Studies and is often called the father of global warming

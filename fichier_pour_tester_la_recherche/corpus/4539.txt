A pyre also known as a funeral pyre is a structure used for burning a body The word pyre comes from the Greek language Greek word for fire Pyres are built outdoors They are usually made from wood The body is placed on top of the pyre or under it Then the pyre is set on fire Pyres are still used for funeral s in some parts of the world They are also used for other types of ceremony ceremonies In those ceremonies there is a big fire but a body is not burned Historically pyres have also been used to kill people by burning them alive

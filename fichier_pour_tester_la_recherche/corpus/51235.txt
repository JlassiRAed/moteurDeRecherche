 F flat major is a major scale based on F flat Its key signature has eight flats Its relative key relative minor is D flat minor and its parallel key parallel minor is F flat minor usually replaced by E minor Part of Richard Strauss Metamorphosen uses F flat major which one commentator has called a bitter enharmonic parody of the earlier manifestations of E major in the piece To make reading and writing music easier F flat major is usually written as its enharmonic equivalent of E major

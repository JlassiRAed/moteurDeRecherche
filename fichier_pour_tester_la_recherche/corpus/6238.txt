The grass snake is a non venom venomous snake It is often found near water and feeds almost exclusively on amphibians The grass snake is widely distributed in mainland Europe ranging from mid Scandinavia to southern Italy It is also found in Middle East and northwestern Africa This species is one of only three snakes to occur in Great Britain the others being the adder and the smooth snake It is distributed throughout lowland areas of England and Wales It is almost absent from Scotland and is not found in Ireland which has no native snakes

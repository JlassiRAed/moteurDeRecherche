 Bernicia was a kingdom in England Northern England It was an Anglo Saxon kingdom established by Angles Anglian settlers in the th century Bernicia occupied about the same area as the modern English counties of Northumberland and County Durham Durham and the former Scottish counties of Berwickshire and East Lothian Bernicia was one of two Anglo Saxon kingdoms located north of the River Humber The other was Deira and in the early th century they combined to become the kingdom of Northumbria

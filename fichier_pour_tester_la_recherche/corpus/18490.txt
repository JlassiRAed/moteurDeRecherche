 Ocimum tenuiflorum is an plant in the family Lamiaceae It is also called Holy Basil Holy Basil as the name suggest has a holy meaning for people of India especially towards North and South India It is usually placed or grown in every household in India and carries religious meanings In bigger households the holy basil or tulsi plant is placed in the centre of the house and prayers are offered in the morning time People usually light an incense stick or agarbatti and pour water on the plant during the prayer

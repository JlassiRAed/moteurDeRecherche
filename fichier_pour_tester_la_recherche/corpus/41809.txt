 Freaks is a MGM horror movie about circus sideshow performers It was directed and produced by Tod Browning The cast is mostly actual traveling carnival performers The movie was based on Tod Robbins story Spurs Director Browning cast real people with deformities as the sideshow freaks rather than using costumes and makeup The movie was not popular In Freaks was selected for preservation in the United States National Film Registry as being culturally historically or aesthetically significant

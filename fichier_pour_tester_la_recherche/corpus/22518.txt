 Cetology is a type of science It is the study of the cetacean s which are whale s dolphin s and porpoise s Cetology is the branch of marine mammal science that studies the approximately eighty species of whales dolphins and porpoise in the scientific order Cetacea The term was coined in the mid th century from the Greek cetus Cetologists or those who practice cetology seek to understand and explain cetacean evolution distribution morphology behavior community dynamics and other topics

 The Epping Ongar Railway is a Heritage railway preserved railway It runs along the last section of the old Great Eastern Railway and London Underground Central line branch line The line runs between Epping tube station Epping and Ongar railway station Ongar There is also an wikt intermediate intermediate stop at North Weald tube station North Weald The line was reopened in late after years of being closed It runs a Sunday and Bank holiday Bank Holiday service using a British Rail Class Class diesel multiple unit The service runs between Ongar and Coopersale Halt railway station Coopersale The service is provided by wikt volunteer volunteer s who take care for the line as well as run the trains The land and wikt infrastructure infrastructure are owned by Epping Ongar Railway Ltd

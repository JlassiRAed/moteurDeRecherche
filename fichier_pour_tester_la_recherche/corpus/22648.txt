 Wade Giles sometimes abbreviated Wade is a Romanization system for the Chinese language It is based on the form of Mandarin language Mandarin used in Beijing The system was produced by Thomas Francis Wade Thomas Wade in the mid th century It was fully developed in Herbert Giles s Chinese language Chinese English language English dictionary Wade Giles was the main system of transliteration in the English speaking world for much of the th century It is used in several standard reference books It is also used in all books about China published before It replaced the Nanjing based romanization systems that was common until the late th century Wade Giles has been replaced by the pinyin system today It still remains in use in Taiwan

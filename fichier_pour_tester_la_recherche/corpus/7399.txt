The Bakhshali Manuscript is a Mathematics mathematical manuscript written on birch bark It was found in near the village of Bakhshali in what was then the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa North West Frontier Province of British India Most of the manuscript is missing only seventy leaf leaves of birch bark remain No one knows how old it is and different scholar s have come up with different dates Most scholars agree that the manuscript is a copy of a more Ancient history ancient text

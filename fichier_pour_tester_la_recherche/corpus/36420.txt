 Osechi is Japanese cuisine Japanese food made to celebrate the coming of the New Year called Shogatsu It is one of the most important annual events in Japan Osechi food is packed in colorful lacquer boxes called jubako Each dish and ingredient in osechi has a meaning such as good health fertility good harvest happiness long life and so on The kind of osechi foods eaten in Japanese homes varies from area to area It is a Japanese tradition to eat osechi dishes during the New Year s holidays which go from January to January

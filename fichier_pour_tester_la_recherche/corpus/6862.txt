 Murugan also known as Kartikeya of Kumara Kottam in the city of Kanchipuram He was a scholar in Tamil literature and a votary of the Shaiva Siddhanta philosophy Murugan is the son of Shiva and Parvathi who has take birth to kill the asuras The six sites at which Murugan sojourned while leading his armies against Surapadman are Palani Swamimalai Thiruparamkundram Pazhamudirsolai Thiruthani and Thiruchendur which all are in Tamil Nadu

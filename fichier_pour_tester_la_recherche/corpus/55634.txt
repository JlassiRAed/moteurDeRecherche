 Pop icon is a celebrity whose popularity stays the same or even increases as time goes by Most pop icons achieve their status through death typically at a young age The worship of pop icons is due only in part to their actual accomplishments Some of the most notable pop icons are James Dean Michael Jackson John F Kennedy and Marilyn Monroe However even celebrities who died old can become a pop icon like Elvis Presley Death nearly always increases the fame of a pop icon Many people respect the dead Others feel a close personal connection with the dead icon and want to remember the celebrity Death also increases any value of all items that belonged to the pop icon

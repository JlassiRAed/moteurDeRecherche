 Macroscopic means physics physical objects that are measurement measurable and can be seen by the naked eye When one uses macroscopic for abstract objects one thinks of the world as we see it without any help Lengths scales are called macroscopic if they fall in the range of more or less than millimeter mm or up to kilometer km One may use the term macroscopic also for a larger view namely a view only available from a large perspective A macroscopic position could be considered the big picture

A character actor is an actor who plays a particular kind of character in a Play play movie television program opera or ballet The character actor is not the main part He is not the love r or hero He has a character which helps to support the main characters and therefore the story Character roles may be roles such as the faithful servant the maid the villain the mother in law the priest etc They may or may not be comic characters For example in The Wizard of Oz the main character is Dorothy The Scarecrow the Lion and the Tin Man are character roles Some actors or singers prefer character roles to main roles It may be because this kind or role suits their personality or their singing voice Some people nearly always play the same kind of character role We say that people like that are typecast Some actors are happy to remain typecast Others may wish to broaden their repertoire and take on different roles

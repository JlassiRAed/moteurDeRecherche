 Fragile Future is the third album by Hawthorne Heights It was released in It was their first album without Casey Calvert because he died in late Casey was the backing vocalist who used death growl s in the songs This album has no death growls or harsh vocals at all because the band decided not to replace Casey with a new person who could do death growls Hawthorne Heights did not have a lot of success on the singles but it was seen as a new start for the band While much of the album was inspired by things that had been happening to the band it never says the words Casey or Victory Records The album art includes a picture of Casey Calvert near a lamppost

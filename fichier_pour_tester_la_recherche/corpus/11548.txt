 Kingdom Hearts II is a video game from the Kingdom Hearts series for the PlayStation It has the Fictional character characters of the Final Fantasy series and Walt Disney characters for example Donald Duck Goofy and Mickey Mouse Kingdom Hearts II starts one year after the end of Kingdom Hearts Chain of Memories and continues the story from Kingdom Hearts Kingdom Hearts and Chain of Memories for PlayStation and Game Boy Advance It was released in Japan on December in North America on March and in Europe on September In Kingdom Hearts II the player controls a year old boy named Sora Sora goes through different worlds fighting different enemies like Heartless and Nobodies Occasionally there will be enemies you have to defeat that are based on the world you are in such as the cursed pirate s in Pirates of the Caribbean

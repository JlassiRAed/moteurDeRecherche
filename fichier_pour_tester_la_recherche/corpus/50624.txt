 Cumulus clouds are white cloud s that are dense and large They are known to look like cauliflower Mostly you can see easily that cumulus clouds have flat bottoms but the tops are always changing shape Seeing a cumulus cloud means that the air is well mixed by up and down vertical draft s When a cumulus cloud grows into a thunderstorm the cloud is called a cumulonimbus cloud This is a sign that the air is rising to the stratosphere

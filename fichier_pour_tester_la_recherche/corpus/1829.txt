 The Hindu Times is the first single off English Rock music rock band Oasis Oasis fifth studio album Heathen Chemistry The song was released on July The Hindu Times was the band s sixth number one single on the UK Singles Chart It stayed on the top for one week before it was overtaken by the Sugababes cover of Freak Like Me In NME listed the song as one of the greatest indie anthems of the s In October NME ranked the song on their list of Best Tracks of the Past Years

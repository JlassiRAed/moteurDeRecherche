 Blackpool Illuminations is an annual Lights Festival It was founded in and first switched on September that year It is held each autumn in the England English seaside resort of Blackpool on the The Fylde Fylde Coast in Lancashire Also known locally as The Lights or The Illuminations they run each year for sixty six days from late August until early November at a time when most other English seaside resorts seasons are coming to an end Dubbed as The greatest free light show on earth they are miles long and use over one million bulbs

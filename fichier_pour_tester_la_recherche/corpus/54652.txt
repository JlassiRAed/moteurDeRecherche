 Rollin is the third single off United States American nu metal band Limp Bizkit s third studio album Chocolate Starfish and the Hot Dog Flavored Water The song was released on October There was a remix version of the song titled Rollin Redman and Method Man It was also produced by Swizz Beatz The remix was ranked on VH s list of Most Awesomely Bad Songs The song was used as the theme song for Americans American professional wrestling professional wrestler The Undertaker from December to May Limp Bizkit performed the song live during his entrance for WrestleMania XIX

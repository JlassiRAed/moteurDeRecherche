 Peterhof meaning Peter s Court is a town in Saint Petersburg on the southern shore of the Gulf of Finland It has a population of about It hosts one of two campuses of Saint Petersburg State University A series of palaces and gardens laid out on the orders of Peter I of Russia Peter the Great and sometimes called the Russian Palace of Versailles Versailles is also there The palace ensemble along with the city center is a World Heritage Site UNESCO World Heritage Site In the time of Peter the Great the sea floor just north of the Petergof site and to the east toward St Petersburg was too shallow for commercial ships or warships But to the west of Petergof the sea floor dropped off to be deep enough for sea vessels Accordingly when Peter the Great decided to build St Petersburg at the eastern end of the Gulf of Finland he built the commercial harbor for St Petersburg and the Kronstadt fort across the kilometers of shallow sea to supply and defend his Navy

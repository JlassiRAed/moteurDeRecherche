The somatosensory system is a sensory system that detects experiences labelled as touch or pressure temperature senses and facial expression Visceral senses have to do with sensory information from within the body such as stomach aches Touch may be considered one of five human sense s however when a person touches something or somebody this gives rise to various touch feeling s the perception of pressure relative temperature and sometimes pain Thus the term touch is actually the combined term for several senses In medicine the colloquial term touch is usually replaced with somatic senses to better reflect the variety of mechanisms involved

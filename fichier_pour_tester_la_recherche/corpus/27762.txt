 Nephthys was a goddess with wide power and capacity and she was often referred to as The Excellent Goddess She could also be dangerous able to kill the enemies of the king with her fiery breath In this capacity she was considered a protector of the king Nephthys was not only a goddess of death decay and darkness but also a magician with great healing powers  Nephthys has a central role in the popular myths of Osiris it is her magical powers that helps to resurrect his body as well as to protect and nurture Horus while he is a child She is in some contexts presented as a protector of the dead in other as protecting the baboon headed Hapi one of the canopic jars In the Late Period she became linked with the goddess  Anuket

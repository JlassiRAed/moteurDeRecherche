 Eleanor the Fair Maid of Brittany was a member of the nobility of Kingdom of England England She was the eldest daughter of Geoffrey Plantagenet and Constance Duchess of Brittany She was seen as a potential threat to the throne by her uncle John of England and cousin Henry III of England She was put in prison from and so became the longest imprisoned member of an English royal family Although still treated honorably as a princess she was never released or permitted to marry

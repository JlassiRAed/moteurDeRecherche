 Phineas and Ferb is an American animation animated television program produced by The Walt Disney animation studio Disney It was first on TV on February It has been nominated for an Emmy award It airs on the Disney Channel and related channels The program is about two brothers from a blended family with a pet platypus who do impossible plans In every episode Phineas and Ferb create big machines in their backyard like a roller coaster or a huge waterslide Their sister Candace tries to show her mother the machines But always when the mother is in the backyard the machines have disappeared Almost all episodes have a song which the characters sing The creators of the show are Dan Povenmire and Jeff Swampy Marsh They also worked together on the Nickelodeon Nickelodeon series Rocko s Modern Life

 The Sutlej River is a Tributary branches of the Indus River It is the longest of the five major rivers that flow through the region of Punjab region Punjab in northern India and Pakistan It is north of the Vindhya Range south of the Hindu Kush and east of the Central Makran range in Pakistan It is the easternmost branch of the Indus River The source of the river is at Lake Rakshastal in Tibet near Mount Kailas It flows generally west and southwest through the region of Punjab The region to its south and east is dry and is known as the Thar Desert

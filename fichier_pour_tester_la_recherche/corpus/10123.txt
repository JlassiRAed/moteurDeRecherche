The Temple in Jerusalem or Holy Temple in the old city of Jerusalem According to classical Judaism Jewish belief it was the footstool of God s presence in the physical world It was the place where Jews brought all the offering s described in the Torah The First Temple also called Solomon s Temple was built by King Solomon in seven years during the th century BCE and has been dated astronomically to BCE This first Temple was destroyed by the Babylonia ns in BCE A Second Temple new temple was built from BCE to BCE That was destroyed by the Roman Empire Romans in CE All of the outer walls still stand although the Temple itself has long since been destroyed The Islam ic Dome of the Rock has stood on the site of the Temple since the late th Century CE The al Aqsa Mosque from roughly the same period also stands on the Temple courtyard

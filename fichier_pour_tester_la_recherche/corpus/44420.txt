 A sow bug or woodlouse is a small land arthropod They are often confused with centipede s millipedes or insects but are actually crustacean s They have fourteen legs and breathe through gill s Because of the gills woodlice need water in the air around them They mostly eat dead plant s and are considered helpful because they act as garbage collectors Some species can roll up into a ball when in danger In such position their hard shell faces out protecting the softer parts of the body Sow bugs do not look like shrimp s or crab s although they are closely related

 Pikmin is a real time strategy video game that was released by Nintendo for the Wii U game console It is the sequel to Pikmin and the third game in the Pikmin series It was released in Japan on July in Europe on July and in North America on August Pikmin has the same gameplay as the last Pikmin games Players control plant like creatures called Pikmin The player uses them to pick up object reach areas and fight enemies There are three characters that players can play as In addition to the five types of Pikmin from Pikmin there is two new types of Pikmin The Rock Pikmin can destroy tough barriers while the Flying Pink Winged Pikmin can attack airborne enemies and carry items in the air The player is able to control the game with the Wii U GamePad or the Wii Remote and Nunchuk combination The Wii U GamePad gives the player a map of the game environment which is visible on its touch screen

The Fishburn was a store ship in the First Fleet The First Fleet carried the convict s and soldier s to Australia to start a penal colony The ships left England in May and arrived in Australia in January This was the start of Europe an settlement of Australia The Fishburn was a ship of tons under the command of Master Robert Brown The Fishburn and the Golden Grove left Port Jackson in November to return to England She had to rest at the Falkland Islands for several days as several sailors were sick She was returned to her owners at Deptford on May No other information has been found about the ship afterwards

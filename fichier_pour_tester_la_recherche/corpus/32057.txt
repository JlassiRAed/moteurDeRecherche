 Chitty Chitty Bang Bang is a British musical film loosely based on Ian Fleming s novel Chitty Chitty Bang Bang Chitty Chitty Bang Bang The Magical Car The movie s script is by Roald Dahl and Ken Hughes The songs are by the Sherman Brothers The song Chitty Chitty Bang Bang was nominated for an Academy Award The film stars Dick Van Dyke as Caractacus Potts Sally Ann Howes as Truly Scrumptious Lionel Jeffries as Grandpa Potts James Robertson Justice as Lord Scrumptious and Robert Helpmann as the Childcatcher The film was directed by Ken Hughes It was produced by Albert R Broccoli John Stears supervised the special effects Irwin Kostal supervised and conducted the music The musical numbers were staged by Marc Breaux and Dee Dee Wood

 The Australian Electoral Commission or the AEC is the Australian federal government agency in charge of federal elections and referendum s State and local government elections are run by the Electoral Commission in each state and territory The AEC also draws the boundaries for seats and makes regular changes to keep all seats roughly equal in population numbers It organizes Commonwealth electoral roll which is a list of people who can vote in elections The electoral roll is used by the state and territory Electoral Commissions to for their elections The AEC also plays a leading role in electoral education and industrial voting

 Bedtime for Democracy is the fourth studio album by Americans American hardcore punk band Dead Kennedys Bedtime for Democracy was released in November The name of the album is a reference to the comedy film Bedtime for Bonzo which starred Ronald Reagan The name of the album may also reflect the band s bitterness from the aftermath of the both financially and emotionally draining trial that they were being subjected to at the time because of the controversial poster that was included with their previous album Frankenchrist When the band started recording Bedtime for Democracy they had already played what would be their last live concert in February of in the San Francisco Bay Area with lead singer Jello Biafra and they announced the band s breakup immediately after the record s release

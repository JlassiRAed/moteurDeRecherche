 Holiday Inn is a brand of hotel s Holiday Inn is one of the world s most recognized hotel brands It is part of the InterContinental Hotels Group Kemmons Wilson had the idea for the business after a family road trip to Washington D C During the trip he was disappointed by the quality of roadside motels at that time Mr Wilson partnered with Wallace E Johnson to build the first four motels on roads entering Memphis Tennessee Eddie Bluestein was the architect who designed these first hotels He named the first one Holiday Inn after a Bing Crosby movie as a joke

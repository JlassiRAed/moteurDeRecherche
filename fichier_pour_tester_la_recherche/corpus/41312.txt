 Mobile genetic elements are a type of DNA that can move around within the genome They include Transposon s Transposon Types Retrotransposons Transposon Types DNA transposons Integron s Insertion sequence s Plasmid s Bacteriophage elements like Mu phage Mu which integrates randomly into the genome Intron Group II intron sThe total of all mobile genetic elements in a genome may be referred to as the mobilome Barbara McClintock was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for her discovery of mobile genetic elements

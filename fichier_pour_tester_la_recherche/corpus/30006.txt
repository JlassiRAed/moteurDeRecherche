 Patriotism means loyalty of person to his her own nation or the leaders of nation A patriot is a person who is on the side of his her own nation or its leaders Patriotism is different from nationalism Nationalist thinks that every ethnic group should have its own nation so nations are to serve the people In other words in nationalism the nation is just a tool to have freedom for an ethnic group while in patriotism the nation itself is the highest value A patriot may also be loyal to imperialism imperialist or colonialism colonialist nations while nationalism is opposed to imperialism or colonialism The English term patriot is first known from the Elizabethan era via Middle French from Late Latin fatherland The abstract noun patriotism appears in the early th century

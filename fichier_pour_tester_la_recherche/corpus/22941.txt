 Timothy J McCarthy is an Americans American police officer He is the current police chief of Orland Park Illinois and a former member of the United States Secret Service During the Reagan assassination attempt he turned into the line of fire shielding Ronald Reagan from one of John Hinckley Jr s caliber bullets Surgeons at George Washington University Hospital successfully removed the round from McCarthy s abdomen and he fully recovered McCarthy is one of only four Secret Service agents to have taken a bullet for the President He received the NCAA Award of Valor in in recognition of his bravery

 Servius Sulpicius Galba was a Roman Emperor for seven months from AD to Galba was an governor of Hispania Tarraconensis which was the province near the Pyrenees He was the first emperor of Year of the Four Emperors He was born near Terracina On the left as you travel towards Fundi in the words of Suetonius Galba was adopted by Livia Ocellina and took her family name until becoming emperor

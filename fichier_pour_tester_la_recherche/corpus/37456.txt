 Lion s mane jellyfish also known as Hair Jelly and Sea Blubber are a type of jellyfish which are mostly found in north Atlantic and north Pacific waters The stings of these jellyfish are painful but not fatal The species is found near the coast of the United States U S shore line The lion s mane jellyfish can grow up to m width by its pileus and its tentacle s up to m long The largest Lions Mane Jelly recorded was FT but most jellyfish are far smaller usually some cm by pileus width and up to cm by tentacle length It eats mainly plankton and is itself eaten by sunfish sea turtles and some seabirds Like all jellyfish it lives only for one year A group of jellyfish is called a smack

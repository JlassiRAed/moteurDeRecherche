 Monsieur Pierre or simply Pierre was the professional name of Pierre Jean Phillipe Zurcher Margolle Pierre was the main person responsible for introducing the Latin American dances to England He set them up for use in competitions and in social dance The system he and his colleagues developed became the basis for all Latin American competitions held under the World Dance Council After World War I ended in he started on a career as a professional ballroom dancer in London Although he spent the rest of his life resident in London Pierre never gave up his French citizenship

 The bass clarinet is a type of clarinet and is a woodwind instrument woodwind single reed instrument The earliest record of a bass clarinet is called a bass tube invented in Paris in by G Lott It usually has a B flat pitch It plays an octave lower than the normal clarinet This means that when a Bass Clarinet makes a note the note that actually sounds is an octave and a whole tone lower There are some bass clarinets that have an A or C pitch as well as E flat but there are not very many of these

 Nunavut is a territory in Canada It was founded in when many Inuit living in the Northwest Territories wanted to have an independent province and government Its capital is named Iqaluit It is in the arctic north of Canada and has a particularly cold climate for much of the year It has many native people The main form of transportation is the snowmobile For the first week of being a separate unit it was the Province of Nunavut but after a week it was renamed Nunavut Territory A symbol of the territory is the Canadian Eskimo Dog Eskimo Dog

The Anglo Normans were mainly the sons grandsons etc of the Normans living in England Most of the Normans came to England at the time of the Norman conquest of England Norman conquest in A few Normans had settled in England prior to the conquest After the conquest the Normans although few in number controlled most of England They often married English wives and their children became Anglo Norman as a result of being of two cultures It was during the reign of Henry I of England the term Anglo Norman was first used in the Hyde Chronicle

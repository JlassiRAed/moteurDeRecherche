The Adephaga is a biological classification suborder of beetles There are more than recorded species in ten family order Coleoptera The beetles of this suborder include ground beetle s tiger beetle s diving beetle s and whirligig beetle s Most of the species belong to the family of carabids or ground beetles The Adephaga is two groups with a common origin They are the Hydradephaga and the Geadephaga ground beetles They are mostly predatory The first fossils of both groups are found in the Triassic period The ground beetles have formidable chemical defences They have special pygidial gland s which squirt out hot burning chemicals Bombardier beetle s are famous for squirting their nasty chemicals at attackers

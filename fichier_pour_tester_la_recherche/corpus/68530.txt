 The Most Eminent Order of the Indian Empire is an order of chivalry founded by Queen Victoria of the United Kingdom Victoria in The Order includes members of three classes Knight Grand Commander Knight Commander Companion No appointments have been made since the year of Indian independence and the Partition of India In the last surviving knight Meghrajji III of Dhrangadhra Halvad The Maharaja of Dhrangadhra died and the order became dormant The motto of the Order is a reference to Queen Victoria the first Emperor of India Empress of India The Order is the junior British order of chivalry associated with the British Raj Empire of India the senior one is Order of the Star of India The Most Exalted Order of the Star of India

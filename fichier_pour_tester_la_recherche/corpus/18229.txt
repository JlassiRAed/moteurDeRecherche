The University of London is a federal mega university made up of affiliates separate university institutions and research institutes Its headquarters Senate House is in Malet Street in the Bloomsbury area of London Borough of Camden Camden This is near University College London and the British Museum The University of London is the largest university in the UK by number of full time students with campus based students and over in the University of London International Programmes The university is not responsible for the teaching the research or the individual students and staff of the constituent colleges They are the responsibility of the colleges The university is an administrative body responsible for standards degree examinations and certification The university was first established by a Royal Charter in which brought together in federation London University Graduates of the University of London may use the post nominal letters Lond or Londin after their degree abbreviations The university s biggest colleges are Birkbeck University of London Birkbeck Goldsmiths University of London Goldsmiths King s College London the London Business School Queen Mary University of London Queen Mary Royal Holloway University of London Royal Holloway SOAS University of London SOAS London School of Economics LSE and University College London UCL

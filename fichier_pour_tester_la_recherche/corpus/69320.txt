Lithang Tulku Tenzin Delek Rinpoche or Tenzing Deleg was a Tibetan Buddhism Buddhist leader from Garze Sichuan He was born in Lithang Tibet He was arrested on April during a raid on Jamyang Choekhorling in Garze Sichuan China He was accused of being involved in a bomb attack on April on the central square of Sichuan s provincial capital Chengdu He was sentenced to life imprisonment He died in prison in Chengdu

 Cultural assimilation or assimilation for short is an intense process of consistent wikt integration integration when members of an ethno cultural group typically immigrant s or other wikt minority minority groups are absorbed into an established generally larger community This means a loss of all or many characteristics which make the newcomers different A region or society where assimilation is occurring is sometimes referred to as a melting pot From a historical perspective Assimilation is the process by which an individual or minority group loses its original culture when absorbed into another culture in the context of colonialism a policy of total integration of colonies into the colonizing country It is often remembered as a painful or coercive event

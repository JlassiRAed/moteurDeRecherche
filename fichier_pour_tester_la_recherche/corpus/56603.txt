 Perranporth is a small seaside resort on the north coast of Cornwall United Kingdom It is six miles northwest of Truro and is the largest settlement in the parish of Perranzabuloe The village s name is Cornish language Cornish for Saint Piran s cove It is believed that Saint Piran founded a church at Perranzabuloe near Perranporth in the seventh century Buried under sand for many centuries it was unearthed early in the th century but again abandoned in the sands in the s

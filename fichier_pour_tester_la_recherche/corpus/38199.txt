 Kerckhoffs s principle is an idea in cryptography was stated by Dutch cryptographer Auguste Kerckhoffs in the th century It is A cryptosystem should be secure even if everything about the system except the key key is public knowledge Kerckhoffs s principle was more briefly stated by American mathematician Claude Shannon as the enemy knows the system that is one ought to design systems under the assumption that the enemy will immediately gain full familiarity with them In that form it is called Shannon s maxim It is opposite to the idea of security through obscurity

 The History of Little Goody Two Shoes is a children s book It was published in London in by John Newbery The story is about a poor orphan girl who has only one shoe A rich man gives her a pair of shoes She is then known as Goody Two Shoes She becomes a teacher and marries a rich widower The name Goody Two Shoes has come to mean anyone who is virtuous It is not known who wrote the book Some say Oliver Goldsmith some say John Newbery himself

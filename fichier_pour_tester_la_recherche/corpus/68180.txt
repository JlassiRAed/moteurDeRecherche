 Stockwell tube station is a London Underground station in Stockwell in the London Borough of Lambeth It is situated between Clapham North tube station Clapham North and Oval tube station Oval on the Northern line and between Vauxhall station Vauxhall and Brixton tube station Brixton on the Victoria Line It is also the only station on the Morden tube station Morden branch of the Northern line which has an interchange with another London Underground Underground line It is in Travelcard Zone Stockwell station is served by bus routes P N and N

 Ethernet is a way of connecting computers together in a local area network or LAN It has been the most widely used method of linking computers together in LANs since the s The basic idea of its design is that multiple computers have access to it and can send data at any time This is comparatively easy to engineer If two computers send data at the same time a collision will occur When this happens the data sent is not usable In general both computers will stop sending and wait a random amount of time before they try again A special protocol was developed to deal with such problems It is called Carrier sense multiple access with collision detection or CSMA CD

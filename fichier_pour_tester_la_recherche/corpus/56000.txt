 Tear down this wall was the challenge made by United States President Ronald Reagan to Soviet Union leader Mikhail Gorbachev to destroy the Berlin Wall The speech was made at the Brandenburg Gate near the Berlin Wall on June It was made to honor the th anniversary of Berlin Reagan challenged Gorbachev who was then the General Secretary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to tear the wall down as a symbol of Gorbachev s desire to increase freedom between Eastern and Western Germany The speech is thought to be one of the greatest speeches by Reagan or by any other president

 Love Actually is a United Kingdom British romantic comedy drama movie The large majority of it is set in London with parts of it set in Marseille France and Wisconsin United States It features several interlinked stories of love It was written and directed by Richard Curtis It stars Hugh Grant Liam Neeson Colin Firth Laura Linney Emma Thompson Alan Rickman Keira Knightley Martine McCutcheon Bill Nighy and Rowan Atkinson

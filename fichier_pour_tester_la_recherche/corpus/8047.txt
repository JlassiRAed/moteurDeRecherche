 Aldgate tube station is a London Underground station at Aldgate in the City of London The station is on the Circle line Circle Line between Tower Hill tube station Tower Hill and Liverpool Street station Liverpool Street It is also the eastern terminus of the Metropolitan line It is in Travelcard Zone and its ticket office is part time only Platforms and at Aldgate are two of the only three platforms on the network to be served exclusively by the Circle line All other Circle Line platforms are shared by the District line District Metropolitan line Metropolitan and or Hammersmith City line Hammersmith City Lines

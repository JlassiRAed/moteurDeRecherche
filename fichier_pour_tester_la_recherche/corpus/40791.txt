 Law Order LA was an United States American television police procedural police drama It was a spin off of Law Order being the fourth spin off It ran on NBC from October to July It is the only American Law Order show to be set outside of New York City being set in Los Angeles Law Order LA followed the same format as Law Order The first half of the show showed the crime the police looking for the suspect and the suspect being arrested The show was adapted to fit the Los Angeles criminal justice system

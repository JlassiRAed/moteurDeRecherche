 British Midland Airways Limited known as BMI close to East Midlands Airport The airline flew to places in Europe the Middle East Africa and Central Asia from its base at London Heathrow Airport where it held of all take off and landing slots and runs over flights a week BMI was owned by Lufthansa who announced an agreement in December to sell the airline to the owner of British Airways International Airlines Group The deal does not include Bmibaby and BMI Regional In April it was announced that bmi was to be fully merged into British Airways by the end of October British Midland Airways Limited had a Civil Aviation Authority Civil Aviation Authority Type A Operating Licence which allowed it to carry passengers cargo and mail on aircraft with or more seats and was a star airline by Skytrax

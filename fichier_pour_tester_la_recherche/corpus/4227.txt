The Vaganova Ballet Academy is a ballet school which was founded in by Empress Anna Ioannovna   in Saint Petersburg Russia The original name of the academy was the Imperial Ballet School In the Soviet Union Soviet regime the school was rebuilt as the Leningrad State Choreographic Institute After that in the name of the school became the Vaganova Ballet Academy by Agrippina Vaganova Every year over children audition and only approximately children are accepted It has eight year curriculum and dancers complete the whole of the curriculum Dancers who take the eight year course may join ballet companies all over the world

 Bangarang is the fifth Extended play EP by Americans American electronic music producer Skrillex Bangarang was released on December Singles from the EP are Breakn a Sweat which features the American rock band The Doors and Bangarang which features American rapper Sirah The song Breakn a Sweat was written for the documentary film Re Generation The iTunes version of the EP features an Orchestral Suite performed by Varien The EP received generally mixed to positive reviews from music critics The EP reached on the Billboard as well as on the Top Dance Electronic Albums chart

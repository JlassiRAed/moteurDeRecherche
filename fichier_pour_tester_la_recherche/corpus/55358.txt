A morpheme is the smallest Linguistics linguistic part of a word that can have a meaning such as un break and able in the word unbreakable There are types of morpheme Free morpheme a morpheme which can be joined with other morphemes Bound morpheme a morpheme which can only be used when joined to other morphemes Derivational morpheme a morpheme which can be derived Inflectional morpheme a morpheme which can change a word s tense number etc Allomorphs different types of the same morpheme

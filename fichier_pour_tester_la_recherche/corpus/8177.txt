A population pyramid is a graph It has two back to back bars These bars show the population in groups of ages with woman in one side and man in the other side The pyramids usually show the development of a country When you look a pyramid you can see the life condition s of the country There are three types of population pyramids Stationary pyramid Expansives pyramid Constrictive pyramidIn this type of graph a wide base means a very high birth rate and a narrow base means a high death rate

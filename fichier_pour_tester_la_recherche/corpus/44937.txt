Alcyonaria Octocorallia is a subclass of the Anthozoa in the phylum Cnidaria There are over different species These animals have an eight fold symmetry Most are colony colonial sessile polyp s Octocorallia look like the stony coral s Stony corals have a six fold symmetry and are classified in the group Hexacorallia Unlike the stony corals most Octocoralllia lack a lasting skeleton For this reason they do not build coral reef s An older name for this group is Alcyonaria Many species form symbiosis symbioses with unicellular algae Octocorallia have many enemies For this reason many are toxic or they taste bad Many species of the Octocorallia can reproduce both sexual reproduction sexually and asexual reproduction asexually It is common to find a colony of Octocorallia where most polyps have the same sex

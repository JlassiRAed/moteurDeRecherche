 The ukulele is a musical instrument with four strings It is also called the uke for short Ukuleles are part of the guitar family of instruments The strings are usually tuned G C E A or A D F B Ukuleles normally have nylon strings or gut strings Ukuleles commonly come in four sizes soprano concert tenor and baritone Sopranino and bass ukuleles also exist Sopraninos are tuned to standard tuning however baritone ukuleles are tuned to D G B E The ukulele was invented in the th Century in Hawaii where people got the idea from small guitar like instruments known as cavaquinho s brought to the island by Portugal Portuguese sailors

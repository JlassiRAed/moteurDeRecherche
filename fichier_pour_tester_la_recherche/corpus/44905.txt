 Neoptera is a classification group that has almost all the wing ed insect s mostly those that can flex their wings over their abdomen s This is in contrast with the more basal order s of winged insects which are not able to flex their wings in this way The Neoptera can be contrasted with the Palaeoptera the ancient winged insects which include the Odonata the Ephemeroptera and extinct groups such as the Palaeodictyoptera

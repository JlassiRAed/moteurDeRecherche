 Kobayr is a th century Armenian Apostolic Church monastery It is located in the village Kober within Lori marz Armenia Kobayr was built on a shelf of a gorge by the prince s of the junior Bagratuni branch Kyurikids in The monastery was later acquired by the Zakarid s and converted into a Chalcedonian monastery Many of the writings in the monastery are written in Georgia Georgian The monastery is being fixed and paid for by the government of Armenia with the help of the government of Italy The ruins of the main church in the monastery contain fresco es of Jesus Christ and the twelve apostles as well as the Church fathers and other Christian figures

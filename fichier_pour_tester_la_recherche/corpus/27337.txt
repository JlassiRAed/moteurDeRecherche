 Prince Philip of Naples and Sicily Duke of Calabria Infante of Spain At his birth he was the eldest son of Charles III of Spain King Carlo of Naples and Sicily and was the heir to the throne but never succeeded His younger brothers Charles IV of Spain and Ferdinand I of the Two Sicilies replaced him in the succession When his father became King of Spain in Philip remained in Naples where he lived until his death from smallpox at the age of thirty He was given the title of Duke of Calabria as heir to the throne

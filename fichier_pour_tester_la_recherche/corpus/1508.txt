 Tropical Storm Cristobal was the third tropical storm of the Atlantic hurricane season It began on July as a weather system away from the Southeast U S Coast It became a tropical storm later that day Cristobal stayed away from land and reached its highest strength as a strong tropical storm on July It moved to the northeast next to the U S East Coast and died out on July near Nova Scotia Because it was a weak storm and never moved onto land Cristobal s damages were mostly moderate rainfall The storm dropped in of rain in Wilmington North Carolina where minor flooding happened Also the remnants helped produce rainfall on Nova Scotia which caused some flooding on roads and in basements

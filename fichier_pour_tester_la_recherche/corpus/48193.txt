 Cryptoclidus was a plesiosaur an aquatic reptile up to feet long It had curved interlocking teeth a wide skull and large flexible paddle like flippers The fragile head and teeth could not deal with large prey That suggests a diet of small soft bodied animals such as squid and small shoaling fish Cryptoclidus fossils have been found in England France Russia and possibly South America Cryptoclidus was named by Seeley in The type species is C eurymerus category Plesiosaurs

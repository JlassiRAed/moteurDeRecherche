 Six Flags New Orleans also shortened to SFNO is an abandoned theme park in New Orleans Louisiana United States The park opened on May and was first known as Jazzland The name was changed to Six Flags New Orleans on April after Six Flags rented the lease in March The park has been closed since just before Hurricane Katrina struck New Orleans in August It is currently owned by the City of New Orleans After seeing the damage that was caused by Katrina Six Flags attempted to end their year lease with the city in July They succeeded in September The abandoned park is a popular urban exploration spot There have been many different plans announced to redevelop the site such as reviving and expanding the park but it still an abandoned amusement park

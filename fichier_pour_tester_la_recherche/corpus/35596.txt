The UCI Oceania Tour is the third season of the UCI Oceania Tour of professional road bicycle racing held in various countries throughout the Oceania region The season begins with the Jayco Herald Sun Tour stage race in October and runs until September The points leader based on the cumulative results of previous races wears the UCI Oceania Tour cycling jersey The final points leader is crowned the UCI Oceania Tour champion Gordon McCauley is the champion of the UCI Oceania Tour

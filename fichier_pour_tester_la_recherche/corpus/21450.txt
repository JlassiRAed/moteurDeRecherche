 Geobox River The Gave de Pau is a river of south western France it is a left tributary of the Adour river It takes its name from the city Pau France Pau through which it flows The word gave is a name given to rivers on the mountains in regions of the western Pyrenees so Gave de Pau means the river of Pau or the river that flows through Pau Many other rivers in the region have the word gave as part of their names

The White Sea is a southern inlet of the Barents Sea It is on the northwest coast of Russia It is divided between Arkhangelsk Oblast Arkhangelsk and Murmansk Oblast s and the Republic of Karelia The major port of Arkhangelsk is on the White Sea It is an important Soviet naval and submarine base The White Sea Baltic Canal connects the White Sea with the Baltic Sea The White Sea is one of four seas named in English after common colour terms The others are the Black Sea the Red Sea and the Yellow Sea

The Territory of Ashmore and Cartier Islands is the collectibe name for two groups of small low lying uninhabited tropical islands The islands are in the Indian Ocean north west of Australia and south of the Indonesian island of Roti The Australian mainland is about to the north The territory includes Ashmore Reef and Cartier Island northeast of Ashmore Reef is not part of the territory because it has no permanently dry land area although large parts of the reef become exposed during low tide

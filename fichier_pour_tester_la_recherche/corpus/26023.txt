 Nadeem Shravan are a music director duo of Bollywood a movie industry in India The duo is made of Nadeem Saifi and Shravan Rathod They started their career in the late s Their first big success came in with the musical film Aashiqui Since then they have composed music for over movies They have won four Filmfare Awards one Star Screen Award and a Zee Cine Award in the category for Best Music Director Some of their biggest hits include Aashiqui

 The Canary also called the Island Canary Atlantic Canary or Common Canary is a small passerine bird It belongs to the genus Serinus in the finch family It is native to the Canary Islands the Azores and Madeira Wild birds are mostly yellow green with brownish streaking on the back The species is common in captivity Different colour varieties have been bred Canaries are domestic birds They are popular pets category Passeriformes

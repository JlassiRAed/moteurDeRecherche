Population Division The Malay Archipelago refers to the archipelago between mainland Southeast Asia Southeastern Asia and Australia It has also been called the East Indies the Indo Australian Archipelago Indonesian Archipelago and other names over time The archipelago is between the Indian Ocean Indian and Pacific Ocean s The group has over island s It is the largest archipelago by area and third by number of islands in the world It includes Indonesia the Philippines Singapore Brunei East Malaysia and East Timor The island of New Guinea or islands of Papua New Guinea are not always included in definitions of the Malay Archipelago The Indonesian Moluccas are included in the archipelago

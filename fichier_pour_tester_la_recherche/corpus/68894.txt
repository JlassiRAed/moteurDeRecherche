 This article will show the current results of the Democratic Party presidential primaries The Democratic Party presidential primary is a process of choosing delegates or people that represent someone to go to the Democratic National Convention People in each state will vote on who they think will be good for the presidency and each candidate gets a certain amount of delegates based on the number of votes they receive The primaries began on February in Iowa The remaining candidates are former first lady and secretary of state and senator Hillary Clinton and senator Bernie Sanders On June the Associated Press reported that senator Hillary Clinton has gotten enough delegates to become the presumptive Democratic nominee

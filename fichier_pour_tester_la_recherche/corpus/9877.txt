 In Your House was a pay per view The original idea was in months where the WWF was not producing a major PPV event they would produce a two hour PPV event for a lower price of United States dollar When World Championship Wrestling started producing three hour PPV s on a monthly basis the WWF was forced to drop the concept The events were numbered numerically Gradually the subtitles became In Your House Current PPV s WWE No Way Out No Way Out WWE Backlash Backlash WWE Judgment Day Judgment Day and WWE Unforgiven Unforgiven all came from the In Your House series

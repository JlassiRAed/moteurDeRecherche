 LittleBigPlanet often shortened to LBP is a puzzle platformer video game In the game you start as a Sackboy in San Francisco California It was made under the title The Next Big Thing by Media Molecule and was published by Sony Computer Entertainment Europe A PlayStation Portable PSP version of the game came out in November and had new levels and content The game received good ratings from critics They liked its presentation including its graphics physics and sound together with its gameplay and the ability to change a lot of things as well as online features More praise was given to its size and future potential based on user created content Small criticism was made for certain parts of the gameplay and level creation tools LittleBigPlanet has won a large amount of awards

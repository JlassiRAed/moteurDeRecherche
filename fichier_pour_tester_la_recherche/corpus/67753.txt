 Priest Holmes is a former American Football runningback He played college football for the Texas Longhorns Holmes went undrafted into the NFL in He was signed by the Baltimore Ravens In he rushed for yards and seven touchdowns after not playing the previous year In Baltimore picked Jamal Lewis and Holmes became a backup The Ravens won Super Bowl XXXV that year Holmes wanting more playing time signed with the Kansas City Chiefs On November he was inducted into the Chiefs Hall of Fame

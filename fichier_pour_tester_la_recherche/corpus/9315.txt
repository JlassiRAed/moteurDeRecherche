The Opel Insignia is a car produced by the German carmaker Opel since its available as a hatchback saloon or an estate Like many Opel models it is sold as a Vauxhall in the United Kingdom In the North American market it has sold as the Buick Regal since the model year as a sedan only Insignia was originally intended to be the second generation Saturn Aura but because GM stopped making the Saturn it was instead offered to Buick and was marketed as fifth generation Buick Regal

 The British Rail Class electrical multiple unit s were originally built for the London Underground London Electric Railway from as their London Underground Standard Stock Standard tube stock They were purchased by British Rail in and transported to the Isle of Wight to work mainline services on the newly electrified Island Line Isle of Wight Ryde to Shanklin line At the time the units had already worked for over forty years on the London Underground but their introduction allowed the final steam locomotives on the line to be withdrawn

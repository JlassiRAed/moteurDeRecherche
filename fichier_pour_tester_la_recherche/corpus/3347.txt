 Muzaffarabad is the capital city of Azad Kashmir It is in Muzaffarabad District on the banks of the Jhelum and Neelum rivers It is very hilly The district is bounded by Khyber Pakhtunkhwa in the west by the Kupwara and Baramulla districts of the Indian Jammu and Kashmir in the east and the Neelum District of Azad Kashmir in the north

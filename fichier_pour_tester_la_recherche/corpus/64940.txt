 Wollemia is a genus of Pinophyta coniferous tree in the family Araucariaceae It is a living fossil recently found in Its fossil record dates back million years and it was thought to be long extinct Wollemia nobilis was discovered in in a temperate rainforest wilderness area of the Wollemi National Park in New South Wales in a remote series of narrow steep sided sandstone gorge s near Lithgow kilometres north west of Sydney In both botanical and popular literature the tree has been called the Wollemi Pine It is not a true pine but is related to Agathis and Araucaria in the family Araucariaceae The Wollemi pine is classified as Critically Endangered It is legally protected in Australia A Recovery Plan has been drawn up outlining strategies for the management of this fragile population the overall objective is to ensure that this species remains viable in the long term image Wollemia nobilis cone jpg thumb left Wollemia nobilis young cone

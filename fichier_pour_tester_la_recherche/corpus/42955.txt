 Whitechapel  is a district in the  East End of London in the  London Borough of Tower Hamlets It is miles east of  Charing Cross  and roughly bounded by  Petticoat Lane  and  London Inner Ring Road Mansell Street Mansell Street  to the west Fashion Street to the north Cambridge Heath Road and Sidney Street to the east and  The Highway  to the south Because the area is close to the  London Docklands  and east of the city it has been a popular place for immigrant s and the working class The area was the centre of the London Jew ish community in the th and early th century It is where the infamous  Whitechapel murders Whitechapel Murders  by  Jack the Ripper  took place in In the latter half of the th century Tower Hamlets became the largest settlement for the British Bangladeshi  community particularly on and around  Whitechapel Road  and  Brick Lane

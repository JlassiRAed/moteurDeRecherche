 Himeros is the god of lust sexual desire or unrequited love He is one of the Erotes Himeros is shown to carry a bow and arrow He created desire and passion in mortals He is often depicted as a young man or child His parents are Ares and Aphrodite and he s brothers with Eros his twin brother His other siblings include Pothos Anteros Hermaphroditus Hedylogos Phobos Deimos and Harmonia

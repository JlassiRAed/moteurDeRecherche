 Pickford s House Museum of Georgian Life and Costume No Friar Gate Derby is a nice Georgian house built by the great architect Joseph Pickford in for his own family Pickford had a number of notable customers One of them was a painter Joseph Wright of Derby Joseph Wright whose works can be found in local Derby Museum and Art Gallery Derby Museum The Pickford s museum that was set up in shows the housing of a late Georgian professional person The ground floor is furnished as it might have been in Pickford s time together with displays of eighteenth and nineteenth century costume It is owned and run by the Derby City Council Pickford s House was intended to showcase his work with the intention of securing new contacts It was also his residence At the rear of the property Pickford had his builder s yard access being by a driveway to the right of the property

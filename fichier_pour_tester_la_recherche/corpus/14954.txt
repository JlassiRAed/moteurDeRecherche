 Viareggio is a city in northern Tuscany in Italy on the coast of the Tyrrhenian Sea With over people it is the main centre of the northern Tuscan Riviera known as Versilia and the second largest city in the Province of Lucca Viareggio is famous for the Passeggiata a promenade and for the oldest Carnival in Europe the Carnevale di Viareggio

 The Munchkins are natives of the fictional Munchkin Country in the Oz books by L Frank Baum They first appeared in the novel The Wonderful Wizard of Oz in which they are described as wearing only blue The movie The Wizard of Oz showed Munchkins as being much shorter than other residents of Oz They are played by either adult dwarfism dwarfs or children dressed in brightly multicolored outfits and their land is called Munchkinland On November the Munchkins were given a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame

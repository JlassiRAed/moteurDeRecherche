The Namtap or Spectacled Dormouse is a species of rodent in the Gliridae family and one of the four Dormouse species which are Endemism endemic to South Africa The Namtap has a bushy tale like a squirrel a fairly short muzzle around its eyes Its cheeks underparts and the upper surfaces of its paws are white Its tail is usually has a white tip It is only found in western South Africa It can usually be found in rocky habitats as well as in trees and outbuildings It is nocturnal and it is good at climbing It mainly eats seeds other plant material and invertebrates

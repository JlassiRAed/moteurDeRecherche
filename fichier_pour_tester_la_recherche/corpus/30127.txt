 Autogenic training is a wikt relax relaxation technique developed by the Germany German psychiatrist Johannes Schultz and first published in The technique involves the daily practice of sessions that last around minutes usually in the morning at lunch time and in the evening During each session the practitioner will repeat a set of visualisations that induce a state of relaxation Each session can be practiced in a position chosen amongst a set of recommended postures Schultz emphasized parallels to techniques in yoga and meditation However unlike some forms of yoga and meditation autogenic training is devoid of any mysticism It is a method for influencing one s autonomic nervous system

The nd Punjabis were a regiment of soldiers of the old British Indian Army Later it started to take in more soldiers from the Punjab Punjab and NWFP regions so the regiment was renamed Punjabis in and later became the nd Punjabis Then after some time they were part of the First Punjab Regiment Between and this regiment fought many wars and won many battles In when India and Pakistan became independent countries after partition the nd became part of the Punjab Regiment of the Pakistan Army

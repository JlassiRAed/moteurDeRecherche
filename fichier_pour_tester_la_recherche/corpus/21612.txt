 Master of Business Administration is a postgraduate academic degree People who study for an MBA already have a degree in another field of study and study management Very often a master s degree is required but sometimes bachelor s degree holders with work experience are also accepted Unlike other areas of study MBA courses are often targeted at people who work and can be done in a part time fashion including distance learning MBA programs usually take two years full time The main target group for MBAs are engineer s lawyer s doctor s of medicine and other with a background form the humanities The idea of the MBA is to give those people qualifications in management positions

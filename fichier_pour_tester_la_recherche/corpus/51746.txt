 Field Marshal Bernard Law Montgomery st Viscount Montgomery of Alamein Order of the Garter KG Order of the Bath GCB Distinguished Service Order DSO Her Majesty s Most Honourable Privy Council PC often referred to as Monty was a British Army officer He fought in World War I and during World War II he successfully commanded Allied forces at the Second Battle of El Alamein Battle of El Alamein a major turning point in the Western Desert Campaign He was later an important commander in Italy and in North West Europe where he was in command of all Allied ground forces during Operation Overlord until after the Battle of Normandy and was the principal commander for Operation Market Garden After the War he became Commander in Chief of the British occupation zone British Forces of Occupation in Germany and then Chief of the Imperial General Staff

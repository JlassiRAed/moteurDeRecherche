 Joseph Thomas Logano is an Americans American racecar driver who drives the No Shell Pennzoil Ford Fusion in the Sprint Cup Series for Penske Racing Until the start of the season Logano drove the Home Depot Toyota Camry for Joe Gibbs Racing Logano won the rookie of the year award and with his win at the New Hampshire Motor Speedway became the youngest drive to win a race in NASCAR history He also won the Pocono presented by NASCAR at Pocono Raceway from the pole ending a race losing streak for pole winners

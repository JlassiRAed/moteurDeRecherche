 Avinashi is a municipality in Tirupur district in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu It is one of the fastest growing western suburbs of Coimbatore Avinashi is one of seven taluk s namely Udumalaipettai Kangeyam Dharapuram Palladam Madathukulam Tiruppur Avinashi tuluk of Tirupur district Avinashi is on National Highway NH which now bypasses the town It is a town with rich cultural heritage and also keeping up with the fast developments of the adjacent city In local administration the Avinashi town is a municipality while in the state administrative structure Avinashi is a Taluk in the district of Tirupur The Corporation of Coimbatore has drafted a Master Plan that aims to develop Avinashi as a satellite town around the Coimbatore city asia stub

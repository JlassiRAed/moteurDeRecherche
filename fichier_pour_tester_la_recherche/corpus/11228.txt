 Negative film is the name for a photographic film that will give images which have their colors inverted after development This inversion means that the complementary color is used A second process Negative films that use colors have multiple layers Each layer is sensitive to a different wavelength of light Most amateur films in use today are negative films The benefits of this kind of film is that it is very tolerant to exposure differences without noticeable loss in quality Another benefit is that getting the film developed is very easy

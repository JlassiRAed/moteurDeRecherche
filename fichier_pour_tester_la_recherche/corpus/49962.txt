 G sharp major is a major scale based on the musical Note note G sharp Its key signature has six sharps and one double sharp To make reading easier G sharp major is usually written as its enharmonic equivalent of A flat major However it does appear as a secondary key area in several works in sharp keys for example in the Prelude and Fugue in C sharp major from Bach s Well Tempered Klavier Book The G sharp minor Prelude and Fugue from the same set end with a Picardy third in G sharp major G sharp major is used for a short time in several of Chopin s nocturnes in C sharp minor

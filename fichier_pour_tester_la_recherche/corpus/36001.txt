 Commonwealth Games infobox The Commonwealth Games is the nineteenth Commonwealth Games and the ninth to be held under that name The Games are being held in New Delhi India from to October the largest multi sport event conducted to date in Delhi and India which has previously hosted the Asian Games in and The opening ceremony took place at the Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium Delhi Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium the main stadium of the event It is also the first time the Commonwealth Games is held in India and the second time the event has been held in Asia While allegations of delays and corruption were a cause of concern before the start of the games the games generally ran smoothly and the opening ceremony was described as spectacular

The World Ocean world ocean or global ocean is a term used to define all the world s ocean s as one big ocean It is divided into five oceans These divisions are the Atlantic Ocean Arctic Ocean Indian Ocean Pacific Ocean and Southern Ocean The World Ocean can be visualized as being centered on the Southern Ocean The Atlantic Indian and Pacific Oceans can be seen as bays or lobes going northward from the Southern Ocean Farther north the Atlantic opens into the Arctic Ocean which is connected to the Pacific by the Bering Strait The approximate shape of the World Ocean can for most purposes be treated as constant although in fact it is not continental drift continually changes its structure

Sumitra Mahajan is an Indian politician and Speaker of the th Lok Sabha She also served as the Union Minister Sumitra Mahajan is the second longest serving women speaker after Meira Kumar Sumitra Mahajan was born in Chitpavan Brahmin family in Chiplun Maharashtra on April She married Jayant Mahajan of Indore She received MA and LLB after marriage from the Indore University Sumitra became the first Indian women to represent the same Lok sabha constituency on the same party eight times in a row Her record was included in the LIMCA book

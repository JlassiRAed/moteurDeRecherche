 Syed or Sayyid or Sayed is a title of honour and respect It is originally a word from the Arabic language which means Sir or Mister or Honourable Person However the word is now also used in different parts of the world by Muslims to refer specially to a descendant of the Prophet Muhammad For example if someone is called a Syed Sayyid in India Pakistan Bangladesh Iran Afghanistan Indonesia Malaysia etc then that person is descended from the prophet of Islam

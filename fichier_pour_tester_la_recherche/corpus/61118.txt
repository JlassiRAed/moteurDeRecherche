A traveler s cheque is a preprinted cheque for a fixed amount The person who buys it must sign it and then sign it again when he cashes it It is a good way for a traveller to take money when going on holiday If a traveler s cheque is stolen it can be replaced so long as the person still has a receipt to prove that he bought it Traveler s cheques were a large part of the business of American Express Since the American Express have been issuing American Express Travelers Cheque Cards These can be used in stores like a credit card

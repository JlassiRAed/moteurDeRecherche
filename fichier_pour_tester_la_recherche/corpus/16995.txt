 Qutb Shah or Qutub Shah was a fictitious person a Folk hero who never existed in history but according to many stories and legends he ruled in Herat Afghanistan sometime around AD and then shifted to India The actual story of a legendary ruler of Herat in Afghanistan was started much later in the th or th century by some Punjabi people Punjabi tribes like the Awan tribe Khokhar Khattar and others In s to s a number of British rulers came to believe this story too and mentioned it in their books but more recent historical research by Prof Ahmad Hasan Dani and others has confirmed that there never existed any person in recorded history by that name at any time either in Afghanistan or Northern India

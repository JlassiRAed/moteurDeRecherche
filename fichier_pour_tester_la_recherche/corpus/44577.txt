 Chef salad cheese cucumbers croutons and tomatoes Chef salad is usually topped with Thousand Island dressing but can be served with any type of dressing Chef salad is thought to be invented by chef Victor Seydoux at the Hotel Buffalo in Buffalo New York Trying to save money he started putting small slices of meat and cheese in salads for some of his customers When his customers started to ask for their salad this way it was added to the menu

 The Cook the Thief His Wife Her Lover is a United Kingdom British independent movie independent movie starring Richard Bohringer Alan Howard Michael Gambon and Helen Mirren and directed by Peter Greenaway This movie was originally tagged by the MPAA under an X rating due to which commercial distribution of this movie was limited The X rating was then surrendered and this movie was released with no rating It was later released onto video with two versions one rated NC and the other under an R rating

 The metatarsus or metatarsal bones are a group of five long bones in the foot They are between the Tarsals tarsal bones of the hind and mid foot and the phalanges of the toes The metatarsal bones are numbered from the side of the large toe the first second third fourth and fifth metatarsal They can be numbered with Roman numerals The metatarsals are like the metacarpus metacarpal bones of the hand The metatarsal bones are often broken by association football soccer players This may be caused by the lightweight design of modern football boot s which give less protection to the foot In some soccer players began testing a new sock that had a rubber silicon pad over the foot to protect the top of the foot

 Artemis is a goddess in Greek mythology and one of the Twelve Olympians She is the daughter of Zeus and Leto and the twin sister of Apollo She is the goddess of hunting wild animals and the wilderness During the th century BC Artemis became also known as the goddess of the moon becoming one with the goddess Selene She is known as a goddess of childbirth as well because she helped Leto give birth to Apollo She is also known as the goddess who asked Zeus to remain chaste and unmarried She was also the patroness of Sparta the city state where a the warriors came from in ancient Greece

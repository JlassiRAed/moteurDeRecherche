 Satyameva Jayate Cite journal It is inscribed in script at the base of the national emblem The emblem and the words Satyameva Jayate are inscribed on one side of all Indian currency The emblem is an adaptation of the Lion Capital of Ashoka which was erected around BCE at Sarnath near Varanasi in the north Indian state of Uttar Pradesh It is inscribed on all currency notes and national documents

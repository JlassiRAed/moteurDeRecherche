A ballad is a form of verse It is often a story set to music Ballads were popular in United Kingdom British and Ireland Irish poetry and music until the th century from the late medieval period It was also used in Americas Australia and North Africa Many ballads were written and sold as single sheet Broadside broadsides Poet s and composer s from the th century used this form In this way they produced lyrical ballads In the later th century ballads were slow form of popular love song The term is now often used as synonymous with any love song particularly the pop or rock power ballad

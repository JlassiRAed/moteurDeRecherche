The Carnac stones are the largest collection of megalith ic standing stone s in the world The more than prehistoric standing stones were cut from local Rock rock and erected by the pre Celts Celtic people of Brittany The stones were erected at some stage during the Neolithic period probably around BC but some may be as old as BC They include rows of stones dolmen s tumulus tumuli and single menhir s In recent centuries many of the sites have been neglected with reports of dolmen s being used as sheep shelters chicken sheds or even ovens

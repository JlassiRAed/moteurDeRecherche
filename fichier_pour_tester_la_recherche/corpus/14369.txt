 Astraphobia also called astrapophobia brontophobia keraunophobia or tonitrophobia is a fear of thunder and lightning It is a type of specific phobia Both humans and animals can have this type of phobia A person with astraphobia often feels fear during a thunderstorm even if there is a low chance that they will be hurt during the storm They will sometimes look for better shelter during the storm They might hide under a bed under blankets in a closet in a basement or anywhere else where they feel safe A sign that a person has astraphobia is if they are very interested in weather forecast s A person with astraphobia will want to hear news of storms that are coming by watching the weather on television or watching the storms on the internet

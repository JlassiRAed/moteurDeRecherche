A voluntary association or union is a group of individuals who enter into an agreement to form a body to work together for a purpose In most of the cases no formalities are necessary to start an association But in some jurisdiction s there is a minimum for the number of persons starting an association in others the association must register with the police or other official body to inform the public of the association s existence This is not necessarily a tool of political control but much more a way of protecting the economy from fraud

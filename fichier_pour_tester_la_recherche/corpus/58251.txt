The Gaur is a bovine species similar to the bison Bos gaurus is the largest living Bovinae bovine It is native to South Asia and Southeast Asia The species is listed as vulnerable species vulnerable on the IUCN Red List since Population trends are stable in well protected areas and are building up in a few areas which had been neglected The gaur is the tallest species of Bos wild cattle The Malayan gaur is called seladang and the Burmese gaur is called pyoung The domesticated form of the gaur Bos frontalis is called gayal or mithun

 Roosevelt Island is a narrow island in the East River in New York City To the west is Manhattan Island and to the east is the New York City borough of Queens Roosevelt Island is part of the Borough borough of Manhattan A vertical lift bridge stands there known as the Roosevelt Island Bridge The island is about Roosevelt Island was called Minnehanonck by the Lenape Native American s Later it was called Varkens Eylandt by people who lived in New Netherland During Britain s colonization of the United States the island was named Blackwell It was renamed Roosevelt Island in after U S President Franklin D Roosevelt Roosevelt Island is owned by the city but is leased to the state of New York Most people who live on Roosevelt Island Renting rent apartments There is also a housing cooperative building and a condominium building

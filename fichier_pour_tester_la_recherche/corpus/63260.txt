 Roe v Wade was a landmark decision by the Supreme Court of the United States US Supreme Court The court ruled that a state law that banned abortion s was unconstitutional The decision divided the United States nation and is still controversial today People divided into pro life and pro choice groups Pro life people said that abortions should not be allowed because it is the killing of unborn babies Pro choice people said that a woman had the right to choose whether to have an abortion or not Roe was limited by a later decision called Webster v Reproductive Health which allowed regulation of abortion in some cases Several states have considered laws banning abortions altogether

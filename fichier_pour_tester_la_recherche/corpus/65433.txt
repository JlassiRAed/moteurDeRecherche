 Cruiserweight is a division in boxing for fighters who weigh over pounds and up to pounds The weight limit was once pounds The World Boxing Council created this weight division in the late s because it felt that some fighters were too small to fight in the heavyweight division but too big to fight in the light heavyweight division So they made a weight class in between Many cruiserweight champions and contenders may eventually fight in the heavyweight division Evander Holyfield was cruiserweight champion

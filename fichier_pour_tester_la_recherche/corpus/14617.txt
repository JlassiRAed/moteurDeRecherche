The Cerapods are a sub order of the Ornithischia They are all herbivorous dinosaurs They have thicker Tooth enamel enamel on the inside of their lower teeth The teeth wear unevenly and this allows them to browse on tough vegetation They are divided into three groups Ornithopoda including the duck billed dinosaurs Pachycephalosauria the thick headed dinosaurs Ceratopsia the horned dinasaurs

 Irritator is a genus of theropod dinosaur which was discovered in the Lower Cretaceous of the Santana Formation The first identifiable dinosaur from Brazil and the first non avian from South America Irritator has teeth similar to Spinosaurus It has a slender jaw with nostrils well back from the front of the snout It also had a large crest on the quadrate bone on top of the skull It was named due to the feelings of the scientists who described it when they found that the snout had been artificially lengthened to resemble a plesiosaur

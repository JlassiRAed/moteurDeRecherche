 Sweeney Todd the Demon Barber of Fleet Street is a musical theater musical with a libretto book by Hugh Wheeler and music and lyrics by Stephen Sondheim The musical is based on the th century legend of Sweeney Todd and is similar to the play The String of Pearls by Christopher Bond Sweeney Todd opened on Broadway theatre Broadway at the George Gershwin Theatre Uris Theatre on March It was directed by Harold Prince with musical staging by Larry Fuller and starred Len Cariou as Sweeney Todd and Angela Lansbury as Mrs Lovett The musical played times It won the Tony Award for Best Musical

 Live Free or Die is the official wikt motto motto of the U S state of New Hampshire adopted by the state in The phrase comes from a wikt toast toast written by General John Stark on July Stark was New Hampshire s most famous soldier of the American Revolutionary War His poor health forced him to decline an invitation to a reunion of the Battle of Bennington He sent his wikt toast toast by letter Live free or die Death is not the worst of evils The motto was enacted at the same time as the Emblem of New Hampshire state emblem on which it appears

 More Monsters and Sprites is the third Extended play EP and first remix album by Americans American electronic music producer Skrillex More Monsters and Sprites was released on June A music video for the song First of the Year which was directed by Tony Truand was nominated for Best Short Form Music Video at the th Grammy Awards The EP also received generally positive reviews from music critics and reached on the Billboard as well as reaching the top five on both the Billboard Heatseekers and Dance Electronica Albums chart

 Baked Alaska is a dessert made of ice cream on top of slices of sponge cake or Christmas pudding and covered with meringue The whole dessert is then baked in a very hot oven for just long enough to cook the meringue The meringue is a good wikt insulate insulator and the short cooking time prevents the heat from getting through to the ice cream Its name but not the recipe was made at Delmonico s Restaurant in to honour the new United States American Alaska territory February is Baked Alaska Day

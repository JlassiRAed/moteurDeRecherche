 The Keel billed Toucan also known as Sulfur breasted Toucan or Rainbow billed Toucan is a colorful Latin America n member of the toucan family It is the national bird of Belize The Keel billed Toucan can be found from Southern Mexico to Venezuela and Colombia Like many toucans Keel billed is a very social bird rarely seen alone It travels in small Herd flocks of about six to twelve individuals through lowland rainforest s It is a poor flyer and moves mostly by hopping through trees

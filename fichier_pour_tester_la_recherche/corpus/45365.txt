 Chester Burton Chet Atkins was an Americans American guitarist and record producer He helped create a smooth country music style known as the Nashville Sound His guitar picking style was inspired by Merle Travis Django Reinhardt and Les Paul and brought him admirers both in the United States and internationally as well as earning him the nickname Mister Guitar Chet Atkins produced records for Perry Como Elvis Presley the Everly Brothers Eddy Arnold Jim Reeves Jerry Reed and Waylon Jennings along with many others Chet Atkins received many honors including Grammy Awards as well as the Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award He won nine Country Music Association Awards Country Music Association Instrumentalist of the Year awards and was inducted into the Country Music Hall of Fame He died from cancer on June at his home in Nashville Tennessee In Atkins was posthumously inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame The following year Atkins was ranked in Country Music Television CMT s Greatest Men of Country Music

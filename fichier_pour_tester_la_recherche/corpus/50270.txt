An ectopic pregnancy is a when an embryo implants itself itself into the wall of the uterus The uterus is the only place in the body where an embryo can grow into a fetus Most ectopic pregnancies happen in a Fallopian tube For this reason ectopic pregnancies are often called tubal pregnancies Rarely ectopic pregnancies can happen in an ovary or in the cervix Ectopic pregnancies cannot become normal pregnancies and will not result in a baby They can also cause serious health problems for the mother

 Mario Luigi Partners in Time is a game for the Nintendo DS Mario Luigi Partners in Time was released in North America on November in Japan on December in Europe on January and in the United Kingdom on February It is the second game in the Mario and Luigi RPG series preceding Mario Luigi Superstar Saga In this game Princess Peach Peach goes with two other Toad Toads in a time machine but she never returns Whenever the machine returns a green alien comes out of it After they beat it Mario and Luigi go to various places in the past

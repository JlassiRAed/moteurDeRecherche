The Tokaimura nuclear accident was a serious nuclear radiation accident in Japan It took place at a uranium reprocessing facility in Tokaimura northeast of Tokyo Japan on September The accident occurred in a very small fuel preparation plant operated by JCO The direct cause of the accident was some workers putting uranyl nitrate solution containing about kg of uranium which exceeded the critical mass into a precipitation wikt tank tank The tank was not designed to dissolve this type of solution and was not designed to prevent such accidents to happen As a result three workers were exposed to neutron radiation doses in excess of allowable limits Two of these workers later died Many emergency workers and nearby residents were hospitalized and hundreds of thousands of others were forced to remain indoors for hours

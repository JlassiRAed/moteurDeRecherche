 The Devil s food cake is an old fashioned chocolate cake made of chocolate or cocoa and baking powder It is moist soft rich and very good for keeping It is a light textured chocolate layer type cake with a deep reddish brown color The cake gets this bright red color from the large amount of red food dye used in the preparation Devil s food cake is usually thought of in terms of dark chocolate but originally it was red Today cooks using modern processed cocoa sometimes add a touch of red food coloring to bring back the authentic color Devil s Food Cakes can be stored in the refrigerator for about days

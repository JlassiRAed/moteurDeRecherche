 Primary colors are sets of color s that can be combined to make a useful range of colors The primary colors are those which cannot be created by mixing other colors in a given color space For subtractive combination of colors as in mixing of pigment s or dye s for printing the primaries normally used are cyan magenta and yellow cite book For additive combination of colors as in overlapping projected lights or in cathode ray tube CRT displays the primary colors normally used are red green and blue

A penetrometer is a device to test the strength of a material It is often used on soil The penetrometer is dropped on soil or it is pressed against soil to measure how deep a hole it makes This is a way to decide if soil is strong enough to build a road on Scientists can use a penetrometer to measure how much moisture is in soil Penetrometers are used on space probe s such as the Cassini Huygens probe to measure the amount of moisture in soil on other planets

 Hinduism Hindus believe that Vishnu had many important Avatar s and the Varaha avatar was the third avatar of Vishnu In this avatar Vishnu took the form of a black varaha Varaha is a Sanskrit language word that means a boar The Hindu scriptures tell a story about this avatar There was a daitya into the waters of the ocean The earth was about to sink in the ocean At this point Vishnu took the form of a black boar He entered the water and carried the earth on its body and brought the earth safely to where it should be Thus Vishnu as a varaha saved the earth from being destroyed category Hinduism category Hindu mythology

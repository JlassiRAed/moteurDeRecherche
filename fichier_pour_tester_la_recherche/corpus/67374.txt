 Queen of the South Football Club is a Scotland Scottish professional Association football football club founded in It is in Dumfries in southwest Scotland They currently play in the Scottish First Division Officially nicknamed The Doonhamers but usually referred to as Queens and sometimes as QoS they have played their entire existence based at Palmerston Park on Terregles Street The club has won national honours namely the B Division the Scottish Second Division and the Scottish Challenge Cup While Queens topped Scotland s top division until Christmas in season their highest ever finish in the Scottish top flight was fourth in Their longest cup run was in the Scottish Cup in which they reached the final and finished runners up The club is currently managed by Gordon Chisholm

The Hittites were an ancient people from Anatolia who spoke an Indo European language Indo European language They established a kingdom centered at Hattusa in north central Anatolia from the th century BC At its peak the Hittite Empire covered most of modern Turkey and Syria This was under the reigns of Suppiluliuma I They had up and down relationships with Ancient Egypt to the south and the Assyria Assyrian Empire in Mesopotamia They were a party to the first known peace treaty which was made with Ramesses II of Egypt by Hattusili III in BC After BC the empire disintegrated into several independent Neo Hittite city states some surviving until as late as the th century BC

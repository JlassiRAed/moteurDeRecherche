 Metabolic rate is the amount of energy used by an animal per unit of time Basal metabolic rate is the amount of energy used daily by animals at rest About of a human s total energy expenditure is due to the basal life processes within the organs of the body About of one s energy expenditure comes from physical activity and another from the digestion of food after eating All of these processes require an intake of oxygen and coenzyme s to provide energy for survival usually from macronutrients like carbohydrates fats and proteins The Krebs cycle produces energy rich ATP molecules and gives off carbon dioxide

 Golaghmuli Valley is a high mountainous valley in Upper  Ghizer District Ghizer  of  Gilgit Baltistan  Pakistan This valley is situated in the east of Chitral District Chitral and north of  Swat District Swat Golaghmuli Valley is separated from the Chitral District Chitral and Swat Swat by high mountain passes To reach Golaghmuli Valley a person must take the Gilgit Chitral road west from Gilgit and then continue his journey straight by Gupis After Gupis one continues northwest to reach the Yasin Valley and the straight road goes to Golaghmuli Valley Golaghmuli Valley has a spectacular natural beauty

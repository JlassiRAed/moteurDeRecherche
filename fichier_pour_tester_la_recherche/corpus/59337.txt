 Phoebe is a Titan Titan in Greek mythology She was associated with the moon She is the daugher of Uranos and Gaia She was married to her brother Coeus and had two daughters Leto and Asteria Leto bore Artemis and Apollo Asteria is the mother of Hecate The Olympians beat the Titans in the ten year war She and the Titans were summoned by Zeus to Tarturus in the Underworld

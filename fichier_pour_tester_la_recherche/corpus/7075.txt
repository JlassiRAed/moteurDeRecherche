 Dawlish Warren is a small seaside resort It is near Dawlish on the south coast of Devon England It is at the mouth of the Exe Estuary and has a beach a National Nature Reserve spanning acres and a golf course There is also one of Europe s largest spits in Dawlish Warren measuring over miles long and miles wide There is a fence running down the centre of the spit to prevent people from wandering and falling into any deep parts of the salt marshes that have accumulated on the inside of the spit

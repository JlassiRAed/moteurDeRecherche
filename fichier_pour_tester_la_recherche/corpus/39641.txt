 The Blue Mountains is a region in New South Wales Australia The foothills start about west of the state capital Sydney It is mainly a sandstone plateau The highest point of the range is Mount Werong It is above sea level A large part of the Blue Mountains is in the Greater Blue Mountains Area World Heritage Site The area has seven national park areas and a conservation reserve The Blue Mountains area includes the local government areas of the City of Blue Mountains the City of Hawkesbury the City of Lithgow and Oberon New South Wales Oberon

 Budai or Budai Luohan pronounced Hotei in Japanese language Japanese also known as the Laughing Buddha is the person who might be the next Buddha after Gautama Buddha He has become incorporated into Buddhist Taoism Taoist and Shinto culture and is based on a Chinese monk He is in many temples restaurants and businesses Budai has become a god of happiness and plenty when taken in by Taoism and Buddhism In Japan Hotei is one of the Seven Lucky Gods

 Fra Angelico was an Italy Italian List of Renaissance artists Early Renaissance painter Giorgio Vasari who wrote the Lives of the Artists said he had a rare and perfect talent Fra Angelico has been known by many different names When he was born he was called Guido di Pietro People who knew him when he was older called him Fra Giovanni da Fiesole The Italians usually call him il Beato Angelico He has been called this for a long time because he was thought to be blessed by God who gave him the talent of painting and also because he was such a good and holy man Cite web Vasari says about him It is impossible to think of enough good things to say about this holy father who was so humble and modest in everything that he did and said and whose pictures were painted with such cleverness and holy faith

 Eid al Adha Festival of Sacrifice or Greater Bairam is a religious festival celebrated by Muslims all over the world It is when Muslims sacrifice a goat sheep cow or a camel sending part of the meat to poor people as a donation It marks the end of the Pilgrimage or Hajj for the millions of Muslims who make the trip to Mecca each year and pray times a day They like to tell Allah what they are thinking and hope for him to help them and remember Abraham Ibrahim and Ishmael s courage and devotion to God

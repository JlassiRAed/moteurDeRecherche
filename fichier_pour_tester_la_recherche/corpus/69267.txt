General Sir James Abbott As part of the Treaty of Lahore signed after the defeat of the Sikhs in the First Anglo Sikh War Hazara and Kashmir were going to be transferred to Gulab Singh Hazara however stayed under the Lahore government and governed by James Abbott James Abbott was the brother of Augustus Abbott and Frederick Abbott who also served as officers in the forces of the East India Company His picture was created by the artist B Baldwin this picture is located in the National Portrait Gallery National Portrait Gallery in London though it is not currently on display

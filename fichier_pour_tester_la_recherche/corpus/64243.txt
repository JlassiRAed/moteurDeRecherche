 Nigel David Short Order of the British Empire MBE Grandmaster at age He challenged for the World Chess Championship against Garry Kasparov at London Still active Short continues to enjoy international success He is also a chess columnist coach and commentator Short s best achievement was in the World Candidates matches of when he beat world class players Boris Gelfand Anatoly Karpov and Jan Timman to reach the final In the World Championship match London he was decisively beaten by the reigning World Champion Gary Kasparov The match caused controversy because the two players broke away from FIDE for reasons mostly to do with the prize money Short has played times for England in Chess Olympiads and European and World Team championships He has been living in Greece with his wife and children for a number of years

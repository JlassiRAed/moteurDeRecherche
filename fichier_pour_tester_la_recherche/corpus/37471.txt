 Miranda Leigh Lambert Shelton is an American country music artist who gained fame as a finalist on the season of Nashville Star where she finished in third place and later signed to Epic Records She is married to fellow country singer Blake Shelton She has four solo albums released as of and one with country supergroup Pistol Annies Some of her most known songs are Gunpowder and Lead Kerosene The House That Built Me White Liar Heart Like Mine and Baggage Claim

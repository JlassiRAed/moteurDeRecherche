The Canadian Albums Chart is the official album sales chart in Canada It is compiled every Wednesday by U S based music sales tracking company Nielsen Soundscan and published every Thursday by Jam Canadian Online Explorer Canoe along with its sister charts the Canadian Singles Chart and the Canadian Nielsen Broadcast Data Systems BDS Airplay Chart The chart consists of positions however Jam only publishes the top for the public Unlike the case with its CD single equivalent album sales are not declining as rapidly in Canada as CD singles are and the Canadian Albums Chart remains the most reliable source for the most popular albums in Canada

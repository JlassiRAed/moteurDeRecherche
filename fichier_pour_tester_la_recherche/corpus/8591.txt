 Tommy Flowers Order of the British Empire MBE was a British engineer During World War II Flowers designed Colossus computer Colossus the world s first programmable electronic computer to help solve encrypted Germany German messages The first Mark with vacuum tube valves ran at Dollis Hill in November and then at Bletchley Park in January A Mark redesign with valves had begun before the first computer was finished The first Mark Colossus went into service at Bletchley Park on June and immediately produced vital information for the imminent D Day landing

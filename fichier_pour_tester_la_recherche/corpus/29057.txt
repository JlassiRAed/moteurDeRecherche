 Ian Stevenson Doctor of Medicine M D was a Canadians Canadian professor of psychiatry He worked at the University of Virginia in the area of parapsychology Stevenson thought that the idea of reincarnation might help modern medicine better understand aspects of human behavior and development Stevenson saw reincarnation as the survival of the personality after death although he never suggested a physical process by which a personality might survive death Stevenson s research was the subject of Jim B Tucker s Life Before Life

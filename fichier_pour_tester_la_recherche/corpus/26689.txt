A Khachkar or Khatchkar is a carved memorial stone found in Armenia It has a crucifix or cross with a rosette or sun circle below it Other parts can be filled with patterns of leaves grapes or knots Sometimes it has a ledge at the top with saints or characters from the bible Much of the time a khachkar is put up for religious reasons such as the salvation of the soul of a living or a dead person They have also been removed for other reasons such as to show a military win building of a new church or as a protection from natural disasters Most Khachkars are in Cemetery graveyards But Armenian gravestones take many other forms and only a small are khatchkars

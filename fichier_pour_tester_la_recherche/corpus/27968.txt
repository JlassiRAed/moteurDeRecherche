 Biphobia is the fear of or bad treatment of bisexual people Biphobia is different from homophobia For example a person might be accepting of women who are attracted to women but not accepting of women who are attracted to men and women Gay men and lesbian women as well as heterosexual men and women can be biphobic Bisexual people may feel like their sexual orientation is less real than that of a monosexual This is internalized biphobia

 Clastic rocks are composed of fragments or clasts of pre existing Rock rock Although in principle almost any rock made of eroded former rock is clastic in practice the term is used for clasts of at least gravel size upwards That means clasts are at least visible to the naked eye and may be huge There are two types of clastic rock by shape Conglomerate conglomerate s have rounded clasts and breccia s have jagged clasts In a clastic rock the clasts are often of a different make up from the ground rock or matrix matrix

 Nonviolence is a philosophy that rejects the use of physical violence People who practice nonviolence when expressing their beliefs may use methods like education persuasion civil disobedience and conscientious objector conscientous objection to military service A demonstration by a group of people in a public place to show their support or to protest is permitted on the condition that it remain nonviolent Sometimes the demonstrators are nonviolent but other people who oppose them will come to the demonstration and behave violently to show their opposition Some religious movements such as the Religious Society of Friends Society of Friends and Jainism are known for the practice of nonviolence There are people who adopt nonviolence in midlife such as St Francis of Assisi People who eat a vegetarianism vegetarian or veganism vegan diet and those who support animal rights usually oppose any violence by humans against animals

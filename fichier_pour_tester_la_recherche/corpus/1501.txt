A Sophie Germain prime is a type of prime number If a prime number is still prime after being multiplied by and adding to the answer then the original prime is a Sophie Germain prime A mathematical definition is a prime number denoted by p is a Sophie Germain prime if p is also a prime number p is called a safe prime if it is a prime number Sophie Germain primes are named after the French mathematician Sophie Germain Many mathematicians believe that there are an infinite amount of Sophie Germain primes but this has not been proven

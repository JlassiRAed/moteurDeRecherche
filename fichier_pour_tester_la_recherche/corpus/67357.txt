 Freshwater biology is the study of plant s and animal s that live in fresh water This includes river s lake s pond s marsh es and other bodies of water It is part of the branch of science called Limnology Much freshwater biology takes place in man made places such as sewage treatment works and water treatment works In these places water is purified using plants and animals Keeping them working as hard as possible helps to make the water as cleaner In recent times freshwater biologists have been looking at how plants and animals change in the autumn and spring This is helping us to understand more about the effects of climate change

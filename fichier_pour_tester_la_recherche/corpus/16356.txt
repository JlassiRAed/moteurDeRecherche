 Conceptual art is art in which the concept s or idea s involved in the work are more important than traditional Aesthetics aesthetic and material concerns Many of the works sometimes called installation s may be constructed by anyone simply by following a set of written instructions This method was fundamental to one of the first to appear in print In conceptual art the idea or concept is the most important aspect of the work When an artist uses a conceptual form of art it means that all of the planning and decisions are made beforehand and the execution is a perfunctory affair The idea becomes a machine that makes the art Conceptual art even questions the nature of art The notion that art should examine its own nature was already part of art critic Clement Greenberg s vision of modern art during the s With the emergence of an exclusively language based art in the s however conceptual artists began a far more radical analysis of art than was previously done One of the first things they questioned was the assumption that the role of the artist was to create special kinds of material objects Conceptual art has come to mean all contemporary art which does not use the traditional skills of painting and sculpture

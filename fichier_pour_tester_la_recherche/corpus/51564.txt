 Buitreraptor is a small dromaeosaur from the Cretaceous of Argentina It was about four to five feet long Buitreraptor has a slender flat extremely elongated snout Its jaws have many small teeth which lack meat tearing serrations or cutting edges They are grooved strongly recurved and flattened This probably meant it ate quite small animals like lizards and mammals Since its close relatives had feathers it is likely that Buitreraptor also was feathered

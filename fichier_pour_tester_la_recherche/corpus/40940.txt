 The Heteroptera is a group of about species of insects in the Hemiptera They are typical bugs Heteroptera is Greek for different wings most species have front wings with both membranous and hardened portions called hemelytra The name Heteroptera is used in two different ways in modern classifications In Carl Linnaeus Linnean nomenclature it is a suborder of the order order Hemiptera In cladistics it is an unranked clade in the Hemiptera clade

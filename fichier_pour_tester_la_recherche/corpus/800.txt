 Hemerobiiformia Myrmeleontiformia Neuroptera is a order of net winged insect s and includes the lacewing s Mantispidae mantidflies antlion s and their relatives The order contains some species The adults of this order possess four membranous wings with the forewings and hindwings about the same size and with many veins They have chewing mouthparts and undergo complete metamorphosis Most of the larvae and adults are predators The clade first appeared in the Permian period

 Uttarkashi District also known as Uttar Kashi District is a district of Garhwal division of the Uttarakhand state in northern India Its headquarters are at Uttarkashi city Uttarkashi District town is high in the Himalaya range The source of both the Ganges and Yamuna rivers are in the district These attract thousands of Hindu pilgrim s The town is on the main route to Gangotri has many Hindu temple s and is thought to be an important Hindu pilgrim age centre

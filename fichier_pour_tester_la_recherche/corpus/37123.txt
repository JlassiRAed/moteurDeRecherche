 Padmasambhava is the person who brought Buddhism to Tibet in the th century The name is in the Sanskrit language and means born from a Nelumbo lotus flower In Bhutan and Tibet he is better known as Guru Rinpoche p where followers of the Nyingma school regard him as the second Buddha The mantra of Padmasambhva is Om Ah Hum Vajra Guru Padma Siddhi Hum and is called the Vajra Guru mantra There is also a prayer called The Seven Line Prayer which is devoted to him

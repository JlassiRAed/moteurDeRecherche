 DS Virtual Console Kirby s Pinball Land is a pinball video game that was developed by HAL Laboratory and published by Nintendo in It is for the Game Boy game console It is the first spin off game in the Kirby Kirby series It was later released for the Virtual Console service on the Nintendo DS in The game features three pinball tables Each table has three floors The player earns points by bouncing off of walls and bumpers and by defeating enemies Players can also earn Maxim Tomatoes which stops the player from losing a ball for a short amount of time On the middle and top floors is a Warp Star If the player takes the Warp Star on the middle floor the player can play a minigame to earn extra points and multipliers The Warp Star on the top floor takes the player to the boss of the table After defeating a boss the player can earn another life by spelling out the word Extra with the letters that appear on the table selection screen After the player plays all three tables they face against King Dedede Defeating King Dedede allows the player to play the tables again for a higher score

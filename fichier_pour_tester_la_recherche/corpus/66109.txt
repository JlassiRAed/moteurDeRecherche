 Hurricane Erika was a Atlantic hurricane that hit extreme northeastern Mexico near the Texas Tamaulipas border It happened on August during the Atlantic hurricane season Erika was the eighth tropical cyclone fifth tropical storm and third hurricane of the season that year The storm was created from a non tropical low pressure area area of low pressure that was tracked for five days before developing in the eastern Gulf of Mexico on August Under the influence of a high pressure area high pressure system Erika moved quickly westward and became stronger under tropical cyclogenesis favorable conditions

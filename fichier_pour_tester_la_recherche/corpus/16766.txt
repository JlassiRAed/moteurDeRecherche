 Striptease is an entertainment by females usually before an audience It is often included in the theatre form called burlesque With music and dance the stripper gradually removes her clothing It is a very ancient dance form and occurs in many societies Stripping is done in a teasing manner but without being obscene While hiding certain parts of the body with hands or pieces of clothing stripper dances around Sometimes plays are arranged the strippers are disguised as Arabic dancers Salome Lolita or other well known people The spectator sometimes identifies with the stripper Erotic dream s and exhibitionism exhibitionist fantasy fantasies may be projected into the striptease

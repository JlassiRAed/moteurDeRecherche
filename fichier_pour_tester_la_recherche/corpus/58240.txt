The Dordrecht Confession of Faith is a statement of faith that was created by Netherlands Dutch Mennonite leaders at a meeting in Dordrecht Netherlands on April It has articles of faith that talk about the importance of salvation through Jesus Christ baptism nonviolence feet washing and not taking oaths It was a very important part of the Radical Reformation and is still a very important document to many Anabaptist groups today such as the Amish In Jacob Gottshalk with sixteen other ministers from Pennsylvania and together they created the Dutch Mennonite Dordrecht Confession of Faith

 Geobox Region West Flanders is one of the five province s of the Flemish Region also named Flanders and also is one of the ten provinces of Belgium It is the westernmost province in Flanders Its capital is Bruges The whole Belgian North Sea coast an important tourism destination lies in West Flanders A tram line runs the length of the coast from De Panne on the French border via the port of Ostend to Knokke Heist on the Dutch frontier

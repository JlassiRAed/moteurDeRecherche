 Dracovenator was a genus of theropod dinosaur that lived in southern Africa during the Lower Jurassic period about million years ago It is closely related to Dilophosaurus and may have sported a pair of crests like it Its name means Dragon hunter It was given this name because dinosaurs are similar to the dragons of mythology and it was a carnivore so it was probably a hunter Dracovenator was a large carnivore about feet long It is the first large meat eating dinosaur from the Lower Jurassic of Africa to be discovered Dracovenator was described and named in based on a hand claw an upper jaw with teeth vertebrae pelvis bones ribs and foot bones There is one named species Dracovenator regenti Dracovenator was discovered in the upper Elliot Formation in South Africa

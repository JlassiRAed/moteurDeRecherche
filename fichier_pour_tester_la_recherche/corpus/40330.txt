In sports an All America team is a group of amateur Sportsperson athletes who are honored for being the best in the sport The players who are on these teams are usually selected by members of the Mass media media or company companies who wikt sponsor sponsor the team In other cases players become an All American by achieving a standard such as placing in the top of the NCAA cross country running cross country race Sometimes these teams play in games against other All American teams but most All American teams never actually compete in events

 Night of Champions was a professional wrestling pay per view show made by WWE It was held on September at TD Garden in Boston Massachusetts It was the fifth WWE Night of Champions event held by the WWE The main event was a match between WWE World Heavyweight Championship WWE Champion CM Punk and John Cena The end of match saw Cena place Punk on the top rope and hit a bridging German suplex for the pin The referee declared that both men had their shoulders on the mat for three seconds and ended the match in a draw

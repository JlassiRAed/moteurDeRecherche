 The Nguni languages are a group of Bantu languages spoken by the Nguni people These languages are spoken in Southern Africa mostly in South Africa Swaziland and Zimbabwe Nguni languages include Xhosa language Xhosa Zulu language Zulu Swati language Swati amaHlubi Hlubi Phuthi language Phuthi and Ndebele language Ndebele The name Nguni comes from the cow breed called Nguni cattle The word Nguni is sometimes used to mean all speakers of Nguni languages as a group This is an incorrect use of the word since many different tribe s speak these languages

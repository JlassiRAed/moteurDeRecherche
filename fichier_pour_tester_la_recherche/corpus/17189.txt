The Maccabean Revolt was an insurrection by Jewish patriot s against the Seleucid Empire and parties who wished to adopt Greece Greek culture The Seleucid Empire which controlled present day Syria and Israel sought to make the Jewish people more Greek like The Seleucid Emperor Antiochus IV Epiphanes installed a Greek idol Zeus in Second Temple Jerusalem s Temple and forbade Jewish practices Jewish individuals who wished to keep their identity and traditions did not like what Seleucids were doing and decided to fight against them Judah Maccabee and his brothers led the rebellion liberated Jerusalem and restored the Temple The Maccabees won the war and reestablished the kingdom of Judah

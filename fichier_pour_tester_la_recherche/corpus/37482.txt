 Ultratop Singles often called Ultratop is the weekly chart of fifty best selling singles in Flanders Belgium Ultratop and is produced and published by the Ultratop organization The chart has existed since March It is published in Dutch French and English and is presented on several Belgian radio stations as well as on TMF Flanders television Its equivalent in the Wallonia region of Belgium is known as the Ultratop Ultratop Singles chart Three other charts from the Ultratop organization bear similar names two separate and usually different charts named Ultratop Albums tracking weekly album performance in the Dutch speaking Flanders and French speaking Wallonia regions respectively and one chart named Ultratop Alternative Albums All of these charts too are published in three languages

A great power is a nation or state that is able to influence other states in most of the world That is possible because it has great economy economic politics political and military strength Its opinions are taken into account by other nations before taking diplomacy diplomatic or military action Characteristically they have the ability to intervene militarily almost anywhere and they also have soft culture cultural power often in the form of economic investment in less developed portions of the world Five great powers are permanent members of the United Nations Security Council

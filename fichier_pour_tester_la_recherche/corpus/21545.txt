 Elwin Al Ira Rollins was a professional Canadians Canadian ice hockey goaltender Born in Vanguard Saskatchewan Rollins played in the National Hockey League NHL for the Toronto Maple Leafs Chicago Black Hawks and New York Rangers He played a big role in the Leafs Stanley Cup victory Rollins also won the Vezina Trophy and the Hart Memorial Trophy Rollins retired in and decided to become a coach After retiring Rollins coached the University of Calgary ice hockey team as well as clubs in Spokane Salt Lake City Houston Tulsa and Phoenix As a coach he had a lot of success including an Allan Cup win with the Spokane Jets in His son Jerry Rollins played in the World Hockey Association WHA which no longer is in operation

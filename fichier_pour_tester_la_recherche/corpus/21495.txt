 Ragunan Zoo founded in is a hectare zoo in Pasar Minggu South Jakarta Indonesia The zoo has over species of animals species of flora Ragunan Zoo is a popular weekend destination for Jakartans family Ragunan Zoo is a combination between zoo and park so it is also known as Ragunan Zoological Park It holds a lot of Indonesian species such as komodo dragon orangutan tapir anoa sumatran tiger Javan Hawk eagle and various colored birds like Southern Cassowary Other popular animals are sumatran elephant kangaroo giraffe and zebra

 Caucasian Albania was the area of modern day Azerbaijan It was a client state of the Roman Empire for four centuries around the time of Jesus Christ Its Suzerainty influence over the region started in the st century BC first century before Christ and lasted until around AD Around AD Albania was again a wikt nominal nominal vassal state of emperor Diocletianus for a few years more Rome controlled Caucasian Albania only as a client or vassal state It was never able to fully make it a part of the Roman Empire like a Roman province province In these centuries Rome brought Christianity to the Caucasian Albanians with a western cultural influence that has lasted until contemporary Azerbaijan Even if few Azerbaijanis are Christians today the writing system they use is the Roman alphabet A second and final influence came from the Eastern Roman Empire when emperor Heraclius was able to take control of Caucasian Albania in AD with help from the Gokturks of the Western Turkic Khaganate in the Third Perso Turkic War

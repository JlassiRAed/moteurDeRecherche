The Gilgit District is bounded by the Wakhan Corridor of in the north and northeast Skardu District in the south and southeast The capital of the Gilgit District is Gilgit town The district includes Gilgit town Naltar Hunza Gojal and Shimshal It also includes many small villages like Minapin Hopar and Hispar The Haramosh valley now in the Gilgit District was previously in the Skardu District The highest peak in the Gilgit District is Distaghil Sar which is the th highest mountain in the world

 Skeletal muscle is a form of striated muscle tissue It is under the voluntary control of the somatic nervous system Most skeletal muscles are attached to bone s by bundles of collagen fibers known as tendons It is one of three muscle types the others being cardiac muscle and smooth muscle Skeletal muscle is made up of individual muscle cells or myocyte s known as muscle fibres Muscle fibres do the work when muscles contract A great deal is known about their structure and how they work They contract when they get a nerve impulse

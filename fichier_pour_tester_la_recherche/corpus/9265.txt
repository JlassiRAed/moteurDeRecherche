 Marc Allan Feldman was an Americans American physician political commentator and activist Feldman was also a practicing physician He had been employed at the Cleveland Clinic since August On January Feldman announced that he was running for President of the United States in the United States presidential election On June an unknown woman called the front desk of Dr Feldman s motel room at the America s Best Value Inn in Brook Park Ohio and reported that he was unresponsive He was pronounced dead at the scene a short time later The cause of death was atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease

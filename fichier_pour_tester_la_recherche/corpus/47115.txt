A stanza is a related group of lines or verse s in a poem A stanza also can be a verse in paragraph form They can keep on going without punctuation It may also be a line in a poem Two most important features of a stanza is the number of lines and the rhyme scheme There are many kinds of stanza An Italian sonnet consists of two four line stanzas and two three line stanzas A French ballad is composed of three eight line stanzas and a four line one Some stanzas are named after poets who invented or often used them An example is Sapphic stanza that was named after famous Greek woman poet Sappho

 Ernest Claes was a Flemish author His real name was Andreas Ernestus Josephus Claes He was born to Petrus Josephus Claes and Maria Theresia Lemmens His parents were farmers and they had nine children He was a good student and studied at the Catholic University of Leuven For some of his works he used the pseudonym G van Hasselt Claes was for years a favorite author in Belgium His novel De Witte was reprinted more than times

 Skate is a skateboarding video game that was published by Electronic Arts It is available for the PlayStation and the Xbox video game systems The game was the third in the Skate video game series The game takes place in the city of Port Carverton The city also embraces skateboarding which was unlike the second game where they had a skateboarding is a crime mentality The game got good reviews with a out of from GameSpot and a out of from Metacritic

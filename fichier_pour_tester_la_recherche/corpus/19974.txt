 U S Route in the eastern part of the United States Its northern end is at the Canada Canadian border in Fort Kent Maine and its southern end is at Key West Florida For most of its route U S Route runs next to Interstate It connects many major cities in the Eastern United States including Boston New York Philadelphia Baltimore Washington DC Richmond Virginia Raleigh North Carolina Augusta Georgia Jacksonville Florida and Miami Parts of the road were built in the s as the Boston Post Road and in the s as the Atlantic Highway

 Behaviour therapy is used for a number of methods of psychotherapy Ivan Pavlov found that it is possible to learn how to react to a stimulus His method is known as classical conditioning today Behaviour therapy tries to use this knowledge to change the behaviour of people In that way people learn how to cope with their fears for example In a similar manner people can be helped to cope with a number of mental disorder s One well known kind of behaviour therapy is exposure therapy People with fears are exposed to them and learn how to cope As an example someone who is arachnophobia afraid of spiders will be exposed to spiders

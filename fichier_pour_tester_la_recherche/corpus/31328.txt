The mantle is a significant part of the anatomy of mollusc s it is the dorsum dorsal body wall which covers the organs of digestion reproduction and movement In many but not all species of molluscs the epidermis of the mantle secretes calcium carbonate and conchiolin and creates a Animal shell shell The word mantle means cloak or cape and does often resemble a cloak The edges of the mantle may extend far beyond the main part of the body forming flaps double layered structures adapted for many different uses including for example the siphon

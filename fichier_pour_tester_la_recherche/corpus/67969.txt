The Summer Palace is a palace in Beijing China It is mainly dominated by Longevity Hill and the Kunming Lake It covers an expanse of square kilometers three quarters of which is water The palace is a UNESCO World Heritage Site Between and the Qing Emperor Qianlong created the Garden of Clear Ripples the garden and its buildings were destroyed by the allied forces Between and it was reconstructed by Emperor Guangxu and renamed the Summer Palace for use by Empress Dowager Cixi It was damaged in by the international expeditionary force during the suppression of the Boxer Rising and restored two years later It became a public park in There are many buildings in the Summer Palace including The Cloud Dispelling Hall the Temple of Buddhist Virtue and the Sea of Wisdom Temple

 House of Usher is a American International Pictures horror movie It stars Vincent Price Myrna Fahey Mark Damon and Harry Ellerbee The movie was directed by Roger Corman The screenplay was written by Richard Matheson It is based on the short story The Fall of the House of Usher by Edgar Allan Poe House of Usher was the first of eight Corman movies based on Poe s stories The movie is also known as Fall of the House of Usher and The Mysterious House of Usher In the movie was listed with the United States National Film Registry as being deemed culturally historically or aesthetically significant

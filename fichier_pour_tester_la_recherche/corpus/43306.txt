 The Keine Sorgen Arena is a football stadium in Ried Austria It is the homeground of SV Ried The stadium is named after a slogan used by one sponsor of SV Ried an upper Austrian insurance company The stadium replaced the former stadium of SV Ried which had only places and was a multipurpose stadium with a running track The new stadium is only used for football and has seats of whom are a VIP area The weststand and the awaystand offer only standingroom

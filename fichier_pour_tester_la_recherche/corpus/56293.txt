 The Birth of Venus is a painting by Sandro Botticelli Venus He pictured her standing on a shell because Venus was said to have been born from foam on the sea in Stanze per la giostra a poem written by Angelo Poliziano The painting shows Zephyr god of the winds holding the gentle breeze Aura blowing her toward the shore where Horae goddess of the seasons is awaiting her with a cloak She is pictured naked to show her innocence and divinity

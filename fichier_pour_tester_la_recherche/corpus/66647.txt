 Yellow is a song by English alternative rock band Coldplay The band wrote the song and Record co producer co produced it with British record producer Ken Nelson Ken Nelson It was for their first album Parachutes The song s lyrics are a reference to band vocalist Chris Martin s unreturned love The song was released across the world in June It was the second single off the album following Shiver Shiver It was the lead single in the United States The single reached number four in the UK Singles Chart This gave the band their first top five hit in the United Kingdom Helped by heavy rotation and usage in promotions the song made the band very popular Various recording artists worldwide have since made their own versions of Yellow It remains one of the band s most popular songs

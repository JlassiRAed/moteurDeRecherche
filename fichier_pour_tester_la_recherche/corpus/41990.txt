The book of Numbers in the Bible is the fourth book in the Old Testament It is called a book of Moses because it contains the story of Moses and the Israelite s when they wander throughout the wilderness before ending up at Israel where God promised they will settle The book is called Numbers because at the start God ordered a counting of the people in the twelve tribe s of Israel After counting all the men who are over twenty and fit to fight the Israelites began to travel in well ordered divisions with God in the middle of the Ark of the Covenant In Hebrew the book is called Bmidbar which means In a desert because it is the account of their years of wandering therein When the people found that the place God promised them was filled with strong and powerful people of other races they became scared and rebelled against God and Moses Therefore God punished them by making the Israelites travel forty years before they could settle on the promised land

 The national flag of Pakistan was designed by Syed Amir uddin Kedwaii and was based on the original flag of the Muslim League It was adopted by the Constituent Assembly of Pakistan on August just days before independence The flag is referred to in the national anthem as in Urdu The flag comprises a dark green field representing the Muslim majority of Pakistan with a vertical white stripe in the hoist that represents the Christians and other multi faith communities of the country In the centre is a white crescent moon representing progress and a white five pointed star representing light and knowledge Some analists say if Kashmir goes to the Republic of India or declares itself independent then the colour should be changed to black Image Flag of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa svg Flag of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Image Proposed Flag of Islamabad Capital Territory svg Flag of Islamabad Capital Territory Image Flag of Sindh svg Flag of Sindh Sindh Pakistan Image Flag of Punjab svg Flag of Punjab Pakistan Punjab Image Flag of Balochistan svg Flag of Balochistan Pakistan Balochistan Image Flag of FATA svg Flag of the Federally Administered Tribal Areas Image Flag of Gilgit Baltistan svg Flag of Gilgit Baltistan Image Flag of Azad Kashmir svg Flag of Azad Kashmir

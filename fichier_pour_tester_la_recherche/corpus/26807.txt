 Thespis or The Gods Grown Old is an operetta by Gilbert and Sullivan It is the first of their fourteen operettas It was commissioned in by John Hollingshead manager and owner of the Gaiety Theatre in the Strand It was not well rehearsed and lasted only performances It was generally well received by the critic s Thespis takes place on Mount Olympus and tells of a theatrical company which takes over the duties of the ancient Greek gods Most of the music has been lost making it impossible to reconstruct the operetta

 Ursa Minor is a constellation in the northern sky It is often called the Little Bear or the Little Dipper Just like the related constellation Ursa Major the North Star called Polaris The ancient astronomer Ptolemy included Ursa Minor in his list of constellations It is now one of the List of constellations modern constellations Ursa Minor is special because it includes the north celestial pole However this will change after several hundred years because the Earth will change the direction of its Axis axis That movement is called Precession of the equinoxes precession

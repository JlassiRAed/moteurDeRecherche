 Centaurus is a constellation in the southern hemisphere of the night sky It is named after a centaur which is a creature that is half man and half horse The astronomer named Ptolemy listed Centaurus when he made a list of constellations Centaurus is also one of the constellations that were made by the International Astronomical Union In the direction of Centaurus is the Shapley Supercluster or Shapley Concentration This is the largest concentration of galaxies in our nearby universe

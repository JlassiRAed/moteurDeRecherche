 Diodorus Siculus or Diodorus of Sicily was a Ancient Greece Greek historian He is known for writing the universal history Bibliotheca historica much of which survives between and BC It is arranged in three parts The first part covers Mythology mythic history up to the destruction of Troy It is arranged geographically describing regions around the world including Egypt India Arabia Greece and Europe The second part covers the Trojan War to the death of Alexander the Great The third part covers the period to about BC The title Bibliotheca meaning library shows that he was using the work of many other authors

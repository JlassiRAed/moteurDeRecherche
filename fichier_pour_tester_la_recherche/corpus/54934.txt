 Joshua Lederberg transduction artificial intelligence and space exploration He was just years old when he won the Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for discovering that bacteria can mate and exchange genes He shared the prize with Edward Tatum and George Beadle who won for their work on the genetics of the fungus Neurospora In addition to his contributions to biology Lederberg did extensive research in artificial intelligence This included work in the NASA experimental programs seeking life on Mars and the chemistry expert system Dendral

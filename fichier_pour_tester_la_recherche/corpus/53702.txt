The hoatzin is a tropical bird found in swamp s river forests and mangrove s of the Amazon Basin Amazon and the Orinoco delta in South America They are the only bird which eats leaves as a main part of their diet Their gut has special adaptations which help their digestion The hoatzin is also notable for having young with claws on two of their wing digits They are the only living member of the family Opisthocomidae Its close relatives probably died out long ago There has been much debate about the hoatzin s relationships with other birds

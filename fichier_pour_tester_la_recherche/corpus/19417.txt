 Aurelius Augustinus Augustine of Hippo or Saint Augustine was a philosopher theologian and was bishop of the North African city of Hippo Regius for the last part of his life Augustine is a very important person in the development of Western Christianity and is considered to be one of the church fathers He described the concepts of original sin and just war In Roman Catholic Church Roman Catholicism and the Anglican Communion he is a saint and Doctor of the Church and the patron of the Augustinians Augustinian religious order Many Protestantism Protestants especially Calvinism Calvinists consider him to be one of the theological fathers of Reformation teaching on salvation and divine grace grace In the Eastern Orthodox Church he is a saint and his feast day is celebrated annually on June Among the Orthodox he is called Blessed Augustine or St Augustine the Blessed Blessed here does not mean that he is less than a saint but is a title bestowed upon him as a sign of respect The Orthodox do not remember Augustine so much for his theological speculations as for his writings on spirituality

 Ojo de Culebra is the sixth studio album by Mexico Mexican Latin singer Lila Downs This album was recorded in New York and Mexico All the songs on this album were composed by Lila Downs and Paul Cohen except I Envy The Wind It includes a version of Black Magic Woman by Peter Green In Ojo de Culebra they mix instruments and electronic music It includes collaborations with Enrique Bunbury La Mari Mercedes Sosa and Ramon Gutierrez among others

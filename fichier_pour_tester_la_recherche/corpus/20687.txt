The long nosed bandicoot Perameles nasuta is a member of the bandicoot family which lives along the east coast of Australia from Victoria Victoria to Queensland They live in small hidden nests in among shrubs hollow logs or old rabbit burrows Bandicoots are protected by law but the areas they like to live in have been cleared for farming or housing They are also hunted by dogs cats and foxes Rabbits have eaten many of the plants and made it unsuitable for bandicoots

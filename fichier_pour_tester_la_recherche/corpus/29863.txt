 Wipeout is an United States American Reality television reality game show that airs on American Broadcasting Company ABC Contestants compete in a series of obstacle courses in what is called the world s largest obstacle course The series started on June It is hosted by John Henson and John Anderson with Vanessa Lachey acting as a on location host There are four rounds of gameplay In the first round players play in the qualifier obstacle course The players who complete the course in the fastest time move on to the next round In the second round players compete in another obstacle course The first players to complete the obstacle course move on to the third round The third round is a third obstacle course Here the first players to complete the obstacle course move on to the fourth and last round The fourth round is the Wipeout Zone The players left compete in one more obstacle course to complete the course in the fastest time The player who completes it in the fastest time wins

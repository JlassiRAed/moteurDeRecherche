 Canadian football is football generally played in Canada and follows much the same general rules as American Football Compared to American football the field is slightly longer and wider by yard and the endzones are to yards deeper There are also only downs instead of and the field goal posts are at the front of the endzone instead of the back There are players per team on the field during play More than one player may be in motion before the snap of the ball and the rules are less strict about players position relative to the line of scrimmage

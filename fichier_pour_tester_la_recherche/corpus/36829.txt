 Snooker is a billiards sport for two players It is popular in the United Kingdom the Commonwealth and parts of Asia It is played on a large table that is covered with a smooth green material called baize and has holes or pockets in each of the four corners and in the middle of each of the long side cushions The players use long thin sticks called cue s to strike the white ball The cue ball must always be hit first when attempting a shot at the target ball There are target balls in total red balls Each target ball must be aimed or potted into one of the pockets in sequence A red ball followed by a colour then a red ball again and so on until only the coloured balls are left The colours then have to be potted in the following order yellow green brown blue pink and black The player who scores more points wins the game or frame A match consists of an agreed number of frames

A tariff is a tax charged on goods as they pass between one country and another A tariff can be placed on goods being brought into the country and goods being export ed from the country to another It is usually done to make money for the government It may also be done for protectionism Protectionism makes it easier for local products to sell by making products from foreign countries more expensive Often one government will have an agreement with another not to place any tariffs on goods that are traded between them This kind of agreement is called free trade

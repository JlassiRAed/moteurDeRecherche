 Promotion and relegation are terms used in many sport s Being promoted means moving up a league to one of a higher status This usually happens when a team wins their league Relegation normally happens to the team at the bottom of the league at the end of the competition They are moved down a league to one of a lower status For example Team A wins the league and is promoted from the Second Division to the First Division Team B finishes last in the First Division and is relegated to the Second Division

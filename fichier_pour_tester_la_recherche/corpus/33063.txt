 Bruksvallsloppet is an international cross country skiing event held in Bruksvallarna Sweden over a Friday Sunday in November every year The event acts as Cross Country Skiing World Cup FIS World Cup preselection The competitions are international During some seasons the event is the final before the World Cup opens During other seasons the event instead occurs between World Cupe events This depends on if the FIS World Cup starts by late October or if it begins by mid late November In Sweden the event is generally considered the first major competition of the season

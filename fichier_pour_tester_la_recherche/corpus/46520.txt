The Constitutional Union Party was an American political party It was one of four major parties to have candidates in the election right before the American Civil War It was made up of former Whig Party Whigs and Know Nothing Party Know Nothings who hadn t joined the Republican Party as well as people from the South who wanted the Union to stay together It had one idea keeping the United States together It ran John Bell for President and Edward Everett for Vice President Most of its vote came from the South and the border states

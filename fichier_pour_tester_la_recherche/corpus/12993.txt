Atri is a mythological Hindu sage and one of the seven Rishis Hindu scriptures have named them as saptarishis that is the seven sages The seven sages were Kashyapa Atri Vashitha Vishvamitra Gautam Jamdagni and Bharadvaja According to Hindu mythology these seven sages had become like divine beings Ancient astrology of Hindus identified these seven sages with the stars of the sky the Great Bear Their wives formed the Pleiades

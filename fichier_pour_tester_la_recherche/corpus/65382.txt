 The Abaco Islands are in the northern Bahamas The islands are Great Abaco and Little Abaco together with the smaller Wood Cay Elbow Cay Lubbers Quarters Cay Green Turtle Cay Great Guana Cay Castaway Cay Man o War Cay Stranger s Cay Umbrella Cay Walker s Cay Little Grand Cay and Moore s Island The Abaco Islands make up five of the Districts of the Bahamas North Abaco Central Abaco South Abaco Moore s Island and Hope Town Towns in the islands include Marsh Harbour Hope Town Treasure Cay Coopers Town and Cornishtown The islands are a noted base for sailing activities in the Bahamas as well as resort tourism The Abacos Islands in the Bahamas have four national parks Pelican Cays Land Sea Park Abaco Wild Horse Preserve Man O War Cay and Great Guana Cay Marsh Harbour Airport and Treasure Cay Airport serve the islands

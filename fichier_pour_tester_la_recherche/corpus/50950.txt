The Dick Cheney hunting incident happened on February when then Vice President of the United States U S Vice President Dick Cheney shot Harry Whittington a year old Texas Lawyer attorney while participating in a quail hunt on a ranch in Kenedy County Texas Both Cheney and Whittington called the incident an accident On February Whittington suffered a non fatal heart attack and atrial fibrillation due to at least one lead shot pellet lodged in or near his heart Cheney did not speak publicly about the incident until February in an interview with Fox News Channel Fox News Early reports indicated that Cheney and Whittington were friends and that the injuries were minor Whittington has since told the Washington Post that he and Cheney were not close friends but acquaintances and has not said if Cheney apologized

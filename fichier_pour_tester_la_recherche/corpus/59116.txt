 Idukki is one of the Districts of Kerala districts of Kerala state India January At that time the district headquarters was at Kottayam In June it was moved to Painavu Idukki which lies in the Western Ghats of Kerala Idukki is the second largest district in area but has the lowest population density Idukki has a vast forest reserve area more than a half of the district is covered by forests The urban areas are densely populated whereas villages are sparsely populated

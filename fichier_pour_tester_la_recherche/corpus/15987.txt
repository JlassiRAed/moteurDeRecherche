The House of Lorraine was a family that originated from the Duchy of Lorraine in modern day France The family later ruled the small duchy of Lorraine itself and later ruled the Grand Duchy of Tuscany The Empress Maria Theresa was married to a Francis I Holy Roman Emperor Duke of Lorraine thus uniting the house of Lorraine with the far more powerful House of Habsburg The House of Lorraine the main and now only remaining line known as Habsburg Lorraine is one of the most important and was one of the longest reigning royal houses in the history of Europe Currently the house is headed by Karl Habsburg Lothringen the titular Emperor of Austria King of Hungary Bohemia Galicia and Lodomeria Croatia Illyria as well as the titular King of Jerusalem

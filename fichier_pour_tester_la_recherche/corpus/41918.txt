 Audience of One is a song by the United States American punk rock band Rise Against written by the band s vocalist Tim McIlrath It is the second single from their album Appeal to Reason Getting to number four on the Billboard Alternative Songs chart Audience of One is Rise Against s third highest charting single behind the last single from Appeal to Reason Re Education which got to number three and the third single Savior In mid October the Los Angeles California L A radio station KROQ began playing Audience of One The song is also used in Guitar Hero World Tour

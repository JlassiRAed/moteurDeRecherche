 First Canadian Place is a skyscraper in Toronto Ontario Canada At metres or feet it is Canada s tallest skyscraper and the eleventh tallest building in North America It is the third tallest free standing structure in Canada after CN Tower also in Toronto and the Inco Superstack in Sudbury Ontario First Canadian Place is located in Toronto s financial district at the northwest corner of King and Bay streets the centre of Canadian financial industry It is home to the Toronto headquarters of the Bank of Montreal the oldest Canadian bank

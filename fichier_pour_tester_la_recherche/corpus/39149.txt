 Swiss Family Robinson is a Walt Disney movie It is based on an book of the same name by Johann David Wyss The movie stars John Mills Dorothy McGuire James MacArthur Tommy Kirk Kevin Corcoran Janet Munro and Sessue Hayakawa as a pirate chief The movie is about a shipwrecked family building an island home It was filmed on Tobago and in England It was released just before Christmas It was a huge box office hit and is one of the top movie moneymakers of all time

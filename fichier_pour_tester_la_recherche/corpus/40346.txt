 Patnitop or Patni Top is a hill top tourist location in Udhampur District on the National Highway A on the way from Udhampur to Srinagar It is located in the Shiwalik belt of the Himalayas and is in Jammu and Kashmir Kashmir The Chenab River river Chenab flows close to this location Origin of the name Patnitop is a distortion of the original name of Patan Da Talab meaning Pond of the Princess In olden times a pond existed in the meadows and the kings princess often used to have her bath there Part of the pond still exists near the youth hostel The Britishers probably could not get the name correctly in their revenue records and over the years it changed from Patan Da Talab to Patnitop geo stub

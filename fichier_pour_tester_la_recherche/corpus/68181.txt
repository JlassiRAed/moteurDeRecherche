 Art Deco is a style of decoration decorative art design and architecture of the s and s in Europe Art Deco followed another design style Art Nouveau which was influenced by Organic matter organic plant like forms Art Deco was one of the first styles of modern architecture It was influenced by different styles and movements of the early th century Neoclassical architecture Neoclassical Constructivism Futurism Later Art Deco of the mid to late s is also sometimes called Streamline Moderne One idea behind art deco architecture was to apparently streamline buildings the same way you would streamline a car for aerodynamics The style was much more common in commercial buildings than in houses many bank s school s and library libraries were built in this style Most of the public buildings built by the Works Progress Administration during the Great Depression are in the Art Deco style

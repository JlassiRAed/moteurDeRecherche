The Skagit River is a river in Canada and the United States It flows about from British Columbia to the state of Washington where it flows into the Pacific Ocean north of Seattle The river begins by flowing south It then enters North Cascades National Park where it is dam med by Ross Dam which forms Ross Lake a reservoir The river then leaves the lake and flows into Diablo Lake also a reservoir It then keeps flowing southwest through a deep river canyon known as the Skagit Gorge It then meets its largest tributary the Sauk River and then turns west

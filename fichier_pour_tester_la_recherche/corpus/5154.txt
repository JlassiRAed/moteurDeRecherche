 Masai Mara National Reserve is a National Park in Kenya East Africa It was established in as a wildlife sanctuary and has been made larger since then Masai Mara is a game reserve type of park meant to protect the animals on the savannah of the Serengeti It is named after the Maasi people who are the traditional people of the area Big as it is the Masai Mara National Reserve is only a fraction of the Greater Mara Ecosystem The Mara is effectively the northern continuation of the Serengeti National Park game reserve in Tanzania

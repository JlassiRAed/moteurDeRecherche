A book cover is any protective covering used to bind together the pages of a book The covering may be of paper or hard paper boards or even plastic The boards may be covered with paper cloth or leather They may be lamination laminated with a plastic layer onto paper There is a distinction between hardback s and softback s which are usually sold at different prices Hardbacks may be covered with a dust jacket designed to attract readers Softbacks are almost always designed to attract sales in bookshop displays The evolution of printing methods has made the production of books very much cheaper than was the case with early printed works As a result books are bought and read in great numbers They compete against each other in the marketplace The function of book design is partly to help the reader but even more to persuade the reader to buy the book

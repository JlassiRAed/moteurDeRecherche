 Guitar Hero is a music video game released on November for the PlayStation The game uses a guitar shaped controller that allows players to simulate the playing of rock music Guitar Hero has earned comparisons to Dance Dance Revolution played with a guitar Despite a high price for the guitar game bundle Guitar Hero was very successful and had several sequels Guitar Hero II and Guitar Hero III Legends of Rock as well as other games in the music game genre The game features artists such as David Bowie Judas Priest Sum and Deep Purple

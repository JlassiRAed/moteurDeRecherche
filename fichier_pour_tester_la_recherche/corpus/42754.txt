A light bulb is a device that produces light from electricity In addition to lighting a dark space they can be used to show an Electronics electronic device is on to direct traffic for heat and many other purposes Early people used candle s and oil lamp s for light Crude incandescent lights were made in the early and middle th century but had little use Improved vacuum pumps and better materials made them shine longer and brighter late in the century Electric generator stations brought electricity to urban and later rural areas to power them Later gas discharge lights including fluorescent lights use less electricity to make more light

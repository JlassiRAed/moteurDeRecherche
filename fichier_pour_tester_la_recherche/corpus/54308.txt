 Mehrabpur is a town and a tehsil in the Naushahro Feroze District of Sindh in Pakistan It is on the main railway line between Karachi and Lahore It is a junction station with a disused branch line to Naushahro Feroze On December at am an overcrowded express train travelling from Karachi to Lahore derailed km south of Mehrabpur killing over people and injuring hundreds more Derailment seems to have been caused by defective rails

About species including Digitalis cariensis Digitalis ciliata Digitalis davisiana Digitalis dubia Digitalis ferruginea Digitalis grandiflora Digitalis laevigata Digitalis lanata Digitalis leucophaea Digitalis lutea Digitalis obscura Digitalis parviflora Digitalis purpurea Digitalis thapsi Digitalis trojana Digitalis viridiflora Digitalis is a genus of about species of herbaceous perennials shrubs and biennals that are commonly called foxgloves The genus is native to Europe western and central Asia and northwestern Africa The term digitalis is also used for preparations containing cardiac glycosides particularly digoxin extracted from plants of this genus

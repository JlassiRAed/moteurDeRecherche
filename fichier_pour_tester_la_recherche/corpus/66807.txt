The Punggol LRT Line is the third line of the Light Rail Transit LRT system in Singapore Its first phase comprises a km line with stations The Punggol LRT Line serves the residents in the town and urban planning area of Punggol Punggol LRT station the centerpiece of the line is located directly above the Punggol MRT station thus linking up the Punggol LRT Line to the MRT system that spans the entire island of Singapore

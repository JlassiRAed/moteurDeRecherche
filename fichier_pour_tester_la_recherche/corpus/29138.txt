 Perranzabuloe is a coastal civil parish and a hamlet in Cornwall United Kingdom The hamlet south southwest of Newquay The name of the parish comes from the medieval Latin Perranus in Sabulo meaning Piran in the sand Piran was a Christian evangelist who founded a church in the seventh century near the coast north of Perranporth The ruins of this church were rediscovered in the th century The medieval parish church of Perranzabuloe was over a long period completely lost in sand dunes A new church had to be built further away from the coast

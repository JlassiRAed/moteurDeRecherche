 Glial cells are support cells for the nervous system In Greek Glia means glue Glia are said to be the glue of the nervous system Glia occur in both grey matter and white matter Glia are thought to have four main functions To surround neurons and hold them in place To supply nutrients and oxygen to neurons To insulate one neuron from another To destroy pathogen s and remove dead neurons Glia may have more functions than just these four

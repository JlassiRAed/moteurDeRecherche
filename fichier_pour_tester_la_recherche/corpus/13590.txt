 Kirby s Dream Collection is a compilation video game for the Wii game console It was developed and published by Nintendo It was made to honor the th anniversary of the Kirby series It includes six games in the Kirby series along with a booklet about the history of Kirby games and a soundtrack It was released in Japan on July and in North America on September It includes the following games Kirby s Dream Land Kirby s Adventure Kirby s Dream Land Kirby Super Star Kirby s Dream Land Kirby The Crystal Shards The game includes challenge stages based off of those seen in Kirby s Return to Dream Land It also includes a museum that shows every title in the Kirby series and has three episodes from Kirby Right Back at Ya an anime based off of the Kirby series

The term economics of fascism refers to the economy economic systems that are used by Fascism fascist regime s In general Fascist regimes wanted to exert strong control over the economy but also defend private property this attitude is generally known as dirigisme The first cases of dirigisme happened in France One of the first such movements is known as Colbertisme after Jean Baptiste Colbert Colbert was the chief minister of finances of King Louis XIV of France The ideas of Social Darwinism had a strong influence on Fascist regimes Some people and enterprises did better than others those that did better needed the support of the state those that did not do as well needed to be eradicated Since most Fascist regimes were also influenced by racism racist ideas the general tendency was to support the companies of the people of the better race Gaetano Salvemini an Italian historian said in that fascism made taxpayers responsible to private enterprise because the State pays for the blunders of private enterprise Privatizing profits and socializing losses Profit is private and individual Loss is public and social

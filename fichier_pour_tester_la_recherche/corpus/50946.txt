 Talagang Tehsil is a subdivision Punjab province of Pakistan The tehsil which is headquartered at the city of Talagang is administratively subdivided into Union Councils of Pakistan Union Councils Talagang Tehsil is one of the largest tehsils in South Asia as it contains villages Some of the villages near Talagang town are Cheenji Adhlaka Jatla Lawa Dhurnal Pachnand Mogla Malakwal Tamman Akwal Chowkhandi Dhook Goria Perha Fathial and Dhook Kund

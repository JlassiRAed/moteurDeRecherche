 The Acrididae are the main family of grasshopper s with of the species of the entire suborder Caelifera The Acrididae are best known because all locust s are of the Acrididae However not all Acrididae are locusts locust is a term which is only given to those grasshoppers which swarm Acrididae grasshoppers have relatively short and stout antenna e and drum like tympana on the side of the first abdominal segment The name Acrididae is derived from Greek akris meaning locust

 Maria Stuarda is an Italy Italian opera in three acts by Gaetano Donizetti libretto by Giuseppe Bardari after Friedrich Schiller s play Maria Stuart It was first performed in Naples on October under the name of Buondelmonte due to censorship problems and in its original version in Milan on December One of Donizetti s finest work it is notable for the confrontation scene between Queen Mary Queen of Scots and Queen Elizabeth I of England which in real life never took place

 Trust Company is an United States American alternative metal post grunge band from Prattville Alabama Prattville Alabama Trust Company was formed in by lead singer Kevin Palmer and drummer Jason Singleton They originally named the band Down but changed it later to avoid being confused with Canada Canadian punk rock band Sum Palmer and Singleton would later recruit bassist Josh Moates and guitarist James Fukai After they released a few demos and a self titled album the band was signed to Geffen Records They released their debut album The Lonely Position of Neutral on July The album featured the single Heart in My Hands

 How Pop Can You Get was the first and only album by United States American band The Bats The Bats Members were Jon Brion Bill Murphy and Don Riff Fertman It was recorded in New Haven and West Haven Connecticut and released on the Gustav label in the fall of Called The Pop album of the Year by OP The Lost Music network the record received much critical acclaim and was even a Billboard magazine Recommended LP when reviewed in the October issue The album however ultimately failed to chart

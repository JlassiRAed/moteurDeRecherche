 Stockholm syndrome is something that sometimes happens to people who are victims of kidnapping In Stockholm syndrome the victim starts feeling loyalty toward the person who kidnapped him or her and even start to have feelings for the person It got its name from a bank robbery in Norrmalmstorg in Stockholm Sweden in There was a bank robbery where two thieves held four people as hostage hostages When the thieves were arrested six days later the hostages hugged and kissed the men who had kept them under control for six days

 The Battle of Troina was a battle that happened between July and August It was part of the Allied invasion of Sicily during World War II Forces of the United States II Corps part of the U S Seventh Army had violent battles around the town of Troina It is in the central portion of Sicily along the Caronie Mountains The battle was in the hills and mountains surrounding Troina The Wehrmacht Germans had fortified positions They hid in these positions and fired weapons at the Allies

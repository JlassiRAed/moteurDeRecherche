 Death in Venice is an opera by Benjamin Britten It is based on the story Death in Venice by the Germany German writer Thomas Mann The libretto for the opera was written by Myfanwy Piper It was the last opera that Britten wrote It was first performed at Snape Maltings near Aldeburgh England on June Britten was too ill to conduct it himself The main part of Aschenbach was sung by Sir Peter Pears

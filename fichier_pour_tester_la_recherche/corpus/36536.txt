On the Internet a header is extra information about a group of data When someone sends an email this information might contain when the email was sent or some data about who sent the email You can think of email headers like the address and postage stamp on the outside of an envelope sent through regular mail Sometimes this happens with other data too Computers often communicate with each other to transmit other kinds of data like how to display a webpage or the contents of an image When this happens other headers also come along with it

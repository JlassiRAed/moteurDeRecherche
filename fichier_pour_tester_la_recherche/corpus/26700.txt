 Jurisprudence is the theory and philosophy of law Scholar s of jurisprudence or legal philosophers hope to obtain a deeper understanding of the nature of law of legal reasoning legal systems and of legal institutions As jurisprudence has developed there are three main aspects with which scholarly writing engages Modern jurisprudence and philosophy of law is dominated today primarily by Western academics The ideas of the Western legal tradition have become so pervasive throughout the world that it is tempting to see them as universal Historically however many philosophers from other traditions have discussed the same questions from Islamic scholars to the ancient Greeks

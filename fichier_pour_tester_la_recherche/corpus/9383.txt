 Coblentz is a small crater that can be found on the far side of the Moon to the south of the much larger crater Bolyai This crater has a circular edge but it has been worn away by asteroid impacts This is most noticeable at the southern end where a gap exists in the rim The interior floor has very few features except for several tiny craterlets A ridge arcs from the northwest rim of Coblentz to join the southern rim of Bolyai There are several patches of dark material just to the south and southwest of Coblentz

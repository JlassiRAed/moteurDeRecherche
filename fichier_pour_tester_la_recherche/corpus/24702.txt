 Chanson is the French language French word for song The word is often used in music to mean any song with French words but it is more often used when talking about songs in which lyrics have been set to music by French classical music classical composers Very often chansons refers to the French songs that were sung in the late Middle Ages and Renaissance People who sang these chansons were called chansonniers They had various forms including ballade Rondeau rondeau and virelai Some composers at the time liked to set popular poetry to music The earliest chansons were for two three or four voices many of them being for three voices By the th century most were for four voices Sometimes the singers were accompanied by musical instrument instruments

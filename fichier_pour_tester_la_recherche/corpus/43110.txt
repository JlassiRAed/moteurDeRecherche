 Scoutcraft is a form of woodcraft skills used in the woods and the backcountry Though it has been practiced for hundreds of years it was first called scoutcraft by Robert Baden Powell and Ernest Thompson Seton who wrote books about it about a hundred years ago What scoutcraft is has varied throughout the years and from country to country For example in England scoutcraft once included citizenship while in America it once included indian lore and signaling At present scoutcraft includes camping hiking first aid and survival skills wilderness survival

 Screamo is a musical genre which came from both emo and hardcore punk in the mid s This genre usually consists of minor chord progressions on guitar with both dissonance and reverb the time signatures used are usually not in instead bands and artists use less used time signatures to create a more tense feel This genre has both a mellow and chaotic feel to it The vocals are usually high pitched screams and lyrics are often poetic or political An example of some Screamo bands include Saetia Orchid Circle Takes the Square Envy pageninetynine and Thursday Cite web

 Amour is an Academy Award winning France French movie The director was Michael Haneke It starred Jean Louis Trintignant as Georges Emmanuelle Riva as Anne and Isabelle Huppert as their daughter The movie was about an elderly couple Anne and Georges who are retired music teachers who live alone together Anne suffers a stroke which paralyses her on one side of her body After she makes Georges very angry Georges grabs a pillow and suffocates Anne to death It received many positive reviews Roger Ebert gave it four out of four stars It won an Golden Globe Award and an Academy Award for Best Foreign Film

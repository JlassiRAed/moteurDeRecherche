The  International Braille Chess Association   is an organization for  Blindness blind  and Visual impairment visually impaired   chess  players The International Braille Chess Association originated unofficially in with the organization of the first international correspondence chess tournament for blind players by  Reginald Walter Bonham the tournament included players representing countries It first organized a tournament in with representatives from seven countries Today it has grown to encompass over member nations around the world  The IBCA hosts two important competitions the  Blind Chess Olympiad  and the Blind World Chess Championship

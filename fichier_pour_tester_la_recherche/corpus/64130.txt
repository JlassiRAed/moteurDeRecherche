In stratigraphy bedrock is the common term for solid Rock rock under the surface of the Earth Above the bedrock is usually an area of broken and weathered rock soil sand pebbles etc The top of the bedrock is known as the rockhead and identifying this via excavations drilling or Geophysics geophysical methods is an important task in most civil engineering projects Loose material on top can be extremely thick so that the bedrock lies hundreds of meters below the surface A solid geologic map of an area will usually show the rock that would be exposed at the surface if all soil or other superficial deposits were removed

 Linkinhorne is a parish and village in southeast Cornwall United Kingdom The village itself is at and is approximately four miles south of Launceston The area is bordered by the River Inny in the north east and Bodmin Moor to the west The valley of the River Lynher runs through the parish The parish is largely rural but west of the B road from Launceston to Liskeard it includes part of Caradon Hill once an industrialised mining area The parish church of St Melor is built of granite and dates from the th century

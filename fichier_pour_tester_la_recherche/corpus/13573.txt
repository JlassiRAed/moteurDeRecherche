 Tempora is the codeword for a computer system used by the British Government Communications Headquarters It collects most internet communications from optical fibre fibre optic cable s The data base of messages can be searched later as required Tempora uses intercepts on the fibre optic cables that make up the backbone of the Internet Thus it gets large amounts of internet users personal data without any individual suspicion or targeting The existence of Tempora was revealed by Edward Snowden He is a former American intelligence contractor who leaked information to former The Guardian Guardian journalist Glenn Greenwald in May This was part of Snowdon s revelations of government sponsored mass surveillance programs Data collected by the Tempora program is shared with the National Security Agency of the United States cite newsEwen MacAskill Julian Borger Nick Hopkins Nick Davies James Ball

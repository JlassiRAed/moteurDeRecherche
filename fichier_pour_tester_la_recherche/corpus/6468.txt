 The Salzkammergut is a resort area in Austria It goes from Salzburg City of Salzburg to the Dachstein mountain range The main river of the region is the Traun River Traun The name Salzkammergut means Estate of the Salt Chamber This is from the Imperial Salt Chamber the group who ran the precious salt mines in the Habsburg empire With its many lakes and mountains the Salzkammergut has many opportunities for water sports bathing hiking cycling and golf Parts of the area around Hallstatt were listed as a World Heritage Site in The World Heritage Site includes the towns Hallstatt Obertraun Gosau and Bad Goisern

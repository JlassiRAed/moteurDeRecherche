 Loch is a Gaelic word for lake It is commonly used in Scotland There are also lochs in Ireland and the word is sometimes used in other coutries The wors is used both for lakes and for narrow or landlocked arms of the sea Sometimes the term sea loch is used for saltwater lochs Many other lochs are normal freshwater lakes like Loch Lomond Biggest by surface area Loch Lomond Deepest and largest by volume Loch Ness

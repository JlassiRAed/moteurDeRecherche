 Moulding is the Shape shaping of raw material using a solid frame of a particular shape called a pattern It is a process used in manufacturing A mould is a hollow container that will hold the liquid material until it turns solid The liquid hardens or sets inside the mould adopting its shape Materials commonly used in moulding include plastic metal glass and ceramic Tyre s are made using moulds where the molten rubber is poured into the mould and then left to set Moulds can vary greatly in size They are usually made of hardened steel which can resist Wear wearing

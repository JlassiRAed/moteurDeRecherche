A protected page is a page where normal users are prevented from editing at all Only a user with Help Sysops and permissions sysop permissions can edit a protected page Likewise only sysop users can protect a page in the first place or unprotect the page to lift the restriction There are several reasons why a particular page might be protected Page protection limits collaboration on the wiki content and should be avoided where possible For sysop information see Help Protecting and unprotecting pages

 DP meaning D iesel P rototype number was a prototype Type mainline diesel locomotive built in by English Electric at their Vulcan Foundry in Newton le Willows to demonstrate its wares to British Rail ways As the Deltics were then in production it was decided to produce the locomotive on the same production line the bodyshell used for DP reputedly being the eighteenth made While DP looked like a British Rail Class Class locomotive in outline there were many detail differences particularly the large bodyside radiator vents at one end and the single roof fan as opposed to the four symmetrically placed fans on the Deltics These differences were the external effect of DP being totally different from the twin engined Deltics internally being the forerunner of the British Rail Class Class

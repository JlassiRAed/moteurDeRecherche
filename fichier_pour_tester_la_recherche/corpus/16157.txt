A tailstrike is when the tail of an airplane hits the runway This can happen during takeoff or landing During takeoff a tailstrike can happen if the pilot raises the nose too early and the plane cannot take off in time During a landing a tailstrike can happen if the pilot raises the nose too high during landing Tailstrikes usually do not have serious effects such as killing people However this means that the airline has to spend money to repair the plane before it can fly again If the plane is not repaired correctly it may cause Fatigue metal fatigue and in the end the plane can crash

 Ocean liner is the name given to ship s that are used to transport people or goods These ships travel along given routes they usually have a schedule Usually ocean liners travel long distances The ships used for short distances for example across a Canal channel a lake or between an island and the coast close by are called ferry ferries Ocean liners are used for transportation Cruise ship s are different they are used for pleasure In the second half of the th century ocean liners have been replaced by aircraft more and more

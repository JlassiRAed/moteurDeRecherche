 The Old City It contains some of the most Sacred site important places for three major religion s These include the Church of the Holy Sepulchre for Christian s the Temple Mount for Jew s and the Dome of the Rock and al Aqsa Mosque for Muslim s The Old City formed the whole city of Jerusalem until when a Jewish neighborhood was built outside the Western Wall The Old City is historically divided into four quarter s although the current quarters were introduced only in the th century Today the Old City is divided into the Muslim Quarter the Christian Quarter the Jewish Quarter and the Armenian Quarter After the war in the Old City was occupied by Jordan and the Jewish people living in the city were forced to move out During the Six Day War in Israel Occupied territory occupied the Old City and the rest of East Jerusalem Today Israel still controls the whole area which it says is part of its national Capital capital In Jordan asked UNESCO to make the Old City a World Heritage Site

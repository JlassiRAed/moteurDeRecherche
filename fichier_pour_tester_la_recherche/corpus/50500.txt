 Mantarur is a hilly area in the southwest of the Northern Territory Australia It forms the southeastern end of the Olia Chain of mountains This outcrop includes Butler s Dome It is on Pitjantjatjara land part of the Petermann Aboriginal Land Trust A family outstation was established here in the s at a wikt rockhole rockhole in the western slopes It has since been abandoned Frank Rees George led a prospecting expedition through the area between and George gave names to several of the landforms including Butler s Dome which is believed to have been named after Richard Butler who was a minister of South Australia

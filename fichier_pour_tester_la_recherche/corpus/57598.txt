 The Tata Institute of Fundamental Research is a research organization in India It is for studies in mathematics and science It is located in Mumbai TIFR was started by Jenhangir Ratanji Dadabhoy Tata It is now government funded This institute has various departments like physics chemistry astronomy and mathematics One of its founding members was physicist Homi Bhabha It was inaugurated by Prime Minister Sri Jawaharlal Nehru Professors in this organization are recruited from institutes including the Illinois Institute of Technology

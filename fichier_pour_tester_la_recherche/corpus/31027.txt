The Beat Generation was a movement of writer s poet s and artist s during the s and s who liked the beat of jazz and bebop music which became a kind of soundtrack to much of their lives and work People associated with the movement were called Beats or sometimes Beatniks a reference to Communism The term came from novelist Jack Kerouac who was sometimes called The King of the Beats Other Beat writers were William S Burroughs and Allen Ginsberg

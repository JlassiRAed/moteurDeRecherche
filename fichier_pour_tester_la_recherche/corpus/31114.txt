 Inbreeding is a term in genetics meaning the crossing of closely related animals or plants Self fertilisation in plants is the most extreme kind of inbreeding It is quite widespread in plants which carry both male and female flower s on the same plant Inbreeding is the opposite of outcrossing which is the mating of unrelated members of the same species Other terms are inbred strain a group of organisms so inbred that they have little or no genetic variation and inbreeding depression which is the decreased fitness brought about by inbreeding For human beings inbreeding is a very destructive trait and almost all cultures in the world have attempted to restrict inbreeding e g by prohibiting marriages between first cousin s The ultimate form of inbreeding is called incest It is mating with blood relatives within the nuclear family

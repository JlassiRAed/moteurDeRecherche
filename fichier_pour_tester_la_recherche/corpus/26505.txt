 Stanley Peak is a m high mountain located in the Ball Range at the northeastern section of Kootenay National Park in Canadian Rocky Mountains The peak is visible from the Trans Canada Highway and Highway Stanley Glacier on the northeast face of the peak can be seen up close by following a hiking trail into a hanging valley between the peak and a southern outlier of Storm Mountain Storm Mountain Stanley Peak can be ascended from a scrambling route by late summer but involves much routefinding among the many ledges and gullies on the north face Climbing routes travel the north and northeast faces

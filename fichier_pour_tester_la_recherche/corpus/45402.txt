 Polecat is the common name for several medium sized Mustelid s They do not form a single clade the name is given to several similar species The European polecat is typical and is the only one native to the British Isles Ferret s are the domesticated form of European polecats In the U S the term polecat is often applied to skunk s which are only distantly related to the European polecat The reason is that polecats give off a scent which some people think is offensive

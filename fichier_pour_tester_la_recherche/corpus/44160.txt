The Ore Mountains are a mountain range in Germany and the Czech Republic They form the border between both countries for km extending from the western border of Saxony to the Elbe river The Ore Mountains are famous for many Christmas traditions As the ore s silver and tin were not found any more in the Mining mines former miners had to look for new ways to feed their families In addition to lace making and weaving the inhabitants went into wood carving Nutcrackers smoking men pyramids are some of many Christmas goods made in the Ore Mountains Seiffen in the East Ore Mountains was a centre of the wooden toy industry

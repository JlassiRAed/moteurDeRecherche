 Caudipteryx was a small feather ed dinosaur It was related to Oviraptor but looked more like Archaeopteryx It was a relative of Protarchaeopteryx It was found in China It was a small insectivore with long thin legs less than a metre long It had a fan of tail feathers and a fringe of feathers along the trailing edge of each arm They are very like bird feathers with a central stalk and vanes on either side Its arms were too short for it to fly

 Libera is an all boy singing group from England It is directed by Robert Prizeman There are roughly members aged to They go to different schools in South London Most members come from the parish choir of St Philip s Norbury in South London Most boys have unchanged boy soprano voices The boys in their early teens sing the lowest parts Libera s songs are either new compositions by Robert Prizeman or new arrangements of classical and contemporary tunes Prizeman often composes and arranges pieces with individual soloists in mind He does this to emphasize the variety of characteristics and qualities in the boys voices

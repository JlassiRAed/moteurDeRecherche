 Huun Huur Tu is a music group from Tuva a Russia n Federation republic on the Mongolia n border Huun Huur Tu performs Tuvan throat singing throat singing in which the singers sing more than one note at a time One of the notes sometimes sounds like a flute whistle or bird but is actually a person s voice The group mostly uses native Tuvan musical instrument instruments such as the igil khomus However in recent years the group has started using western instruments such as the guitar Huun Huur Tu s music is mostly Tuvan folk music but they also experiment with Western instruments and electronic music

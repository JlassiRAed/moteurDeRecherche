 Publius Cornelius Scipio Africanus was a general in the Second Punic War and a politician of the Roman Republic He was best known for defeating Hannibal of Carthage Scipio won the battle of Zama in North Africa He got the last name Africanus and became known as one of the best commanders in military history The battle was a complete disaster for Carthage who had to beg for peace and were given humiliating terms by Rome

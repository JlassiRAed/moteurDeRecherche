 Payphone is a song performed by United States American pop rock band Maroon released on April under A M Octone Records in the United States It serves as the lead single on the band s fourth studio album Overexposed Overexposed set to be released on June The song was written by producer Benny Blanco bandmates Adam Levine Maroon James Valentine and featured vocalist Wiz Khalifa The song was recorded in southern Hollywood Los Angeles California

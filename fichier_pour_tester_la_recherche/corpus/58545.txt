In Greek mythology the Golden Fleece is the Wool fleece of the gold haired winged sheep ram It is in the story of Jason and his group of Argonauts They set out on a quest ordered by King Pelias to get the fleece so that Jason can rightfully claim the throne of Iolcus in Thessaly The story is very old it was current in the time of Homer in the th century BC There are many different versions of the story In later versions the ram is said to have been the child of the sea god Poseidon and Themisto Jason and the Argonauts starring Todd Armstrong

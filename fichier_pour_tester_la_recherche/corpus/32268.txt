 Mecca or Makkah called Mecca in older texts officially Makkah al Mukarramah from the Red Sea Mecca is the holiest city in the Islam ic world Every year millions of Muslims take a pilgrimage to Mecca This is called the Hajj where they follow in the footsteps of Muhammad All Muslims who are able to perform the Hajj are expected to do so at least once in their life The Kaaba and Masjid al Haram the holiest mosque in Islam is here Mecca is closed to non Muslims except for some journalists reporting on the Hajj

A housemaid or formerly maidservant or in current use maid is a female employed in domestic worker Maids perform typical domestic chores such as cooking ironing washing cleaning the house grocery shopping caring for pet s and taking care of children like a nanny In many places in some poor country countries maids often take on the role of a nurse in taking care of the elderly and people with disability disabilities Many maids are required by their employers to wear a uniform In June International Labour Organization has made its Convention No for domestic worker s including maids

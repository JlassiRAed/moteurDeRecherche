 Taoyuan City is a special municipality of Taiwan Republic of China in the northwestern part of Taiwan the island next to New Taipei City Taoyuan District is in this municipality and serves as the seat of Taoyuan City Taoyuan means peach garden since the area used to have many peach blossoms Taoyuan is the native home to the Pingpu tribe of Taiwan aborigine Aborigines It is now an important industrial region of Taiwan Taipei Taoyuan International Airport which serves Taipei is inside this county

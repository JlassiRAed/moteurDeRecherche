The Sunda clouded leopard or Bornean clouded leopard is found on Sumatra and Borneo It is a genetic ally distinct species related to the Clouded leopard The Sunda clouded leopard is the largest cat in Borneo weighing around to kg The canine teeth are two inches long which in proportion are longer than other living felines Its tail which may be as long as its body helps it balance when running and jumping In Borneo they occur in lowland rainforest In Sumatra they appear to live more in hilly montane areas It is not known if there are still Sunda clouded leopards on the small Batu islands close to Sumatra

The Clan McDuck is a family of ducks in the Disney fictional universe based on the style of Scottish families The most famous member is Scrooge McDuck According to the timeline of Don Rosa the clan has been known since AD when a member yet unnamed clan sold the stone for the construction crew of Hadrian s Wall The Clan Scrooge was originally called Clan MacDuich but left the Gaelic spelling of the name in and became the Clan McDuck Donald his sister Della Duck and his sons Huey Dewey and Louie are the descendants of the clan through their maternal lineage

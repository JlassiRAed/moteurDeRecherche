The Galleria Nazionale d Arte Antica or National Gallery of Ancient Art is an art gallery in Rome Italy located on two sites the Palazzo Barberini and the Palazzo Corsini The Palazzo Barberini was designed for Pope Urban VIII a member of the Barberini family by Italian architect Carlo Maderno Allegory of Divine Providence and Barberini Power to glorify the papal Barberini family The Palazzo Corsini formerly known as Palazzo Riario is a th century palace that was rebuilt in the th century by architect Ferdinando Fuga for Cardinal Neri Maria Corsini For a partial list of artworks see Palazzo Corsini entry The gallery s collection includes works by Gian Lorenzo Bernini Caravaggio Giovanni Baglione Hans Holbein the Younger Hans Holbein Perugino Nicolas Poussin Giulio Romano Raphael Carlo Saraceni Giovanni Battista Tiepolo Tiepolo Tintoretto and Tiziano

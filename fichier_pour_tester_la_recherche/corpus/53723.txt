The Populist Movement also known as the People s Party was a movement in late th century America It grew out of groups such as the Grange Movement It was largely a movement of farmers who were poor and felt big businesses such as railroad s were to blame Its leaders included Thomas Watson and William Jennings Bryan The populists had a series of demands known as the Omaha Platform Here are some of the things the Omaha Platform wanted Though the Populists never and only controlled a few state governments for a few years many of the demands of the Omaha Platform such as the income tax and direct election of Senators were later done as part of the Progressive Movement
